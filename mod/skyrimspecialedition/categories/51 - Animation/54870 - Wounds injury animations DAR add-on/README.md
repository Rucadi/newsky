# Wounds injury animations DAR add-on
- Author: samuelga24
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/54870


﻿![](https://i.imgur.com/3goVYUy.png)﻿
  
This animation mod is an add-on for the mod [Wounds](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/17581) created by [IronDusk33](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/5182604?tab=about+me), Wounds is an immersive mod that adds a list of many localized injuries you can get from combat, this animation mod adds visual display of the injuries you are suffering, using Dynamic animation replacer this mod plays unique and different animations depending of the injury or combination of injuries you have.
  
  
So, this DAR mod will boost your immersion by dynamically changing almost all animations depending of what injury you got in the heat of the combat, whether a broken right arm, cut left leg, concussion, broken right leg, concussion, cut left arm, etc, or many of them together, reflecting visually and at the moment the effects of battle on the player.
  
  
The goal of this mod is to always have a set of animation that displays your current and specific injured state for every posible movement you do, whenever you move, jump around, fight or do all that can be done in skyrim, given the big scope of this project it is still a work in progress, it currently has over 5000 animation files.

**Compatibility**
  
  
-Compatible with other DAR mods, these animations ONLY play when you get injured and only then will this mod "conflict" with other animations mod you might have (which is logic-wise what you want to happen), once your injury is treated or fully healed your other animations will be back as normal; this mod has higher priority than most DAR mods on purpose, this is to prevent these animations from being unninmersively interrupted by actions you shouldn't be able to do when injured/cripled.
  
  
-Compatible with combat mods like Skysa, ABR, and such (I think, only tested Skysa and it works well), this mod has animations for when the character is wielding weapons and moving with them but does not have actual combat animations for weapons (striking/attacking). 
  
  
-[Movement behaviour overhaul](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/38950)﻿ is compatible but it causes some animations to jitter because it adds some transition animations that would not contrast with vanilla upright standing animations but can be noticed with these leaned forward animations, so use the "non- combat only" version of MBO to avoid this.
