MODS Introduction:
  
  
Combined with Bloodborne and Ninja Gaiden production out of the scythe animation, support SCAR, can be used for NPC
  
  
Support Precision
  
  
Please see the requirements for the weapons used, you can also open this directory
  
  
meshes\actors\character\animations\DynamicAnimationReplacer\\_CustomConditions\2800000
  
  
Open the \_conditions.txt inside, change the DAR conditions, and add your favorite weapons
  
  
If the animation does not work, change the 280000 folder to a larger number
  
  
  
  
You open \_conditions.txtThen you can see IsEquippedRight("BB Pack by Team TAL.esp" | 0x0000087F) OR
  
IsEquippedRight("war scythe.esp" | 0x00000800) OR
  
IsEquippedRight("war scythe.esp" | 0x00000802) OR
  
  
Add a new line IsEquippedRight("XXXXXXXXXXXXX.esp" | 0x XXXXX)
  
  
Write the name of your weapon's ESP, and the address of the weapon in the ESP,Note that some ESL weapons may not be detected
