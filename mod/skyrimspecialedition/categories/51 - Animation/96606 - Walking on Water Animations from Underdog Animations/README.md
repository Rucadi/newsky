# Walking on Water Animations from Underdog Animations
- Author: Edryu and Hiro-Tsaxaman
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/96606


﻿﻿**Walking on Water Animations from Underdog Animations**

  
This mod uses an idea found out in the comments from the mod [UNDERDOG - Animations](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/51811) that uses the sneaky walk animations, and makes them play when walking in water using [Open Animation Replacer](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/92109) and [Wade In Water Redone](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/71418).
  
  
**Credits**Thanks to Tsaxaman for making the assets from his mods open.
