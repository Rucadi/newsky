# Smooth Moveset - OAR
- Author: Smooth
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/149713


  
  
  
  
 
  
In Skyrim, most animation transitions are abrupt there's often no actual transition animation when switching between states. 
  
Smooth Moveset addresses this by adding seamless transitional animations creating a more natural and immersive experience.
  
  
  
The first feature introduces context-aware idle transitions.
  
  
When you draw your weapon, your idle animation will change depending on the situation.
  
  
If an enemy recognizes the player as hostile, a combat idle is maintained.
  
  
Otherwise, the animation smoothly transitions to a non-combat idle.

Even while your weapon is already drawn,
  
  
encountering an enemy will automatically switch your idle into a combat stance.
  
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/149713/149713-1746887784-1283380106.gif)
  
  
  
  
 
  
  
If you're using TDM (True Directional Movement), you’ll notice that locking onto an enemy
  
  
forces a combat idle, and unlocking returns you to the non-combat stance—all with smooth transitions.
  
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/149713/149713-1746887804-305560703.gif)
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/149713/149713-1746887804-1557441494.gif)
  
  
  
  
These context-sensitive idles also apply after attacking
  
  
if combat ends, the animation returns to non-combat idle, and vice versa.
  
  
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/149713/149713-1746887778-2011809813.gif)
  
  
  
  
  
  
Small but immersive details like stop animations after movement are also included,
  
  
adding a subtle sense of weight and realism.
  
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/149713/149713-1746887812-688407404.gif)
  
  
  
  
  
  
IED (Immersive Equipment Displays) to manage draw animations reliably without scripting conflicts
