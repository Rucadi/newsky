# Don't sheathe bound weapons DAR animations
- Author: samuelga24
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/70354


DAR animation mod that changes the way you and NPCs sheathe bound weapons to a dispel like way, also edited the way some weapons are summoned to fit with 2h weapons better, works with all vanilla bound weapons and also with the bound weapons added by the following mods (they are not requirements):
  
  
-[Bound Armory Extravaganza](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/14063)-[Animated armoury](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/15394)
  
-[One handed warhammers](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/62271)
  
-[Forgotten Magic Redone SE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/12711) 
  
-[Mysticism - A Magic Overhaul](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/27839)-[Arcanum - a new age of magic](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/23488)
  
-[CROSS\_Crucible\_SE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/20203)
  
-[Spellsiphon - Immersive Combat](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/26627)
  
-[Simple bound weapons expansion](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/21013)
  
-[Colorful Bound Weapons SE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/37268)﻿
