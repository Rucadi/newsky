# Vanargand Animations II - Unarmed Normal And Power Attacks
- Author: Verolevi
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/117328


Vanilla replacer version of my [Unarmed animations](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/110676), replaces the Unarmed left and right normal and power attacks, along with the dual power attack.
