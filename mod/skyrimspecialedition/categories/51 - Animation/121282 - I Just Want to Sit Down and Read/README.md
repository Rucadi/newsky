# I Just Want to Sit Down and Read
- Author: GiraPomba
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/121282


**[font=Georgia]INTRODUCTION[/font]**

  
This mod allows the book reading animation called from other mods to play in situations where it wouldn't be normally possible. Such as riding a horse, sitting cross legged, at a bar stool and etc...
  
  
The process is not automatic, mods need to be patched to call the animations.
  
  

**[font=Georgia]REQUIREMENTS[/font]**

  
- [Offset Movement Animation - Nemesis - Modders Resource](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/110408)
