# Drunk or drugged animations OAR
- Author: samuelga24
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/62191


This OAR mod dynamically plays drunken animations whenever you get intoxicated by using the alcohol/drug system of any of the different mods listed down below, adding the so-needed visual display of being intoxicated aside from the blurry screen and the occasional stumbling. It also has animations for NPCs for those mods that get NPCs drunk as well.
  
  
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
[left][/left]
  
I edited some vanilla animations to have them look like drunk animations for a lot of the actions you do in Skyrim, there are idle, running and walking, sneaking, seated in chairs, tables and bar stools animations, also there are animations for when weapons are drawn, when using fists and magic.
  
  
This mod does not have scripts as it uses Open animation replacer to play the animations as long as the intoxication effects from the any of the mods are active.

**Recommended mods**
  
  
-[Drink it off](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/54498) or [Stress and Fear](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/116522)﻿. Your adventures and combats around Skyrim ﻿stress you which can cause you to become sluggish (debuffs), giving you a reason to drink (and become drunk). 
  
-[Skyrim on skooma](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/80975) it adds many fun events whenever you use skooma.
  
-[Eating animations and sounds](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/42602) This mods displays the exact food or drink you are eating instead of the mug for all drinks and the bread for all food.
  
﻿-[Time flies](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/39426) advances the time when you do activities (drinking and eating included).
