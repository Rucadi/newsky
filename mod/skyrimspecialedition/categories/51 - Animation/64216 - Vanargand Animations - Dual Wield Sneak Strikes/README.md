# Vanargand Animations - Dual Wield Sneak Strikes
- Author: Verolevi
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/64216
This is the **Axe/Mace** (Strikes) dual wield version, and will replace the following sneak animations: 
  
  

  
* While dual wielding and holding at least one Axe/Mace in either hand the Sneak movement, the dual attack and the special 3-hit power attack, as opposed to vanilla, the character will now remain in a stealthy position while performing these attacks.

  
* While holding an Axe/Mace in the left hand, the left hand normal and power attacks.

  
* While dual wielding and holding an Axe/Mace in the right hand, the right hand normal, power and forward power attacks.
  
[left]Note that this can be used alongside [Vanargand Animations - Sneak Strike Attacks](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/55420)﻿ which will now automatically play its version of the right hand animations only when not dual wielding to avoid clipping with left hand equipment.
  
  
[size=3]For Swords/Daggers animations instead, check [Dual Wield Sneak Thrusts](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/64577)﻿﻿.[/left][/size]
