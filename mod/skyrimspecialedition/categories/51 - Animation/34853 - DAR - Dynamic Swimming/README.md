Hey there. This small plugin uses the Dynamic Animation Replacer - Framework to change the Swimming-Animation depending on Stamina-Level and Armor-Weight.
  
  
  
[font=Comic Sans MS]*Features:*[/font]
  
- When the Player has low Stamina or wears heavy Armor the Vanilla (almost drown xD)- swimming animation will be played. 
  
- When conditions above are not given then the [Stronger Swimming Animations](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/32625)﻿ (Without kick) will be played.
  
- When you wear heavy Armor and have the heavy Armor Perk "Conditioning" [Stronger Swimming Animations](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/32625)﻿ (with kick) will  be played.
  
  

Please check the Original Page ([Here](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/32625)﻿﻿) for Preview-Animations of the Swimming Animations.

  
  
*[font=Comic Sans MS]Install:[/font]*
  
Just Unzip the archive into your Data-Folder or use a Mod Manager of your choice to do so.
  
  
*[font=Comic Sans MS]Uninstall:[/font]*
  
Just remove the files or uninstall the mod via your Mod Manager.
  
  
*[font=Comic Sans MS]Requirements:[/font]*
