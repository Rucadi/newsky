**How to Use**
  
The combination is MFFF. Furniture is none.
  
You can play it from the "TRT" icon (the crown icon) in the OStim menu.
  
  

  

**Installation**
  
Install with your mod manager (MO2, Vortex, etc.).
  
Open Nemesis (Unlimited Behavior Engine) and click Update Engine.
  
Click Launch Nemesis Behavior Engine to apply the patch.
