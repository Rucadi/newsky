# Unofficial shadowman2777's mod's Ostim conversions
- Author: shadowman2777 - SimonPhil
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/73853


Do not direct message me problems or questions or Mods to convert (I won't convert them). Other people might have the same question or issue, so make a post on this or a bug report if you need. Trust me I am happy to get your comments, but on the page its about, not as a DM.
  
  
I will no longer be making any new files for OG or NG. I will only be supporting Standalone supported conversions as of 11/15/2024.
  
  
shadowman2777 made all of these, I have only converted the scrips to start scenes from OStim instead of Flower Girls or SexLab scenes.
  
  
Some of his files with extra scripts and sound files are not included with my packages they are still needed from one of his pages. My conversions are based off of the SexLab version, if his contains voice files this will require the SexLab version or you will have to manually fix the voice file path.
