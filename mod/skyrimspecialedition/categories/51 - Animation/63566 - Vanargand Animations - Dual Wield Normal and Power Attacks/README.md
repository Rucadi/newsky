# Vanargand Animations - Dual Wield Normal and Power Attacks
- Author: Verolevi
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/63566
These animations will replace, While holding a one handed weapon in the left hand, the standing, walking and running left hand normal attack, the standing and directional left hand power attacks including the special/combo dual attacks with all one handed weapons.
  
  
This is the vanilla replacer version, to be used as is, or to be placed in your folder structure of choice (Nemesis PCEA, Combo/Stances Animation Framework, etc...)[size=3].
  
[size=3]Compatible with both the Special Edition and the Anniversary Edition versions[/size] of the game.
  
Not compatible with SkySA.
  
  
[/size]Comparison with vanilla
