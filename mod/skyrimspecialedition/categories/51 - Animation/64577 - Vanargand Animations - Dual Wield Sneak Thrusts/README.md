# Vanargand Animations - Dual Wield Sneak Thrusts
- Author: Verolevi
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/64577
This is the **Swords/Daggers** (Thrusts) version, and will replace these sneak animations:
  
  
[size=3]

  
* While dual wielding daggers and/or swords the Sneak movement, the dual attack and the special 3-hit power attack, as
    
  opposed to vanilla, the character will now remain in a stealthy position while performing these attacks.
While holding a Sword or a Dagger in the left hand, the left hand normal and power attacks.
  
* While dual wielding and holding a Sword or a Dagger in the right hand, the right hand normal, power and forward power attacks.
  
This can be used alongside "[Vanargand Animations - Sneak Thrust Attacks](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/55031)" which will now automatically play its version of the right hand animations only when not dual wielding to avoid clipping with left hand equipment.
  
[/size]
  
For Maces/Axes animations instead, check [Dual Wield Sneak Strikes](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/64216)﻿.
