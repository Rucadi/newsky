# Water Douses Fire
- Author: Kamikaze
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/34344


[size=5]**DESCRIPTION**
  
While fighting dragons I found myself wanting to jump into the water to keep myself from being burned alive, only to find out it has no effect. I don't care if it's magic, Water Douses Fire!
  
  
You need quick reflexes to take advantage of this because fire effects usually don't last long (~1-2 secs). You also need to be fully submerged seeing as your whole body is on fire.
  
  
The plugin is flagged as an ESL so it won't take up precious slots in your load order![/size]
  
  
**[size=5]COMPATIBILITY**
  
Compatible with all fire magic effects that use the MagicDamageFire keyword as they should.[/size]
