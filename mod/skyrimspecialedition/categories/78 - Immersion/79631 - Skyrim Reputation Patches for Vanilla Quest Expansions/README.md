# Skyrim Reputation Patches for Vanilla Quest Expansions
- Author: DK
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/79631


If you enjoy quest expansions such as JaySerpa's [House of Horrors - Quest Expansion](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/57285) or Parapets' [Save the Icerunner - Lights Out Alternate Routes](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/34681) but also use Skyrim Reputation and want your new choices to be reflected in SR's reputation system, I have a collection of integration patches that does just that!

**Please make sure to overwrite the respective mods being patched as well as Skyrim Reputation itself. Some patches include plugins and others are just updates to Skyrim Reputation's scripts to reflect new quest paths.**
  
For details on how each patch affects your reputation, the FOMOD installer provides a brief explanation for each mod but
  
generally speaking, I tried to follow the scoring scheme established by Skyrim Reputation.
