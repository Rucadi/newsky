# Oddities From Morrowind - CC Integration
- Author: CptnJTK
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/63582
> *"**Rare trinkets and the finest oddities from Morrowind!" - Brand-Shei, prolific liar*

Adds ingredients, food, drinks, and clothing from Morrowind to the shop inventory of Brand-Shei in the Riften grand plaza, Revyn Sadri at Sadri's Used Wares in Windhelm, and Ambarys Rendar at New Gnisis Cornerclub in Windhelm.
  
  
Items are selected from the Dragonborn DLC as well as the Rare Curios, Ghosts of the Tribunal, and Netch Leather Armor Creations.
