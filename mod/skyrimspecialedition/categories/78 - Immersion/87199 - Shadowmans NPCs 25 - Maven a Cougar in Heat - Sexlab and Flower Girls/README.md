A small mod about Maven Black Briar.
  
Inspired by **[THIS](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/82944)** replacer that I recently came across and started using.
  
  
-180 lines of dialogue.
  
  
Talk to Maven anytime. After going through the (lengthy) introductory scene, new dialogue options will appear.
  
-Ask Maven for some coin. She will provide it in exchange for some service.
  
-If you get a bounty in Riften, ask her to remove it for you. In exchange for a little favor of course.
  
-Maven will randomly gift you a bottle of Black Briar Reserve.
  
  
*Hemming and Maul will be jealous of the player's relationship with Maven, and will make comments about it.*
  
  
Keep in mind that when the sex scene starts, you will be teleported to Maven's bed in Black Briar Manor. Even if she is the Jarl.
