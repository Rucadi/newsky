# IED - Weapons Repositioner
- Author: Ungeziefi
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/65148


**Description**
  
Since the original [Weapons Repositioner](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/58273) is replaced by IED, this preset will replicate the functionality of Repositioner by applying offsets to various types of equipment when certain backpacks are equipped or generally the backpack slot.
  
  
More accurate positions are available for the backpacks from [Seasoned Traveller Armor](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/70222) (Including the walking stick) and [Vitrium](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/57242).

**Requirements**
  
[Immersive Equipment Displays](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/62001)
  
  
**[b]How to use**[/b]
  
1) Open the IED menu (Backspace by default)
  
2) Click "View" on the far top left
  
3) Select "Gear Positioning"
  
4) Open the preset drop-down list and select "IED - Weapons Repositioner"
  
5) Hit "Apply"
