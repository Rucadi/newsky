# burn Burn BURN
- Author: tarlazo
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/56892


**For those who need more explanations despite the self-explanatory images.**
  
 
  
Southwest of Evergreen Grove, the aftermath of what is likely a dragon attack reveals two dead horses, burnt corpses, crates, sacks, and a burning carriage. A chest remains intact amidst the wreckage.
  
  
**Vanilla** :( -- The fire and some smoke will disappear after the player has already viewed the area, leaving behind an unscathed but forever-smoking carriage, the burnt corpses, the dead horses, the sacks, a freshly-painted self-refilling chest, and the crates.
  
  
**Modded** :) --The fire, all the smoke, the dead horses, the sacks, the chest, and the crates will disappear after the player has already viewed the area, leaving behind only a burnt carriage and the corpses.
  
  
  
**FAQ**Q)  Did someone steal the disappeared stuff?
  
A)  No, it turned to ashes.
  
  
Q)  What happened to the ashes?
  
A)  The wind blew them away.
  
  
Q)  Where are the horses?
  
A)  In the the belly of some wolves.
