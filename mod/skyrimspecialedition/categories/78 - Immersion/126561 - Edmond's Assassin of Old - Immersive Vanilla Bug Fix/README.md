# Edmond's Assassin of Old - Immersive Vanilla Bug Fix
- Author: Edmond Noir
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/126561


Assassin of Old Remastered
  
- An Immersive Vanilla Bug Fix -

  
  
  
Description
  
By vanilla you will get a quest to find "The Assassin of Old" which is a 1000+ year old assassin found in a Nordic Ruin. When you find him he looks like a Nord man who died yesterday. After looking in the Creation Kit it looks like Bethesda intended to make him a skeleton but likely it was too much time and money to implement. That stated, I decided to go in and fix this visual inconsistent and un-immersive bug myself remastering the Assassin of Old to look the part. Additionally the mod is also flagged ESL.
