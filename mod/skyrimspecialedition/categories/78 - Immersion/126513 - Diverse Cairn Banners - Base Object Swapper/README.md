[font=Georgia]**Diverse Cairn Banners**[/font]- Base Object Swapper -

  
  
**Description:** ﻿
  
 ﻿In the vanilla game, banners on rock cairns only come in red. With this mod installed, you have a chance to encounter more variations of those banners including Generic Blue/Green/Yellow banners, Hold banners (Major 9 holds), and Guild/Faction banners (Thieve Guild, Dawnguard, Arkay Temple, Redoran, Telvanni, Dunmer Temple). Each banner will appear at appropriate locations. For example, hold banners only appear in the proper hold or surrounding settlements and guild banners appear near the hold that houses the guild.
  
 ﻿This mod utilizes vanilla assets so your banner retexture will work out of the box.
  
  
**Requirement:**
