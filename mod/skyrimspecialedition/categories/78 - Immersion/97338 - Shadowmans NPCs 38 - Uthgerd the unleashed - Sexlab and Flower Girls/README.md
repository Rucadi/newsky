# Shadowmans NPCs 38 - Uthgerd the unleashed - Sexlab and Flower Girls
- Author: shadowman2777
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/97338


Talk to Uthgerd at her house after you defeat her for the new dialogue to appear. After a short intro scene, new options will appear.
  
  
-330 lines of dialogue.
  
-You can workout with her (Pushups, squats, situps). Dont activate that when you are too close to her to avoid clipping.
  
-Uthgerd has now installed a pullup bar at her house. She will randomly do pullups. You can also use the pullup bar yourself, and Uthgerd will make comments. (Spliced vanilla lines)
  
-You can ask her to cook for you.
  
-You can ask her if she minds you exploring other women.
  
-You can ask her to bathe together. Use this dialogue in the right spot, in the water.
  
-Various sex scenes. You can dominate her, or be dominated.
  
-You can ask her to unequip her weapon when she is inside a building.
  
  
My last big mod with vanilla audio. Enjoy.
