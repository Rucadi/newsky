# Break The Lock - OIF
- Author: kuroishitam
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/154901


A simple mod which enables you to break a locked door or container by bruteforce it. Hit anything locked with any sword, axe or mace (or explosive barrel if you use optional file) to break the lock. To balance it, you need to powerattack the object 3 times (lock level novice to adept), 4 (expert level) or 5 times (master level) and there is also a 10-30% chance to break the lock depending on what weapon and the lock difficulty (configurable via json).
  
  
**Zhitsak version**. This version changes the chance to be also depending on the level of 1-handed & 2-handed skill in addition to lock level. 2-handed weapon has 5% higher chance to break the lock than with a 1-handed weapon. The number of interactions has also been changed from 3 to 2.
  
  
If you successfully break the lock, there will be a notification above and the object will do the opening animation. Any lock will be breakable, no matter what the lockpick difficulty is. Quest related locked item is excluded as for not to break any quest. Breaking in front of npc will turn them hostile to you, so beware.
