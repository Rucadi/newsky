# Northern Scenery - Bleak Falls Barrow
- Author: HeilingerKrest
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/94647


[font=Arial]**Overview :**
  
  
Northern Scenery is a continuous project dedicated to enhancing the visual appeal of Skyrim's outdoor environments and landscapes. Currently, this mod focuses on improving this specific area:
  
  
[/font]

  
* [font=Arial]**Bleak Falls Barrow :** The path & entrance to Bleak Falls Barrow. [/font]

[font=Arial]
  
My intention is to **extend these enhancements to other locations :**  dungeons, Nordic ruins, other regions, and the areas surrounding cities. As I delve deeper into the lore, I am committed to further advancing this mod.
