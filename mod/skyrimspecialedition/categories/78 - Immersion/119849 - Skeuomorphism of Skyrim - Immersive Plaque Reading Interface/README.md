**Description**
  

  
* Read plaques in a 3d menu that looks like the plaque instead of a dialog box.
Supports:
  
[list]* [Dark Brotherhood Tenets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Five_Tenets)
    
  [list]
Improved model.
  
* Higher resolution texture with bigger text, for reading.
Model compatible with other tenet retextures if you let them overwrite the texture.
  
* Compatible with any mod that adds more tenets to the world, such as [Wyrmstooth](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/45565)﻿.

[\*]Way Shrines along the 7,000 steps to [High Hrothgar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:High_Hrothgar).
  
[\*]Plaques in the [Labyrinthian](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Labyrinthian)﻿.
  
[\*]Puzzle tablets in [Vahlok's Tomb](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vahlok%27s_Tomb).
  

* Includes untranslated [Dragon Language](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dragon_Language) and translated English versions as [Lost Legacy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Legacy) quest progresses.

[/list][\*]No worldspace or cell edits.
  
[\*]Uses [Base Object Swapper](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/60805)﻿ and minor script edits.
  
[\*]Uses some vanilla textures. Objects may look different depending on your texture mods.
  
[\*]Text layout may look different depending on your font mods.
  
[\*]ESL.
