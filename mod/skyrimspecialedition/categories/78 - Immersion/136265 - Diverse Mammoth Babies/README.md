# Diverse Mammoth Babies
- Author: Bectile-Mihail-Etayorius
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/136265


I love Mihail's baby mammoth models, and wanted to give them more variety for fun. I made red, black and white textures with different eye colors, which makes a total of four colors they can be. The odds of the white ones appearing are about half that of the other colors.
  
  
This mod **does not add** extra baby mammoths to the game, only recolors existing ones. May be considered for the future.
  
  
This mod supports mammoth calves from:
  
**[Baby Mammoths - Mihail Monsters and Animals (SE-AE version)](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/100006)[Mammoth Expansion - Mihail Monsters and Animals (SE-AE version)](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/69391)[SkyTEST - Realistic Animals and Predators SE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/1104)**SkyTEST 2022 Redux ESL version  (no longer on Nexus)
  
  
*If you are only using the original SkyTEST you must have one of Mihail's mammoth mods installed for the meshes and textures or you will have invisible baby mammoths.*
  
  
If the camera shake from the baby mammoths annoys you, check out my other mod here [Quiet Mammoth Babies - No Camera Shake - OAR DAR versions](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/123582)
