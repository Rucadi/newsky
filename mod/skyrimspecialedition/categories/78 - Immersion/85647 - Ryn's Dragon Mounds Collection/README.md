 Ryn's Dragon Mound Collection
  
  
[/font][font=Georgia]A collection of all 22 dragon mound overhauls, flagged as individual ESL files for maximum compatibility and modularity.
  
Following a few rules of design I set for myself, all of Skyrim's dragon mounds are more consistent in their structure, more exciting to explore, and above all, should be **mostly incompatibility free,** due to the the way I handled navmeshing and terrain edits.
  
[/font][font=Georgia]Each location, while sharing similarities with the rest, is unique in its own right, often with dynamically changing aspects that correspond with the main questline.[/font]
