**Description**
  

  
* NPCs now react to your aiming your **bow** or **crossbow** at them.
    
  [list]
They will **complain**. This is **voice acted** for generic vanilla voice types. Non-vanilla voice types will not say anything.
  
* Less confident NPCs will temporarily **flee**, depending on their **confidence level** and **level**.
Confident NPCs will **draw weapons** before **reacting violently**.
  
* You will get **bounty** if things escalate. Happens after **10 seconds of continuous aiming**.

[\*]The following NPCs will react:
  

  
* NPC must be in **interaction/activation distance** and facing the player.
**Followers** are excluded.
  
* NPCs with **2 or higher relationship rank** with player are excluded.
NPCs that are **in combat** or **hostile** to player are excluded.
  
* NPCs must **detect** and have **line of sight** to the player.
NPCs that are **restrained** by special furniture such as shackles or binds are excluded.
  
* NPCs won't react when the **player is in combat**.
