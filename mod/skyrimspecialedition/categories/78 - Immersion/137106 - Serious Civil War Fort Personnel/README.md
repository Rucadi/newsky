# Serious Civil War Fort Personnel
- Author: Enodoc
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/137106


The Civil War forts now feel like proper military garrisons, equipped with non-combat personnel to support the cohort of soldiers stationed there. Quartermasters can be found tending the armories and forges, Acolytes heal the wounded and look after the spiritual needs of the soldiers, Cooks tend to the mess halls and kitchens, and maybe occasionally, the odd enemy soldier can be found in the fort prisons.
  
  
Features
  
  

  
* Quartermasters can be found at each fort's forge.
Acolytes (priests/healers) can be found near a new shrine inside each fort.[list]
  
* Imperial forts now all have a Shrine of Stendarr, patron of the Imperial Legion.
Stormcloak forts now all have a Shrine of Talos, patron of the Stormcloak Rebellion.
  

  
[\*]Servants and Cooks can be found in the kitchen areas of some of the larger forts.
  
[\*]Prisoners can occasionally be found in the prison cells of forts you control.
  
[\*]All prison cells are now locked, with keys that can be found somewhere in the fort.
  
[\*]Fort Sungard now has a Jailor, who looks after the prison cells in the Oubliette.
  
[\*]Fort Sungard Oubliette has an additional prison cell, bringing the total to four.
  
[\*]The stables at Fort Hraggstad and Fort Amol are now equipped with horses.
  
  
[\*]Many more soldiers now have a sleep cycle added to their daily schedule.
  
[/list]
  
Civil War Forts
  
  
This mod only touches the nine Civil War Forts - [Fort Amol](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Amol) (Eastmarch), [Fort Dunstad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Dunstad) (The Pale), [Fort Greenwall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Greenwall) (The Rift), [Fort Greymoor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Greymoor) (Whiterun), [Fort Hraggstad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Hraggstad) (Haafingar), [Fort Kastav](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Kastav) (Winterhold), [Fort Neugrad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Neugrad) (Falkreath), [Fort Snowhawk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Snowhawk) (Hjaalmarch) and [Fort Sungard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Sungard) (The Reach). Other locations are not affected.
