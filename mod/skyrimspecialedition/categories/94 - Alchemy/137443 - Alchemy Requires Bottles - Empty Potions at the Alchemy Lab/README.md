# Alchemy Requires Bottles - Empty Potions at the Alchemy Lab
- Author: GiraPomba
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/137443


**[font=Georgia]INTRODUCTION[/font]**

  
  
**Mod 1:** Press Shift + E (or your Dynamic Activation key) at an Alchemy Lab to discard potions and poisons and get empty bottles in return.
  
**Mod 2:** Displays the number of empty potion bottles left in the Crafting Menu.
  
  

**[font=Georgia]REQUIREMENTS[/font]**

  
  
- [Alchemy Requires Bottles Redux](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/44053)
  
- [Dynamic Activation Key](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/96273)
