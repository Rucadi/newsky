# Alchemy Requires Bottles Redux
- Author: Pragasette
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/44053


[font=bebas\_neuebook][Classic Edition﻿](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/106155) • Special Edition • [GitHub﻿](https://github.com/pragasette/alchemy-requires-bottles-redux)﻿[/font]

  
  
  
[heading]Features[/heading]
  

  
* Require and consume an *Empty Potion Bottle* to craft a potion or a poison.
Purchase *Empty Potion Bottles* from apothecaries.
  
* Get back an *Empty Potion Bottle* when consuming a potion or a poison.
Forward changes from [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/266).
  
* Support *Mortar and Pestle* from [Campfire](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/667) and [Complete Alchemy and Cooking Overhaul](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/19924).
The plugin is flagged as ESL and doesn't count against the ESP limit.
  

  
  
[heading]Requirements[/heading]
  

  
* [SSEEdit](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/164)

  
  
[heading]Installation[/heading]
  

  
* Use your mod manager of choice, [Mod Organizer 2](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/6194) is recommended.
Use [SSEEdit](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/164) to ensure changes applied to merchant chests are preserved, in case another mod is editing the same records: see [xEdit Conflict Detection and Resolution](https://tes5edit.github.io/docs/5-conflict-detection-and-resolution.html) and [Patching](https://www.reddit.com/r/skyrimmods/wiki/patching) to know more.
