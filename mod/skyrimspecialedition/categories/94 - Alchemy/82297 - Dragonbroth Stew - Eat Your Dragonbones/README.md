# Dragonbroth Stew - Eat Your Dragonbones
- Author: Guitarninja
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/82297


Cook your own Dragonbroth Stew from the corpses of dead dragons. Enjoy your own bowl today!
  
  
Requires one salt pile and a dragon bone. With survival mode enabled and the optional patch, you can use fire salts to make a hot version.
  
  
Not technically required but made to be somewhat consistent with ApothecaryFood by SimonMagus.
