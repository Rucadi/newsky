Ice floes are bobbing now. Using assets from [Isoku's](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/users/2463371) [Realistic Water Two](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/41076) Legendary Edition.
  
Ofcourse, i have permission to use them. But credits to him for making these assets a long time ago.
  
  
Plugin is esl flagged and does not require Realistic Water to work. Mod is standalone and compatible with everything.
  
Just install with favorite mod manager. Meshes will use the textures from ice mods you use. Mod also comes with it's own "cracking" sound FX and volume slider for it can be found in "settings" menu.
  
  
Thats it and nothing more.
