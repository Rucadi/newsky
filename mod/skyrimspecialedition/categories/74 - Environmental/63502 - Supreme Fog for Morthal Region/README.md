***[font=Comic Sans MS]This mod is a reworked version of [Foggy Morthal and Swamp](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/23571) and created from scratch.
  
  
I like the town Morthal very much for my own personal playthrough. But i do like to see it more like a Silent Hill kind of village. That is why i also created the mod [No Morthal Snow - Redone.](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/95749)Â Ofcourse you don't need that mod along with this mod. But all pics are made with both installed.
  
  
![](https://i.postimg.cc/25GpM6fR/Screen-Shot876.png)
  
  
So, what does this mod do?
  
It enhances the foggy weather in Morthal region including swamps and makes it always foggy. Also there will be no music playing in this region like at all.
  
This will give you Silent Hill feel when you are in the swamps of Hjaalmarch.
  
[/font]******[font=Comic Sans MS]That's it!
  
  
[/font]****[font=Comic Sans MS]**To have this mod even creepier than it already is i've made "SFfMR - The Sounds of Dawn Addon". The addon itself adds creepy critter, animal, distantFX, etc, etc that totally fits the vibe of Supreme Fog for Morthal Region and most can be heard deep in the night when all else is dead silent. But in a realistic way.**
  
  
**(This addon is incorporated as of version 1.5 of main mod)**
