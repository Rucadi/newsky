- Author: Clofas and ToosTruus
- Game: skyrimspecialedition

[font=Georgia]**﻿By Clofas & ToosTruus**[/font]
  
  
![](https://imgur.com/VJWS9Gt.png)﻿

* [font=Georgia]**Redesigns** every exterior waterfall location into something more immersive, including **Dragonborn** & **Dawnguard** DLCs[/font]
[font=Georgia]Optional plugin for **Blackreach**[/font]
  
* [font=Georgia]All new **models** and **textures** for waterfalls & rapids[/font]
[font=Georgia]Brand new **sound effects** to replace waterfalls, rapids, & rivers[/font]
  
* [font=Georgia]Optional **Water Overhaul** that complements NWF[/font]
[font=Georgia]Several **patches** included[/font]
  

[font=Georgia]**![](https://imgur.com/YnQ6Gca.png)﻿**[/font]

**Brighter** or **Darker** foam
  
  
I recommend **brighter** foam.
  
*\*Keep in mind that your **weather** and/or **ENB** will have a significant impact on
  
color and brightness of your foam\**[/font]

***Not compatible with any other water mod!***

[font=Georgia]**Sound FX**
  
[/font][font=Georgia]Includes new sound fx to replace all **waterfalls**, **rivers**, **creeks**, and **rapids**.
  
Sounds are similar to vanilla but *slightly more subtle*.
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
![](https://imgur.com/2Oe3CAB.png)﻿
  
  

  
* ***Natural Waterfalls*** includes new meshes and textures for many waterfall-related objects. Let NWF assets **overwrite everything**

[/font][font=Georgia]
  
  

  
* ***Not*** compatible with any **foam textures**. NWF meshes point to different texture paths.

If you’d like to use other foam textures, you will need to either change the meshes to point to those textures, or rename those textures to match NWF
  
  
  

  
* [font=Georgia]***Natural Waterfalls*** makes many **exterior** worldspace edits. [/font]

[font=Georgia]At most, you’ll have some clipping here and there if other mods edit the [/font]same areas, but otherwise is ***compatible*** with mostly everything.
