# Conifers for Falkreath - Nature of the Wild Lands Falkreath Forest Replacer
- Author: Laminin
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/154658


Swaps the trees in Nature of the Wild Land's Falkreath forest for coniferous trees. Optionally replace shrubs or disable them.

  
  
  

![](https://i.imgur.com/aFM9OOV.png)
  
**Features**

  
? Swaps Falkreath's forest trees for pines and spruces.
  
? Only swaps the trees in the Falkreath Forest area.
  
? ESL flagged plugin.
  
? Optionally swap small shrubs to coniferous variants as well.
  
? Optionally disable small shrubs for extra performance.
  
? Works with DynDOLOD.
  
  
  

![](https://i.imgur.com/aFM9OOV.png)
  
**Install**

  
1. Install [Nature of the Wild Lands](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/63604)
  
2. Install this mod (Conifers for Falkreath)
  
3. Enjoy
