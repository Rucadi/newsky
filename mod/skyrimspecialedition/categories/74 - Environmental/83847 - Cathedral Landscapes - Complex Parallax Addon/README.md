# Cathedral Landscapes - Complex Parallax Addon
- Author: Ent
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/83847


Cathedral Landscapes covers much of the natural terrain with a consistent look, and has excellent matching grass to boot. I'm happy to offer an addon that tries to add an additional layer of 3D detail to an already beloved mod. Please direct any questions or bug reports regarding parallax to me on this page, the authors of Cathedral Landscapes are not directly involved with this addon.
