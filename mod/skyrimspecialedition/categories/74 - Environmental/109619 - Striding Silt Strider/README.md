**How To Update**
  
If Update Info states that the marker position needs to be updated, please follow the steps below.
  
The marker used to set the travel route of the silt strider is persistent, so the position information is baked in the save data. 
  
To update marker position,
  

  
1. Disable this mod and launch skyrim
Get in interior cell and save.Then, quit game.
  
2. If you are concerned, you can use  Fallrim Tools  to clean your save data.
Activate new version of this mod.
  

**Variation of Silt Strider**
  
**Dusty**
  
  
You can take a walk with Dusty. You can not control dusty.  It moves on a defined route.
  
  
**Courier and followers**
  
  
  
  
**Wild Silt Strider**
  
  
Silt Strider have trap collision box in their feet. So, npc take damage when they touched their feet. Tamed Silt Strider do not cause damage to the player, followers or citizens. But, be careful. Wild silt striders kick away anyone who gets close.
