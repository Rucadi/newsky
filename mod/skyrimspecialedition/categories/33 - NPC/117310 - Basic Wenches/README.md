# Basic Wenches
- Author: Nerd of Prey
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/117310


[Old Skyrim version here](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/118166)

  
 **﻿﻿ ﻿~ GET READY FOR BASIC WENCHES ~**
  
  
 ﻿This mod adds a total of 20 NPC's to the game - one cute barmaid in each inn and tavern in Skyrim.
  
  
 ﻿They have high-poly heads and pretty hair.
  
  
 ﻿They wear the standard tavern wench outfit.
  
  
 ﻿They use your installed clothing, bodies and skin textures.
  
  
 ﻿All sell drinks. Most will let you rent a room. Sometimes you can ask them about rumors or work.
  
  
 ﻿They're functionally clones of the innkeepers in each region. 
  
  
  
 Also - I cannot stress this enough - ﻿This mod does not do anything else!
  
  
 ﻿It lacks the ambition - and the feature bloat - of name-brand wench mods.
  
  
 ﻿Unless bugs are identified, I don't even plan to update it.
  
  
 ﻿ESL Flagged.
  
  
 **﻿ ﻿~ THAT'S BASIC WENCHES ~**
