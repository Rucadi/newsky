# Dawn of Skyrim NPC replacer
- Author: araviss
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/140033


**![](https://i.imgur.com/fxRO3L9.png)﻿
  
﻿Dawn of Skyrim NPC replacer**

This mod gives a lore friendly makeover to NPCs added by Dawn of Skyrim. They will now fit into a modded game. [Here](https://imgur.com/a/dawn-of-skyrim-replacer-wip-BVU3DCq) ﻿is before and after, I tried to somewhat match the original look/vibe of the character, more or less. They will use whatever body, skin or eye replacer you have installed. Below are listed mods that I highly recommend for the intended look.
  
The only requirement is the original mod the replacer is for.

**UPDATE: now available for both versions!**

**Recommended mods:**
  

  
* scars: [Northborn scars](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/720) by Northborn
skin textures: BnP [male](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/65402) ﻿/ [female  ﻿](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/65274)﻿by swefrida,  [Forgotten argonian roots](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/1704)﻿ by FafnyB
  
* eyes: [Fia's eyes](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/68912)﻿ by fialka and for argonians - the eye texture from [Argonians Enhanced Remade](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/93212?tab=description)﻿ by Blackwolf24 and Krittakitty

**Instalation**
  
Just use your preferred mod manager and select what you want in fomod. Load after main mod.
