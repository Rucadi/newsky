> Khajiit are bound to the [Lunar Lattice](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lunar_Lattice), the ja'Kha'jay. The phases of [Masser](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Masser) and [Secunda](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Secunda) at birth determine the form a Khajiit takes in life.
>   
> -Jobasha, [Interview with Three Booksellers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Interview_With_Three_Booksellers)﻿

  

This mod is an addon for [Project ja-Kha'jay- Khajiit Diversity Overhaul](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/57610)﻿ to extend the effects of the Lunar Lattice to [Relationship Dialogue Overhaul](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/1187)﻿﻿. It will require Project ja-Kha'jay and Relationship Dialogue Overhaul, and [Relationship Dialogue Overhaul - Update and MCM](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/44601)﻿ (and USSEP, by extension) to work.
  
  
It is essentially a mini-addon, as it only edits one character: the female Khajiit Razita. She is a potential follower and travels with Ri'saad's Caravan.
