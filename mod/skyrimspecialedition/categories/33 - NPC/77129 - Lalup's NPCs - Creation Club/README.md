# Lalup's NPCs - Creation Club
- Author: Lalup
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/77129


**DESCRIPTION**
  
  
A visual overhaul of 109 named NPCs for the Anniversary upgrade. The goal was to enhance the appearance of these NPCs to complement similar overhauls by Pandorable, Kalilies, and the Northbourne series, by Southpawe and Zxamonxz.
  
  
The motivation behind this mod was to address the lack of options for visually overhauling these NPCs. When considering my personal load order, I was not satisfied with simply switching their head to a High Poly Head or changing their hair. Instead of waiting for someone else to address this shortcoming, I decided to resolve the issue myself. 
  
  
Please provide feedback where appropriate. This is my first time overhauling NPCs and uploading a mod to the Nexus, so please be understanding with any requests or advice.
  
  
**INSTALLATION & REQUIREMENTS**
  
  
The Anniversary upgrade is required!
  
  
Similar to the previously mentioned overhauls, this mod is designed to utilize the textures and meshes you have installed.
