This mod is part of my series to add new interactions with the non-follower NPCs and insert roleplaying opportunities for the player.
  
  
  
  
**What does this mod do:**
  
  
This mod expands Whiterun's NPCs with new dialogues and gives the player new roleplaying opportunities in **influencing the NPCs' lives.**
  
At its current state, **only Olfina and Jon** are expanded, but **more NPCs are expected to be added** in the future.
  
  
---
  
  
**Features:**
  
1. Expands Olfina Gray-Mane and Jon Battle-Born with 73 lines, revolving around their relationship and the outcome of "Missing in Action"
  
2. Choices regarding Olfina and Jon's relationship
  
3. Possibility of reconciling Olfina and Jon, or breaking Olfina and Jon apart and romance Olfina instead
  
  
This mod is **100% compatible with any mod**, including dialogue expansion mods from other authors.
  
  
**Safe to add mid-save.**
  
  
There is a potential for **future expansion** with any NPC in Whiterun, including **optional** synergy with the overarching Shezarrine Saga
