# Project Cougar - Grelod the Kind
- Author: PROJECT COUGAR
- Game: skyrimspecialedition

[font=Georgia]**PROJECT COUGAR presents:**[/font]
  
*Grelod the Kind*
  
  
-
  
  
Project Cougar is a mod collaboration by [NightmareZone](http://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/17024379) and [Canliberk](http://https//nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/5027009?tab=user+files) for the wonderful [Immersive & Adult](http://next.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/collections/xxsqm4)﻿collection. 
  
Our vision is to replace the elder women of Skyrim to be better looking but *still**mature.*
  
  
No more scary melted wax ladies. 
  
No more ugly granny hats. 
  
*Skyrim belongs to the Cougars now.*
  
  
This mod will replace Grelod the Kind.
  
The **ONLY** Grelod replacer on Nexus mods.
  
I didn't want to make her too attractive because she's a terrible person
  
Still, she looks awesome\* and terrifying
  
(\*as awesome as the most evil NPC in Skyrim can be)
  
  
It's a light Plugin so it won't eat into your limit.
  
She has her own face details, but she will have whatever skin you have installed.
  
  
**If you enjoy this series, be sure to Track and Endorse! :\*
