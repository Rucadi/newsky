# Azura's Gift - Dunmer Replacer of Karliah Jenassa Brelyna or Followers
- Author: diegorom7
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/135656


*\*Now with an Assets option available for this and my next mods, where they will look the same as my pictures, the default option still remains for those who prefer to use their own skin mod\**

  
  
You can pick and choose to **replace** Jenassa, Karliah and Brelyna appearance. Only their visual look will change and doesnt add more.
  
[left]
  
﻿**[size=5]Followers**[/left][/size]
  
They are Essential, Marriageable, Dont trigger traps, and will level with the player. With similar builds as the NPCs they can replace, a mercenary, rogue and mage respectively. Custom combat style and class with perks.
  
  
**Location:**
  
  
Jenirah: Dragonsreach
  
Kalirah: Haelga's Bunkerhouse
  
Brynah: Windhelm's Palace of Kings
