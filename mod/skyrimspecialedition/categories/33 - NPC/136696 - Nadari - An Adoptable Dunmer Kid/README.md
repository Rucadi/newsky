# Nadari - An Adoptable Dunmer Kid
- Author: Comicalmuse
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/136696


**Adoptable Version**
  
Adds a cute little ~~brat~~ child for you adopt. You can find her at the **New Gnisis Cornerclub in Windhelm**, ready for immediate adoption. She'd make a great sister for [Yumi](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/18404), [Emily](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/62004), [Claudette](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/80372), and [Sammy](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/91149).
  
  
**Follower Version**
  
Nadari is newly arrived in Windhelm from Morrowind, and after spending a few days in the city, she would rather be anywhere but here. Why not take her along on your adventures?
  
  
**Class**: Nightblade
  
**Perks**: Light Foot, and a few class-related ones.
  
**Spells**: None. (teach her new spells with EFF, AFT, etc)
  
**Level**: levels with player, 1-100
  
**Location**: New Gnisis Cornerclub, in Windhelm 
  
**Voice Type**: female child
  
**Note**: follower version requires [Relationship Dialog Overhaul - RDO SE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/1187), otherwise she won't have any dialog options.
  
  
Install using a mod manager of your choice, or do it manually if you like living dangerously.
