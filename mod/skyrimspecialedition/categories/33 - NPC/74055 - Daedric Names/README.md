# Daedric Names
- Author: sasnikol
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/74055


**Overview
  
  
Daedric Names** is a [NND](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/73081)-based mod that give appropriate names to unnamed A*tronachs*, *Dremora,* *Dark Seducers* and *Golden Saints*.
  
  
According to the [lore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedra):
  
  
> ***Daedra** are creatures and sentient beings that can be found across the vast expanses of [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Oblivion).*

And are **[known](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Names)** to use names. As such, **Daedric Names** gives names to all otherwise unnamed and generic Daedra that you encounter in Skyrim.
  
  
Similarly to my other [NND](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/73081) mods, **Daedric Names** tries to be as close to the lore as possible and avoid name generators, but due to the lack of existing lore names relies on semi-generated names for Atronachs.
  
  
  
**Atronachs**
  
> *﻿Elemental Daedra, commonly known as **Atronachs**, are highly intelligent [daedra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daedra) often summoned by conjurers.*

First named Atronachs appear in Morrowind ([Nomeg Gwai](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Nomeg_Gwai), [Zedias-soko](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Pilgrimage_to_Mount_Kand)), and become more common in ESO. They usually have Daedric names with a prefix that reflects their element. These prefixes are as follows:
  

  
* Nomeg - Frost
Magdra - Flame
  
* Zynel - Storm
Mynar - Iron
  

  
Rarely, Atronachs adopt unique non-Daedric names, like [Blaze](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Blaze) or [Brimstone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Brimstone).
  
  
So that's exactly how **Daedric Names** creates names for Atronachs. The lore has much more Daedric names than exact Atronach names, so by utilizing those names with prefixes, we can get plenty of Atronach names that are based on the lore. There is also a small chance that you encounter an Atronach with unique name.
  
  
Here are some examples:
  

  
* Magdra Macharus
Nomeg Tigun
  
* Zynel Ozata

  
  
**Dremora**
  
> *﻿**Dremora**(also known as "The Kyn") are a warlike [Daedric](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daedra) [race](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Races). They consider themselves to be above other Daedra.*

Though Dremora is related to Daedra, they have distinctive names. **Daedric Names** gives Dremora around 140 [lore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Names#Dremora)﻿ names.
  
  
  
**Dark Seducers and Golden Saints**
  
> ﻿**Dark Seducers**, also known as the **Mazken**, are scantily-clad, humanoid [Daedra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedra) that use [dark](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dark) serpentine-themed armor and weapons.

> ﻿**Golden Saints**, also called the **Aureal**, are a haughty, golden-skinned race of Daedra in service to [Sheogorath](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Sheogorath).

There are no special naming features for either Dark Seducers or Golden Saints, so **Daedric Names** gives them around 240 common Daedric names that can be found in the lore.
  
  
  
**Compatibility**
  
**Daedric Names** is simply config files for [NPCs Names Distributor](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/73081) so it inherits all compatibility properties of that mod.
  
  
It has built-in support for Daedra from:
  

  
* [Glamoril - The Maze of Labyrinthian](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/43477)
[The Wheels of Lull - Unwound Edition](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/748)
  
* [Sun and Stone Atronachs](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/75215): ﻿Both Atronach types will have element-neutral names without prefixes.
[Shadow Spell Package](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/13299): Shadow Atronachs will have element-neutral names without prefix.
  
* [Elemental Destruction Magic](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/440):
Wind Atronachs are given Storm element names (Zynel).
  
* Water, ﻿﻿Earth and Posion Atronachs will have element-neutral names without prefixes. These Atronachs are considered "exotic" and will display their title (e.g. species name: "Water Atronach")
[Daedric Entity Restoration Project - D.E.R.P.](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/3604):
  
* ﻿Earth and Flesh Atronachs will have element-neutral names without prefixes. These Atronachs are considered "exotic" and will display their title (e.g. species name: "Flesh Atronach")
Dark Seducers and Golden Saints from this mod will be detected.
  
* [Shrike](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nocturnal_Shrike)﻿, [Xivilai](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Xivilai)﻿, [Auroran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Auroran)﻿, [Herne](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Skaafin#Herne) ﻿and [Winged Twilight](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Winged_Twilight)﻿ will draw names from general Daedra Names Definition as they are all sentinel Daedra.
[Daedroth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daedroth)﻿, [Clannfear](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Clannfear), [Hunger﻿](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hunger), [Scamp﻿](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Scamp) will draw names from bestial﻿ Daedra Names Definition.
  
* [Watcher](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Watcher) will use bestial Daedra Names mixed with appropriate titles found in the [lore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Names#Watcher)﻿﻿.
[Ogrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ogrim) will use one of the [known](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Names#Ogrim) ﻿Ogrim names.
  
* [Spider Daedra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Spider_Daedra) will use one of the known﻿﻿ Spider Daedra names as well as occasional general Daedra names.
[VIGILANT SE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/11849):
  
* ﻿[Daedroth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daedroth) and [Scamp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Scamp) will draw names from bestial﻿ Daedra Names Definition.
 [﻿Ogrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ogrim) will use one of the [known](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Names#Ogrim)﻿Ogrim names.
  

  
All other atronachs, dremoras, saints and seducers that use their respective vanilla races are supported. If you see one of those without a name, please check compatibility section of [NPCs Names Distributor](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/73081) for instructions how you could make them get a name.
