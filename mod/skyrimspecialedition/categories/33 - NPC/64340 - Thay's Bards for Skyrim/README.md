# Thay's Bards for Skyrim
- Author: Thay S
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/64340


[font=Times New Roman]This mod adds more bards to the game, to those who use custom player homes with option to hire bards to work for you (such as my own player homes :D) It also unlocks the function that keep a vanilla bard from player at your player home, so now you can hire a vanila bard at any point of the game.
  
This mod will be constantly updated with new bards, for I do create a lot of presets and it's a way to bring them to your game ;)
