# Naz's Asset Collection
- Author: nazarethblood
- Game: skyrimspecialedition

**Why would I use an asset collection?**
  
  
Simple. The benefits to the user are two fold: 
  
  
1) Greatly reduces the amount of physical space needed when using a number of my followers as all of the normal, subsurface and specular maps for my chosen skin are included here (there are 8 types of normal and specular maps included). The diffuse textures (the top part of the skin that you see directly) are housed with the followers themselves which allows for tattoos and war paints to be applied to individual followers. 
  
  
2) In theory, if the same assets are being referenced by multiple followers instead of being referenced individually then it should lighten the load a little for your graphics card. We are all modders here and love to mod our games to the hilt so every little bit helps in my opinion. 
  
  
**So what is in this asset collection exactly?**
  
  
For starters, probably the most realistic and detailed skin textures available on the Nexus, BnP - Female Skin by [Swefrida](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/84577193?tab=user+files)﻿ and [Khisartin](https://www.ddsworkshop.net/)﻿. Specifically, I use version 1.7 at 4K resolution with permission. If you like the way my followers look then I recommend you download the skin to use as the base in your game so all of the NPCs can look amazing. Don't forget to endorse it as well!
  
  
I have also included a number of bodyslide presets which I use in the screenshots of all of my followers. These are being used with permission and I encourage you to go and show these body enthusiasts some love by downloading (even if you already have it here) and endorsing.
  
  
**Which collection do I download?**
  
  
I offer two collections right now, one for 3BA and one for BHUNP, and each has the corresponding skin textures available. Please download the asset collection that is right for you. If you use any other body type or just want to save even more space I still offer a default body option on all of my followers. 
  
  
**Why can't I find a follower that is shown in the screenshots?**
  
  
That's a teaser to show that they will be released soon. My recommendation is to follow me on Patreon so that you can get notified of my releases. It costs nothing and you get to stay up to date!
