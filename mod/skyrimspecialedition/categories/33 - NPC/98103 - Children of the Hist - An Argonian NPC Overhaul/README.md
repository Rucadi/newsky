# Children of the Hist - An Argonian NPC Overhaul
- Author: SassiestAssassin - formerly allosnywisegirl
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/98103


> ﻿*We are the People of the Root, called 'Argonians' by the warmbloods. We go where we will, wet or dry, and the currents of blood magic run swift in our veins.*

  
![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/8aab70fa0332943c08798f8cedf360e5/ceb7f603ab70170e-67/s2048x3072/f9dded66a7806b5200edfdc16e5e4c44dba633c5.png)
  
  
The Saxhleel are a diverse group, with tribes' appearances varying due to the will - or *gloor*- of their Hist tree. Scales, head shapes, and even tails may vary between them. Some have feathers, fins, horns, spikes, or a combo adorning their heads and tails. They range from the colorful Bright Throats, to the mysterious Naga, to the feathered Sarpa and even some that look practically humanoid.
  
  
In this mod, you will find highly customized Argonians with new 'hairs' based on other games in the series as well as a variety of textures (eyes, feathers, and even body scales) and other unique details like custom tails and even tongue color variations. My goal was to make Argonians more colorful and representative of the tribal variety suggested by the lore.

**Available Patches**

  
* 4k Textures - (Vanilla or CBBE/SoS/Himbo) replaces the custom textures with 4k torso textures (the rest are 2k or less)
1k Textures - [size=3](Vanilla or CBBE/SoS/Himbo) - replaces all textures over 1k with 1k textures[/size]
  
* [size=3]AI Overhaul﻿ - forwards the USSEP data as well. NOT for use with the Lite version. Main or Beta/Scripted versions available.[/size]
Creation Club Fishing Addon - replaces Swims-in-Deep-Waters
  
* USSEP

  
Some Argonians use custom textures. The textures included are for Vanilla bodies, and their are texture replacer options for CBBE and SoS/HIMBO in the Optional files. There are no genitals for the male options, I have no intentions of adding them.
