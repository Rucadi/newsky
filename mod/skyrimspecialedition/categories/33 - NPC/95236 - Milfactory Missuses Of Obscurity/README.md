Hey hey people, Silver here. Have you ever been in a situation that you have to travel all the way to Riften, do  follow along the thieves guild questline, got a dunmer nightingale companion with a very sweet and soft voice, just to realise she is very.... visually underwhelming? If so, Milfactory Missuses of Obscurity got you covered. Karliah and all the ladies in the Guild now have the look they deserve. Hopefully their milkshake will now bring all the boys to the bar Ragged Flagon, including you!
  
 
  
This mod overhaul the appearance of all Thieves Guild ladies, including: Sapphire, Karliah, Vex, Tonilia and Niranye. I highly recommend using Relationship Dialogue Overhaul to make some of them into companions as you progress down the Thieves Guild Quesline.
  
  
Some additional info:
  

  
* Like all of my other mod, Milfactory Asset Hub is required
Karliah got her weight bumped up to 100 for maximum mommyness. Who doesn't like a dunmer mommy?
  
* Vex and Tonilia has 75 weight, Sapphire unchanged at 70, Niranye has 55 weight.

Q: I got texture mismatch on their body. How to fix that?
  
A: Since the asset hub uses whatever body you have installed in your game, you need to pick the correct version of the Asset Hub
