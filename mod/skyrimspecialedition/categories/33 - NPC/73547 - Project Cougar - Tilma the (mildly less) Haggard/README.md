Tilma the Haggard

Project Cougar is a mod collaboration by [NightmareZone](http://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/17024379) and [Canliberk](http://https//nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/5027009?tab=user+files) for the wonderful [Immersive & Adult](http://next.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/collections/xxsqm4)﻿collection. 
  
Our vision is to replace the elder women of Skyrim to be better looking but *still**mature.*
  
  
No scary melted wax ladies. 
  
No more ugly granny hats. 
  
*Skyrim belongs to the Cougars now.*
  
  
This mod will replace Tilma the Haggard
  
It's a light Plugin so it won't eat into your limit.
  
She has her own face details, but she will have the skin you have installed.

INSTALL:
  
There are two versions now available under main files (see gallery for example of changes).
  
Download whichever one you prefer, and then install.
  
  
**IMPORTANT - for those updating from 1.1 to 2.0:**
  
You will need to regenerate/fix any patches which affect this mod. There are changes in the esp to reduce the chance of random conflicts and also to make it much simpler for users to merge the .esps if they wish to do so (eg. VR users). The original cougar file will remain on Nexus untouched and still fully supported by us - updating isn't necessary if you do not want to. But if you do, be aware that a patch for 1.1 won't work the same with 2.0. I know it's sort of a pain and I apologize! This will be the only update of this nature for this mod, and it's really for the best in terms of avoiding compatibility issues, even if it's briefly inconvenient. Thank you for your patience and understanding.
