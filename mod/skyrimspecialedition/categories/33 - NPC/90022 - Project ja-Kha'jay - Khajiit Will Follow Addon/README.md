> Khajiit are bound to the [Lunar Lattice](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lunar_Lattice), the ja'Kha'jay. The phases of [Masser](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Masser) and [Secunda](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Secunda) at birth determine the form a Khajiit takes in life.
>

**[size=4][b][u][size=5]![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/6417317a31331ec3ae7e5aba81fc4eb8/74aa2322e0ee61c0-37/s75x75_c1/c0a8691a3f3d3c17cf15dc0894bc3ef27686961b.png)**[/size][/u][/b][/size]
  
  
This mod is an addon for [Project ja-Kha'jay- Khajiit Diversity Overhaul](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/57610)﻿ to extend the new types of Khajiit to the NPCs of [Khajiit Will Follow](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/2227).
  
It will require both Project ja-Kha'jay and Khajiit Will Follow to work. All 7 furstocks from the original mod are represented here

  
You'll see:
  

  
* **[Dagi/Dagi-Raht](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Khajiit#Dagi)﻿-** "Physically, they have facial features that are comparable to that of lynxes and are short in stature, making them among the smallest of the furstocks.”
**[Cathay-Raht](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Khajiit#Cathay-raht)﻿-**"Khajiit of this furstock were nicknamed as 'jaguar-men' by the [Imperial Geographical Society](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Imperial_Geographical_Society)."
  
* **[Ohmes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Khajiit#Ohmes)﻿-** "They resemble Bosmer, though they are sometimes shorter. To avoid being mistaken as such, many Ohmes tattoo or paint their faces to resemble a feline aspect."
**[Pahmar/Pahmar-Raht](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Khajiit#Pahmar)﻿﻿-** "Due to them being the strongest of furstocks, they are usually deployed as bodyguards and warriors... they resemble Senche-Tigers."
  
* **[Suthay/Suthay-Raht](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Khajiit#Suthay)﻿﻿**- "They are similar in height and build to the races of man, but [...] they have digitigrade legs. The Suthay-raht furstock was the  most common in Morrowind near the end of the Third Era."
**[Tojay/Tojay-Raht](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Khajiit#Tojay)﻿﻿-** "Little is known of the Tojay, except that they live in the southern marshesand jungle regions of Elsweyr, as well as the Tenmar Forest."

Compatibility Notes Spoiler
  
Besides editing the appearances of every Khajiit Will Follow NPC, this mod also:
  

  
* Changes S’ariq’s default mood from Angry to Neutral
Removes shoes from S’ariq’s default outfit
  
* Effectively removes two lines of dialogue that refer to all of Skyrim’s Khajiit being Cathay
Scales up Senche (Bikhai’s horse) and scales down Dagi (Nanak’s horse) to suit their new proportions better
  

**Also note:** S’ariq’s, Bikhai’s, and Nanak’s textures are only made for vanilla male bodies. If you’re using Tempered Skins for Males/ Skysight be sure to get the Better Male Feet patch. I believe it will also work for SoS’ feet.
  
  
[BA Khajiit Speak Redux](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/37197) will potentially need a patch due to the dialogue edit I made.
  
**KWF Vigilant Patch Patch** (spoilers for KWF Vigilant!)
  
[spoiler]
  

  
* Edits the appearance of the KWFV NPCs to match JKJ KWF, as well as the Reclaimer
The versions of your followers that are being worn as skins for creatures now have somewhat-off appearances, instead of being exact copies
  
* Scales all follower skeleton versions and burnt bodies to match their new heights
Edits the pelt and tail item colors and descriptions to match changes to Nanak’s and Bikhai’s cost color
  

  
[/spoiler]
