# Children of the First - An Altmer NPC Overhaul
- Author: SassiestAssassin
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/122167


> *As ye are true Children of the et'Ada, thou shalt honor us by honoring thy own lives. For in each of you is housed the Divine Spark, and thus the record of thy actions is a sacred duty."* *[right]-Xarxes, Almeriad[/right]*

  
![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/145faa88713b303f4b1669515bf6b131/05756607ce0d265c-b1/s2048x3072/f87d5ba3a3e42ced01c21ba1f00856e3e2adfd03.png)
  
  
The Altmer, or High Elves, believe themselves to be the closest descendants of the Aldmer, the first elves of Tamriel. Their culture revolves around order, and treading the Path that will return them to the glory of their ancestors.
  
  
I've tried to incorporate Altmeri culture where possible: artful makeup, gilded details, pearls and abelone, shells and gems are common adornments for those who can afford them. The Thalmor, in defiance of the frivolous styles of nobility, favor darker, sleeker looks. Farmers and other peasants are even more practical. [size=3]Additionally, it takes into account character traits, occupation, as well as the ever-important societal status of each individual.
  
  
As with the other mods of this series, I've added a variety of new (more saturated) skin tones, hair styles and other facial features, including teeth, brows, pigment variations, and eye colors. Like the other elves, they share more prominent ears and brow bones and tilted eyes with large irises. I've taken into account social status, age, lifestyle, etc. in my design choices as well.
  
  
Like the other mods in this series, all NPCs use a set of new, custom textures, assigned one of 4 different ones based on their age. All NPCs use high poly heads.

**About**
  

  
* ESPFE
Does not edit body meshes in any way.
  
* Will NOT use your body textures: see the Children of Dawn's Beauty page for details.
No HDT-SMP hairs.
  
* [size=3]Not strictly compatible with mods that edit the same NPC records (appearance, skills, inventories, voice type, etc.) **Whatever record is loaded last will show up in game.** When in doubt, use xEdit to check and be aware of what any mods that work via SKSE/other script do.[/size]
Should work fine with EasyNPC if you want to use it.
  
* [size=3]If you are having issues, make sure no other mods editing the same NPCs in **ANY** way are loaded under this one. Use SSE Edit to check for conflicts. I will not be providing support if you're using Face Discoloration Fix.[/size]

**About the Skypatcher**[size=3]
  
I have made a Skypatcher version for this mod, but please note: I couldn't get the skin to work for me (and from my reading it seems to be a common problem with the function,) but the data is correct. Some people have reported CTDs linked to  Skypatcher's NPC function. I have not experienced this, but be aware.
  
  
**Please note:** It was made with [Skypatcher AE version 5.0.3](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/106659), and may cause issues with anyone using other versions (let me know.)
  
  
[size=3][size=3]For me, personally, I will not be using the Skypatcher, as I vastly prefer to create a bashed patch and then manually edit it to ensure everything works how I want them to.[/size][/size][/size]
