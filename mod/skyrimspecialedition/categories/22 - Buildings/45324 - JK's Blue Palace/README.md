﻿**- Mod Description -**[font=Trebuchet MS][/font]
  
A lore-friendly overhaul of the Blue Palace in Solitude, featuring a new library, new dining hall, revamped basement (with rooms for the defeated Jarls from the Civil War), two new entrances to the Palace (one to the service quarters and the other to the new library), tons of new decorations, and a balcony overlooking the city.
  
The ESP has been cleaned with the SSEEdit auto clean feature. Plugin is flagged as an ESL, so it won't count towards the ESP limit.
  
  
[font=Trebuchet MS]**- Performance -**
  
  
My Rig: MSI MEG Z390 Godlike Motherboard, Intel I9 9900K @ 3.6 GHz
  
32 Gb DDR4 3600 MHz RAM, EVGA NVidia RTX 3080TI 12 Gb, playing at 1440p resolution alongside Aequinoctium ENB + a mix of 4K and 2K Textures and I get a solid 60 FPS in both exteriors and interiors (locked FPS).
  
[/font]
