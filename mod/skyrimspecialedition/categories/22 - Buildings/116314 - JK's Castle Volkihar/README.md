**﻿- Mod Description -**
  
  
A lore-friendly overhaul of Castle Volkihar. Overhauls Castle Volkihar's interior, exterior, docks, courtyard, Valerica's study and ruined tower. Adds new rooms to the castle including a small library, a vault and bathing area. Doors added to the Castle allowing direct access to the docks, north tower and ruined tower leading to the Volkihar Balcony. All doors are locked with keys and made available during the Dawnguard questline. The Courtyard (garden) and Valerica's study are fully restored once Valerica returns from the Soul Cairn.
