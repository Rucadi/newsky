**﻿- Mod Description -**
  
  
A lore-friendly overhaul of the Nightingale Hall.
  
  
**- Performance -**
  
  
My Rig: MSI MEG Z390 Godlike Motherboard, Intel I9 9900K @ 3.6 GHz
  
32 Gb DDR4 3600 MHz RAM, EVGA NVidia RTX 3080TI 12 Gb, playing at 1440p resolution alongside Aequinoctium ENB + a mix of 4K and 2K Textures and I get a solid 60 FPS in both exteriors and interiors (locked FPS).
  
  
**- Installation -**
  
  
Install with your preferred mod manager.
  
  
**- Requirements -**
  
  
- Skyrim up to date, will work with both SE and AE.
  
  
**- Compatibility -**
  
  
- [JK's Guild HQ Interiors Patch Collection](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/61416?tab=description)﻿ by Janquel, Czasior, Teabag86, and DrMonops.
  
- Not compatible with other mods that overhaul the Nightingale Hall. (with the exception of texture mods)
