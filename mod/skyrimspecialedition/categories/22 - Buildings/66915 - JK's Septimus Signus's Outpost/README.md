﻿**- Mod Description -**[font=Trebuchet MS][/font]
  
A lore-friendly...ish? overhaul of The Septimus Signus's Outpost.
  
  
[font=Trebuchet MS]**- Performance -**
  
  
My Rig: Asus P8B75-M/CSM Motherboard, Intel I7 2600 @ 3.4 GHz
  
8 Gb RAM, NVidia GTX 980TI 6 Gb, running alongside Aequinoctium ENB + 2048 Textures and I get 60 FPS average.
  
[/font]
  
[font=Trebuchet MS]**- Installation -**
  
  
[/font][font=Trebuchet MS]Install with your preferred mod manager.
  
  
[/font]**- Requirements -**
  
  
- Skyrim up to date, will work with both SE and AE.
  
  
[font=Trebuchet MS]- Compatibility :
  
  
[/font]- [JK's Interiors Patch Collection](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/35910)﻿: A collection of small patches/tweaks to help various mods play well with JK's Interiors series.
  
- Not compatible with other mods that overhaul the interior and exterior of The Septimus Signus's Outpost. (with the exception of texture mods)
