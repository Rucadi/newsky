[heading]Main Files[/heading]
  
FOMOD
  
Contains all other files. Can be installed manually or via an installer with a mod manager (like vortex) that supports FOMODs.
  
  
QUEST
  
Embark on a quest upon exploring the bandit stronghold Mistwatch, located in the south-east corner of the volcanic tundra of southern Eastmarch. Once obtained, rebalance the weapon for use with two hands and back to one at any forge. Create your own replicas if you have the ebony smithing perk. Upgrade Raven's Beak at any grindstone. Enchant at Arcane Enchanter.
  
  
CRAFT
  
No Quest. Craft Raven's Beak directly at any forge. Requires ebony smithing perk.

DSR MESHES WITH MIRRORED STRAP
  
Dual sheath redux compatibility meshes with the hanger strap mirrored for left 1 hand weapon.
  
  
DSR MESHES WITHOUT STRAPS
  
Dual sheath redux compatibility meshes which remove the sheath strap. Can be used without DSR if you'd like to remove the strap from the 1 handed version of the weapon.

STEP-BY-STEP
  
0: go to and enter Mistwatch
  
1: pick up "Malfunctioning Device" and read "Discovery Notes"
  
2: go to atronach standing stone
  
3: observe the movement of the malfunctioning device while in your inventory or while equipped in your left hand
  
4: wait until the time of day is between ten at night and two in the morning
  
5: go north, you should see something at Bonestrewn Crest
  
6: go to the altar which is marked by the large spotlight at Bonestrewn Crest
  
7: activate altar, still between 10PM and 2AM
  
8: observe the small points of light as they move into place, keeping an eye on the larger point of light
  
9: align the illuminated pattern visually, or by standing in the center of the black circle at the altar
  
 ﻿OPTION A: 
  
 ﻿ ﻿A1: activate the larger point of light and wait ~3 seconds
  
 ﻿ ﻿A2: ﻿use the map marker and/or clairvoyance to navigate to the next location
  
 ﻿OPTION B:
  
 ﻿ ﻿B1: eye up where on the landscape the larger point of light is positioned
  
 ﻿ ﻿B2: use visual landmarks to navigate to the indicated location
  
10: now between two sets of waterfalls, search the area during the time of day indicated by the malfunctioning device (remains the same as before)
  
11: approach the strange object in the area
  
12: observe the object fall into the water and explode into a portal
  
13: jump into the portal
  
14: chase the large light through the sky
  
15: approach and activate the egg
  
16: fall
  
17: take Raven's Beak from it's crater
  
18: celebrate your victory
