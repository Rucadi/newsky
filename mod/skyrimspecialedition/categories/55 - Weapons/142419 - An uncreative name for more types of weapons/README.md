# An uncreative name for more types of weapons
- Author: raymondgcx1
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/142419


New weapon types with level list interactions
  
  
Greatswords
  
Zweihander: Slower than regular greatsword with more reach and stagger uses Greatsword Perks
  
Giant Sword: more damage that zweihander with less reach uses Greatsword Perks
  
Spear: less damage faster and more range than greatsword uses Greatsword Perks
  
Swords:
  
Broadswords: sword with the stats of a standard mace still uses sword perks
  
Heavy Swords: sword with the stats of a standard greatsword with less reach still uses WarAxe perks
  
Heavy Thrusting swords: less weight and damage than Heavy Sword but higher crit damage uses Mace perks
  
rapier: uses the same balancing from animated armoury but speed and reach are 1.1 uses sword perks
  
Shotel: less damage, weight, and stagger than standard sword, but with higher speed and crit damage, uses Waraxe perks
  
  
Warhammers
  
Lances: higher value, weight and reach than standard warhammer, but lower speed and crit damage. Ignores 50% of armor, uses Warhammer perks
  
  
WarAxes:
  
Heavy Axe: War axe with the stats of a battleaxes uses war axe perks
  
  
Maces:
  
Heavy Mace: Mace with the stats of a warhammer uses Mace perks
  
  
BattleAxes:
  
Giant Axe: increased damage more reach but slower compared to warhammers uses battleaxe perks
  
  
Daggers:
  
Stiletto: Less damage more speed more range increased crit damage, and more sneak damage uses dagger keywords
  
  
  
Each weapon currently has unique weapons that aren't implemented at this time
  
I plan on adding them into the world through optional dugeons.
  
I also plan on an addon to add unique merchants for the harder to abtain materials such as redguard, blades, nord hero, silver, and stalhrim
