This mod is a model replacer for the weapon **Stormfang**, a unique 2-handed weapon found on Solstheim.
  
  
**Stormfang** is wielded by a [Reaver Lord](https://elderscrolls.fandom.com/wiki/Reaver_Lord) at [Brodir Grove](https://elderscrolls.fandom.com/wiki/Brodir_Grove_(Dragonborn)). *[[Source]](https://elderscrolls.fandom.com/wiki/Stormfang_(Dragonborn))*
  
  
  
 - The overall texture appearance of the weapon is dependent on your installed textures for the **Steel Greatsword** and **Nordic Greatsword.**
  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
  
An unenchanted version of the weapon called **Strunviir** can also be crafted at the forge after finishing the quest "**Lost Legacy**".
  
  
  
 - The quest "**Lost Legacy**" is initiated by Tharstan, an npc in Skaal Village, after finishing the quest **"A new source of Stalhrim".** - You can also get the weapon **Strunviir** where the **Lost Legacy** quest takes place (Vahlok's Tomb); at the very end of the cave situated at the center of the Word Wall's floor.
  
  
 - Crafting and Tempering requirements for **Strunviir** are the same as the **Nordic Greatsword.**
  
  
  
  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
There are three versions of the mod available; one that inherits **USSEP** changes for the Stormfang which will be dependent on **USSEP** as well, A **Standalone** version which only adds Strunviir, and the **Main replacer**.
