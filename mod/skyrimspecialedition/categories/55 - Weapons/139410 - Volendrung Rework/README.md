Enchantment Changes
  
Volendrung is Abnormally heavy, when equipped, the speed of Stamina recovery , and each swing of the weapon consumes <10%> Stamina. But Each attack that hits the target will increase your two-handed weapon attack damage and lasts for <10> seconds. Power attacks absorb <50> points Stamina from the target, and there is a chance to cause the <Volendrung's Whirlpool> effect, using mysterious power to knock away the target and causing damage. The target will be briefly stunned after being knocked away.

• New Skills
  
(New skills are used similarly to race powers, or are passive effects.)
  
When you equip Volendrung, 3 skills are automatically added to you.
  
  
**![](https://imgur.com/BDjXv7k.gif)**
  
  
• Volendrung's Rage: Resonates with the wrath of Volendrung, increasing the chance of inflicting <Volendrung's Whirlpool>, increasing critical strike chance by <20%>, increasing movement speed by <50%>, greatly increases stamina recovery speed, increases stamina by 200 points, lasts <20> seconds, and after the effect ends, reduces stamina recovery speed by <200%>, lasts <20> seconds. Cooldown time <200> seconds.
  
  
**![](https://imgur.com/rdwbCeM.gif)**
  
  
• Volendrung's Earthshatter: Strengthens each Power attack of Volendrung. Each Power Attack will cause an earthquake in a straight line. Target touched by the earthquake will take <50> damage and be stagger. The damage of the earthquake is increased by the level of the two-handed skill, and lasts <20> seconds. Cooldown <150> seconds.
  
  
**![](https://imgur.com/UTSwPwg.gif)**
  
  
• Volendrung's Earthshaker: Strengthens each Power attack of Volendrung. Each Power Attack will cause an shakes the ground, dealing <100> damage and knockdown Target away, be knockdown Target will be Movement Speed ​​reduced by <50%> for <10> seconds. The damage of the shakes the ground is increased by the level of the two-handed skill, and lasts <20> seconds. Cooldown <150> seconds.
  
  
  
(The cooldown effect of all skills is universal. If the cooldown effect of a skill has not ended, all skills cannot be used.)
  
  
  
  
If you use the Precision Mod, there will also be a unique Weapon Trail.
  
Comes with an optional appearance file that matches this mod, adapted to the style of this rework
