# Battle Scythe
- Author: screamooo
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/112141


Add 21 Battle Scythe that are available.
  
  
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
**2.0 Update**
  
  
**Balance:**
  

  
* Attack speed slightly increased, and weight slightly decreased (still slightly faster than a greatsword).
Overall scythe model and handle length adjusted (slightly smaller but still large).
  
* Added 6 unique scythes (distributed to named enemies via an optional file).
Unique weapons do not consume enchantments but are mostly triggered in specific conditions.
  

**Unique Weapon Locations and Abilities:**
  
  

  
* **[Judgment]** - The only craftable one, it can be forged by combining an Ebony Scythe with Dawnbreaker (reversible, but the Ebony Scythe is lost).
    
  [list]
**Ability**: Inflicts long-lasting fire damage on undead enemies.
  

[\*]**[Tulnir's Torture Scythe]** - Acquired during the Morvunskar quest.
  

  
* **Ability**: Guarantees paralysis on low-health targets.

[\*]**[DEAD BEATS]** - Acquired during the Darklight Tower quest.
  

  
* **Ability**: Deals fire damage, applies soul trap, and increases base attack power when affected by conjuration spells.

[\*]**[Cave Walker]** - Acquired from named vampire enemies in the Dawnguard DLC.
  

  
* **Ability**: Absorbs health and stamina when in caves or at night.

[\*]**[C'thAn]** - Acquired from a Dragon Priest in the Dragonborn DLC.
  

  
* **Ability**: Slows movement speed and reduces attack power of the target.

[\*]**[Kaalgrontiid's Fangs]** - Acquired from the final boss in the Dragonborn DLC.
  

  
* **Ability**: Lowers armor skills on hit and causes sustained bleeding damage when hitting a moving target

[/list]
