 *> ﻿...The Sentinel Zweihander is a non-unique yet finely crafted weapon, epitomizing the zweihander's role in warfare and its demand for mastery. Forged by skilled hands, it is a testament to both martial tradition and the raw strength required to wield such an imposing blade effectively.

>   
>  ﻿Historically, zweihanders were designed for specialized roles on the battlefield. Their immense size allowed them to cleave through enemy pikes and disrupt formations, holding their ground even when surrounded by multiples enemies, granting their wielders a pivotal role in turning the tide of battle.
>   
>   
>  ﻿Zweihander were first introduced in Skyrim﻿ through caravans and merchants that came all the way from High Rock, although not very popular in Skyrim, these weapons will oftenly catch the eye of skilled swordsman, and will prove highly effective in combat...*
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/p0WIucb.png)
  
  
The Sentinel Zweihander is might weapon that, in the right hands, will prove very effective in battle.
  
  
When equipped, grants you a perk called **"Sentinel's Inspiration"**. The higher your **Two Handed skill** is, more buffs you unlock.
  
  
**At 40 Two Handed** - Grants you -10% damage recieved when attacking and moving at the same time.
  
  
**At 50 Two Handed** - Grants you -20% damage recieved when attacking while standing still.
  
  
**At 60 Two Handed** - Grants you +20 extra damage on critical hits during a backward, forward or side power attack.
  
  
**At 70 Two Handed** - Grants you +30 extra damage on critical hits during a standing power attack.
  
  
**At 80 Two Handed** - Grants you +20% more damage blocked with the Sentinel Zweihander.
  
  
The weapon has damage a bit higher than ebony and has very long reach.
  
  
**The weapon can be enchanted.**
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/1qGhxuN.png)
  
  
  
The weapon can be crafted at any forge.
  
  
But
  
  
first you must have the "**Steel Smithing**"﻿ perk and have the **Smithing skill at least at level 70.**
