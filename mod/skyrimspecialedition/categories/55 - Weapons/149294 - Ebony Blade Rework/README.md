# Ebony Blade Rework
- Author: PLSET
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/149294


Rework Mephala's Ebony Blade to make it more worthwhile and more powerful. won't waste the life your friend sacrificed to make it Unsealed.
  
（This mod is ESL Flag, no need to take precious ESP slot)
  
  
  
• Main changes
  
Item value increased
  
Damage value increased.
  
Added WeapTypeNodachi keyword so Oar can detect it and assign actions
  
Repurposing Unused conten
  
Enchantment charge increased to 10,000 points.
  
  
Incomplete Power Ebony Blade
  
Use "Unsealed of Deceit" Skill
  
When the Ebony Blade is sufficiently nourished by the Blood of Deceit, it can be Unsealed and restored to its original strength. The Unsealed cannot be reversed.
  
  
True Ebony Blade
  
Enchantment Changes
  
Unsealed by the Blood of Deceit, equipping and draw out this weapon will cause crazed to devour you, reducing health and magicka by <15> per second, and increasing the stamina consumed by a power attack by <2> times, absorbing the target's health by <60> points and magicka by <20>. power attack will torn apart the target's soul, increasing the damage it receives by <3> times, lasting <3> seconds. Can kill targets with less than <20%> health. When you attack a target that is not hostile to you, you will have a 100% chance to trigger a critical hit. Attacks with this weapon do not cause hostility.

• New Skills
  
(New skills are used similarly to race powers, or are passive effects.)
  
When you equip Ebony Blade, 3 skills are automatically added to you.
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/i4IYayl.gif)
  
  
  
• Spider of Dread: Summons a Spider Daedra with Mephala's power. It spreads Frenzy, and enemies within <20> yards of it with a level lower than or equal to yours will be affected by Frenzy, causing them to attack each other for <10> seconds. It also sprays dark webs that bind enemies, and attacks will inflict poison effects. The summon lasts <80> seconds. Cooldown <180> seconds.
  
  
﻿![](https://i.imgur.com/FrY0xnN.gif)
  
  
  
• Deceit Slash: Inspire the darkness of Ebony Blade, increasing critical damage by <2> times and dealing an additional <20> damage for <2> seconds. When you attack a target affected by <Soul Torn>, you deal an additional <80> damage. When you attack with power attack, if the target is equal to or lower than your level, they will be greatly feared and have their movement speed greatly reduced for <10> seconds. The darkness of Ebony Blade lasts <20> seconds. Cooldown <180> seconds.**(The Empowered Mortal Draw animation is limited to users in MCO or BFCO environments.)**
  
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/8QucpQ0.gif)
  
  
• Premeditated Death : Transform into Mephala's Deceitful Shadow, and immediately leave combat. No one will be able to detect you, and no one will react to your actions. Lasts <30> seconds. Cooldown <280> seconds.
  
  
  
  
(The cooldown effect of all skills is universal. If the cooldown effect of a skill has not ended, all skills cannot be used.)
  
  
  
if you use the Precision Mod, there will also be a unique Weapon Trail.
