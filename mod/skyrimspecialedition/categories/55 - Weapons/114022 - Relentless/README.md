**About:**
  
  
After an unsuccessful attempt to get a custom sword created, YouTuber [Relentless Zen](https://www.youtube.com/@RelentlessZen/) contacted me and together we recreated a concept that would be fit for any Dovahkiin to wield. 
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/163525/163525-1762233568-202427057.png)
  
  
**What this mod includes:**
  
  
**Relentless**
  
• One-Handed Sword: 11 Damage / Two-Handed Sword: 20 Damage
  
• Ebony Material
  
• Unenchanted
  
• Additional Fire & Ice Glowing Rune versions
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/163525/163525-1762233568-202427057.png)
  
  
**How to Obtain:**
  
  
• **Relentless** (Unenchanted): Crafted at the Skyforge, Skyforge Smithing required (A voiced quest is planned later)
  
  

![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/163525/163525-1762233568-202427057.png)
