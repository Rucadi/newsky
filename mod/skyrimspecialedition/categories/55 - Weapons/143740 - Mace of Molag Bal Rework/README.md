# Mace of Molag Bal Rework
- Author: PLSET
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/143740


Reworked the mace given by the dreaded daedric prince.
  
（This mod is ESL Flag, no need to take precious ESP slot)
  
  
  
• Main changes
  
Item value increased
  
Damage value increased.
  
Enchantment charge increased to 10,000 points.
  
  
  
Deal <15> magicka and stamina to the target respectively, absorb <15> points, and temporarily suppress these two recovery for <7> seconds. When the target's stamina or Magicka is <0>, the damage taken is increased by <50%>. Heavy attacks have a chance to release <Bal's Spikes>. Targets touched by the spikes will take <40> damage and suffer <20> bleed damage for <5> seconds. Each target killed will restore your health and <20%> Magicka and stamina, and recharge Mace based on <20 times> the target's level.

• New Skills
  
(New skills are used similarly to race powers, or are passive effects.)
  
When you equip Mace of Molag Bal, 4 skills are automatically added to you.
  
  
![](https://imgur.com/hrSAlxe.gif)
  
  
  
• Bal's Torture: Let the mace release a <30> code of extremely malicious aura. All targets affected by this aura will receive a <50%> increase in physical damage and magic damage, and targets whose level is lower than yours will also be feared for <20> seconds. Cooldown <120> seconds.
  
  
﻿![](https://imgur.com/oW6OwfQ.gif)
  
  
  
• Bal's Spike Hell: Use the mace to release Bal's Spikes, which will release Bal's Spikes around the Mace, growing a large number of extremely sharp Bal's Spikes. Targets that touch the spikes will take <50> damage and <40> bleed damage for <3> seconds. Cooldown time <150> seconds.
  
  
![](https://imgur.com/9fcTE17.gif)
  
﻿![](https://imgur.com/L6AVFbR.gif)
  
  
  
• Bal's Conquer : Let Bal's conquering will descend upon the mace. When you attack with less than <30%> health and a level lower than your mortal target, it will become your slave, following and fighting for you for <360> seconds. The conquered slave will go insane, taking <25>% less damage, dealing <25>% more damage, And can steal the enemy's health through melee attacks. You can kill the slave with Bal's conquer kill at any time. Conquering Will lasts <60> seconds. Cooldown <720> seconds.
  
  
![](https://imgur.com/BuDmO6Y.gif)
  
  
  
• Bal's Conquer Kill : Releases a wave of energy that instantly kills all slaves conquered by <Bal's Conquer> within <100> yards.
  
  
  
  
(The cooldown effect of all skills is universal. If the cooldown effect of a skill has not ended, all skills cannot be used.)
