> ﻿*...The voulge was a polearm combining the chopping power of an axe with the reach of a spear, back in High Rock, is usually seen being wielded by commoners, low ranked soldiers and sometimes low rank officers.
>   
>   
>  ﻿In battle, it excelled at cleaving through armor and shields, using its heavy blade to deliver devastating blows. Its extended reach allowed soldiers to strike from a safer distance, making it effective in both offensive charges and defensive formations.
>   
>   
>  ﻿The simple idea behind the weapons design, combined with its overral effectiveness, will catch the eyes of higher-ranked officers, which will sometimes commision finer crafted pieces of this weapon, with ornated arabesques and additional complex edges and curves in the weapon's shape. Some voulges from time to time make its way to Skyrim, and among those one or two better crafted pieces are being carried aswell, which will be probably be given as a present to a captain or rich person to its personal armoury...*

  
![](https://i.imgur.com/p0WIucb.png)
  
  
The Royal Voulge is highly effective weapon in combat, due to its greatly extended reach and versatile usage.
  
  
When equipped, the weapon bestows you with a perk called **"Royal Dominion"** The higher your **Two Handed skill** is, more buffs you will unlock.
  
  
**• 50 Two Handed:** 
  
Ignores 15%﻿ armor if the target is mostly wearing **heavy armor**
  
﻿ ﻿ ﻿ ﻿       Ignores 30% of armor if the target is mostly using **light armor**
  
 ﻿ ﻿ ﻿       Ignores 22% of armor if the amount of **light and heavy armor** from the target meets in the middle.
  
  
**• 60 Two Handed:** 
  
+50% critical hit damage with this weapon.
  
  
**• 70 Two Handed:** 
  
Blocks 20% more damage with this weapon.
  
  
**• 80 Two Handed:** 
  
If an enemy is currently swinging his weapon, hitting the target has a 30% chance of disarming if he is **NOT** wielding a Greatsword, Warhammer, Battleaxe or One Handed Mace.
  
  
**• 90 Two Handed:** 
  
Power Attacks do 10% more damage.
  
  
**• 100 Two Handed:** 
  
Power Attacks costs 10% less stamina.
  
  
**Weapon Mastery:** 
  
Having **100 Two Handed** and the **following perks** from the Two Handed Skill Tree will unlock mastery with this weapon, which bestows you bonus buffs.
  
  
**Requirements**:
  
Spoiler- Barbarian Rank 5
  
- Champion's Stance
  
- Devastating Blow
  
- Great Critical Charge
  
- Sweep
  
- Limbsplitter Rank 3
  
- Warmaster 
  
  
**Bonus:** 
  
+10% damage with the Royal Voulge
  
-10% less physical damage recieved 
  
50% chance of ignoring 80% of a target armor during a power attack.
  
  
Weapon damage is a tiny bit higher than steel and has very long reach.
