- This mod replaces the appearance of 12 unique weapons, and adds unique armaments to select NPCs from the mod [Wyrmstooth](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/106606?tab=description).
  
  
- The Broken Steel Sword weapon can be fixed into a "Repaired Steel Sword" in the forge. Wyrmstone and Brimstone variant will be added at a later time.
  
  
- Unique NPC weapons can only be obtained through console or by stealing them from the npcs themselves at this time. [/size]*...Sorry and good luck stealing them...*
  
  
- It would be best to install and use on a fresh save where you haven't started Wyrmstooth yet for the NPC weapons to take place.
  
  
- The appearance of the following weapons will depend on your current installed textures:
  
  
\* Bloodletter - Iron Axe and Iron Dagger textures
  
  
\* Vulom's Staff - Conjuration Staff textures
  
  
\* Embersunder - Stalhrim Greatsword and Ebony Blade textures
  
  
\* Lurius' Sword - Imperial Sword and Imperial Scabbard textures
  
  
\* Daenlit's Bow - Dragonbone Bow textures
  
  
\* Chaoswielder - Rueful Axe and Daedric Sword textures
  
  
\* Broken and Repaired Steel Sword - Steel Greatsword textures
  
  
\* Alka's Staff - Forsworn Staff textures
  
  
\* Valstadt's Basher - Giant's Club textures
  
  
\* Staff of Malentis - Rueful Axe and Restoration Staff textures
