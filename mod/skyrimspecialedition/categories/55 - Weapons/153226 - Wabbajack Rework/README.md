• New Skills
  
(New skills are used similarly to race powers, or are passive effects.)
  
When you equip Wabbajack, 3 skills are automatically added to you.
  
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/YG0wDpA.gif)
  
  
  
• Wabbacheese: Summons a cheese version of Wabbajack, named Wabbacheese, whose attacks have a chance to knock down the target, dealing <20> points of melted cheese damage for <5> seconds, and using it to knock a target to death will bake the target's soul essence into delicious cheese and place it on their corpse for <60> seconds. Cooldown <120> seconds.
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/RbTnjZS.gif)
  
  
  
• Floating Cheese :Creates a cheese vortex hole that continuously shoots large amounts of cheese, dealing <80> damage to targets within <10> yards, with a chance to knock down the target. A small pool of melted cheese will briefly form after the cheese hits, dealing <30> damage over time. The cheese vortex hole lasts <40> seconds. Cooldown <150> seconds.
  
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/OpbKjMF.gif)
  
  
  
• Cheese Storm : <Can only be used outdoors>, creates a huge cheese vortex storm in the sky, continuously launching huge amounts of cheese from it, causing <100> damage to targets within <30> yards and knocking down the target. The huge cheese vortex storm exists for <60> seconds. Cooldown <600> seconds.
  
  
  
(The cooldown effect of all skills is universal. If the cooldown effect of a skill has not ended, all skills cannot be used.)
