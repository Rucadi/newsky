# Light Greatswords
- Author: screamooo
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/123515


Add 15 Light Greatswords that are available.
  
  
Add 4 Unique Light Greatswords that are available.
  
  
  
It is a quick two -handed weapon that is lighter than the Grade Sword.
  
The attack speed is the same as the two -handed sword or slightly faster.
  
  
The damage is similar to the Grade Sword, but the weight is extremely limited, so the stiffness is weaker than one -handed sword.
  
  
  
**If you like this mod, please post a screenshot of your play!
  
That's my only motivation and pleasure!**
