# Ronnie's Unique Weapons
- Author: RonnieMagnum
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/134120


![](https://i.imgur.com/PvhLN37.png)
  
  

[font=Georgia]What this mod includes[/font]

  
[font=Georgia]30 unique weapons created by [RonnieMagnum](https://next.nexusmods.com/profile/RonnieMagnum), combined together in one package.
  
The weapons are all hand placed in the world for the player to find[/font].
  
[font=Georgia]The following is a list of the weapons included in this mod, their type, and links to the original mod page for those who want to see images and more details. 
  
Each has spoiler tags that contain information about their locations for those who do not wish to find them organically.[/font]
