- This mod has 6 variants that replaces the appearance of the Imperial sword.
  
  
 *﻿\*Variant 1 uses a different blade texture. As for the other parts and/or variants, it will use your current Imperial Sword texture.
  
  
 ﻿[i][color=#ffff00]\*Variant 4 Sword and Variant 3 Greatsword takes its likeness from ES:O, Variant 5 from Suran329's art for Beyond Skyrim, and Variant 6 from Morrowind's Imperial Broadsword.
  
  
 ﻿\*Replacers don't contain any Greatsword files; only the Standalone file adds them.*[/color][/i]
  
  
  
- Standalone File adds all variants of the Imperial sword, with 3 new greatswords to the forge, distributed to select NPCs (listed below) along with a unique sword for (hopefully) all Penitus Oculatus Agents.
  
  
 *﻿\* The PO Sword is a mash-up of the Variant 3 and 6's model.
  
  
 ﻿\* The Greatsword Variant 2 is dependent on your current Imperial Sword and Steel Greatsword textures.*
  
 ﻿
  
- Crafting materials for the mod's Imperial Swords has adapted USSEP data, but I've changed the Leather Strips requirement into Firewood. The plugin of this mod doesn't change the USSEP data for crafting the vanilla Imperial Sword.
  
  
- To be able to craft the Penitus Oculatus Sword, you must meet the following requirements:
  
  
 ﻿1. Have the Steel Smithing Perk **AND EITHER OF THE TWO:**
  
 ﻿2a. Have completed the quest "Joining the Legion" **OR**
  
 ﻿2b. Have a Penitus Oculatus Sword in your inventory.
