# Repeater Crossbows 1.2.1
- Author: ENR
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/166955


![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/60464/60464-1765592101-82968775.png)﻿
  
**Overview:**Adds 8 kitbashed Onehanded Crossbows.
  
*Generally, meant to be used offhand, similar to Bloodborne's firearms.*
  
They use the regular stave animations and thus require soul gems for recharge,
  
their effects are totally unique.
  
  
  
**Stats:**Spoiler
  
  
Repeating Crossbow:
  
Standard Reload.
  
-Comes in explosive *Fire, Frost and Shock* variants.
  
-Has *Enhanced* versions.
  
  
Stonebow:
  
Slow Reload.
  
Low/High Ammo count.
  
- *Regular* version fires a small stone that causes knockdowns but deals low damage.
  
- *Ice* version deals no damage but freezes targets.
  
- *Oil* version leaves a puddle of oil which will continue to act as a hazard for a few seconds.
  
- *Poison* version causes a low damage over time effect.
  
- *Poison cloud* version explodes and deals a damage over time effect to multiple targets.
  
- *Stink Cloud* version causes enemies hit by the cloud to flee.
  
  
Elven Repeater
  
Fast Reload.
  
- Has *Fire, Frost and Shock* Versions.
  
  
Mechanical Crossbow
  
Fast Reload.
  
-Comes in explosive *Fire, Frost and Shock* variants.
  
  
Slayer's Crossbow
  
Fast Reload.
  
High Ammo count.
  
-Comes in explosive *Fire, Frost and Shock* variants.
  
- Left + Right hand versions.
  
  
Twinbow
  
Slow Reload.
  
All shots cause stagger.
  
-Comes in explosive *Fire, Frost and Shock* variants.
  
- Has *high-power* version which causes knockback at the cost of low ammo.
  
  
Orcish Ballista
  
Super slow reload.
  
High power shots that cause knockdown.
  
-Comes in explosive *Fire, Frost and Shock* variants.
  
  
Hidden Bow/Wristbow
  
Standard Reload.
  
Fires Silently
  
- Has *Fire, Frost and Shock* Versions.
  
- *Poison* version causes high damage over time.
  
- *Paralysis* version, has a chance to inflict paralysis on hit.
  
  
Tribow
  
Slow Reload.
  
All shots cause stagger/Knockdown
  
-Comes in explosive *Fire, Frost and Shock* variants.
