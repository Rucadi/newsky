- This mod has 5 variants that replaces the appearance of the Iron Sword and Greatsword.
  
  
  
- Standalone file adds all variants of the Iron Sword and Greatsword to the forge, along with leveled-list integration.
  
  
  
- Actual look is dependent on your vanilla textures, and the screenshots use [Amidianborn](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/35382) textures.
  
  
 ***﻿\* The sword scabbard has 2 parts which will be dependent on your Steel Scabbard texture and Iron sword texture.***
