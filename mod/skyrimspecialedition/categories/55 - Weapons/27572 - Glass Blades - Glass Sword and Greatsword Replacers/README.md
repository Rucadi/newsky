This mod replaces the appearance of the Sword and Greatsword Glass weapons where two options have Skysan4298's Parallax Settings applied to the nif files.
  
  
  
  
**As of 3.0:**
  
  
  
**- There are now 5 new variants of replacers available for each weapon.**
  
  
**- Each replacer and standalone variant has 3 types; Vanilla, Opaque and Transparent.
  
  
 \* [b][color=#e69138]Though they work just fine in 3rd person,**almost every[/color] Transparent variant might appear janky in 1st Person view in SSE due to how I've processed most of the vanilla mesh of the blade part. Using either the Opaque or Vanilla option is recommended.[/b]
  
  
**- Standalone files are also updated to accommodate the new variants. It is advised to use the new standalone version in a save where you haven't used the previous version to avoid errors.
  
  
[b]- All-in-one pack contains the Alternate textures with a matching scabbard look/motif. By default, Skysan's Parallax textures and a hopefully matching scabbard color motif for the original Glass texture will be installed.**[/b]
  
  
**- Load this mod after Skysan's**[Transparent and Refracting Equipment](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/4899), **where you'll also need their textures if you plan to use their modified vanilla textures for Parallax settings.**
  
  
  
  
  
**DSR Patch files are included.**
