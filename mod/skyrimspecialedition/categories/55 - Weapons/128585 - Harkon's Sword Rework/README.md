# Harkon's Sword Rework
- Author: PLSET
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/128585


Reworked Harkon's Sword to make it more worthy of an ancient vampire lord.
  
（This mod is ESL Flag, no need to take precious ESP slot)
  
  
• Main changes
  
Item value increased
  
Damage value increased.
  
Weapon material changed to ebony.
  
No Need Enchantment charge.
  
  
Enchantment Changes
  
Absorbs <10> health, magicka, and stamina points. Vampires using this sword can absorb an additional <20> points. Killing mortals with this sword Devour their essences, which has devour <X> essences. Consuming essence can greatly enhance health, magicka, and stamina absorption for a short period of time. Max devour to <10> essence.
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/njhmVU7.gif)
  
  
（Devour essences）
  
  
  
  
• New Skills
  
(New skills are used similarly to race powers, or are passive effects.)
  
When you equip Harkon's Sword, 3 skills are automatically added to you.
  
  
  
﻿
  
• Blood Unleashed: Consumes <3> Essence to Unleashed the Harkon's sword, Increases health, magicka, and stamina absorption strength by <30>.
  
  
﻿![](https://i.imgur.com/FWQA2K6.gif)
  
  
[size=4]• Harkon's Ambition: Consumes <5> Essence, manifests Harkon's ambition, and absorbs <10> Health, Magicka, and Stamina from targets within <20> yards for <15> seconds**.**[/size]
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/efhDCuA.gif)
  
﻿
  
• Sacrifice to Bal: Consumes all Essence to sacrifice them to Molag·Bal, increasing your critical hit chance by +5 per Essence.Powerful attacks will apply blood flames to the target,The damage intensity and duration of the Blood Flame are based on the number of Essence you sacrifice, and last for <10> seconds.
  
  
**![](https://i.imgur.com/hFv9Dxb.gif)**
  
  
[size=4]If you use the Precision Mod, there will also be a unique Weapon Trail.[/size]
  
  
  
**![](https://i.imgur.com/Zf0D6te.gif)**
