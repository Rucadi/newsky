# Dawnbreaker Rework
- Author: PLSET
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/141470


**Reworked the Dawnbreaker to be more of a powerful anti-Undead holy sword than a useless glow iron sword, and introduced an optional Undead version. [size=4](Does not include modifications to the model's appearance, they are other mods)**
  
**（This mod is ESL Flag, no need to take precious ESP slot）**
  
  
  
[/size]• Main changes
  
Item value increased
  
Damage increased.
  
Enchantment charge increased to 10,000 points.
  
And Undead Version.
  
  
Enchantment Changes
  
Deals <30> Sun damage, or <60> if the target is an Undead. Attacking Undead has a chance to trigger a Lesser Sun explosion, dealing <70> Sun damage to enemies within <15> yards. Killing an Undead target has a chance to cause a <50> yard powerful Sun explosion, which will ignite, Fear or kill Undead targets with less than <20%> health for <15> seconds. Attacking the target will curb the Undead target's recovery ability for <10> seconds. Undead will take <30> sun damage per second using this sword.

**Convert Dawnbreaker**
  
  
To convert Dawnbreaker, you need to use the Skyforge at the right time.
  
  
Dawnbreaker to Eclipser
  
When the weather is Eclipse (shoot the sun with Bloodcursed Elven Arrow), use 2 Daedra Heart, 5 Filled Black Souls Gem, 10 vampire dust, 5 Blood potion and Dawnbreaker to convert at Skyforge.
  
  
**You can also convert back when you don't want to be a undead.**
  
Eclipser to Dawnbreaker
  
When the time is between 6:30 and 7:00 (Dawn Break Time), use 2 Daedra Heart, 5 Filled Grand Souls Gem, 5 Gold Ingot, 1 Flawless Diamond and Eclipser to convert at Skyforge.
  
  
You can also convert the one-handed and two-handed versions of Dawnbreaker and Eclipser at the Skyforge using 1 Daedra Heart and 10 Ebony Ingot.
