# Ultra Shields
- Author: screamooo
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/126024


Add 51 LargeShield and Add SmallShield
  
  
**2.0.0 Changelog**
  

  
* "Large Shields" have been renamed to "Great shields."
"Small Shields" have been renamed to "Bucklers."
  
* Unique shields have been redesigned.
Most greatshield designs have been updated.
  
* Two new greatshields have been added.
Greatshield armor ratings have been adjusted to match their respective tiers.
  

  
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
[ LargeShield ]
  
  
Large Shields all have heavy armor and are heavy and so much more defensive, increase defense efficiency by 25% and increase all elemental resistance by 15%.
  
**When using optional mode, movement speed is reduced by 30% and one-handed physical attack power by 20%.**
  
  
[ SmallShield ]
  
  
Small shields all have light armor and have a small area, light weight, and low defense.
  
**When using the optional mode, the shield is not affected by the shield debuff effect and the shield can be used.**
