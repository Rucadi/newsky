**Description**

  
* Adds a Shrine of Azura at the [Shrine of Azura](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Azura)﻿ (the place with the giant statue).
Now you can pray to the Shrine of Azura while at the [Shrine of Azura](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Azura)﻿.
  
* The new Shrine of Azura grants the same blessing as the Shrine of Azura at the [Ravenrock Temple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Temple)﻿.
100% compatible with anything that changes Shrine of Azura at the [Ravenrock Temple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Temple) or the [Blessing of Azura](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blessings)﻿ effect.
  
* Automatically compatible with [Pilgrim](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/54099)﻿ via [Base Object Swapper](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/60805)﻿ (requires [Base Object Swapper](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/60805)﻿).
    
  [list]
Replaces Pilgrim's **Morrowind-style** shrine with a shrine that is more consistent with the **Skyrim-style** statue of Azura.
  

[\*]Automatically compatible with [Wintersun](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/22506)﻿ via [Base Object Swapper](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/60805)﻿ (requires [Base Object Swapper](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/60805)﻿).
  

* Reverts the altar activator back to a static, so you can only get the blessing from the Shrine of Azura, instead of getting it from the altar.

[\*]No worldspace or cell changes.
  
[\*]Uses your textures. Screenshots uses SRO and [Remiros' Statues HD](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/50744)﻿.
  
[\*]ESL.
