**Description**
  
**This is a complete overhaul of the Inner Sanctum location. My goal was to make this location more interesting to explore while keeping it vanilla and lore friendly. And I think I was able to accomplished that. I added a lot of unique clutter, unexpected encounters, and secrets. I really hope this mod can surprise you!**
