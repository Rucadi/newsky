# Grave of the Toucouleur - A Dungeon Mod
- Author: ENR
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/161988


﻿![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/60464/60464-1760884986-542464344.png)﻿
  
  
﻿The Toucouleur was a ship which terrorized the Northwestern seas for nearly two decades,
  
before vanishing for good, along with its vast bounty...
  
  
Recommended level: Very high... or at least have lots of followers
  
 that you can summon in and a supply of healing potions.
  
  
Location: Head north of the Dawnstar Sanctuary, underwater.
