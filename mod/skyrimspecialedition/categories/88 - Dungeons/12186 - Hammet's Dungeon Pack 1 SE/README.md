[left][font=Comic Sans MS]Hammet's Dungeon Pack 1
  
by: venjhammet
  
  
﻿Features a total of Thirty (30) dungeons created and added to Skyrim and Solstheim and a new world space called Desolate Veil to discover and explore.
  
 
  
Things to expect in these dungeons:
  
 
  
1.  These dungeons use vanilla assets only
  
2. These dungeons ranges from normal to hard but levels with the player
  
3. These dungeons are radiant quest enable
  
4.  Some dungeons are puzzle-driven and require in-depth exploration
  
5.  Areas can be very dark, confusing, large, and scary yet immersive
  
6.  Has "unique enchantments" for weapons and armor sets and new spells
  
7.  Dungeons are meticulously detailed in design and are fully navmesh
  
8.  This mod is for "Nexusmods exclusive only". So please be respectful and avoid posting on another mod site.
  
9.  Please stop asking about porting to console, I will port them myself soon
  
10. This Dungeon Pack is compatible with all of my Standalone Dungeons, Hammet Dungeon Pack 2, Vominheim and Valefrost (Please read the sticky comment section for further details.
  
11. This mod comes with five (5) powerful followers to accompany you on your journey.
  
      a. Ginvur Nedalin - a male dark elf assassin at Rheogur's Tower, Raven Rock.
  
      b. Arani Valestorm - a female nord conjurer at Silver Blood Inn, Markarth
  
﻿      c. Sirkvur Stonearm - a male nord spellsword at Frostfruit Inn, Rorikstead
  
      d. Remzir - a male khajiit necromancer at Winking Skeever, Solitude
  
﻿      e. Tarven - a female woodelf archer located at Dead Man's Drink, Falthreath
  
12. please note that no one is allowed to change or alter any rewards in this mod, whether that includes new textures for weapons and    armor, NPCs, or compatibility patches, without my explicit permission. A few individuals have been granted permission and are currently working on approved patches. I kindly ask that this rule be respected to maintain consistency and integrity across the mod.
