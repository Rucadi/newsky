A new craftable light armor and dagger found under Daedric category plus five back tattoos. Available in UNP and offering a huge range of customization and upgrades from skimpy to elegant.
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/53846-1-1417138481.png)
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/53846-1-1412462830.png)
  
  
  
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/53846-3-1412459723.png)
  
  
 This is the tenth armor of a series that will hopefully reach twenty four armors. The idea originated from Minidresses Collection mod by Tiwa44 who did an awesome job converting some of the long dresses to mini versions.
  
  
These armors will not offer any protection in real life so don't use them at home!
