# Amsedillir - Runes Of Thorns 3BA-BHUNP-UBE
- Author: Amsedillir
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/127085


![](https://i.ibb.co/kVvFcghp/A7dlr-Description4.png)
  
[font=Georgia]***Adds armor set "Runes Of Thorns" based on the body 3BA, BHUNP, UBE
  
The skirt also works with FSMP.
  
The armor is divided into 8 parts, with the possibility of creating them.
  
It also includes a sword in two versions: a one-handed sword and a two-handed sword
  
Visually suitable for fast and agile characters.
  
It works through the ESL plugin.
  
  
Thanks to*** [/font][font=Georgia]***[MSF](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/153218)***[/font][font=Georgia]***, you can freely choose the appearance of the armor at any time if you get bored of the old one without having to upgrade or enchant again.***[/font][font=Georgia]***(Press F10 to open the MSF menu)***[/font]
