# Akaviri Dragonguard Armor
- Author: Caenraes
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/85900


I may have bit off more than I can chew with this one. I'm very happy with how the armor turned out but there's still a few issues with clipping and stretching in some areas. I did the best I could implementing it but there's only so much you can do for Skyrim and I have very little experience with implementing 3D assets.
  
  
You can find the armor and sword in a chest in the courtyard of Sky Haven Temple. It's heavy armor and has similar stats to Dragonbone.
