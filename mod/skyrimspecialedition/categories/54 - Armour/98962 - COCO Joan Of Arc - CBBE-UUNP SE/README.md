# COCO Joan Of Arc - CBBE-UUNP SE
- Author: COCO
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/98962


[font=Comic Sans MS]**﻿![](https://i.imgur.com/H4SMPaZ.png)﻿﻿**[/font]**A made from scratch outfit by COCO, featuring a unique outfit based on Ruler from Fate/Apocrypha. The outfit comes with CBBE 3BA & COCOBody CBBE/UUNP bodyslides support. The Qipao & Waist Metal come with HDT-SMP cloth physics.**
