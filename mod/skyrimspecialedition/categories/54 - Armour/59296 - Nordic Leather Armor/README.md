# Nordic Leather Armor
- Author: Darenii
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/59296


This mod adds Nordic Leather Armor and is the successor of my other Nordic Carved Light mod.
  
Craftable with Advanced Armors Perk.
  
  
Cuirass: 32 Armor 6 Weight
  
Helmet: 14.5 Armor 3 Weight
  
Gauntlets: 9 Armor 1.5 Weight
  
Boots: 9 Armor 1.5 Weight
  
Circlet: 13 Armor 1 Weight
  
  
Install via modmanager of your choice.
  
  
CBBE Bodyslide included.
  
Uses vanilla textures.
  
  
**Recommended:
  
[aMidianBorn Book of Silence SE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/35382)**
