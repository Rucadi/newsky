My patches by Xtudo****Description**
  
**My patches and tweaks for the beautiful armor mod made by Ellxe.
  
  
My Tweaks**
  
It will replace **[ELLE] Lastriem.esp**. It's fine to update mid-game, you won't lose any already crafted item.
  

  
* **Added clothing version**.
**Removed the Crafting Book from the world**, to avoid vanilla records edits, and possible conflicts with many other mods. **Now you can craft it at any Tanning Rack**.
  
* Fixed ArmorMaterial keywords of some items.
Added missing **ArmorMaterialLeather** keyword to the armored items.
  
* Added Warm keyword to armored items, for the Survival Mode.
Removed Weapon Adjust value from the all the items but the main armor.
  
* Adjusted prices and weights.
Edited/adjusted crafting recipes.
  
* Rings are now concealed.

  
**Optional files**
  

  
* **Non-HDT patch**. Rebuild the **Belt** and **Necklace** BodySlides after installing. Default and UBE compatible.
**Classical CBBE-UNP version**. My Tweaks are included. Non-HDT. Top and Pants are merged.
