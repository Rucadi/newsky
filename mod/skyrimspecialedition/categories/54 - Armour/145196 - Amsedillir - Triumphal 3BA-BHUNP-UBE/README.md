# Amsedillir - Triumphal 3BA-BHUNP-UBE
- Author: Amsedillir
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/145196


*![](https://i.ibb.co/kVvFcghp/A7dlr-Description4.png)﻿﻿﻿*

[font=Georgia]**The mod adds light armor "Triumphal".
  
The armor consists of 5 elements, also includes a one-handed sword.
  
The armor's characteristics are slightly superior to those of ebony.
  
The cape and torso have 3 colors each.
  
The skirt uses FSMP
  
  
Сraft requires the presence of ebony smithing.
  
  
If you want a version with PBR textures, pay attention to the [PBR-conversion](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/166190)﻿.**[/font]

  
  
  
*![](https://i.ibb.co/h1gmw97C/A7dlr-Installation4.png)﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿*

**[font=Georgia]Download and install the mod through your mod manager.
  
Create a costume for your body through bodyslide.
