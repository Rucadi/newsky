TITUS MEDE I - My version
  
By Xtudo**
  
**Description**
  
**The impressive armor of Titus Mede I, the fearsome Colovian warlord.**
  

  
* Male and female versions.
Weight sliders.
  
* Light (Steel Armor stats) and Heavy (Ebony Armor stats) versions.
4K and 2K versions.
  
* No HDT required.
Open helmet with full support for beast races.
  
* Survival "warm" keyword added.
You can craft all the items at any forge, and you can temper them too. Advanced Armors Smithing perk is required for the light armored version, and Ebony Smithing Perk for the heavy one.
  
  
* Standalone. BSA. ESL.

  
**Optional patches**
  

  
* **No chains.**Only textures. Removes the chains from the skirt. Requires one of the Main download installed first.
**Emperor's clothes patch.** The Emperor will wear the armor, minus the helmet. If there is a problem, maybe you'll need to use "resetinventory" on him.
  
* **Civil War reward.** Standalone 2K or 4K versions. No recipes. You will get the armor as the final reward of the Civil War quest.
