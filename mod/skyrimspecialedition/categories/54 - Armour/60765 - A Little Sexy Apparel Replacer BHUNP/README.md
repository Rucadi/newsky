**Installation**
  
[/font]

  
* [font=Trebuchet MS]Install [a little sexy apparel replacer with LSAR Addon by Cotyounoyume](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/15326), enable the esp.[/font]

  
* [font=Trebuchet MS]Place this mod in a higher priority than the LSAR main package.[/font]
[font=Trebuchet MS]Build meshes in [BodySlide and Outfit Studio](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/201):[/font]
  
* [font=Trebuchet MS]In Bodyslide, choose group "**A Little Sexy Apparel Replacer BHUNP SE**" or filter outfits with keyword "LSAR" in the upper right box.[/font]
[font=Trebuchet MS]﻿﻿Select a desired body preset, or tweak whatever you want. [/font]
  
* [font=Trebuchet MS](if you're using [OBody NG](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/77016)﻿, choose **-zeroed sliders-** preset and enable **Build Morphs**)[/font]
**[font=Trebuchet MS]﻿Batch Build.[/font]**
