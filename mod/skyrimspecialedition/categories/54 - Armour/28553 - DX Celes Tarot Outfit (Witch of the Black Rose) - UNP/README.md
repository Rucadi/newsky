# DX Celes Tarot Outfit (Witch of the Black Rose) - UNP
- Author: Deserter X - dianka33
- Game: skyrimspecialedition

You can find the crafting manual south west of Riften at the top floor of Darklight Tower.
  
  
All the recipes will only be available as long as you carry the crafting manual in your inventory.
  
When you are done crafting store the manual and your crafting interface will be free again.
  
  
  
CBBE versions of DX armors by Docteure & Jeir

A new light or cloth Daedric outfit. Suitable for sorceresses and witches.
  
  
  
  
[i][font=Times New Roman]The light version offers almost as good protection as dragonscale
  
but it's hard to upgrade since it requires a high level blacksmithing skill.
  
  
• The tights come in two variations, the first one is plain without enchantment
  
and the second one requires an extra Daedra Heart to give it the Archmage enchant.
  
[i][font=Times New Roman]• The horns make Alteration and Illusion spells cost 25% less mana.
  
[/font]*
  
• There are five themes to choose from:
  
  
• [Original](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/28553/28553-1566788048-2030891842.png)﻿
  
• [Gothic](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/28553/28553-1567200698-1331948636.png)﻿
  
• [Arcane](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/28553/28553-1567201624-226087619.png)﻿
  
• [Inferno](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/28553/28553-1567201646-946065492.png)﻿
  
• [Blizzard](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/28553/28553-1567201795-194093579.png)﻿
  
  
All the themes work with both UNP and CBBE versions.
  
Install the one you like after the main file of your preference.
  
[/font][/i]
