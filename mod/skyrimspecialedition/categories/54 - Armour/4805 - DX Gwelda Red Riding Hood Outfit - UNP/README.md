A new craftable light Elven armor.
  
  
Has many options, varying from hair colors to choices between pantyhose, socks and panties.
  
  
Available for UNP with Racemenu High Heels support.
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/61012-0-1437515708.png)
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/53846-3-1412459723.png)
  
  
This is the fourteenth armor of the series. It's not what I had initially planned but TKHBMVP sent me an awesome link with real life pictures of this costume and I couldn't help it but try to convert it into a Skyrim outfit.
  
  
You can find the book inside Arcadia's Cauldron in Whiterun. You can find plenty of cotton and red mountain flowers around Whiterun.
  
  
I hope you enjoy it! :)
  
  
The idea of this series originated from [Minidresses Collection mod by Tiwa44](http://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/47376/) who is doing an awesome job converting some of the long dresses to mini versions.
