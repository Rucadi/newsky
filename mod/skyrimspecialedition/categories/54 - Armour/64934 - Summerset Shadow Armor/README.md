The [Summerset Shadow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Factions_S#Summerset_Shadows) are a rival guild of thieves that you encounter during the Thieves Guild questline. They don't really have any unique visual identity besides being Altmer in Thieves Guild Armor. My buddy [Catir](https://www.nexusmods.com//users/14941634) had the idea to adapt the [Elven Hunter Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven#Elven_Hunter_Armor) into something suitable for them and then my other buddy, [Jelidity](https://www.nexusmods.com/users/4569617), made it happen.
  
  
I'm just here to publish the mod I guess.
  
  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/PierreDespereaux/PierreDespereaux/master/assets/images/banners/Features.png)
  
  

  
* Replaces the generic Thieves Guild Armor used by the Summerset Shadow with a new unique armor based on Elven Hunter Armor.
The appearance of Linwe's Armor set changed to be consistent with the new style.
