# Immortal Remnant - Redguard Elite Reintegration Patches
- Author: ThirdEyeSqueegee
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/91010


These patches distribute BuddhaSante's Immortal Remnant armors to Alik'r warriors (specifically, the black/silver variant to one of the Alik'r warriors the player meets near the Whiterun entrance, and the black/silver/red variant to Kematu) and carries forward changes from other mods including [Heavy Armory](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/6308), [Tamrielic Distribution](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/71372), and [Lawless - A Bandit Overhaul](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/88080).
