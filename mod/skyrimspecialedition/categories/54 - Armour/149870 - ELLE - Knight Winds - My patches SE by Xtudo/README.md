KNIGHT WINDS
  
My patches by Xtudo****Description**
  
**My patches and tweaks for the beautiful armor mod made by Ellxe****.
  
  
Tweaks and edits****It will replace [ELLE] Knight Winds.esp**. It's fine to update mid-game, you won't lose any already crafted item.

  
* Improved collision on ground/inventory items.
Items renamed for a more lore-friendly feeling.
  
* Removed armor rating from the breeches (pants) as they are clothing.
Added ArmorMaterialLeather to all armored items.
  
* Adjusted prices.
Adjusted armor values.
  
* Adjusted crafting recipes.
Added tempering recipes.
  
* Added proper colors to the inventory items.
Added proper first person models for the armor and gloves.
  
* **New no high heels boots added**. They are intended to be use together with the Main files. [BodySlides for the no high heels boots here](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/165944)﻿, by [ittybityyspiderkitty](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/168397143) (thanks!).

  
**Optional files**
  

  
* **4K** (original size, already included in the main file), **2K** and **1K** optimized textures.
**Varinia - Custom Voiced Retired Spy and Companion Outfit replacer**. Standalone. She will wear the Obsidian version. It won't affect any Varinia face replacer.
  

  
**My Version****It will replace [ELLE] Knight Winds.esp.** Based on the PC LE/Xbox version.

  
* It has an unique shape, similar to CBBE and UNP.
Top and pants are merged in one outfit.
  
* Boots are no high heels.
Heavily reduced clipping.
  
* Non HDT.
It's fine to switch mid-game, but you will lose the high heels boots and pants.
  

  
**Requirements**
  

  
* [ELLE Knight Winds 3BA - BHUNP](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/89980).
Optional: [Varinia - Custom Voiced Retired Spy and Companion](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/148853)﻿.
  
* Optional: [BodySlides for the no high heels boots here](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/165944)﻿.
