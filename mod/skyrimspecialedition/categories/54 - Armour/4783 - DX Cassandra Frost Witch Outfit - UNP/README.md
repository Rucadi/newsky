A new craftable light Elven outfit and a crystal staff.
  
  
Has three hat sizes to fit most hair and optional underwear.
  
  
Available for UNP with Race Menu High Heels support.
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/61012-5-1439381236.png)
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/53846-3-1412459723.png)
  
  
This is the fifteenth armor of the series. It was suggested by [Nermal\_66](http://www.nexusmods.com/games/users/883544/?) and it's from a comic character from the Tales from Oz.
  
Here is the [picture](http://static.comicvine.com/uploads/scale_large/6/67663/3676937-03a.jpg) used as reference.
  
  
You can find the crafting manual inside the Midden, below College of Winterhold.
  
\*It's on the table near the Atronach Forge along with all the materials needed to craft the outfit once. Just look around a bit.
  
  
Enjoy! :)
  
  
The idea of this series originated from [Minidresses Collection mod by Tiwa44](http://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/47376/) who did an awesome job converting some of the long dresses to mini versions.
