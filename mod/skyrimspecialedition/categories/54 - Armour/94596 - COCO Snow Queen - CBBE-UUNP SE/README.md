# COCO Snow Queen - CBBE-UUNP SE
- Author: COCO
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/94596


[font=Comic Sans MS]**![](https://i.imgur.com/H4SMPaZ.png)﻿﻿**[/font]**A made from scratch outfit by COCO, featuring a unique elven mage style outfit. The outfit comes with CBBE 3BA and COCOBody CBBE/UUNP bodyslides support. The Ribbon Arm, Ribbon Skirt, Skirt, and Skirt Rib come with HDT-SMP cloth physics.**
