# Elven Enforcer Armor
- Author: furexarot
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/109638


**Basic info:**
  

  
* Unisex, functional weight sliders, beast races support
CBBE 3BA bodyslide
  
* Craftable, temperable
Distributed via SPID
  
* Choose between 2k or 1k textures ([4k and 8k](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/109933))﻿
Optional dark gold recolor
  

**Adds 13 pieces of equipment:**
  

  
* Elven Enforcer Armor (3 variants with different pauldrons)
Elven Enforcer Gauntlets (2 variants)
  
* Elven Enforcer Boots (2 variants)
Elven Enforcer Helmet and Riot Mask (each with open and closed variants)
  
* Elven Enforcer Beak Mask
Blind Justice (blind mask)
  

You can craft and temper them with Elven Smithing perk. The stats are similar to Elven Armor. 
  
**Recommended﻿:**[SPID](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/36869)to distribute the armor to NPCs
  
[CBBE 3BA Body](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/30174) for HDT-physics (modest amount where appropriate)
  
[Bodyslide﻿](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/201) to apply your custom presets
  
  
*Made using assets from**nisetanaka's**[TAWOBA](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/83383)**,****xavbio's*** ***[Elven Armors and Weapons Retexture](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/87283/) and cabal's [aMidianBorn Book of Silence](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/35382)***
  
I run a somewhat lore-friendly modlist, so I generally avoid bikini mods, but recently I checked "The Amazing World of Bikini Armors" out and it had a lot of genuinely good mashups. I thought to myself, *"wow, this would fit right into my modlist if the gazongas were covered up somewhat"*. That's what I started with, and it kinda snowballed from there.
