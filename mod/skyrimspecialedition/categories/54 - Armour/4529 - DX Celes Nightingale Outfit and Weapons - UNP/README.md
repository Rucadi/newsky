A new craftable light Daedric armor, sword, dagger, bow and crossbow with/out poisonous arrows and bolts.
  
  
Available for UNP with Racemenu High Heels support.
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/61012-4-1436239736.png)
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/53846-3-1412459723.png)
  
  
This is the thirteenth armor of the series and one of the most challenging to date. I always wanted to make a glow-ish type of armor and Nightingale seemed like a good opportunity. But to make the glow work together with the rest of the textures needed a lot of creative thinking.
  
  
If you rush to get the book don't forget to gather nirnroots from the farm because you are going to need plenty. Also bring a lot of lockpics because this loot is sealed.
  
  
The idea of this series originated from [Minidresses Collection mod by Tiwa44](http://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/47376/) who is doing an awesome job converting some of the long dresses to mini versions.
