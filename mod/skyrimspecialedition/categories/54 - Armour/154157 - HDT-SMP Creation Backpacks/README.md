**![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/images/1704/120770913-1727266923.png)﻿**Transforms static, rigid backpacks into dynamic, lifelike accessories that respond naturally to your character’s movements and the environment.
  
  
Everything jiggles and wobbles as you'd expect.
  
  

![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/images/1704/120770913-1751661170.gif)          ![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/images/1704/120770913-1751661433.gif)﻿﻿

  
  
The potion bottle is missing from the Adventurer's Backpack. This isn't a glitch; I removed it deliberately because it was clipping through the rope.
  
  
I didn't use collisions, so performance should be much better compared to HDT-SMP Vanilla Armors. My tests revealed no frame loss.
