# COCO Battle Angels - CBBE-TBD-UUNP SE
- Author: COCO
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/64787


[font=Comic Sans MS]**![](https://i.imgur.com/H4SMPaZ.png)﻿**[/font]**A made from scratch outfit by COCO, featuring a unique warrior style outfit. The outfit comes with CBBE 3BA, TBD SE, COCOBody CBBE/UUNP bodyslides support. The crown and cloak come with HDT-SMP cloth physics.**
