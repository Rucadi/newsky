Mod Content**
  
This mod installs the *DCR - King Crusader Heavy War Regalia* that's an addon to the DCR - King Crusader Armor set.
  
  

  
* Heavy War Sword - "Dynamis Remake".
Heavy Battle Cloak - New Item to the armor.
  
* Heavy Battle Hood with souls mask - An upgraded item to the original hood.

  
**Where to find the War Regalia?**
  

  
* The new items can be found in Bloated Mans Grotto, in the same location as the previous armor set.
