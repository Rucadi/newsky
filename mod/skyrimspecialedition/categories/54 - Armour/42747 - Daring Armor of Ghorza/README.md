# Daring Armor of Ghorza
- Author: Nzymia
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/42747


[font=Georgia]This variation of [Ghorza's Armor](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/36459) by original author [atomec](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/users/2118592), separates the armor into several different pieces and presents them in a slightly more daring design.[/font][font=Georgia] A SSE conversion of the original can be found [here](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/16966). For permission information see spoiler below.[/font][font=Georgia]
  
  
Spoiler atomec has specified in the description of his mod: "When using the plug in or parts of it for projects that are published I ask for a reference to me as the author of Ghorza's Armor." I interpret this such that he is Ok with the use of his assets in other projects and gladly comply with his request.
  
  
I have written to atomec twice, once in July 2020 and once again in November, but unfortunately have not received a response. Atomec has last been active on the Nexus in December 2017. I, therefore, presume he has moved on to more pressing matters.
  
  
If anyone reading this should have contact information to atomec, i would be grateful if they could bring my contact request to his attention. If atomec should object to the use of his materials, this mod will be removed.

[font=Georgia][size=5]**How to obtain:**
  
  
[/size][/font][font=Georgia][size=3][font=Georgia][size=4][size=3][size=4]Items can be crafted at the forge and require orcish smithing. Alternatively, the items can be purchased from blacksmith Ghorza Gra Bagol in Markarth.[/size][/size][/font][/size]
  
  
**Installation:**
  
  
[/size][/font]

  
1. [font=Georgia]Install textures from the original mod, or from the SSE conversion, or from any of the Ghorza Armor retextures available on the Nexus. Only textures are needed (this mod contains no Ghorza Armor textures. Textures in the screenshots are [Tru3Magics Ghorza Armor Retexture](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/38549)﻿. Don't forget the separate textures for the shield!).[/font]
[font=Georgia]Then overwrite with this mod.[/font][font=Georgia] (Esp is in ESPFE format and will not count towards the plugin limit!)[/font]
