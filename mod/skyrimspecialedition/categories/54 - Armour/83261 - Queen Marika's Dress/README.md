# Queen Marika's Dress
- Author: RyanReos - full_inu - Odd
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/83261


Queen Marika is the newest model made by [RyanReos](https://twitter.com/ryanreos), a famous and universally beloved author of many sexy outfits.
  
  
Initial Skyrim UNP port was done by me, outfit fully modular and features HDT-SMP cloth physics on skirt and hair.
  
BodySlide support provided by [Odd](https://twitter.com/xxxoddxx) - for CBBE 3BA and TBD bodies. After installing bodyslide files, you will need to build meshes in BodySlide.

**Requirements:**

[XPMSSE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/1988)
  
[Racemenu SE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/19080)
  
[Faster HDT-SMP](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/57339)
  
Body mod of your choice: [UNP](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/43773) or [3BA](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/30174) or [TBD](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/20024)

  
  
Use [AddItemMenu﻿](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/17563?tab=description) or [Simple Mod Item Spawner](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/64653) to get items in game, alternatively can craft items in any forge (recipes will shown with 1 (one) "Alduin is Real" book in inventory).
