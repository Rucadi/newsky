# COCO Fairy Queen - CBBE-TBD-UUNP SE
- Author: COCO
- Game: skyrimspecialedition

[font=Comic Sans MS]**![](https://i.imgur.com/H4SMPaZ.png)﻿﻿**[/font]**A made from scratch outfit by COCO, featuring a unique elven enchanter style outfit. The outfit comes with CBBE 3BA, TBD SE, COCOBody CBBE/UUNP bodyslides support. The Arm Cloth, Low Cloth, and Skirt come with HDT-SMP cloth physics.**
