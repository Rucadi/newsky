# BD's Armor and Clothes Replacer - CBBE 3BA (3BBB)
- Author: Baddog
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/32518


[CBBE 3BA](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/30174?) Bodyslide conversion of [BadDog's Armor and Clothes replacer](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/89849)﻿ + [Dawnguard](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/94864), [Dragonborn](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/97684).
  
  
BD's Armor and Clothes is a vanilla armor and clothes replacer mod for female
  
There is various zap options and [Uniboob patch](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/32264?) applied
  
  
You need the [CBBE 3BA](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/30174?) and [CBPC](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/21224) to activate the body physics of clothes
  
Search with BD CBBE 3BA or take advantage of group feature when use BodySlide
