# SpellBreaker Rework
- Author: PLSET
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/144558


Reworked The Weak Spellbreaker and is now a Powerful Dwemer tone artifact against mages.
  
（This mod is ESL Flag, no need to take precious ESP slot)
  
  
  
• Main changes
  
Item value increased
  
Armor value increased.
  
  
Enchantment Changes
  
When blocking, deploy a Tonal Ward, consuming <30> magicka per second, reducing magicka regeneration speed of enemy targets within <30> yards by <50%>, negating <150> spell damage and increasing <150> armor and <15%> magic resistance and magic absorption, while self-regenerating magicka is impossible, absorbing spell attacks and converting them into <5%> magicka, and separating its Tonal Energy, separating <X> Tonal Energy. Maximum separation <100> Tonal Energy.
  
  
When Shield Bash, consumes <20> Tonal Energy to release Tonal impact, dealing <200> magicka and <50> health damage to targets within <15> yards. When the tone ward is broken, the tone will be Disorder, reducing its own magicka by <100> points, lose tone energy by <20> points, and causing <40> points of stamina damage, <80> points of magicka and <30> points of health damage to targets <30> yards away.

• New Skills
  
(New skills are used similarly to race powers, or are passive effects.)
  
When you equip Spellbreaker, 3 skills are automatically added to you.
  
  
![](https://imgur.com/Gwx5NZt.gif)
  
  
  
• Relative Tone: Consumes <40> Tonal Energy, dealing irresistible Tonal damage equal to <3/10> of the target's magicka to a <30> yard radius. Cooldown <120> seconds.
  
  
﻿![](https://imgur.com/8w8Tj5A.gif)
  
  
  
• Reverse Tone: It consumes <60> points tone energy to strengthen the tone ward ability to resist spells by <100> points, and the converted spell damage will be reversed to restore <5>% of magicka and health. However, if the wrad is broken, Severe backlash reducing its own health by <200> points, lasting <60> seconds. Cooldown <200> seconds.
  
  
![](https://imgur.com/TsRU07q.gif)
  
  
  
• Chaotic Tone : Consumes <100> Tonal Energy, unleashing a <100> yard wave of powerful, chaotic tones that disrupt the affected target, making them unable to cast spells, greatly increasing the cooldown of Shout, making it impossible to regenerate magicka, and dispelling summons and buffs for <60> seconds. Cooldown <600> seconds.
  
  
  
  
  
(The cooldown effect of all skills is universal. If the cooldown effect of a skill has not ended, all skills cannot be used.)
