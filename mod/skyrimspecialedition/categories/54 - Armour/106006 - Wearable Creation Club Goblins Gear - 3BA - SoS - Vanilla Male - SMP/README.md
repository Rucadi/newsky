# Wearable Creation Club Goblins Gear - 3BA - SoS - Vanilla Male - SMP
- Author: Imp
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/106006


Adds playable versions of the gear CC Goblins are adorned with.
  
Available for CBBE 3BA, Vanilla Male, and SoS bodies.
  
  
Crafting recipes for the items are unlocked after the completion of the "Blue in the Face" quest.
  
Most items are crafted at a tanning rack, but items made with iron must be crafted at a smithing station.
  
  
Crafting ingredients and stats wise they are on mostly on par with vanilla items of the same type.
  
  
Bodyslide files are available with zaps to make the armors skimpy if you wish.
