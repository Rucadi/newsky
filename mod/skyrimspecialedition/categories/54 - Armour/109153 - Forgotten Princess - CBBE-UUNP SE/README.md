# Forgotten Princess - CBBE-UUNP SE
- Author: Dint999 - Deedlitz - Daymarr
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/109153


[font=Comic Sans MS]**﻿![](https://i.imgur.com/H4SMPaZ.png)﻿﻿**[/font]**A made from scratch outfit by Dint999, featuring a unique light armor battle dress set. The outfit comes with CBBE 3BA & COCOBody CBBE/UUNP bodyslides support. The dress, shoulder top, cloak, crown and panties come with HDT-SMP cloth physics.**
