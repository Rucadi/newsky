# Daring Gigaduex Armor Set
- Author: Nzymia
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/42154


[font=Georgia]This variation of the [Gigaduex Armor Set](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/21836) by Gigaduex and uploaded by [ZapgiL](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/39376710) aims to give the outfit more variability by introducing some new items and allowing various combinations of individual pieces.
  
The original uploader [ZapgiL](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/39376710) has granted open permission to use the assets and modify his wonderful creation for which I express my gratitude!
  
[/font][font=Georgia]
  
  
[/font][font=Georgia]The outfit now includes the following pieces:
  
[/font]
  
  

  
* [font=Georgia]**Archer Armor** with a shortened skirt[/font]
[font=Georgia]**Archer Boots** now with moderate high heels[/font]
  
* [font=Georgia]**Archer Gloves**, original adapted to body shape[/font]
[font=Georgia]**Archer Pants**[font=Georgia][size=4], they're actually short pants[/font][/size][/font]
  
* [font=Georgia]**Archer Bra,** newly designed from original meshes to fit the Archer Shoulders[/font]
[font=Georgia]**Archer Shirt**, with sleeves[/font]
  
* [font=Georgia]**Archer Shoulders**[/font]
[font=Georgia]**Archer Skirt**, short[/font]
  

  
[font=Georgia]All Archer pieces come in brown or black leather[/font]
  
  

  
* [font=Georgia]**Leather Pants**, original adapted to body shape[/font][font=Georgia][/font] [font=Georgia]in two colors[/font]
[font=Georgia]**Long Boots**, now with moderate high heels in two color variations[/font]
  
* [font=Georgia]**Nevaeh Gloves**, original adapted to body[/font]
[font=Georgia]**Scout Armor,** just the corset and belt actually to be combined with everything else[/font]
  
* [font=Georgia]**Scout Boots**, now with moderate high heels[/font]
**[font=Georgia]Scout Gauntlets[/font]**[font=Georgia][size=4][font=Georgia], original adapted to body[/font][/font][/size]**[font=Georgia][size=4][font=Georgia][b]**[/font][/font][/size][/b]
  
* [font=Georgia]**Scout Top**, just shoulders with sleeves actually[/font]
[font=Georgia]**Scout Bra**, same design as Archer Bra but fitting over the Scout Top[/font]
  
* [font=Georgia]**Scout Pants**[font=Georgia][size=4][font=Georgia][size=4], original adapted to body[/font][/size][/font][/font][/size]

  
[font=Georgia]Most Scout pieces come in three color variations (blue, purple, red), but gloves and boots come in two and pants come in four variants [font=Georgia][size=4] (as in the original[/font]).[/size][/font]
  
  
  
  
[font=Georgia][size=5]**How to obtain:**
  
  
[/size][/font][font=Georgia][size=3][font=Georgia][size=4][size=3][size=4]Items can be crafted at the forge or obtained using the [AddItemMenu - Ultimate Mod Explorer](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/17563)![/size][/size][/font][/size]
  
  
**Installation:**
  
- Install with any mod manager or manually. Esp is in ESPFE format and will not count towards the plugin limit!
