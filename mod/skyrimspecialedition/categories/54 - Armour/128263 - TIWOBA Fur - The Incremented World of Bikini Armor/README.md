﻿**The Incremented World of Bikini Armor (TIWOBA)** is an unofficial continuation and tribute to **The Expanded World of Bikini Armors (TEWOBA)**, which is an unofficial continuation and tribute to **The Amazing World of Bikini Armor (TAWOBA)**.
  
  
This project intends on finishing up the vanilla armor sets that were left out from the previous two mods, in a way that it continues **Nisetanaka**'s legacy of open-permission modular armor sets.
  
This one's focus is the **Fur (Bandit)** set.
  
  
It features **30 new modular armor pieces** for different body parts, in a way that you can mix and match to your heart's content. BodySlide files for **CBBE 3BA** with **ReSqueeze** options are available.
  
My latest BodySlide preset, **[Aphrodite's Daughters 3](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/75596)﻿**, already comes with slider values compatible with this mod's **ReSqueeze sliders**, as well as compatible with all my other **ReSqueeze** mods.
  
  
**I am not affiliated to or with any of the creators of the other two mods.**![](https://iili.io/deStVEB.png)
  
  
﻿All armor pieces can be crafted at a **Forge** or an **Anvil** for 
  
1x **Wolf Pelt** and 2x **Leather Straps**.
  
There's an additional plugin available in the mod's install options for **Leveled List integration** so NPCs can be found wearing the armor, and another one for extra recipes that use other animal pelts instead of only wolves'.
