# Bone Cultist Armor
- Author: furexarot
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/111224


**Basic info:**
  

  
* Unisex, full beast races support
CBBE 3BA and HIMBO v5 bodyslide
  
* Craftable, temperable
Distributed via SPID
  

**Adds 8 pieces of equipment:**
  

  
* Bone Cultist Armor (2 variants, with and without red cloth)
Bone Cultist Gauntlets 
  
* Bone Cultist Boots
Bone Cultist Helmet (open and closed variants)
  
* Bone Cultist Hood (with and without hair sticking out from underneath)

  
Crafting has the same prerequisites as the vanilla Bonemold set, but requires less common materials. The stats are similar to Dwarven equipment.
  
**Recommended﻿:**[SPID](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/36869)to distribute the armor to Reavers on Solstheim
  
[Bodyslide﻿](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/201) to apply your custom presets
  
Models use vanilla texture paths, so both [Bonemold](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/94859) and [Dragon Priest Armor and Weapons Retexture](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/101101?tab=images) are recommended for best visual appearance (the hood uses a custom texture, and it's matched to xavbio's textures).
