**ReSqueeze**is a series that aims to make some armors\* quite a bit
  
*Tighter*, while also providing some small fixes and changes*.*
  
This is achieved by manually editing sliders, mesh weights, UV maps, NIF properties and vertex counts, for the
  
ultimate **Squeeze** experience.*[size=4]*\*Term used way too loosely[/size]
  
[/font][font=Arial]
  
[/font][font=Arial]﻿The object of study this time is **[T.A.W.O.B.A.](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/9547)﻿**
  
[/font]

**How to use:**
  
\_
  
1. Install with your favorite mod manager, or just drag the content from
  
the files directly into your **Skyrim Special Edition\Data**folder
  
\_
  
2. Open **BodySlide** and type **[3BA SQUEEZE]** on the **Filter Outfits...**search bar on the top left corner
  
  
*﻿**![](https://iili.io/JXPEgNs.png)*
  
\_
  
**﻿**[/font][font=Arial]3. Either use the **Aphrodite 3 SQUEEZE**preset or tweak your selected
  
preset with the first 4 sliders to your liking, both left and right
  
![](https://iili.io/JXPEURn.png)
  
\_
  
4. ﻿Click **Batch****Build...**on the bottom left corner
  
[size=2]\_
  
5. ﻿**Right-click**any of the outfits inside the new window and click **Select All** 
  
![](https://iili.io/JXPESHX.png)﻿[/size]
  
\_
  
6. Click **Build** and wait for BodySlide to finish processing the outfits
  
[size=2]\_
  
7. Enjoy your very much **Squeezed** boobies[/size]
  
\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_
  
  
**OBody:**[font=Arial]
  
\_
  
1. Install with your favorite mod manager, or just drag the content from
  
the files directly into your Skyrim Special Edition\Data folder
  
\_
  
2. Open **BodySlide** and type **[3BA SQUEEZE]** on the **Filter Outfits...**search bar on the top left corner
  
  
*﻿**![](https://iili.io/JXPEgNs.png)*
  
\_**﻿**[/font][font=Arial]3. Choose the **Zeroed Sliders** preset and tweak it with the first 4 sliders to your liking, both left and right
  
**![](https://iili.io/JXPM46l.png)**
  
[size=2]\_
  
4. ﻿Click **Save**to save the preset[/size]
  
\_
  
5. ﻿Click **Batch****Build...**on the bottom left corner
  
[size=2]\_
  
6. ﻿**Right-click**any of the outfits inside the new window and click **Select All** 
  
![](https://iili.io/JXPESHX.png)﻿[/size]
  
\_
  
7. Click **Build** and wait for BodySlide to finish processing the outfits
  
[size=2]\_
  
8. Enjoy your very much **Squeezed** boobies[/size][/font]
