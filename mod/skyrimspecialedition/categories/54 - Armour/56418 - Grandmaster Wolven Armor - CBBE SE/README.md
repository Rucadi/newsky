# Grandmaster Wolven Armor - CBBE SE
- Author: fxxkthisworld
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/56418


[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]DESCRIPTION:
  
  
Adds the Grandmaster Wolven armor for female characters with a CBBE body. Contains 7 different colors of armor.
  
  
The armor is light, has the characteristics of dragon scale armor.
  
  
You can craft these items at the forge. Requires the "ebony smithing" perk.
  
  
REQUIREMENTS:
  
  
[CBBE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/198) or [CBBE 3BA](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/30174)
  
[BodySlide and Outfit Studio](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/201)
