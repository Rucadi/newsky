# Hircine Artifact Rework
- Author: PLSET
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/151860


Reworked both of Hircine's artifacts to make them playable and more useful.
  
（This mod is ESL Flag, no need to take precious ESP slot)
  
  
  
Savior's Hide
  
  
  
• Main changes
  
Item value increased
  
Slightly reduced armor value
  
  
Enchantment Changes
  
Reduce all damage from animal enemies by <30>%, increase damage to animal enemies by <2> times, increase magic resistance by <20>%, have a <20%> chance to be immune to physical attacks, gain <5> points of <Beasthunt> by killing animals, increase health and stamina by <40> points for every <20> points of <Beasthunt>, when your <Beasthunt> points are above <60>, all animals within <10> yards with a level lower than or equal to yours will be feared for <10> seconds. Currently have <xl> points of Beasthunt, up to <100> points of Beasthunt.

Cooldown <200> seconds.

Ring of Hircine
  
  
  
• Main changes
  
Item value increased
  
Additional Werewolf Transformation Mechanic Changes
  
  
Enchantment Changes
  
Unarmed damage increased by <40> points, unarmed attacks ignore the target's <50%> armor value, equipping this ring can be transformed into a werewolf infinitely, all magic and physical damage taken by non-beast blood is reduced by <15%>, and increased to <40%> with beast blood. Killing a human will gain <5> points of <Beastliness>, and for every <10> points of <Beastliness>, unarmed damage increased by <10> points, when you reach <60> points of <Beastliness>, movement speed increased by <50%>, and the stamina consumption of heavy attacks reduced by <50%>, currently has <x> points of Beastliness, up to <100> points of Beastliness.
