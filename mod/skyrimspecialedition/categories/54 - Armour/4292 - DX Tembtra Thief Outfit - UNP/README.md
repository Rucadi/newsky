A new craftable scaled light armor found under steel category. Available in UNP and offering a wide range of customization from skimpy to badass.
  
  
The pouches are optional and add 100 points of extra carry weight.
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/53846-3-1415087390.png)
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/53846-1-1412462830.png)
  
  
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/53846-5-1415087390.png)
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/53846-3-1412459723.png)
  
  
 This is the ninth armor of a series that will hopefully reach many armors. The idea originated from [Minidresses Collection mod by Tiwa44](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/47376/)﻿ who did an awesome job converting some of the long dresses to mini versions.
  
  
These armors will not offer any protection in real life so don't use them at home!
