﻿- Mod Description -
  
  
A lore-friendly overhaul of Tel Mithyrn including the exterior and all 4 interiors:
  
 Neloth's Tower, Steward's House, Apothecary and Kitchen.

- Performance -
  
  
My Rig: MSI MEG Z390 Godlike Motherboard, Intel I9 9900K @ 3.6 GHz
  
32 Gb DDR4 3600 MHz RAM, EVGA NVidia RTX 3080TI 12 Gb, playing at 1440p resolution alongside Aequinoctium ENB + a mix of 4K and 2K Textures and I get a solid 60 FPS in both exteriors and interiors (locked FPS).
  
  
- Installation -
  
  
Install with your preferred mod manager.
  
  
- Requirements -
  
  
- Skyrim up to date, will work with both SE and AE.
  
  
- Compatibility -
  
  
- [JK's Tel Mithryn Patch Collection](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/154109)﻿ by Czasior.
  
- Not compatible with other mods that overhaul Tel Mithryn (with the exception of texture mods)
