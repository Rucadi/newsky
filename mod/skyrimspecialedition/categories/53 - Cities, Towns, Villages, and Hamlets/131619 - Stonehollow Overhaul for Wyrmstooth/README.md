Stonehollow is the main settlement on the island of [Wyrmstooth](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/45565). It has a charming Imperial vibe with its white plaster walls, but there are inconsistencies in the various models used throughout the town. This mod aims to unify the aesthetic by enhancing both Nordic and Imperial styles.
  
  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/PierreDespereaux/PierreDespereaux/master/assets/images/banners/Features.png)
  
  

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/PierreDespereaux/PierreDespereaux/master/assets/images/UniqueStonehollow/UniqueStonehollowComparison.gif)

  

  
* Replaces the vanilla farmhouses with new models utilizing a unified tiled roof.
Overhauls the Stonehollow Hall of the Dead into the undercroft of a new chapel rather than sharing a mesh with Whiterun.
  
* Adds a new Chapel of Zenithar in place of where the old Hall of the Dead building was.

  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/PierreDespereaux/PierreDespereaux/master/assets/images/banners/Compatibility.png)
  
  
To maintain compatibility, minimal changes have been made to the worldspace. However, there is still a possibility of conflicts with other mods that edit the Stonehollow area.
  
  
You will need to run either [xLODGEN](https://dyndolod.info/Help/xLODGen) or [DynDOLOD](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/68518) in order to have your LODs match the new models.
