**Overhauls Karthwasten to feel more lived-in and grounded, better reflecting its history as an ancient mining town and showing the harsh reality of life in the Reach.**
  
**✓** Lore Friendly & Immersive  **✓** ESL Flagged  **✓** Performance Friendly
  
  
> **Immersive & Logical Location Overhaul**
>   
> Once the capital of the Reach, Karthwasten is a long-standing mining town built around the ancient Sanuarach Mine. Yet despite its history and importance, there’s no indication it was ever more than a few shacks. 
>   
>   
> **The Walls of Karthwasten** transforms the settlement into a rugged mining town, offering a glimpse into the glory days of old Karthwasten—when it stood as a place of power, pride, and enduring history in the Reach.
>   
>   
> **What This Mod Does**
>   
> **New Exterior Architecture**—Battered walls & towers that reflect the Reach's harsh nature.
>   
> **Enhanced Foliage & Terrain**—Improved foliage, rockwork, and terrain for a more natural look.
>   
> **New Decorative Details**—New decorative clutter to make the town feel more active & alive.
>   
> **Historical Identity**—New details highlighting Karthwasten’s history of warfare and silver.
>   
> **Unique Regional Aesthetic**—Distinct design that sets Karthwasten apart while fitting the region’s tone.
>   
> **Performance Considerations**—Occlusion culling to lower draw calls and ensure smoother performance.
>   
>   
> For the intended look, I recommend using this mod alongside [High Quality Ivy](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/113578).
>

> **Q:** Did you edit any of the interiors?
>   
> **A:** No, I haven't touched any of the interior cells. All changes are in the exterior worldspace.
>   
>   
> **Q:** Did you edit or add any new NPCs?
>   
> **A:** No. This mods focus is around static objects like walls, towers, foliage, etc...
>   
>   
> **Q:** Did you create any custom LOD?
>   
> **A:** No, this mod doesn’t include any pre-generated LOD. I’ve provided the necessary meshes, but you’ll need to use DynDOLOD.
>   
>   
> **Q:** What texture is used for the windmill and towers?
>   
> **A:** The mod uses whatever farmhouse wall and riften roof textures you have installed. I've also added a misc file that switches the Riften roof textures for farmhouse textures instead.
>   
>   
> **Q:** Another mod conflicts with yours. Can you make a patch for it?
>   
> **A:** Feel free to request a patch, but no promises.
