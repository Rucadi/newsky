Description[/b]
  
**This is a complete overhaul of Tel Mithryn location. I tried to give this location more unique look and enhance that** *Morrowind vibe***. I used high quality custom objects, flora models and clutter from resources created by some very talented modders. I hope you will like the result!**
  
  
**Features**
  
**- Unique flora and objects from Morrowind** 
  
**(thanks to Phitt's Resources and Ghosts of Tribunal Creation)**
  
  
**- More magical clutter in Tel Mithryn (it's a Magic Tower after all)**
  
  
**- No lighting edits (should make this mod more compatible with lighting overhauls)**
  
  
**- No direct navmesh edits (navcut boxes were used)**
  
  
**- Plugin flagged as ESL and will help you avoid 255 mods limit**
  
  
  
**Requirements**
  
**Ghosts of Tribunal Creation (Included in Anniversary Edition)**﻿
