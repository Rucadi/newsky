﻿**- Mod Description -**[font=Trebuchet MS][/font]
  
A lore-friendly overhaul of the Solitude outskirts, including the Katla's Farm and stables, the Solitude Sawmill, the docks, continuing all the way to the Solitude side entrance.

[font=Trebuchet MS]**- Performance -**
  
  
My Rig: Asus P8B75-M/CSM Motherboard, Intel I7 2600 @ 3.4 GHz
  
16 Gb DDR3 1333 Mhz RAM, NVidia RTX 2080TI 11 Gb, running alongside Aequinoctium ENB + 2048 Textures and I get 60 FPS average in interiors, 40 to 50 in exteriors.
  
[/font]
  
[font=Trebuchet MS]**- Installation -**
  
  
[/font][font=Trebuchet MS]Install with your preferred mod manager.
  
  
[/font]**- Requirements -**
