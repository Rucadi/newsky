**How to use and fix presets with sculpt data**
  
**Use the Ears**
  
  

  
* [font=Arial]In Racemenu under '**Head**', select '**Facepar**t' 3 (For High Poly Head, optional but recommended) [/font]

  
* [font=Arial] Go to '**Extra**' and **choose your ear type**. Use the '**Hide Ears**' slider to hide the vanilla ears. It has to be above 1[/font]

  
**Fix sculpt data**
  
[font=Arial]**It's a fast and clean method. you don't need to sculpt anything.**[/font]
  
  

  
* [font=Arial] Go to the '**Sculpt**' tab in Racemenu[/font]
[font=Arial] Use the '**Add Mask**' brush and mask your full head blue[/font]
  
* [font=Arial] Use the '**Subtract Mask**' brush to remove the mask from the area covered by my ears[/font]
[font=Arial] Press **Z** or click on **remove sculpt data**. Done.[/font]
