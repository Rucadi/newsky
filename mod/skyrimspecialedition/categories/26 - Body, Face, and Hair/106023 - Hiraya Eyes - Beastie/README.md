Hiraya Eyes - Beastie, a derivative of [Kala's Eyes](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/29895)﻿
  
  
Made substantial edits from the original eyes. With inspiration from other eyes mods out there.
  
  
**This mod adds:**
  
  
- Custom drawn eyelashes
  
- 4k, 2k, 1k, 512p texture sizes
  
- Eyes for non-beasts
  
[size=4]- ESL-flagged .esp
  
- Option for eyes to glow[/size]
