**Features:**
  
  
[size=3][size=3]✧ [/size][/size]**PROPER RACEMENU INTEGRATION**[size=3][size=3] Adds and condenses [Viera Ears](https://www.patreon.com/posts/viera-ears-1-4-100612577)﻿ by [Randall Steele](https://www.patreon.com/c/randallsteele)﻿ to its own Slider in the "Hair" category[/size].
  
  
[size=3][size=3]✧ [/size][/size][/size]**3 VARIATIONS**[size=3][size=3][size=3] There are three versions, 1 with no SMP enabled, 1 with SMP enabled, and 1 that is shorter with SMP enabled. Just like the original mod.
  
[/size][/size]
  
[size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3]✧ [/size][/size][/size][/size]**RECOLORED**[size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3] This mod includes a recolored version of the ears that may match better with hairs from the Tullius Hair 3 set, HG Hairdos, and KS Hairdos with its original textures.[/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size]
  
  
[size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3]✧ [/size][/size][/size][/size]**TOUCHED-UP** I tried to smooth up some of the low-res stuff from the original textures.[/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size]
  
  
✧ [/size][/size][/size][/size]**ESL-FLAGGED**[size=3][size=3][size=3] This mod will not take up normal plugin slots in your load order.[/size][/size][/size]
  
  
[size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3]✧ [/size][/size][/size][/size]**STANDALONE**[size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3] This mod is not dependent on the original mod.[/size][/size][/size][/size][/size]
  
  
[size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3]✧ [/size][/size][/size][/size]**COMPATIBLE**[size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3] *Should* be compatible with mods like CotR, UBE, and other custom race mods.[/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size]
  
  
[size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3]✧ [/size][/size][/size][/size]**DYNAMIC COLOR**[size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3] Like the original mod, the color of the ears will change depending on your hair color.[/size][/size][/size][/size][/size]
  
  
✧ [/size][/size][/size][/size][/size]**UNISEX**[size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3] The ears can be used on both male and female[/size][/size][/size] characters.
  
[size=3]
  
[size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3]✧ [/size][/size][/size][/size]**PERSISTENT**[size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3] The ears are attached to your character, and can be saved with your RaceMenu presets[/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][/size][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3]
