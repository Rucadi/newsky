# Fishtail Hair by Koralina
- Author: Koralina
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/155092


**Installation:**
  
  
Use MO2, drag and drop into the downloads, install and enable.
  
  
  
**Information:**
  
  

  
* This hairstyle is made from scratch by me
The hair is **unisex** and separate collisions are used for male and female characters
  
* The textures are 4k with mipmaps
The mod requires [Faster HDT-SMP](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/57339)﻿ to work
  
* Screenshots are done by me and [SeekingAsy1um](http://next.nexusmods.com/profile/SeekingAsy1um)
