[font=Verdana]**Tribal Combatants**is an ongoing collection of **Racemenu**overlays containing **20 textures**for your character's customization, including **10 Facepaints**with their matching **Bodypaints**. The collection is based\* on **real native populations**, with this specific one being based\* on **South American natives**.
  
 
  
\* The warpaints included are not supposed to represent the real individual tribes/communities, they are only lightly based around pictures of their respective traditional body paints, with a heavy touch of my imagination.[/font]
