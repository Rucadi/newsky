﻿**Installation:**
  
[size=3]Put the data folder into your SkyrimSE folder (where TESV.exe is)
  
  
[/size]Or just download it with the your mod manager of choice.
  
  
  
  
**After loading the preset the face is deformed and not at all as in the screenshots?**
  
Go to the "sculpt" section in racemenu and then press "import sculpt".
  
Select the one you already loaded as a preset and then accept.
