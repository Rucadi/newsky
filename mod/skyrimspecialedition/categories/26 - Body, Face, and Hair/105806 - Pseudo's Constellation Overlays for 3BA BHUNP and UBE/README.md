- Author: Pseudohedron
- Game: skyrimspecialedition

![](https://i.ibb.co/x2nNxgX/Screen-Shot207.png)

  
  

﻿![](https://i.imgur.com/tv20kRr.png)

  
This began as a tiny project for a character I made. A collection of overlays designed around the in-lore Elder Scrolls birthsign constellations. They are quite simple in design, however others found interest in them so I decide to share! Currently, they are for use with CBBE, and I made them using the 3BA body as a reference. They are designed to be used individually or as a whole collection. Enjoy :)
