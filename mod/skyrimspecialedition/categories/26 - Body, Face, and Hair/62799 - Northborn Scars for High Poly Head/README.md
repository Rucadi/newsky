[b]NORTHBORN SCARS**
  
[/b]**High Poly Head**
  
> ***[size=5][b]Features:***
>   
>   
> Northborn Scars[/b] (along with any other scar mod) are semi-compatible with High Poly Head - No, your game will not crash if you do not install this mod, scar will just look a VERY off.[/size]
>   
>   
> This mod is especially helpful for those who are creating/editing NPCs and need the scars to look right when exported.
>   
>   
> This mod replaces the .esp included with **Northborn Scars** to make all the scars look right (by using meshes provided by High Poly Head).
