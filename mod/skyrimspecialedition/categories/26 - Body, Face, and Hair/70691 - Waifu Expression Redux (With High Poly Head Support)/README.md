[font=Verdana]*[font=Trebuchet MS]Introduction:
  
  
High Poly Head, was another mod that took the community by storm, but there was just one problem for me. I wasn't really feeling the expressions, and some of the gimmicks I was used to pulling off on screenshots with vanilla head, were just not possible with High Poly. People kept recommending me Expressive Female Animation, but for me it had the same problem of its design not really being what I needed. So I ended up taking a look at the head tri files myself, and decided to rebuild expressions to something I actually liked.[/font][font=Trebuchet MS]
  
[/font]*

[font=Trebuchet MS]**WHAT IT DOES:**Â **Re-sculpting of facial expressions that works both on Vanilla and High Poly Head.**
  
 Needs mfg console or a similar mod (like Opparco on SE) to work.
  
Among the main differences with other mods, I've made expressions a bit more "intense" and it's possible to make more exaggerated expressions such as a full teeth smiling grin, I've made Aah and Big Aah to be more open, same with Oh, I've made a more marked Happy dialogue/mood and so on. Big and little differences.
  
The expressions were originally intended for HPH, but I've crafted the same edits on a vanilla head tri file, so it has files for both.

**Using on Followers and other NPCs:**
  
If a follower is using their own tri files, they are usually located somewhere on the follower's file tree, something like Meshes/Actors/CustomFollower/Head or something similar, *it depends on the author's design*. But once located (And it should be easy, because head tri is usually named "FemaleHead.tri" or something very recognizable) it's a matter of knowing whether it's vanilla or high poly head, and you can overwrite those tris with custom ones, like IE, the ones that come with this mod.
  
  
ANOTHER POSSIBILITY, is that the author didn't natively customize head meshes for the character, or that it's just a vanilla Skyrim NPC you want to see this on. Those, end up using the base directories for either Vanilla or High Poly heads, in which case, this mod is already acting on them.
