***Disclaimer**:
  
These are kitbashed and their texture quality will depend on which texture mods you are running.*
  
  
Although several hair mods exist, most options for Redguard hair are incredibly bad.
  
So I decided to make a few that I think are lore-friendly enough to fit into the ES universe.
  
These hairstyles are available for humanoid races.
  
I will probably make them available for Elves in a future update.
  
*(Maybe with some shoulder length or longer ones in the future.)*
