**Features:**
  
  
[size=3][size=3]✧ [/size][/size]**BETTER ORGANIZATION**[size=3][size=3] Adds [Praedys' Wearable Horns](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/54531)﻿ to its own Slider in the "Hair" category[/size]
  
  
[size=3][size=3]✧ [/size][/size][/size]**55 DIFFERENT HORNS**[size=3][size=3][size=3] Introduces the[/size][/size] 55 different horns from [Wearable Horns](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/54531)﻿ into RaceMenu
  
  
[size=3][size=3][size=3]✧ [/size][/size][/size][/size]**UNISEX**[size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3] The ears can be used on both Males and Female[/size][/size][/size] characters
  
  
[size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3]✧ [/size][/size][/size][/size]**BEAST COMPATIBILITY**[size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3] The horns are enabled for both human and beast races[/size][/size][/size][/size]
  
[size=3]
  
[size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3]✧ [/size][/size][/size][/size]**PERSISTENT**[size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3] The horns are attached to your character, and can be saved with your RaceMenu presets[/size][/size][/size][/size][/size]
  
  
**Purpose:**
  
  
[size=3]✧ Better organization of new headparts to use for your characters[/size]
  
  
[size=3][size=3]✧ Removes the need in having to equip different horns. With this mod they will stay on your character and are not equipment.
  
  
[size=3][size=4]**How to use:**
  
  
✧ Go to the "Hair" tab in RaceMenu, and you will see a slider for Praedys Horns. You can select the horn you want there.[/size][/size][/size][/size]
