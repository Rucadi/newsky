* [b]This mod use ESP-FE or ESP Flagged as ESL (Light Plugin)**
  
**[font=Trebuchet MS][size=3]Make sure you have [b][[b]RaceMenu](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/19080)**﻿ and all it requirement before installing this makeup mod [/b][/font][/b]
  
- **[font=Trebuchet MS]Ancient Beauty Makeup will add 28 texture set of makeup [b]overlays 15 set regular makeup & 13 set face paint only different is face paint can glow and can make the eyeliner color darker and deeper than regular makeup , you can mix it to create a nice looking blend especially for the eye shadow and eyeliner part also for a better result the eyeliner should be use in the end of makeup order , you can add subtle glow for the glitter to make the eyeshadow more interesting this effect is really great for ENB user**[/font][/b]
**Come with 2 optional texture resolution 2K & 4K , for the maximum quality i personally use 4K texture in all my screenshot**
  
- [font=Trebuchet MS]This makeup support [**High Poly Head**](https://vectorplexis.com/files/file/283-high-poly-head/) , [**UBE Ultimate Body Enhancer**](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/92989) & [**COtR - HQ Characters Creation**](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/27405)﻿[/font]
**Install my mod with your favorite mod manager then activated the mod , find the makeup overlays in the racemenu makeup selection , type " T " to choose the texture then type " SDZ21 Ancient Beauty Makeup " and all the texture will show up , you can change the color of the makeup and opacity to your liking**
