# Subtly's CVEO Addon
- Author: SubtlyLiminal
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/161395


This is a conversion of my [previous addon](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/158982) for [Eyes Nouveaux](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/131210) to work with [CVEO](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/160705).
  
  
Two modules:
  
  
**Main**: Additional eye colors for humans, orcs, and elves.  All added to the custom heterochromia system.
  
**Vampire**: Many vampire eyes with custom glowmaps  All added to the custom heterochromia system.
  
  
The modules can be installed independently of eachother.  Both require CVEO.

- 6 high elf eyes
  
- 3 wood elf eyes

The new eye colors fall into 3 categories:
  
  
- Additional eye colors from Khisartin's Eyes Mod 2 (like the dark brown eyes)
  
- Additional eye colors edited by myself from Eyes Nouveaux and Eyes Mod 2
  
  
This was originally designed to go along with Novelyst's cut from [Eyes Nouveaux](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/131210).  Sclera colors for mer are the same as in
  
Novelyst's Cut.  I am working on porting Novelyst's cut as well and will link the port when I'm done.
  
  
**Vampire Module:**
  
  
Based on the various options in the original Eye Mod 3 mod, but greatly
  
expanded.  They are enabled for all humanoid races.  This is a separate
  
plugin because there's so many and maybe people who don't play vampires
  
won't want them.  They glow.  Also, there are 2 very similar dark
  
sclera colors.  You have the option of using sclera version 1, version
  
2, or both at once.
