It's a time to surrender to "Muse of Passion" with this beautiful BodySlide Preset for 3BA.
  
  
Muse of Passion is compilation of my prefrences of beautiful women. The best thing we can do is to share with our passion for women!
  
  
Preset based on [Caliente's Beautiful Bodies Enhancer -CBBE-](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/198) and [CBBE 3BA](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/30174) by Acro748
  
  
**INSTALLATION**
  
  
First of all be sure that you have installed [CBBE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/198), [CBBE 3BA](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/30174) and [BodySlide and Outfit Studio](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/201).
  
To install "Muse of Passion" you can use Mod Organizer 2, Vortex or do it manually.
