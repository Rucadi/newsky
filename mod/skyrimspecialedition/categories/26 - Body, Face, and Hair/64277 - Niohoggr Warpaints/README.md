# Niohoggr Warpaints
- Author: Collygon
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/64277


**For ritual or war, from druids to warriors, Niohoggr Warpaints brings a collection of Celtic and Nordic themed warpaints to Skyrim.
  
  
[![](https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/51905922160_50f96b0e46_c.jpg)](https://flic.kr/p/2n5Kqhq)

  
To use:**
  
 ﻿You will find all warpaints in the makeup tab in racemenu
  
  
**Compatibility:**
  
 This should be compatible with everything.
  
**Requirements:**
  
 ﻿Racemenu
  
  
**Install:**
  
 ﻿Drop the files into your skyrim data folder or use your preferred mod manager.
  
  
**Uninstall:**
  
 **﻿**Delete the files from your skyrim data folder or use your preferred mod manager﻿.
  
  
**FAQ:** ﻿**Q: Why do they look pixelated?** ﻿**A:** Make the following changes in your SKSE.ini
  
 ﻿ ﻿[Display]
  
 ﻿ ﻿iTintTextureResolution=2048 (or 4096)
  
  
 ﻿**Q: Can you \_\_\_?** ﻿**A:** I will not make changes to this mod outside of bug fixes.
