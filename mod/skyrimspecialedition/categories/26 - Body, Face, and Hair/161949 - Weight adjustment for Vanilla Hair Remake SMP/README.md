When I saw the release of the mods [Sassy Salt and Wind Hair Retexture](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/160675)and [Dynamic NPC Hairstyles](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/161062), I decided to reinstall [Vanilla hair remake](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/63979) to style my own vanilla-plus NPCs. The SMP ver's physics behavior performs excellently indoors, and since it doesn't involve any collision detection, it has very low CPU performance cost. However, I felt that it also made the hair flutter too intensely in the wind. So, I tried learning about SMP to modify the XML files—increasing the hair’s weight and making some other adjustments.
  
  
Installation:
  
Place my mod below *Vanilla Hair Remake* to overwrite it.
  
  
**Additional note:
  
I highly recommend using [SMP Wind](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/76776) and adjusting your preferred parameters in the MCM menu if you want to enhance, reduce, or add more variation to the hair and clothing physics——even if you don’t plan to use my mod.**
  
  
  
当看到 [Sassy Salt and Wind Hair Retexture](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/160675)﻿ 和 [Dynamic NPC Hairstyles](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/161062) ﻿这两个MOD发布的时候，我决定重新装回 [Vanilla hair remake](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/63979) 来打造我的原版+NPC们。原MOD的SMP版的物理表现在室内的效果很好，且不涉及到任何碰撞因此对cpu的性能消耗很低。但我觉得﻿它在风中飘动的太厉害了，于是我试着学习SMP知识去修改xml文件来增加头发的重量，以及做一点其它调整。
  
  
安装：
  
将我的MOD放在Vanilla hair remake的下面去覆盖它。
  
  
**补充：
  
强烈推荐 [SMP Wind](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/76776) 并在MCM菜单中调整你喜欢的参数，如果你希望NPC的头发和衣服的飘动效果需要增强/减弱/有更多变化。即使你不打算装我的MOD。**
