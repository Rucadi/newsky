**- MOD OVERVIEW -** ﻿more tails from the tail guy.
  
replace beast race tails with smp physics tail, you can choose between long and short version for each race and gender, also give khajiit tail fluffy fur by using shell mesh, and is compatible with texture replacer by MONSTERaider. ![](https://imgur.com/0hTvQWm.gif)![](https://imgur.com/99Wpk0n.gif)![](https://imgur.com/MafAuqr.gif)![](https://imgur.com/wAOn4DV.gif)![](https://imgur.com/IxJgxSi.gif)
  
  
**-Installation-** ﻿install with your mod manager of choice , and let it overwrite mods that change beast race.
  
  
**-Requirements-**[FSMP - Faster HDT-SMP](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/57339)
