**What’s in a Name?**

  
[left]As is the case with a majority of my Bodyslide presets, this one truly earns its name at the highest weight. As you move between 0 and 100, however,
  
you will find a full, gradual transition of form, allowing for your character and the females in your game to display a wide variety of size and
  
voluptuousness. I test all my presets in game at various weights to ensure they move smoothly, as the shape and proportions you put on a body can actually be felt in the physics of the game. Whether you want the frailest waif Elf archer, a busty vampire, or a SNU-sized bikini barbarian, I think you will enjoy this body. It will work well w/ just about any normal map; skinny or muscular, soft or chubby, depending on the weight of your character.
  
  
As (almost) always, I’ve also included feet slider data for use with MSF (More Sliders for Feet). I pay special attention to the size and shape of the feet for every individual preset. Don’t Sleep on MSF; It’s a Great Mod!
  
  
As I stated, the body has a wide range of size and shape.  I create my presets with DW’s 3BA w/ UBE anus, so even though it is technically 3BA, it contains more sliders than the standard version for a wider range of customization when creating the body, and you will need it as well in order to enjoy the preset at its full potential.[/left]
  
  

Download w/ your Mod Manager or extract the files manually.
