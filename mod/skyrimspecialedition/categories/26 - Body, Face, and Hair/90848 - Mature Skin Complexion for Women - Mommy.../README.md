DESCRIPTION**
  
  
This is a high quality 8k/4k/2k skin texture based on Mature skin by Maevan2. 
  
Textures have been compressed and optimized so expect little to no impact on performance depending on your machine.
  
I overhauled and updated Maevan2's textures to bring them up to today's standards. 
  
Bc7 comperssion, 8k body diffuse, HD specular maps, improved normal maps with pores, wrinkles and skin texture.
  
  
  
**INSTALLATION**
  
  
Download and Install using a Mod Manager
  
Make sure my texture files win all conflicts by placing my mod last in your install order.
