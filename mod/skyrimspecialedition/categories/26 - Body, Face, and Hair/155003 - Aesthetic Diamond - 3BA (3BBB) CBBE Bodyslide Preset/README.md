# Aesthetic Diamond - 3BA (3BBB) CBBE Bodyslide Preset
- Author: Stalker
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/155003


**Greetings! This is my first mod, and I’m really happy I was able to share it**

  
This is my personal BodySlide preset for the CBBE 3BA body, which I’ve been refining over time. It offers a visually striking, curvy, and elegant body shape that blends beauty and boldness.
  
The preset is designed for players who enjoy characters with a confident, feminine appearance — combining aesthetic balance with fantasy appeal.
  
  
The preset slightly varies between low, medium, and high body weights.
  
The **main difference** lies in the **chest structure**, providing subtle but noticeable changes in shape and size across the weight range. This allows for more flexibility in character appearance and outfit compatibility.
  
  
**Features:**
  
  

  
* Custom CBBE 3BA BodySlide preset
Smooth curves and bold proportions
  
* Focus on elegant yet sensual silhouette
Compatible with CBPC / SMP physics
