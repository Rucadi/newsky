# Mecha Deep Elf Skin renewal
- Author: Fridam
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/103895


[left][font=Georgia]Ever thought that Deep elves/Dwemer should look like they are a part of their own technology? These elves have metallic parts embedded into their skins. This is a replacer for the skin from The Timelost Dwemer - A Deep Elf Race. The skin is BHUNP/CBBE 3BA/COCO/SOS full body friendly and is built upon the BnP skin series. Have lots of details wich is made with lots of love and consideration with some handpainted parts. The textures is 4k or 2k and BC7 compressed for maximum quality.
  
[/font][/left][font=Georgia]
  
![](https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53305754834_95a59e731f_o.png)
  
  
**[b]? Features**[/b]
  
  
All skins are violet with slight redness on the joints/chest/eyesocket/ears etc... The skin have mechanical parts embedded into the skins and a net like effect all over the body. [size=3]We also replaced the eyes with high quality eyes based on Khisartin's eye mods. In game this skin can vary from paler brown to darker violet depending on what skin color choice you make.
  
  
**[b][b]? Installation**[/b]
  
[/b]
  
First install your body type for example CBBE 3BA and SOS full then install [The Timelost Dwemer - A Deep Elf Race](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/22499) and its requirements. Afterwards install the [SOS patch (NSFW site)](https://www.loverslab.com/files/file/7954-the-timelost-dwemer-a-deep-elf-race-sos-plugin-oldrimse/). Last ﻿install one of the files from this mod with a modmanager. For manual installation put all files into data folder and make sure to overwrite both the SOS patch and Timelost Dwemer mod.
  
  
**[b]? Compatibility**[/b]
  
  
Not compatible with CotR/Demonica or SAM. Also not compatible with vanilla body or other non nude bodies. You will also get a neckseam if using other head normals/diffuses from other skins together with the normals/diffuses for the body from this mod.
  
  
The female skin is compatible with CBBE based bodies such as 3BA, COCO body for CBBE and Dibella body. It is also compatible with UNP based bodies such as BHUNP and COCO body for UNP. The male skin is compatible with SOS full and HIMBO full bodies.
  
**[b]? FAQ**[/b]
  
  
***LE?***
  
Does not work on LE, we use BC7 compression for maximum quality.
  
  
***Am I allowed to make translations?***
  
Yes without permission, however the translation have to depend on the BnP mod.
  
  
***Can I use your skin on followers/NPCs?***
  
Yes, but you first have to contact us via message about it so we can discuss terms of use.
  
  
***What ENB are you using?***
  
A tweaked PI-CHO ENB.
