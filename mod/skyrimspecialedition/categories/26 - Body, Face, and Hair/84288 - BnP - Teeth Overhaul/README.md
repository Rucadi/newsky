# BnP - Teeth Overhaul
- Author: Fridam
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/84288


[font=Georgia]![](https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/52737552460_4cbc0a86c0.jpg)﻿﻿[/font]

[font=Georgia]A meticulously crafted, high-quality teeth and mouth overhaul that gives you the brightest smile ever with a 3D look. The textures are made with lots of love, care and consideration with realism in mind from real photography. The textures are also entirely custom, so you will find no vanilla parts here. We made this mod with compatibility in mind, so full support for Vanilla and Expressive facegen morphs models is available. BnP teeth now also fully support RSV. Choose from 2k or 4k resolutions with BC7 compression for maximum quality.

[/font][left][font=Georgia]﻿[/font][font=Georgia][size=3]**[color=#ff7700][color=#f4cccc]?[/color] Features[/color]**
  
  
This mod replaces the vanilla teeth with photorealistic teeth where the diffuse is made from scratch from real photography. It also replaces the normals. We have also made edits to the vanilla and EFM mesh with added specular, subsurface and cubemap to give the teeth some realistic shine.
  
[line]
  
From the main files you can select different kinds of teeth textures to be installed after the main files, perfect to put on NPCs. Bear it in mind that these teeth also replace your standard teeth, which means that the player and all NPCs that are linked to standard teeth will get those teeth. Select from:

[font=Georgia][line][size=3]
  
The misc files provides the RSV patch. [size=3][size=3]It patches the BnP teeth for RSV, which distributes all the BnP teeth options to NPCs.[/size]
  
[/size]
  
**[color=#ff7700][color=#f4cccc]?**[/color] Installation[/color]Install with a mod manager or manually drag and drop the files into the data folder. Finally make sure our mod win all conflicts by placing it as low as possible in your load order, i.e. after the conflicting mod.
  
  
[color=#f4cccc]**[color=#ff7700][color=#f4cccc]?**[/color] Compatibility[/color][/color]
  
  
Not compatible with mods that alter the same teeth textures and meshes as this mod.
