# BnP - Female Skin
- Author: Fridam
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/65274


[font=Georgia]![](https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/52736700144_9b79e8beb4.jpg)
  
﻿A semi-realistic, meticulously crafted high-quality skin texture replacer for females with insane attention to detail and characteristics. Parts of the textures are made from photoscans and 3D sculptures with hand drawn parts made with lots of love, care and consideration with realism and a touch of fantasy in mind. You will find a lot of microdetails on this skin, such as pores, wrinkles, spots, moles, freckles and vellus hair to resemble more like real skin. All parts of the female texture are covered, even Astrid, afflicted and the complexions. We made this mod with compatibility in mind, so full support for CBBE, UNP and COCO-based bodies is available. Full support for RSV and standard replacer versions is also provided. Choose from 4k or 2k resolutions with BC7 compression for maximum quality.

[font=Georgia][font=Georgia][size=3]**[font=Georgia][size=3]*RSV textures:***[/font][/size][/font]
  
Patch is availible [here](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/102667). RSV [(Racial skin variance)](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/81668) gives all races, minus beasts, unique skins. It affects non-playable races such as Dremora etc. It also gives the player unique skins apart from NPCs, so you can use whatever player skin you like. RSV affects NPCs through a standalone folder named RSV and the player's skin from the standard female/male folder so the player (you) can use a unique skin. It can also distribute different arrangements of muscularity for each race depending on weight, if you choose. RSV gives unique textures to the NPCs' faces through SPID. You also get extra skins such as Fantasy elves, Dremora etc for RSV both for player and NPCs. [size=3][size=3][size=3]All textures have been made to fit each race characteristics as best as we see fit. This is the setup we have selected for all the races:[/size][/size][/size]
  
  
[left]***Breton***
  
A soft skin with moles spread out like the stars in the sky, rosy lips and lots of imperfections. Have a soft chubby body with slight fat on belly.[/left]
  
***Imperial***
  
A skin with a natural brown undertone, full of cuts and bruises. Skin is harsh, aging with light freckles and with dark brown lips. Have a toned, mediocre (medium) muscled body.
  
  
***Nord***
  
A blushed skin with light pink lips. The skin gets light frostnip and dryness while exposed to extreme cold. Have a body with lots of muscle that is perfect for a Dovahkiin.
  
  
***Redguard***
  
A darker skin with black lips. The skin is resistant to heavy sunlight and rarely gets burnt. Have lighter palms/feet and a strong boxer-inspired body with rock hard abs.
  
  
***Altmer***
  
Slight pale highlighted skin with a warm yellow undertone and orange-colored lips. The skin is fair and easily gets stretch marks if growing in length. Have a sporty body with just the right amount of muscle.
  
  
***Dunmer***
  
A cool undertone skin with a translucent feeling and[font=Georgia] cold purple lips[/font]. Here you see ribs and veins through a thin layer of skin. Have a skinny body with bumpy abs/spine and protruding blood vessels and bones.
  
  
***Bosmer***
  
A natural skin with pink lips. The skin is sensitive to the sun and has lots of pretty freckles. Have a petite body with little muscle.
  
  
***Orsimer***
  
A skin covered in dust and mud with light brown lips. Dirty from head to toe. Have extreme muscles that perfectly suit a warrior with heavy armor.

**[b][color=#ff7700][color=#f4cccc]?[/color] Installation**[/color]
  
[/b]
  
Install with a mod manager or manually drag and drop the files into the data folder. Finally make sure our mod win all conflicts by placing it as low as possible in your load order, i.e. after the conflicting mod. It is very important to read/follow the installation instructions, else you will get seams. For more detailed installation instructions and load order look at the sticky post in comments section.

?**[/color] FAQ[/color][/color][/b][/color][/left]
  
***LE?***
  
Does not work on LE, we use BC7 compression for maximum quality. If using them on LE you have to port them with CAO.
  
[/font][font=Georgia]
  
***Am I allowed to make patches for other body types?***
  
Depends on what patch it is. If you want to make a patch, contact us first.
  
  
[font=Georgia][size=3]***Am I allowed to make translations?***[/font]
  
Yes without permission, however the translation have to depend on the BnP mod.
  
  
***Can I use your skin on followers/NPCs?***
  
[/size][/font][font=Georgia]Yes, but you first have to contact us via message about it so we can discuss terms of use.
  
  
***Why the esp for the replacer version?***
  
The esp is esl flagged and will not take a spot in your LO. It fixes a vanilla bug wich have male afflicted hands for the female afflicted. It also gives the Bosmer a unique face normal instead of using the Altmers.
  
  
***What ENB are you using?***
  
A tweaked PI-CHO ENB.
  
  
***Non nude version?***
  
If you don't want a nude body, we recommend to use [this](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/74257) for CBBE.
