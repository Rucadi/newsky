4 Recolors for *each* of the hairstyles *(and wigs)* in *[Koralina's Aesthetics - Hair Collection - SMP](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/114769)﻿*, by *[CoralineKoralina](https://next.nexusmods.com/profile/CoralineKoralina/mods)﻿﻿*!

[/size][/i][/font]

[font=Georgia]*![](https://i.postimg.cc/CKfv3NCH/All-Recolor-Charts.png)
  
  
I recommend using a completely****unsaturated gray*** *color if you want the intended look for each of the recolors (****as I used in my screenshots****),* 
  
*but some textures even look good with a different color tint!*
  
﻿
  
- **Cyber Collection**: Inspired by the colors shown on the mod's *[Cyberpunk 2077 counterpart](https://www.nexusmods.com/cyberpunk2077/mods/13523)﻿*.
  
- **Sweet Collection**: A mix of ombres using a *pastel* color palette.
  
- **Gothic Collection**: A mix of ombres using a *darker* color palette.
  
- **Grayscale Collection**: *Grayscale* textures with an ombre effect.
  
*Textures are****4k***,*as they are in the Original mod.
