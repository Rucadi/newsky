- Author: AllieWadeMods
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/162809


I made this for my game and thought I could share.
  
  
**== Consider this an initial release ==**
  
I might tweak them more and add new ones in the future. Feel free to track the mod if you want to keep up with the updates.
  
  
*Male version for HIMBO is coming soon.*
  
I tried to give a bit of variety to the distribution of female bodies while keeping believable and harmonious proportions, avoiding ginormous bazoongas that end up over-stretching the outfits and look kinda bad. I still want my ladies to be able to move and fight.
  
  
If you don't like them or they look "too similar" to each other, you can easily find some of the other hundreds of presets that are available on Nexus.
  
I made this according to my taste. You're free to tweak the presets as you see fit for your game.
  
  
------------
  
  
**OBody configuration**
  
  
If you use [OBody](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/77016) and want to distribute the presets to your NPCs I included a premade configuration file (again according to my gameplay) - you can use it as is or change it however you like including also your other presets.
  
I tried to assign the presets to some factions and races in a way that made sense to me, I also blacklisted the pregnant presets so that it only applies to Seren and Rhiada the only two canonically pregnant NPCs so that they show their baby bump [100% more immersion!].
  
For the other NPCs not included in the config, Obody will distribute the presets at random as usual.
  
  
**The config file requires**[OBody NG Preset Distribution Assistant NG](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/159128) - this little gem will add my edits to your OBody config file without replacing your custom .json
  
Please read their mod page for instructions on how to use, I cannot offer support for script mishaps.
