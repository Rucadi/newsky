- Author: wrongii

**What is it?**
  
It's 3BA bodyslide presets. You know the drill~
  
*Natural, Realistic, Immersiv**e*
  
**What's the difference from my last preset?**No CBPC config because SMP collision is better.
  
No armored, outfit and cleavage presets because they are handled by [OBody's ORefit](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/51084).
  
The weight difference in an individual Bodyslide preset isn't as drastic as my [Wrong Body](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/31101) preset.
