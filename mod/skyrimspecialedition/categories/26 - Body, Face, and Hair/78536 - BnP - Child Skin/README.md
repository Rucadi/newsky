[font=Georgia]**[b]? Features**[/b]
  
  
Main files ﻿includes high quality skin for [font=Georgia][size=3][RS Children](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/2650)﻿, [Simple children](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/22789)﻿ and [TKAA](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/48297)﻿[/font] with lots of details and is fully clothed. RS Children comes with new eyes made by Khisartin and brows made by Koralina.
  
  
**[b][b]? Installation**[/b]
  
[/b]
  
Install with a mod manager or manually drag and drop the files into the data folder. Finally make sure our mod win all conflicts by placing it as low as possible in your load order, i.e. after the conflicting mod.
  
  
**[b]? Compatibility**[/b]
  
  
Not compatible with mods that edits the children's skin textures. You have to let this mod overwrite all conflicting mods.
  
  
Compatible with [font=Georgia][font=Georgia][size=3][RS Children](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/2650)[/font][/size][/font], [Realistic RS Children](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/39359), [font=Georgia][font=Georgia][size=3][TKAA](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/48297)[/font][/size][/font], [Children of the North](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/78283) and [font=Georgia][font=Georgia][size=3][Simple children](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/22789)[/font][/size][/font].
  
**[b]? FAQ**[/b]
  
  
***LE?***
  
Does not work on LE, we use BC7 compression for maximum quality.
  
  
***Am I allowed to make translations?***
  
Yes without permission, however the translation have to depend on the BnP mod.
  
  
***Can I use your skin on followers/NPCs?***
  
Yes, but you first have to contact us via message about it so we can discuss terms of use.
  
  
***What ENB are you using?***
  
A tweaked PI-CHO ENB.
