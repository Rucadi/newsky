# BnP - Male Skin
- Author: Fridam and Enroys
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/65402


[font=Georgia]![](https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53243408356_99359943fc.jpg)
  
﻿A semi-realistic, meticulously crafted high-quality skin texture replacer for males with insane attention to detail and characteristics based on Svein skin by talented Vobla. Parts of the textures are made from photoscans and 3D sculptures with hand drawn parts made with lots of love, care and consideration with realism and a touch of fantasy in mind. Most of the body normals are made by scratch in Blender, so you will find new body normals you have not seen before. You will also find a lot of microdetails on this skin, such as pores, wrinkles, spots, moles, freckles and vellus hair to resemble more like real skin. All parts of the male texture are covered, even Snow elves, afflicted and the complexions. We made this mod with compatibility in mind, so full support for [SOS full](https://www.loverslab.com/files/file/5355-schlongs-of-skyrim-se/)﻿﻿, [HIMBO](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/46311)﻿, [font=Georgia][size=3][SOS light](https://www.loverslab.com/files/file/3705-schlongs-of-skyrim-light-se/)﻿, [TNG](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/104215)﻿[/font] and [SAM light](https://vectorplexis.com/files/file/60-sam-light/)﻿ bodies is available. Full support for RBT and RSV for SOS full/TNG/HIMBO full and standard replacer versions is also provided. Choose from 4k or 2k resolutions with BC7 compression for maximum quality.

[size=3][size=3]**[size=3]*[size=3][size=3][size=3][b][size=3][i]RBT textures***: [/size][/size]
  
[/size][/i][/size][/b][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3][size=3]RBT gives all races, minus beasts, a unique body texture. Unique head textures will only work for the player. [size=3]**[size=3]*RSV textures:***[/size]
  
Patch is availible [here](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/102667). RSV [(Racial skin variance)](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/81668) like RBT gives all races, minus beasts, unique skins. It also affects non-playable races such as Dremora etc. It also gives the player unique skins apart from NPCs, so you can use whatever player skin you like. RSV affects NPCs through a standalone folder named RSV and the player's skin from the standard female/male folder. It can also distribute different arrangements of muscularity for each race depending on weight, if you choose. RSV also gives unique textures to the NPCs' faces through SPID. You also get extra skins such as Fantasy elves, Dremora etc for RSV. [size=3][size=3][size=3]All textures have been made to fit each race characteristics as best as we see fit. This is the setup we have selected for all the races:[/size][/size][/size][/size]
  
  
[left]***Breton***
  
A soft skin with blush, small amount of body hair, moles spread out like the stars in the sky and lots of imperfections. Have a lean body with little muscle.[/left]
  
***Imperial***
  
A skin with a natural brown undertone, a quite hairy body and full of cuts and bruises. Skin is harsh and aging with light freckles. Have a toned medium muscled body.
  
  
***Nord***
  
A blushed skin with lighter body hair. The skin gets light frostnip and dryness while exposed to extreme cold. Have a body with lots of muscle that is perfect for a Dovahkiin.
  
  
***Redguard***
  
A darker skin with dark curly body hair. The skin is resistant to heavy sunlight and rarely gets burnt. Have lighter palms/feet and a strong boxer-inspired body with rock hard abs.
  
  
***Altmer***
  
Slight pale highlighted skin with a warm yellow undertone and no hair. The skin is fair and easily gets stretch marks if growing in length. Have a sporty body with just the right amount of muscle.
  
  
***Dunmer***
  
A cool undertone skin with a translucent feeling with slight stubble on chest. Here you see ribs and veins through a thin layer of skin. Have a skinny body with bumpy abs/spine and protruding blood vessels and bones.
  
  
***Bosmer***
  
A natural skin with no hair. The skin is sensitive to the sun and has lots of pretty freckles. Have a smooth body with little muscle.
  
  
***Orsimer***
  
A skin covered in dust and mud with heavy body hair. Skin is dirty from head to toe. Have extreme muscles that perfectly suit a warrior with heavy armor.

The optional files provides extra options to be installed after the main files. Select from:
  
[/size][/font][font=Georgia]***Extra options:***
  
Where you can select extra skins for the player to use (However will also affect NPCs if not using RSV). You can select between Dremora, Human vampire, Monster vampire, Elder, Snow elf, Akaviri, Fantasy (Orsimer, Dunmer, Altmer and Bosmer) and Clean skins. This option is only available for SOS full/HIMBO full because this file only makes sense for RSV.
  
  
***BnP sk fix:***A completely black subsurface textures for those who have weird lightning lines on the skin.***Extra speculars for SOS full/HIMBO full:***You can select from glossy, matte and wet speculars for replacer and RBT version. These options is already included in SOS light and SAM light options.***BnP pubes resource:***xcf files for Gimp to add pubic hairs to the textures you want. Decompress textures through Paint.net or Photoshop. Save as BMP. Open xcf file and add your texture. Then overwrite your texture and save as dds with mipmaps.[/font][font=Georgia][line]
  
The misc files provides different patches to be installed after main and optional files. Currently supports:
  
[/font][font=Georgia]***SAM light head normals:***
  
Head normals with blended SAM light neck.[/font][font=Georgia] This can be used together with [SavrenX Schlong of SAM](https://vectorplexis.com/files/file/900-savrenx-schlong-of-sam-sosam/), most of SavrenX's body normals and [SAM Light Texture Add-on](https://vectorplexis.com/files/file/152-sam-light-texture-add-on/). Make sure to first install the [BnP SAM patch](https://vectorplexis.com/files/file/934-bnp-skin-sam-light-patch/) on Vectorplexis site.
  
  
***DVA male patch:***
  
Replaces the [DVA](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/96817) textures with BnP head normals so you don't get neck seam while using DVA and BnP together.

***LE?***
  
Does not work on LE, we use BC7 compression for maximum quality. If using them on LE you have to port them with CAO.
  
  
***Why no pubic hair?***
  
In our opinion 3D pubes look best and more realistic. If you have 2D pubes they don't match your hair color plus it become double the work for us. So don't ask us to add pubic hairs.
  
[/font][font=Georgia]
  
***Am I allowed to make patches for other body types?***
  
You are only allowed to make schlong patches. If you want to make other patches, contact us first.
  
  
[font=Georgia][size=3]***Am I allowed to make translations?***[/font]
  
Yes without permission, however the translation have to depend on the BnP mod.
  
  
***Can I use your skin on followers/NPCs?***
  
[/size][/font][font=Georgia]Yes, but you first have to contact us via message about it so we can discuss terms of use.
  
  
***What ENB are you using?***
  
A tweaked PI-CHO ENB.
  
  
***Non nude version?***
  
If you don't want a nude body, we recommend to use [this](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/7902?tab=files) and overwrite with our textures.
