Skullbreaker is a complete overhaul of the swing and impact sounds for maces, warhammers and fists. Expect gorey squelches, metallic thunks and bassy impacts on all relevant materials. Each sound has been hand-tuned to feel the most satisfying when you're smashing bandit skulls and breaking through barricades.**HIGHLY RECOMMENDED: [Dynamic Impacts by Goshbrindal](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/140320) pairs PERFECTLY with my sound effects. Make sure to use the AOS-ISC version for my recommended setup.**
