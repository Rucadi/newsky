**![](https://i.imgur.com/IY0SBqS.png)**[font=Georgia]**Vanilla effect shaders often recycle ambient sound effect loops to ensure there's no variety whatsoever.**
  
**![](https://i.imgur.com/IY0SBqS.png)**
  
**Unique Shaders Sound FX aims to change this by replacing or adding various ambient sounds.**
  
**![](https://i.imgur.com/IY0SBqS.png)**

Frost & Frost Cloak FX Shaders
  
Shock FX Shaders
  
Soul Trap FX Shader

Turn Undead FX Shader
  
Chaurus Poison FX Shader

Ghost FX Shaders

Reanimate FX Shaders[/font]
