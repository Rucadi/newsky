# Nordic Winds
- Author: Clofas
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/112370


**Nordic Winds**
  
Requires [Sound Record Distributor](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/77815)﻿ 
  
  
  
﻿﻿**Features:**
  
§ Unique wind loops for exterior regions
  
§ Replaces vanilla wind loops without touching any vanilla records or sound files
  
§ FOMOD installer
  
§ No compatibility conflicts!
  
  
  
“**Nordic winds sing songs of old lore, whispers through pines, tales evermore.”**
  
 
  
Each exterior region has been given a unique wind loop, so no two regions will be the same.
  
  
**FOMOD Options:**
  
§ Silence Vanilla Wind Gusts & Volcanic Tundra Rumbles (Recommended)
  
§ Silence Volcanic Tundra Rumbles Only
  
§ Alternate Eerie Marsh Loop
  
§ Region Key & How-To .txt file for instructions on how to replace sounds
  
  
 
  
  
Please endorse if you like this mod!

  

***Note on Compatibility:***
  
Mods that utilize Sound Record Distributor have no direct conflicts.
  
Some weather mods add their own wind sounds or leave the vanilla wind sounds, but these are attached to specific weather records. These are not affected by this mod. Nordic Winds replaces *only* the regional wind loops attached to specific exterior regions.
