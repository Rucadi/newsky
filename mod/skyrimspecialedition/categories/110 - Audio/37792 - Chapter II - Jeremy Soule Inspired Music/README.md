[font=Times New Roman]Chapter II
  
[/font][font=Times New Roman]**Introduction**[/font]
  
[font=Times New Roman]Chapter II is a music mod containing around 2 hours of new compositions designed to be seamlessly integrated with Soule's original OST for Skyrim. The instrumentation, orchestration and production of these compositions are closely matched to make for a seamless and immersive experience.
  
[/font]
  
[font=Times New Roman]**Installation**[/font]
  
[font=Times New Roman]Drop the files in the Data folder of your Skyrim directory.
  
[/font]
