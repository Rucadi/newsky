# Bleeding Edge - Bladed Weapons SFX Overhaul
- Author: pentapox
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/77384


**[b]You MAY NOT use my mods in paywalled modlists. Already I've seen some 'modlist authors' doing this and I strictly do not condone this. Modlists and load orders should not be hidden behind patreon subscriptions.
  
  
  
[color=#ff0000][size=4][b]****[Audio Overhaul for Skyrim SE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/12466) contains VOLUME SLIDERS for magic, weapons and other categories. If you do not like the volume, I highly suggest you install this as it is compatible with Immersive Sounds instead of leaving comments about your ears being hurt. It will spare both you and I a headache.**
  
  
[/size][/color][/b][/b]**Bleeding Edge is a crisp, visceral rework of bladed weapon impacts and swing effects!**Natively compatible with [Immersive Sounds - Compendium](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/523) and vanilla sound setups out the box, Bleeding Edge turns your favourite bladed weapons into vicious killing machines. Metallic swing accents for all weapons make every battle into a cacophony of steel and impact effects for every supported Immersive Sounds material descriptor make even your terrain collisions feel impactful.
  
  
**Available for Xbox users with the wonderful port made by [StergioValente](https://www.nexusmods.com/users/151204093), go check out his bethesda net profile and more!
  
[Xbox port of Bleeding Edge](https://mods.bethesda.net/en/skyrim/mod-detail/4296099)﻿[StergioValente's Bethesda Net profile](https://mods.bethesda.net/en/skyrim?author_username=Devilsplay77&number_results=20&order=desc&page=1&product=skyrim)**
  
  
**If you use Audio Overhaul for Skyrim SE:** Check out the optional files as I've added a new Skypatch for AOS's dagger impacts in the optional files.
  
  
**HIGHLY RECOMMENDED: [Dynamic Impacts by Goshbrindal](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/140320) pairs PERFECTLY with my sound effects. Make sure to use the AOS-ISC version for my recommended setup.**
