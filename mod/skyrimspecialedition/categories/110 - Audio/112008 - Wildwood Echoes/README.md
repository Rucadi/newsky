[font=Georgia]Requires [Sound Record Distributor](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/77815)Features:
  
 § 30+ New Ambient Sounds to liven up the forests of Skyrim
  
§ Different sounds for different times of day
  
§ No compatibility conflicts
  
  
  
"In wildwoods deep, nature's choir resounds, echoes of life in every sound"
  
  
Embark through the forests of Skyrim to hear the many ambient sounds nature provides.
  
Listen to the trees cracking and growing, branches snapping, foxes yelping, wolves howling, dogs barking, insects buzzing, frogs croaking, winds softly blowing, and axes chopping.
  
  
  
**Please keep in mind as of Version 1.3, the sounds have been adjusted and will be more subtle than the preview videos below.**
