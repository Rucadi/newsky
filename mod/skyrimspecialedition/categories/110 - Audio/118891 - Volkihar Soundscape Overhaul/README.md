[font=Georgia]**﻿****Features:﻿**
  
§ **45** New Sounds overhauling the Soundscapes of Castle Volkihar
  
§ Features new sounds for the Castle Approach, Courtyard, Boss Room, Guild Hall, and both Dungeons.
  
§ Utilizes [Base Object Swapper](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/60805)﻿ and [Sound Record Distributor](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/77815)﻿ to be as compatible as possible
  
  
![](https://imgur.com/aLp5oDx.png)
  
**﻿****Compatibility:**§ Most Lighting mods will need **2** records patched for all sounds to play properly in **Volkihar Dungeons.**
  
[color=#f6b26b]§ ELFX Shadows Enhancer, LUX, & Skyrim is Luminous Patches available via **FOMOD installer**
  
[/color]§ Compatible with All other Audio mods
  
§ Patches are**[[color=#f9cb9c]](https://imgur.com/pAahfFM)**[very simple to create](https://imgur.com/a/5iWjlsk)﻿﻿﻿﻿[/color]and will take mere seconds. Feel free to patch your favorite lighting mod and upload to Nexus or send to me and I will add it to the **FOMOD.**
