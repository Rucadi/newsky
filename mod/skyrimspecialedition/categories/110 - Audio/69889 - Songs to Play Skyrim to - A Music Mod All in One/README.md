Welcome to Songs to Play Skyrim to!
  
This is an all in one version for all my current and upcoming music mods. 
  
  
 All music is DMCA free and free to use for modders in their own projects. 
  
  
To install, download with mod manager or unzip into Skyrim Special Edition folder. 
  
  
You are welcome to make patches for this mod to make it compatible with other music mods. 
  
There are many talented composer in the Skyrim modding scene, and you shouldn't have to choose just one mod! 
  
  
[Follow Me on Twitter](https://twitter.com/JA_Johnson_) to keep up with development and of course my wacky antics
  
  
I hope you enjoy!
