# Murder of Songbirds
- Author: Clofas
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/111766


**Murder of Songbirds**
  
Requires [Sound Record Distributor](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/77815)﻿

  

**Features:**
  
Ø Many new types of bird sounds added to the environment seamlessly
  
Ø Different birds for different times of day
  
Ø Regional Variety
  
Ø No compatibility conflicts

  
  

﻿﻿ Hawks will call in Tundra regions,
  
Woodpeckers in forests,
  
& Seagulls on coasts.
  
Owls and Bats will be heard at night in forested regions,
  
while songbirds sing in the day.
  
Crows will be abundant, especially in the swamp.
