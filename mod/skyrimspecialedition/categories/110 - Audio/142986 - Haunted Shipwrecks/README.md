[font=Georgia]
  
Uses [Sound Record Distributor](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/77815)﻿ and hand-placed sound markers to transform shipwrecks into haunting environments. 
  
  
Sound effects have all been given conditions to trigger while at specific locations, at night, and/or during specific weather conditions.
  
  
Most locations will be normal on a clear, sunny day at noon... But when the sun sets and the fog hits, things will be much different.[/font]
  
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/cF2MZGQ.png)

[font=Georgia]
  
  
This mod uses [Sound Record Distributor](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/77815)﻿ to add sounds to regions and also places sound markers around shipwrecks.﻿ It also adds sound markers to two interior cells, the Brinehammer and Ice Runner shipwrecks. Place this plugin higher than your interior lighting plugins.[/font]
