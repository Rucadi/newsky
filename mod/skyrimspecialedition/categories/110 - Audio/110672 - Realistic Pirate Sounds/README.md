> *"﻿Avast, dog!"
>   
> — Corsair*

  
In Skyrim, there is a random encounter that can trigger after completing the quest "[Rise in the East](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rise_in_the_East)", featuring [two Blood Horkers ambushing you to avenge their fallen crew](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:World_Interactions#Blood_Horker_Revenge). The intriguing aspect of this encounter is that it contains a few lines of unique dialogue not said by any other Blood Horker or similar pirate throughout the entire game.
  
  
I have taken it upon myself to assign these lines to select pirate and sailor enemies where I deemed it appropriate. It's worth noting that these new lines are exclusively available for the [MaleNord voicetype](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Voice-MaleNord).

﻿

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/PierreDespereaux/PierreDespereaux/master/assets/images/banners/Features.png)

  

  
* Replaces generic combat barks for pirate and sailor enemies with new unique ones.
3 new barks in total: "[Yarr!](https://youtu.be/yXbjJybF7XU?si=AknsbELRz1tR9G9r&t=5)﻿", "[Avast, dog!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yXbjJybF7XU&t=12s)﻿", and "[Die, ya cowardly lubber!](https://youtu.be/yXbjJybF7XU?si=FBr9enWyQ92xiJD1&t=18)﻿".
  

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TateTaylorOH/TateTaylorOH/master/assets/images/banners/Compatibility.png)
  
  
This mod edits no vanilla records and should be compatible with virtually everything.
