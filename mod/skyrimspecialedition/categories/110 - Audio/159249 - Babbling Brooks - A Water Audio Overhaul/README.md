# Babbling Brooks - A Water Audio Overhaul
- Author: Clofas
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/159249


![](https://i.imgur.com/xXeNLwY.png)
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/5f8ptnE.png)
  
[font=Georgia]**45 New Sound FX** to replace almost all ambient water loops.
  
  
**New sounds** for Coasts, Drains, Brooks of Whiterun, Canal Water in Riften, Rapids, Rivers, Streams, Volcanic Tundra & Steam Pools, Boat Lapping Water, Churning Water, Splashing Drips, Blackreach Water, Cave Dungeon Water, & Spraying Leaks.
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/506YZyv.gif)
  
﻿
  
Select all or choose what you'd like to replace via FOMOD.
  
  
**Optional & Highly Recommended plugin** provides more variety to some bodies of water along with volume adjustments. Edits several moveable statics and sound markers to give Heavy Rapids Rings, Tundra Stream Transitions, & Churning water their own audio loops. Also adds more splashing drips, drains, & streams to each sound descriptor. 
  
  
Plugin also provides a **volume slider in the Audio settings** menu.
  
  
Most **waterfall sounds are not included**, you can find sound replacers for those in [Natural Waterfalls](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/87261)﻿ or other mods. I plan on redoing all waterfall sounds in a future update here.[/font]
  
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/nyDH3gY.png)
  
[font=Georgia]
  
Place **Babbling Brooks** below anything that edits the same sound files in order for sounds to work.
  
  
Place below both [Audio Overhaul](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/12466)﻿ and [Natural Waterfalls](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/87261) - Sounds﻿. 
  
  
Plugin would need patching for anything that edits the **Tundra Stream Transition** moveable static records.
  
Patch is provided for [Terrain Parallax Blending Fix](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/88261)﻿, included in FOMOD.[/font]
