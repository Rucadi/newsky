# Mehrunes Razor Perk Tree
- Author: Jason
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/111253


Using Mehrunes' Razor you can carve yourself into a more Draconic shape. This mod adds a new perk tree to the game. ***All perks require Mehrunes Razor to be equipped to unlock them***. (Note once you have the perk whether or not you have the Razor equipped has no impact on them.) You'll spend normal perk points to unlock the perks.
  
  
***Accessing the perk tree:***To unlock the perk tree you'll need "Mankar Camorans Lost Grimoire" which can be found in the Shrine of Mehrunes Dagon.
  
  
*The Perks:
  
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
**Mastery of the Razor:***
  
By studing Mankar Camoran's lost Grimoire. You've gained deep knowledge of the Razor. Allowing you to carve yourself into a more draconic shape. (This perk does nothing. Essentially you have to sacrifice one perk point to unlock the skill tree.)
  
*Draconic shape**:*
  
You carve yourself into a more Dragonic shape with the Razor. Your shouts are 10% more powerful and last 10% longer.
  
***Draconic hide:***Your health and armor rating are increase by 25.
  
***Rank 2:*** Your Health and armor rating are increased by 40. Health regenerates 10% faster.
  
  
***Draconic  Legs:***Your move 10% faster when sprinting and the armor rating of your boots is increased by 20%.
  
***Rank 2:*** Stamina regenerates 10% faster and you take 25% less fall damage.
  
  
***Draconic Arms:***Your unarmed damage is increased by 10. And your gauntlets have 20% greater armor rating. 
  
Rank 2: Blocking is 10% more effective and your carry weight is increased by 10.
  
  
***Draconic Form:***
  
Dragon Aspect lasts 50% longer and you deal increased critical damage while it is active.
  
***Rank 2:*** Dragon Aspect lasts 75% longer. Your attacks ignore 20% of armor and your critical damage is further increased when it is active.
  
***Rank 3:*** Dragon Aspect lasts twice as long. Your power bashes do 50% more damage when it is active.
  
  
***Inner Frost:***
  
Frost spells do 5% more damage. Frost Breath and Ice Form are 25% more powerful.
  
Frost Spells do 10% more damage. Frost Breath and Ice Form are 50% more powerful. Your sideways power attacks do Frost damage on hit.
  
  
***Inner Fire:***
  
Fire spells do 5% more damage. Fire Breath is 25% more powerful.
  
Rank 2: Fire spells do 10% more damage and Fire Breath is 50% more powerful. Forward power attacks do fire damage on hit.
  
  
***Inner Storm:***
  
Shock spells do 5% more damage. Storm call lasts 25% longer.
  
***Rank 2:*** Shock spells do 10% more damage. Storm call lasts 50% longer. Your standing power attacks do shock damage on hit.
  
  
***Inner Force:***Your weapons do 5% more damage. Unrelenting Force and Cyclone do additional damage.
  
***Rank 2:*** Your weapons do 10% more damage. Your foes stagger 25% more.
  
  
***Draconic Armor***
  
Armor made of Dragon bone or scales grants additional protection and damage reflection.
  
(Note: this perk should work with armor from mods. If you're wearing a full set of dragon armor it will grant a total of 100 additional points of armor rating. And reflect 5% of physical damage back on your opponent.)
  
  
***Requirements:***Custom Skills Framework (And its requirements)
  
Bug Fixes SSE (Optional but the first rank of "Dragonic Limbs" wont fully work without it installed). 
  
  
***Known issues:***None. I tested all the perks until I was confident they they all worked properly.
