# Dawnguard Perk Tree
- Author: Jason
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/147658


***The perks:***----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
**Abominations bane:** Your weapons do 25% more damage against undead. Vampiric drain does 50% less damage against you.
  
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
**Arkay's Light:** Your sun spells are 25% more powerful and undead take 10 points of sun damage when they attack you.
  
  
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
**Arkay's Grace:** When you fall under 20% health. You take 40% less physical damage and your healing spells are twice as powerful.
  
  
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
**Blazing Crossbow:** Enhanced Crossbows inflict fire damage on hit.
  
  
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
**A well oiled Bow:** Enhanced Crossbows do 10% more damage and increased critical damage.
  
  
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
**Furry Friend:** Your attributes regenerate 10% faster while you have an animal companion. And the health of your Husky or Armored troll follower is increased by 100.
  
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
**Slayer of ones own kind:** While in Vampirelord form your spells are 10% more powerful. While out of it You are 10% harder to detect when sneaking and do double sneak attack damage against vampires.------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
**Restored Soul:** Your Health, Mana and Stamina are increased by 25.
