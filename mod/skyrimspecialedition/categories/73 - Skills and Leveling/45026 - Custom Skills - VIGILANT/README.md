**Leveling**[line]
  
While wearing **Amulet of Stendarr (ID: CC844)**, You can gain skill XP by defeating the following.
  
-Undead Ghost, Vampire
  
-Witch, Warlock, Necromancer
  
-Werewolf, Werebear
  
-Daedra
  
  
One skill point is awarded when skill level reaches a multiple of 4. You can earn skill points by repeatedly Legendarizing skills.
  
This skill does not affect Player Level.
  
  
  
**Perk List**[line]
  
**Enhance Amulet of Stendarr**
  
**Prayer** : More 3/6/10% chance of a critical hit when wearing Amulet of Stendarr.
  
**Steadfast belief** : 10/20/30% more critical damage when wearing amulet of Stendarr.
  
**Charity** : Buying prices are 10/20/30% better if wearing Amulet of Stendarr.
  
**Righteous** : Protection spells like Stoneflesh are 1.5x/2.0x/3.0x longer when wearing amulet of stendarr.
  
**Keeper** : Increases armor rating for Shield by 10/25/50/75/100% when wearing amulet of stendarr.
  
**Share Knee Pain** : When wearing amulet of stendarr, Reduce incoming damage from guard/vigils by 50/100%. Also Reduce damage to guard/vigils.
  
  
**Enhance spells**
  
**Garlic** : Blocks 10/20/30/40/50% of a Drain spell of Vampire.
  
**Creaking Gate** : Ward spells do 25/50/75/100/150% more effective.
  
**Long Lecture** : Turn Undead spells do 15/30/50/75/100% more effective.
  
**Great Noon** : Sun spells do 15/30/50/75/100% more effective.
  
  
**Physical Resistance**
  
**Holy Water** : Reduce incoming damage from undead by 3/6/10/15/20%
  
**Silver Powder** : Reduce incoming damage from werewolf by 3/6/10/15/20%
  
**The Blessed** : Reduce incoming damage from Daedra by 3/6/10/15/20%
  
**Magic Jammer** : Reduce incoming damage from Summoned or reanimated one by 3/6/10/15/20%
  
**Blood of Anu** : Reduce incoming damage from Daedric Weapon by 3/6/10/15/20%
  
  
**Enemy's Weakpoint**
  
**Exorcist** : Weapons do 3/6/10/15/20% more damage against Undead.
  
**Wolf's bane** : Weapons do 3/6/10/15/20% more damage againt Werewolf.
  
**Inquisition** : Weapons do 3/6/10/15/20% more damage against Witch, Warlock, Necromancer.
  
**Daedra Banisher** : Weapons do 3/6/10/15/20% more damage against Daedra.
  
**Blood of Padomay** : Do 3/6/10/15/20% more damage against those who wearing daedric armors.
  
  
  
**FAQ**[line]
  
**Q. Compatible with other perk mod?**
  
A. Perfectly. Custom Skills Framework give us independent perk tree.
  
  
**Q. Item/object does not works.**
  
A. Check version of .NET Script Framework  , Custom Skills Framework.
  
  
**Q. There exist version for Classic/Legendary?**
  
A. No. Custom Skills Framework does not support older skyrim. It's only for Skyrim SE.
