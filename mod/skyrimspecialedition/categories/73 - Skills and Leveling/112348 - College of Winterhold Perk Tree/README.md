# College of Winterhold Perk Tree
- Author: Jason
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/112348


This mod adds a new Perk Tree for the College of Winterhold. You can access the perk tree via the "Student Handbook" you'll find in the room your given in the "Hall of Attainment." You spend normal perk points to unlock the perks. What perks you can unlock depends on what quests you've completed at the College. 
  
  
***Perks:*****First Lessons:**
  
Magicka regenerates 10% faster and your Magicka is increased by 10.
  
  
**A light in the Dark:**Mage light and candle light last twice as long. Your health regenerates 20% faster at night time.
  
  
**Mage's Circlet:**
  
Your Magicka regenerates 20% faster. Your Magicka is increased by 20 when wearing a Circlet.
  
  
**Aren's Ward:**Wards are 20% stronger and cost 20% less.
  
  
**Secrets of the Archmage:**
  
All attacks have a 10% of passing harmlessly through you. All spells cost 5% less.
  
**Gate stone of Oblivion:**Conjuration spells last 10% longer and cost 10% less. You take 20% less melee damage from Daedra. 
  
  
**Relentless Survivor:**
  
Healing spells cure 10% more. Your health regenerates 40% faster when below 50% health.
  
  
**Altered Flesh:**Flesh Spells last 50% longer and your armor rating is increased by 40. 
  
  
**Hide and Seek:**Invisibility spells last twice as long and your sprinting speed is increased by 50% while invisible.
  
  
**Wandering scholar of Destruction:**Destruction spells are 5% more powerful and your Stamina regenerates 10% faster.
