# Beastial Nature perk tree
- Author: Jason
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/141599


**"Draw on your inner monster to fuel your strength"****The perks:**
  
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
**Beastial Nature:** Power attacks do 10% more damage in both human and beast form. Eating raw deer or rabbit heals 5 times as much.
  
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
**Beastial Endurance:** Sprinting speed is 10% faster and your power attacks cost 10% less stamina in both Beast and human form.
  
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
**Beastial Strength:**Unarmed power attacks do 10 additional points of damage and ignore 25% of armor rating.
  
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
**Strength of the moons:**Your health is increased by 25 during the day and 50 and night.
  
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
