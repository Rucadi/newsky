# Molag's Favor
- Author: Jason
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/150918


Have you ever thought. "Man I really wish I could tare out some guys soul and send it to a evil god for a tiny amount of power!" Then boy do I have a mod for you!!!!!...... Anyway, here are the perks. 
  
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
**Molag's Favor:** Your weapon damage is increased by 1 for each perk you take.
  
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
**Death's Door:** Your weapons do 10% more damage against those who have 30% or less health.
  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
**Death's Strength**: Destruction spells do 10% more damage when you have 30% or less health.
  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
**Cull the Innocent:** Your power attacks do 25% more damage against those who think they're above Immoral acts.
  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
**Cull the Cowards:** Your Power attacks do 25% more damage against those with a cowardly disposition
  
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------**Armor of Faith:** Your armor rating is increased by 10. You gain additional defenses bonuses the more perks you take. -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
**Command the Weak:** Calm Spells are 10% more powerful and last 20% longer.
  
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
**Break the weak****:** Fear spells are 10% more powerful and last 20% longer
  
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
**Turn the Weak:** Frenzy spells are 10% more powerful and last 20% longer
  
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
**Armor Breaker****:** Your Power attacks do 5% more damage and ignore 15% of armor.
  
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
**Relentless assault****:** Your power attacks cost 10% less stamina and your stamina regenerates 5% faster.
  
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
**Cruel Efficiency:** Destruction spells cost 5% less to cast and your magicka regenerates 15% faster.
  
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
**Killers Endurance****:** Your health regenerates 10% faster in combat.
  
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
  
  
**How to get perk points:**
  
There's a new spell tome in the "Abandoned building" in Markarth, near the shrine to Molag Bal. Take it and use the new spell to offer Molag Bal Black souls as tribute. Every 10 filled black souls you offer to him with grant you one perk point.
  
  
**How to access the perk tree:**There's is a bloody cup near the spell tome called "The Vessel of faith" pick it up and click on it when it is in your inventory to access the perk tree. Alternatively if you use the mod "Custom Skills menu" you can just access the perk tree through it.
