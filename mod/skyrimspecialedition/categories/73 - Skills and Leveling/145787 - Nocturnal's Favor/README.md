# Nocturnal's Favor
- Author: Jason
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/145787


This mod adds a new shrine to Nocturnal to Nightingale Hall. It's really a crafting station. For 25,000 gold you can craft a scroll that will allow you to cast a ritual spell once that Raises your skill level of "Nocturnal's Favor" by 20. For an assortment of flawless gems you can craft a scroll that will grant you 1 perk point for the tree.
  
  
The tree uses its own perk points not regular ones. That can only be unlocked by offering gems as tribute.
  
  
**The perks:**
  
  
***A silver tongue:***Persuasion and Intimidation attempts are 30% more likely to succeed
  
  
***Aquatic Escape***:
  
You move 25% faster and your stamina regenerates 50% more quickly while swimming.
  
  
***Avoid the Law***:
  
You take 10% less damage from guards and bribes cost 10% less.
  
  
***Roof Top Excursions***
  
You jump 10% higher and take 20% less fall damage.
  
***Nocturnal's Grace:***Lock picking and pick pocketing is 10% easier a night time***Adrenalin of a Thief:***You run 10% faster while in combat in cities.***Shadow Hide You:***You are 25% harder to detect while sneaking while in dark spaces.***Mind Tricks***:
  
Illusion spells are 5% more powerful and invisibility spells last 10% longer
