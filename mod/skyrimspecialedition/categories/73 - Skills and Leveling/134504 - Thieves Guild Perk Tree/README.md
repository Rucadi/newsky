# Thieves Guild Perk Tree
- Author: Jason
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/134504


***The Perks:
  
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------*****A Chance Arrangement:** Locking and Pickpocketing is 10% easier
  
  
**Expirenced Debt Collector:** Unarmed attacks do 5 additional points of damage and your intimidation attempts are 25% more likely to be sucessful. 
  
  
**Scoundrel's Folly:** You are 10% harder to detect while sneaking. And Sneak attacks of all types do 10% more damage.
  
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
**Agent of Nocturnal:** What this perk does varries depending on which Nightingale power you have.
  
  
[**Crescent Moon:** Shadowcloak of Nocturnal lasts twice as long. Your movement speed and jumping height is increased by 20% while invisible (This works on all sources of invisibility not just the Shadowcloak
  
  
**Half Moon:**Nightingale Subterfuge lasts twice as long and you are 10% harder to detect while sneaking.
  
**Full Moon:**Nightingale Strife it twice as powerful and your health regenerates 10% faster.]
  
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
**Guild Master's Fortune:**10% better prices in cities where the Thieves Guild has influence and the guild master tribute chest contains additional treasure.
  
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
  
  
**How to access the perk tree:** I've added a coin purse near your bed in the Thieves guild. That once added to your inventory can be used to access the perk tree at any time by clicking on it. Alternatively use the mod "Custom skills menu"
