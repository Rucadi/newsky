# Lost Knowledge Skill Tree (Daedric secrets of the Thu'um)
- Author: Jason
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/132040


***Perks:***
  
  
**Hideen Aura:**Aura whisper lasts 50% longer and turns you invisible while active.
  
  
**Furies Haste:**Your movement speed and jumping height are increased by 50% while Elemntal Fury is active.
  
  
**Beasts Endurance:**Your Stamina regenerates 50% faster while animal allegence is active.
  
  
**Chronic haplessness:**Drain Vitality lasts twice as long
  
  
**Ancient Fury:**Battle Fury lasts 20% longer and is 20% more powerful.
  
  
**Etheral Abyss:** Your Magicka and Stamina regnerate 25% faster while Etheral and become Etheral lasts 20% longer.
  
  
**Flames of Oblivion:** Fire breathe does 20% more damage and causes lingering fire damage.
  
  
**Kynes Tranquility:**Kynes peace heals 20 to 50 points of health on use. 
  
  
**Mastery of Force:** Unrelenting force and Cyclone do bleed damage on hit.
  
  
**Multi Front Attack:**Marked for death also drains Stamina and Magicka
  
  
**Oblivionsfrost:**Frost breath does 20% more damage and causes lingering frost damage.
  
  
**One with the Storm:**Storm call lasts twice as long. And your destruction spells are 25% more powerful while it is active.
  
  
**Run Through Time:** You move 50% faster while the rest of the world moves 50% slower while slow time is active.
  
  
**Sluggish reflexes:**Disarm works on higher level enemies and you do 10% more damage to unarmed opponents.
  
  
**Statues made to last:**Ice Form lasts 40% longer
  
  
**Universal Fear:**Dismay works against undead, daedra, and automatons and higher level enemies.
  
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
***How to get perks Points for the skill tree:***There's a spell tomb called "Hidden Truths" you'll find at the end of "Black Book: The Hidden Twilight" (The one in Tel Mithryn). It unlocks a spell that lets you convert dragon souls into perk points.

***How do I acess the Skill Tree?***
  
  
Through one of the two mods listed as requirements. Get one or the other not both.
  
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
Compatibility:
  
Mods that modify shout duration or the power of all shouts should be compatible out of the gate. Mods that modify individual shouts may or may not be incompatible depending on which shout they modify and how they do it.
  
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
**Exsamples of Compatible mods:**
  
  
Dragon Soul Relinquishment SE
  
  
Individual Shout Cooldowns
  
  
Edmond's More Draconic Aspect
  
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  
**Exsamples of Incompatible mods:**
  
  
Stormcrown - A Shout Overhaul
  
Bards College Expansion (The paid creation) 
  
  
  
**Installing mid game:**Installing mid game should be fine so long as you haven't started any of the Black Book quests yet.
