**Hammet's Dungeon Pack 2
  
Unique Rewards LE by Xtudo**

**Description**
  
**What better than a wonderful unique reward after finishing a difficult dungeon full of dangers.**
  
**This mod do that, it places the cherry on top of the cake. :)**
  
  

  
* **12 unique weapons. 2 unique robes. [b]1 unique ring.**[/b]
The patches are just a visual replacer, they keep the original armors/weapons stats, temper requirements, etc.
  
* It doesn't touch any vanilla items, nor locations.
ESL. It's safe to update/install mid-game.
  

  
**Dungeons included**The standalone versions of this dungeons are not compatible with this pack.
  

  
* ﻿Anchazgar (Unlikely Spell).
Blood Iron Veil.
  
* Spire of Chaos.
Hilleye Cabin.
  
* Narzak's Vault.
Silent Vale.
  
* Silver Yol.
Twin Sonaak.
  
* Nirnmire Grotto.
Volengrim.
  
* Darkfrost End.

  
**Optional files**
  

  
* [Rings.](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/102880/102880-1697721057-1820388870.jpeg)﻿﻿﻿ Replaces the Valdgar's Ring with an amazing unique model from JS Unique Utopia SE - Rings. ESL.
