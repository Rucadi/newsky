**Hammet's Dungeon Pack 1
  
Unique Rewards LE by Xtudo**

**Description**
  
**What better than a wonderful unique reward after finishing a difficult dungeon full of dangers.**
  
**This mod do that, it places the cherry on top of the cake. :)**
  
  

  
* **44 unique weapons. 3 unique armors. 1 unique follower.**
The patches are just a visual replacer, they keep the original armors/weapons stats, temper requirements, etc.
  
* It doesn't touch any vanilla items, nor locations.
Works with both versions, SE and AE.
  
* ESL. It's safe to update/install mid-game.

  
**Optional downloads**
  

  
* **Arani Valestorm replacer**. ESL. Based on the lovely [The Wolven Widow - SerketHetyt's Elisif Overhaul](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/62209) mod by my dear friend [serkethetyt](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/53966057) (thanks you very much!). Her new weight is 20 (from the original 10), if you already meet Arani, you'll need to set her weight to 20: Select her via console, setnpcweight 20, disable, enable.
**Not compatible with v3.0, archived. Shadowgrim Burrow replacer - Mythic Dawn version**. Replaces the Shadowgrim Armor set, and adds the Shadowgrim Horror (one handed mace). Requires the textures from [Mythic Dawn armor SE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/84280)﻿ and the meshes from [Mythic Dawn Armor SE - My patches and fixes](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/84325)﻿. ESL.
  
* **Not compatible with v3.0, archived. Shadowgrim Burrow replacer - Dremora Markynaz version**. Replaces the Shadowgrim Armor set, and adds two new Shadowgrim Shields. Requires the textures from [Dremora Markynaz Armor SE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/79753)﻿﻿ and the meshes from [Dremora Markynaz Armor - My patches and fixes SE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/79766)﻿﻿﻿. 2K and 4K versions available. BSA. ESL.
**Not compatible with v3.0, archived. Ingulfrid's Armor set and Staff**. Requires only the textures from [[LE] DM BDOR Corbicanus by Team TAL](https://www.patreon.com/posts/le-dm-bdor-by-57766920). 5 new unisex armor pieces, and a new Staff. BSA. ESL.﻿
  
* **Not compatible with v3.0, archived. Ingulfrid's Armor set and Shield - Ysmir Armor version**. Requires meshes and textures from [Ysmir Armor](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/112454) ﻿and [my fixes for it](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/112480?)﻿. BSA. ESL.﻿
**Darkmire Crypt - Ingulfrid Sanctum - Statue replace.** It will replace the mage statue with an amazing new model. Standalone. ESL.
  
* **Helm of Morvanyr replacer**. Requires [JS Helm of Yngol SE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/51346)﻿﻿ installed first.
**Skyrim Immersive Creatures (SIC) Patch**. It has some important compatibility fixes. Highly recommended if you have SIC installed (to avoid CTD). These fixes are already included in Vominheim - SIC patch. So, it's no required if you have that file already installed.
