**A Note About A Skyrim Engine Bug**

  
* Reloading earlier saves without restarting SkyrimSE.exe is problematic because the game world doesn't completely reset.
[Here is a detailed video demonstrating an example of this bug](https://youtu.be/PJPzMAXSprU)﻿.
  
* If you encounter issues with this mod's quest or other quests, it might be due to this issue.

  
**Quest Walkthrough**Spoiler

1. You must be at least level 15.
Travel to any vanilla inn and speak to the vanilla innkeeper (if you loaded directly into an inn, leave and come back).
  
2. Read the note given to you by the innkeeper.
Go to the bandit camp specified in the note.
  
3. Loot Rellien Cecollius's body for his journal.
Loot the 3 keys from the dungeons specified in the note.
  
4. Open the chest in the final dungeon.
Obtain the armor and the crafting plans.
