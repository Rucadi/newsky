# King's Pawns Armor Pack
- Author: Chakra SSE
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/54044


This mod adds four standalone heavy armors and weapons to Skyrim, fitting for knight playthroughs .ESL version Included.
  
  
You must find the armor within Solitude, inside the Temple of The Divines, take the left hallway closest to the shrines and loot the chest (Armor will be marked as stolen)
  
  
Alternatively you can use Additem Menu/Console Commands to obtain the gear.
  
  
Vanilla weight slider is supported. Armor works on females but uses the same meshes.
  
  
What can you get in this mod?
  
  

  
* King's Pawn Helmet (4 Variants)

  
* King's Pawn Armor (4 Variants)

  
* King's Pawn Gauntlets (4 Variants)

  
* King's Pawn Boots (4 Variants)

  
* King's Pawn Sword (4 Variants)
  
Q: Can I get beast support?
  
A: No.
  
  
Q: Can you make the armors all separate?
  
A: No.
