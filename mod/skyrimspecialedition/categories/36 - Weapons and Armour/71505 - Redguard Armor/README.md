[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]**Redguard Armor**
  
Made by [**NordwarUA**](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/53732701)
  
  
Heavy & Light Armor
  
  
New set of craftable Redguard Armor and Weapons, beast races and both genders supported.
  
You can craft the set without perks, but the upgrade benefits from Advanced Armors.
  
Compatible with Survival Mode, and both **1.5.97** and **1.6+** versions of the game.
