# Mysterious Knight - CBBE-TBD-UUNP SE
- Author: Dint99 - Deedlitz - Daymarr
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/71925


[font=Comic Sans MS]**![](https://i.imgur.com/H4SMPaZ.png)﻿**[/font]**A made from scratch outfit by Dint999, featuring a unique heavy armor knight style outfit based on a drawing by [Seunghee Lee](https://www.artstation.com/seunghee)﻿. The outfit comes with CBBE 3BA, TBD SE, and COCOBody CBBE/UUNP bodyslides support. The cape and belt come with HDT-SMP cloth physics.**
