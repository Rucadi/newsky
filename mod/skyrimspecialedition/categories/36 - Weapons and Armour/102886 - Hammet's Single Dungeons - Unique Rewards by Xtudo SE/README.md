Hammet's Single Dungeons
  
Unique rewards by Xtudo**

**Description**
  
**What better than a wonderful unique reward after finishing a difficult dungeon full of dangers.
  
This mod do that, it places the cherry on top of the cake. :)**
  
  

  
* These 11 dungeons aren't included in any pack.
The patches are just a visual replacer, they keep the original armors/weapons stats.
  
* It doesn't touch any vanilla items, nor locations.
ESL. It's safe to update/install mid-game.
