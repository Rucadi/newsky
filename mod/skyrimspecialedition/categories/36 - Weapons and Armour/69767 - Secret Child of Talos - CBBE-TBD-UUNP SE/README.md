# Secret Child of Talos - CBBE-TBD-UUNP SE
- Author: Dint999 - Daymarr
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/69767


[font=Comic Sans MS]**![](https://i.imgur.com/H4SMPaZ.png)﻿**[/font]**A made from scratch outfit by Dint999, featuring a unique light armor knight style outfit based on a drawing by [Choi Tae Hyun](https://www.artstation.com/artwork/RzXzm)﻿. The outfit comes with CBBE 3BA, TBD SE, and COCOBody CBBE/UUNP bodyslides support. The dress, belt, neck, and boots come with HDT-SMP cloth physics.**
