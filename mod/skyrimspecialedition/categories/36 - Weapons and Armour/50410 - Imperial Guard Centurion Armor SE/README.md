**What this mod includes:** 
  
  
**Imperial Centurion Armor Set**
  
• Chestplate
  
• Gauntlets
  
• Greaves
  
• Helmet
  
• Cloak
  
• Sword
  
• Shield
  
  
Each piece is +3 higher than the standard Imperial Heavy set. Can be worn by both Male & Female.
  
  

![](https://i.imgur.com/ZM71deY.png)

• All are crafted at the forge under the **Steel** category
  
  

![](https://i.imgur.com/ZM71deY.png)
