# Broom of Magnificent Pixels
- Author: SkoomaMayhem
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/97220


Dragons? Draugr? Daedra? In the grand scheme of Skyrim, they pale in comparison to the unappreciated hero of the mundane – the humble broom. Introducing the Broom of Magnificent Pixels, the second in a line of my 'Magnificent Pixels' mod series, crafted to breathe new life into the unsung artifacts of everyday Skyrim life.
  
  
Now, you can marvel at your broom in glorious 4K detail as it leans against the hearth of your Whiterun home, or in the corner of The Bannered Mare. Every bristle, every grain in the wood, lovingly rendered for your aesthetic enjoyment. This is not just any broom – it's a broom worthy of the Dragonborn!
  
  
Why, you might ask, should a broom merit such attention? But remember, adventurer, in a world of chaos and battle, it is the humble broom that sweeps away the dust and grime, restoring order and cleanliness. Perhaps there's a lesson there, in the heart of our beloved Skyrim.
  
  
As I briefly mentioned, this mod is the second in my 'Magnificent Pixels' series, where I will lovingly upgrade more of Skyrim's underrated objects to 4K glory. Because, at the end of the day, even the smallest details contribute to the grandeur of our adventures.
  
  
So, dust off your old perceptions and let the Broom of Magnificent Pixels sweep you off your feet. Because in Skyrim, even a broom can be magnificent!
