# Comfy Coffins
- Author: sothasimp
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/134461


![](https://i.postimg.cc/QC1XjNtK/header.gif)
  
  
**Come peruse our fine selection of coffins and caskets, from the ostentatious to the dirt cheap! Goth GFs and blood enthusiasts welcome :)**
  
Replaces common coffins, Solitude coffins and vampire coffins. Optional animated meshes. Mid poly, mostly 2k textures, no ESP
  
  
  
**[b]?﻿  COMPATIBILITY  ?﻿**[/b]
  
  
Compatible with everything that doesn't replace coffin meshes. Compatible with [Animated Clutter](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/14398) and [Animated Coffins](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/63564) with the patch under optional files.
