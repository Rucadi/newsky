This mod replaces the Hanging Moss in game with hi-res textures (2K). I´ve tried various moss textures, but none gave a pleasant result, so i came up with this mix of Hanging Vines and Moss. 
  
  
Also covers hanging moss in Solstheim (Dragonborn DLC)
  
  
My mods [Rally's Water Foam](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/28922)﻿ and [Rally's City Roofs](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/20896)﻿ are used for the images
  
  
  
                             ![](https://img.bugthesda.net/i/44607c3c-2c5c-47dd-b4cf-25bea95bcc30.png) ﻿ ﻿ ﻿ ﻿ ﻿ ﻿
  
- download with your prefered mod manager, install and overwrite when promted
  
- or download manually, extract and put the "Textures" folder of this mod in your Skyrim directory
  
  
  
![](https://img.bugthesda.net/i/4bbdaedf-c27d-4bea-9959-dbe1e7ae8927.png)
  
- disable/uninstall the mod from your manager or remove the files from your Skyrim directory
  
  
  
![](https://img.bugthesda.net/i/0b21e9c9-0d29-45ef-9702-b4d7cfb7687b.png)
  
These textures are compatible with the vanilla meshes
