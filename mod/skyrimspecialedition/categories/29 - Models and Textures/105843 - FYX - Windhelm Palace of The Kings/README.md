# FYX - Windhelm Palace of The Kings
- Author: Yuril
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/105843


**This FYX**
  
  

  
* **Main Files**
    
  [list]
Fix some stretched UV and some unnecessary seam
  
* Fix lots of holes
Fix symmetry on the mesh whpalace3.nif
  
* Place the beams in the right place
Remove unnecessary parts from the mesh whpalace5.nif and some on the other meshes
  
* The decals are now different on each side of the mesh whpalace1.nif
Replaces the correct decals for the Palace front base
  
* Meshes are a few KB less than Vanilla
NO ESP, install/uninstall anytimes !
  

[/list]
  
  

  
* **Optional File**
    
  [list]
Fixes the problem caused by the snow shader and parallax. Before it was impossible to have parallax without bugs (Blue/gray meshes or which disappear at a certain angle) on the whpallace1.nif (SoS) and the 5 Palace meshes (BDS2). Now you can have parallax on these meshes.
  

**The bug in GIF**SpoilerBefore![](https://i.imgur.com/9IJxLGX.gif)After![](https://i.imgur.com/lyfdmEa.gif)

  
* [Simplicity of Snow](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/56235) : I cut the mesh into three and place it in the CK (Left, Center, Right), This solution seems to work and you can now have parallax on whpalace1.nif meshes.﻿ ESP ESL Flagged
[Detter Dynamic Snow 2](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/9121) (Only) : Same, and the BD2 shader is applied to the 5 meshes of the palace (7 now). You can have parallax on the entire palace now.﻿
  

[/list]
  
Enjoy !
