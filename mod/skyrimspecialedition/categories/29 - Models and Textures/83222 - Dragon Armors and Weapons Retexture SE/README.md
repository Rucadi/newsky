**WHAT IS INCLUDED?**
  
  
This mod retextures the Dragonplate armor, Dragonscale armor, jagged crown, dragonbone weapons, CC Dragonplate insulated armor, CC Dragonbone mail, CC Studded dragonscale armor, CC Dragonbone crossbow and CC Bone arrows. Also contains optional mesh replacers to add sleeves to the dragonplate, dragonplate insulated and dragonbone mail armors.
  
  
  
  
  
  
This mod also contains a optional consistency retextures for [Elsopa - quivers redone SE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/65921)'s Dragonbone Quiver and [Elsopa - Shields Redone - Part 1](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/99142)'s Dragonbone and Dragonscale shields
  
  
  
  
  
**FOMOD OPTIONS**
  
  
Fomod lets you pick and choose what you want. 
  
  
File available in 3 texture sizes:
  
  
Main file / full size: 4k chest textures, 2k everything else
  
Half res  2k chest, weapons and shields, 1k everything else
  
Low res: 1k chest, weapons and shields, 512 everything else
