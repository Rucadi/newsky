# Tavern Games - Cards Retexture - Forgotten Myths. -En--Ru-
- Author: Ifrinov
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/63326


Installation:
  
The usual process. The archive's content is to be unpacked into the Data folder via 7zip, or simply installed with your Mod Manager.
  
  
\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_
  
All the artwork is Bilibin's (Иван Билибин) illustrations or concept arts (sometimes slightly edited to fit the setting or the design), except for so called "Ancient Giant" (Leshen), which was drawn by A. Suchkov (Александр Сучков), but, sadly, I was unable to find any more of his artwork.
