Description**
  
**This mod changes the cubemaps of both armors for a more metallic version. It is not better, it's different.**
  
  
For those that didn't know I have two followers mods, the sisters [Sarah](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/36356) and [Cybele](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/38326); and Sarah is also my character.
  
Sarah wears the wonderful Dark Knight Armor by DeserterX and Dianka33, it really fits so well to her.
  
Cybele wears the most beautiful Falka Armor by Kozakowy, as Mage, it's perfect for her.
  
  
So I did some tweaks to the cubemaps of both armors to fit my tastes. Check the images, maybe you like the changes too.
  
They work for both Skyrim LE and SE.
  
  
**I did it for my game and now I'm sharing it, I hope you like it.**
