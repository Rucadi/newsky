This mod retextures the Guard Armors, Guard shields, Stormcloak Officer Armor and CC Civil War Champions' Stormbear Armor, shield and weapons. Optional Retextures available for [Helgen Reborn](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/5673)﻿, [Falskaar](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/2057)﻿, [Midwood Isle](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/28120)﻿ and [Open Face Guard Helmets](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/13943)﻿.
  
  
The Stormcloak officer Armor's Textures are base on Cabal's [Amidianborn Book of Silence](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/35382)﻿ version. All other textures are based on Vanilla Textures
  
  
  
  
  
This mod contains optional mesh replacers for the Guards and Stormcloak Officer sets, adding sleeves and pants. You can choose not to install them and only use my textures. Meaning it is compatible with every mod that works with vanilla textures. 
  
  
  
  
  
**FOMOD OPTIONS**
  
  
Fomod lets you pick and choose what you want. 
  
  
Textures are 2k except Chest armor textures, which are 4k.
  
  
Half-res version available in Optional files (Chest textures are 2k, everything else is 1k)
