# Animated Ships - Finer Sails
- Author: Artisto
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/112517


Makes existing sail material appear more detailed by introducing a higher thread count fabric with a high quality hand edited normal map. Along with color grading, added stitches, water stains, and other small details turning each sail into a work of art. Most files share the same footprint with no to low impact on performance. Original [Animated Ships](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/110260) mod is required.
