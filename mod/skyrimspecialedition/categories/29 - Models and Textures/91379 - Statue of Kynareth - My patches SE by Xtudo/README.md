STATUE OF KYNARETH****My patches by Xtudo**
  
**Description**
  
**My patches for the amazing lore-friendly statue of the strongest of the Sky spirits, Kynareth.**
  

  
* **2K textures**.
**JK's Temple of Kynareth** patch. Fixes navmesh conflicts between the two mods. ESL.
  
* **Exterior version.** For Vanilla, JK's Skyrim, Spaghetti's Cities, Dawn of Skyrim, ELFX-Exteriors, Lanterns Of Skyrim II and City Trees. Standalone, it adds a new Statue. ESL.
**Rotated Exterior version.** Optional file that will rotate the exterior statue to face the Gildergreen. A file for each exterior version is included (14 files), just select and install the right folder. It's fine to update/switch mid-game.
  
* **LUX version**. For Vanilla and JK's Temple of Kynareth (The original "Lux - JK's Temple of Kynareth patch.esp" is still required). ESL.
**ELFX**. No patch required for the interior statue. ELFX-Exteriors and ELFX-Exteriors - JK's SKyrim versions for the exterior statue. ESL.
  
* **Paradise City for Vanilla Skyrim - Great Trees for Cities**. No patch required.
**Immersive Citizens - AI Overhaul**patch. Fixes navmesh conflicts between the two mods. ESL.
  
* patch. Fixes navmesh conflicts between the two mods. Install the ELFX or LUX original patches AFTER my mod. ESL.
**The Great Town of Ivarstead SSE** patch. Standalone. Places the lovely new statue inside the Hall of Kyne. Only the original meshes and textures are required.
  
* **The Way of the Nords'** patch. Standalone.
**The Great Village of Kynesgrove** patch. Standalone. Places the lovely new statue inside the Hall of Kyne. Only the original meshes and textures are required.
  
* **Vanilla Statue of Talos replacer.** It will replace the default Statue of Talos with the Statue of Kynareth. Two statue models replaced. I kept the old base and collision, to minimize problems/clipping with clutter placer over the old statue.
**Vargr - A Whiterun Overhaul**. Works with both version Default and Complete.
  
* **JK's High Hrothgar patch**. Replaces the Statue of Talos with the Statue of Kynareth.
**Spaghetti's Towns and Cities Lanterns Addon**.
