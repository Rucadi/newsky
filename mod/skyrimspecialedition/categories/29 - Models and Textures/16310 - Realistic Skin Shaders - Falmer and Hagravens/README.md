[heading]Description[/heading]
  
  
Falmer and hagravens look more real and alive, with subsurface scattering for skin, environment mapping and reflections to eyes, and anisotropic lighting + highlights for hair enabled.  
  
  
All falmer are covered, including feral vampire falmer and female falmer from Dawnguard. Additionally, the female falmer circlet's gemstone has been replaced with a new high poly multifaceted emerald. Environmental mapping and cubemaps has also been enabled on Falmer armor.
  
  
Hagravens have new demonic looking black eyes, while glenmoril witches have reddish-pink eyes like in vanilla. The included esp is for enabling the new skin shaders on glenmoril witches.
  
  
[heading]Notes[/heading]
  
  

  
* Compatible with all texture replacers, including ones that modify gemstones.
Not compatible with any mesh replacers
  
* This doesn't require an ENB, although the SSS effect is greatly enhanced when using one. SSS effect intensity may vary depending on your ENB SSS settings.
