**Description:**
  
 ﻿There are quite a handful of Markarth retextures on Nexus, however, none of them really fit my vision of the city. So, I set out to create my own version of Markarth. I recreated every vanilla design and combined them with photorealistic material similar to my Windhelm resulting in a retexture that is faithful to the city's original look and feels. Dwemer Ruins' architecture is given the same treatment with extra grunge to make it appears appropriately old while still giving off the idea that the Dwemers are master craftsmen.
  
 ﻿Stony AF Markarth and Dwemer Ruins covers all stone architectures, woods, and Solstheim Onyx. Dwemer Metals are not covered (I highly recommend [Ancient Dwemer Metal](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/75610)). This mod is available in **2K,** **4K, and 4K-2K**. Parallax textures are included with all options. An optional file is available for new textures used in [Depths of the Reach](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/77718) and [Project AHO](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/15996).
  
**Requirement:**
  
- [Markarth Fixed AF](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/82728): Soft requirement. This mod fixes many bugs and inconsistencies with Markarth meshes. These textures were made to be used alongside it. Additionally, I also retextured SMIM metal for consistency.
  
  
**Compatibility:**
  
 ﻿Incompatible with other Markarth/Dwemer Ruins retexture. (load my mod after)
  
 ﻿Compatible with [Markarth - A Reflective Experience](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/43864) and [Skyrim - A Mountainous Experience](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/43866) (highly recommended, load [Markarth Fixed AF](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/82728)﻿ after both)
  
 ﻿Compatible with [Ancient Dwemer Metal](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/75610) (highly recommended)
  
 ﻿Patch available for [Dwemer Pipework Reworked](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/46507) V5, make sure textures in the patch load after both mods (huge thanks to [CALEB2](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/1027876) for allowing me to patch for their great mod, highly recommended) V4 or earlier doesn't need a patch
