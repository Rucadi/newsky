# CleverCharff's Photorealistic Ash Pile
- Author: CleverCharff
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/32720


A retexture of the ash piles with buried pieces of bone and sharper ash texture. Glows like the vanilla ash textures. I only made a 2K version since a 4K version really wasn't noticeable in comparison. With or without bone pieces.
