# Better Claws and Gauntlets for Anniversary Edition
- Author: Ahrtak
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/160444


I like the Better Claws and Gauntlets mod, and now with the Anniversary Edition, I decided to make a mod that improves the gauntlet claws for beast races, giving them the same treatment as in Better Claws and Gauntlets.
  
  
  
**[font=Georgia]Gauntlets[/font]**Alternative Armors - Leather / Leather Scout Gauntlets
  
  
Alternative Armors - Daedric Mail / Daedric Mail Gauntlets
  
  
Alternative Armors - Steel Soldier / Steel Soldier Gauntlets
  
  
Spell Knight Armor / Iron / Steel / Ebony Spell Knight Gauntlets
  
  
Fearsome Fists / Brawler's Steel Gauntlets
