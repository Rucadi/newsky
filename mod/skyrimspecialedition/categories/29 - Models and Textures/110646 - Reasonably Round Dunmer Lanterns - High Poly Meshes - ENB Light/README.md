# Reasonably Round Dunmer Lanterns - High Poly Meshes - ENB Light
- Author: See Credits
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/110646
**[font=Bebas\_Neuebook]REASONABLY ROUND DUNMER LANTERNS[/font]**
  
Optimized, high-poly lanterns with subtly flickering particle light.
  
Shouldn't affect performance much on anything but the most baked potatoes.
  
  
Includes options for brighter glow and meshes without particle light.
  
Consistency patches for [Lux](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/43158)﻿ and [Dovahnique's Diverse Lanterns](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/78788)﻿ are available and highly recommended.
  
  
--------------
  
  
Comparison for *dlc2darkelflantern01.nif* (the heaviest mesh):
  
  
**Vanilla:** **4,364** *Triangles* / **6,398** *Vertices*
  
  
**Dovahnique's Diverse Lanterns﻿: 70,208** *Triangles* / **44,798** *Vertices*
  
  
**Reasonably Round Dunmer Lanterns:** **16,652***Triangles* / **12,602***Vertices*
