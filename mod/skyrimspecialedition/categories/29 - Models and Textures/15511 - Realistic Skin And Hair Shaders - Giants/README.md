[heading]Description[/heading]
  
  
Enables subsurface scattering for skin, environment mapping and reflections to eyes, and anisotropic lighting + highlights for hair.  Giants look more real and alive.
  
  
Also replaces the vanilla 2D hair beads with new 3D meshes.
  
  
Giant Toes (the ingredient) have subsurface scattering added as well.
  
  
[heading]Notes[/heading]
  
  

  
* Compatible with all texture replacers
Not compatible with any mesh replacers
