# Gold Septim - Coins Retex
- Author: Master85
- Game: skyrimspecialedition

[font=Comic Sans MS]**![](http://static-8.nexusmods.com/15/mods/110/images/59704-4-1415251412.jpg)   
Gold Septim - Coins Retex**[/font][font=Georgia]   
 ***small and simple mod that changes the coins*** [/font]***appearance***

  
   
    
   
   

* **There are 2 versions:**[/\*]

  

1. the first uses textures in **HD** (twice the resolution of the original)   
   [/\*]
the second uses textures **Vanilla** (with the same resolution as the original)   
[/\*]
  

  
   
   
   

* Install:   
  [/\*]
choose one of the two versions and extract the archive into your "skyrim special edition" folder   
[/\*]
  
   

* Uninstall:   
  [/\*]
delete coin01.dds and coin01\_n.dds from  [Data/textures/clutter] into your "skyrim special edition" folder[/\*]
