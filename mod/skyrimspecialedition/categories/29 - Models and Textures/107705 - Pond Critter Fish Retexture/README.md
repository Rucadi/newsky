# Pond Critter Fish Retexture
- Author: Haladoon
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/107705
No Esp. Just simple retexture of the small fish. BC7 difuse and Normal.
  
  
Version 1.2
  
Adds 30% specular to the meshes of the live and dead pond fish to give off slimy fish look. Also skins the fin on dead version to prevent alpha glow in certain lighting.
