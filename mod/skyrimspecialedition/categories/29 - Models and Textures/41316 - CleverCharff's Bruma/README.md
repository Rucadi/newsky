# CleverCharff's Bruma
- Author: CleverCharff
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/41316


Replaces textures for the city of Bruma in Beyond Skyrim Bruma. All exteriors and interiors within the city walls. Requires BS Bruma: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/10917
  
  
Working separately on the other parts of Cyrodiil next.
  
  
Thanks to JhonSilvers for putting the 1K/2K files together
