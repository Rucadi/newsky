**Description**
  
**The most awesome [Pompous Dragonsreach Door](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/28727) and [Epic Gate of Whiterun](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/24534)mods will now match the [Skyland Whiterun or AIO](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/34179) color palette.**
