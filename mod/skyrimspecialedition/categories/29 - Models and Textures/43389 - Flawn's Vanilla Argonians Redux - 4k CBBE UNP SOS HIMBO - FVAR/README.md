***-[font=Tahoma]Flawn's Vanilla Argonians Redux[/font]-***

**What does this do?:**
  
This is a complete and customizable Argonian texture overhaul based on 4k upscales of the vanilla textures. This mod fixes several vanilla issues that, to my knowledge, no other retextures fix. The textures are 4k and 2k with BC7 Compression. This mod is compatible with UNP, CBBE, SOS, and HIMBO based body meshes. (BHUNP, 3BA, TNG, etc.)
  
  
  
**Options:**
  
Body
  

  
* Default - Rough and scaly, for those who want their Argonians less human looking.
Smooth - Human-like smooth skin, not very realistic but who cares?
  
* Nippleless - Same as default but without nipples, the most vanilla looking. (For females)
Full Scales - Heavy scales directly based on the vanilla textures. (For males)
  

Eyes (Optional)

  
* Replacer - Simple eye texture replacer.
Replacer Plus - Replaces vanilla eye textures while adding new ones.
  
* Standalone - Adds new eye options without replacing vanilla ones.
Glowing - Replace eye mesh with out that glows.
  

Feet
  

  
* Human-Like - Uses the regular human foot mesh.
Clawed - Replaces foot mesh with a clawed reptilian one
  

NPCs (Optional)

  
* Vanilla Adapted - Vanilla NPC faces made to use the new head mesh.
Flawn's Edits - NPC faces edited to my liking, some are only slight edits but some are more redesigned.
  

  
  
**Addons:**
  

  
* Muscles - Alternate body normal maps that give a muscly appearance. (For CBBE, UNP, and HIMBO)
Flat Chested - Alternate body textures, good for small/flat chested body presets. (For CBBE and UNP)
  

  
  
**Installation Order:**

  
1. Download whichever main file fits your female body mesh. If you're not planning on using the female textures then download either.
Download any addons you want and make sure they overwrite the main file.
  
2. Place any ESP plugins from this mod low in your loadorder.

  
* Use a mod manager like Vortex or MO2, manual installation isn't recommended.
