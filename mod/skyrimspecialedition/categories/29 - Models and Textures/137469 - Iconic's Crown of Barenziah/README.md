# Iconic's Crown of Barenziah
- Author: Iconic
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/137469


**Crown of Barenziah**
  
  
Retextures the Crown of Barenziah, gem boxes, and stand.
  
Added proper environment map.
  
Multilayer parallax gems, **or** Alternative real looking gems from Saerileth's mod [Barenziah's Glory](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/6343).
  
Check the videos to see them in action.
  
High quality Vanilla+ style.
  
Available in 4K, and 2K.﻿﻿﻿
  
  
  
Compatibility
  
Compatible with everything except other retextures, or crown meshes.
