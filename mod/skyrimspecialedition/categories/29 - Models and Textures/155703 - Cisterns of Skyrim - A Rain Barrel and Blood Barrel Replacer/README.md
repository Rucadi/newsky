# Cisterns of Skyrim - A Rain Barrel and Blood Barrel Replacer
- Author: sothasimp
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/155703


![](https://i.postimg.cc/yxycJprm/header.gif)
  
 
  
  
A model and texture replacer for the vanilla rain barrels and blood barrels, with a few optional bonus features:
  
  

  
* An addon that replaces the static rain barrels with functional activators that have pouring water, animated handles and optional needs/bathing mod integration
A clean ESP-FE that places 16 rain barrels and 6 blood barrels at appropriate locations in Skyrim (there's only 2 rain barrels and 4 blood barrels in vanilla)
  
* Occasional water/blood drips from rain barrel and blood barrel spigots for a touch of immersion
2D or 3D blood barrel puddles that use your installed blood spatter decal (screenshots show textures from [Sanguine Symphony](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/148388)﻿)
  

  
**Disclaimer:** The extra features were intended as an exercise / crash course for me in embedded NIF animation and rudimentary scripting, so be sure and let me know if anything isn't working so I can fix it and learn! :)
  
 
  
 
  
![](https://i.postimg.cc/ysTWNtQ4/usewater.gif)
  
 
  
 
  
The **Functional Rain Barrels** addon replaces rain barrel statics with functional activators, complete with animations and pouring water. A lightweight script triggers on activation which checks for supported mods and adds options to refill bottles and waterskins, drink straight from the spigot, wash up, all of the above or none of the above depending on which mods are in your LO. With no supported mods it just turns the handle and pours water on the ground for a bit, to the consternation of farmers everywhere :) [size=2]Supported mods currently include [SunHelm](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/39414), [Last Seed](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/56393), [iNeed](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/19390), [Realistic Needs and Diseases](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/3487), [Drinking Fountains of Skyrim](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/10971), [Complete Alchemy and Cooking Overhaul](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/19924),[Edmond's Immersive Needs](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/129738), [Waterskin](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/110895), [Keep it Clean](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/17846), [Dirt and Blood](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/38886) and [Bathing in Skyrim - Renewed](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/135288). No requirements except either [Base Object Swapper](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/60805) or the Cisterns of Skyrim Distribution ESP-FE.
  
  
**Note:** It has been brought to my attention that drinking rainwater might not be the most health-conscious option, but the Dovahkiin of legend should at least have the freedom to make poor decisions, right? [/size]  
  
The **Functional Blood Vats** addon replaces blood barrel statics with functional activators that pour you a Potion of Blood on activation. You're limited to 3x per in-game day in the interest of sorta balanced gameplay. No requirements except either [Base Object Swapper](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/60805) or the the Cisterns of Skyrim Distribution ESP-FE.
  
 
  
The **Cisterns of Skyrim Distribution** ESP-FE adds 16 rain barrels and 6 blood barrels to appropriate farms and vampire lairs, respectively - there's only 2 rain barrels and 4 blood barrels in vanilla so you won't really see them otherwise. It also adds the pouring water effect on activation and satisfies the [Base Object Swapper](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/60805) requirement of the Functional Rain Barrels and Functional Blood Barrels addons.
  
  
  
  
  
![](https://i.postimg.cc/08L3xQmk/useblood.gif)
  
  
  
  
Screenshots show rain barrels added by [JK's Whiterun Outskirts](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/78351) to Battleborn and Chillfurrow Farm and blood vats from the Dawnguard DLC at Castle Volkihar and Redwater Den. ENB is [Pi-CHO](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/35082) for NAT weathers with a few saturating LUTs enabled so the blood is liable to look a little less red in your game depending on your setup.
