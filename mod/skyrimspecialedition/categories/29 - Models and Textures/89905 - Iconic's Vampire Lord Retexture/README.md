# Iconic's Vampire Lord Retexture
- Author: Iconic
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/89905


**Vampire Lord**
  
  
Retextures the male and female Vampire Lord, and Vampire Lord armor.
  
Keeps the original vanilla look, but does away with the hideous (and comical) green skin.
  
Also contains an optional mashup of my textures blended with the demonic [Vampire Lord Retexture](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/8402).
  
Available in 4K, and 2K.﻿﻿﻿
  
  
  
**Compatibility**Compatible with everything except other retextures.
