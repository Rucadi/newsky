**Description**
  

  
* Incorporates mesh improvements from [Drengin's Blue Palace Terrace](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/35180)﻿ to the vanilla [Blue Palace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Palace)﻿ exterior.
Mesh only.
  
* No ESP.
I made this for myself years ago, but recently saw that ﻿[Drengin's Blue Palace Terrace](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/35180) became [open permissions](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/96798/96798-1690415767-1565106411.png)﻿, so I wanted to share the edits with everyone. 100% of DP goes to [Drengin](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/61542871)﻿.
  
* Optional [Lux Orbis](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/56095) mesh in files section.
