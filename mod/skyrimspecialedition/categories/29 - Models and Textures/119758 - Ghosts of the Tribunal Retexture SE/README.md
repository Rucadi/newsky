**WHAT IS INCLUDED?**
  
  
This mod retextures the Indoril armor, Her hand armor, ordinator shield, 3 variants of the common robes, 3 variants of the extravagant robes, Robes of the lich, the masks of Almalexia, Dagoth ur, Sotha Sil and Vicec, the Redoran Watchman's Helmet, Cleaver of st. Felms, Ebony Mace & Scimitar, Hopesfire, Light of day, Magebane, Skull Crusher and Trueflame. Ghosts of the Tribunal CC is required for this to work.
  
  
  
  
Chest textures are 4k, everything else is 2k
  
  
Half-res version available in Optional files (chest/robes are 2k, everything else 1k)
  
  
Low-res version also available in Optional Files (chest/robes are 1k, everything else is 1k)
