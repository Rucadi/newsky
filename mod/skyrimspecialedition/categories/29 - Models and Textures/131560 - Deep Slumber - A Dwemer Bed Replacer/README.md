# Deep Slumber - A Dwemer Bed Replacer
- Author: sotha simp
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/131560


**![](https://i.postimg.cc/sfk2cXBg/thumbds.gif)**
  
  
  
[color=#ffffff]?[/color]﻿  Unwind after a long day of tuning tonal locks and assembling automatons on beds stone-wrought and upholstered with that peerless Dwemer craftsmanship that endures literally for millennia
  
  
?﻿  Vanish from Nirn into preternaturally cozy blankets woven with precision on a Dwemer-patented mechanical loom
  
  
?﻿  Dream of smiting Nords and subjugating Snow Elves on textiles bedecked with an an appealing geometric Dwemeri motif, in purple and gold because dwarves are known Huskies fans. Go Dawgs
  
  
tl;dr It's a dwemer bed replacer with 2k textures, the stone base is vanilla and will use whichever Markarth stone textures you have installed. Screenshots show [Frankly HD Markarth - The White City Redux](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/18050).
  
  
  
![](https://i.postimg.cc/Dw7BFjnN/thumbdsc.gif)
  
  
  
**[b][b][color=#ffffff]?[/color]﻿**  [/b]PATCHES  **[b][color=#ffffff]?[/color]﻿**  [/b][/b]
  
  
Optional [Base Object Swapper](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/60805) versions available: BOS Distributed puts new beds in Markarth and timeworn beds in Dwemer ruins, BOS No Dwemer Ruins excludes beds in dungeons so you only get comfy beds in Markarth.
  
  
Patches provided for fellow [Use Those Blankets](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/75481) enjoyers so PCs and NPCs alike can get cozy in matching blanket cocoons :)
  
  
Patches provided for [Double Beds for Spooning](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/21826). Even the cold and inscrutable Dwemer like a cuddle now and then uwu
  
  
Patch for [Golden Dwemer Pipeworks Redone](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/67114) provided by blo0d1k, thanks!
  
  
  
**[b][b][color=#ffffff]?[/color]﻿**  [/b]COMPATIBILITY  **[b][color=#ffffff]?[/color]﻿**  [/b][/b]
  
  
Compatible with any mod that doesn't replace Dwemer bed meshes. The BOS No Dwemer Ruins option works with other Dwemer bed mesh replacers, you'll just see the other mod's meshes in Dwemer ruins.
