**SkyTEST - Chicks
  
Model replacer and Better idle sounds**
  
**by Xtudo**

**Description**
  
**Replaced the chicks from the SkyTEST mod with the lovely models made by Mihail.**
  
  

  
* 2K and 1K versions available.
Two different models included: Chicken and geese, with different textures.
  
* Improved idle sounds.
ESP flagged as ESL.
  
* This is a much better version of my [SkyTEST - Mihail's chick replacer and better idle sound SE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/35634)﻿ mod.

  
**Optional files**
  

  
* **SkyTEST 2022 Redux** version. Standalone.
**Fluffy patch.** 2K-1K versions included. Requires one of the Main files or Redux version installed first. Only meshes and textures. Overwrite if asked.
  

  
- [LE version here.](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/114643)
  
  
**I did it for my game and now I'm sharing it, I hope you like it.****Requirements**
  

  
* [Chicks- Mihail Monsters and Animals (SE-AE version) (''chicken'').](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/84110) Only its **MESHES** folder is required. Neither its ESP nor its textures.
[SkyTEST - Realistic Animals and Predators SE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/1104)﻿ v1.65.02.
  
* Optional: [SkyTEST 2022 Redux - Realistic Animal and Predators 4K](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/75983)﻿ v1.1.
