*Ever wanted to play a khajiit race and the only thing that made you change your mind is their looks?, well not anymore!, this mod will make your khajiit look more like human with sexier appearance, Feminine Khajiit comes with two main variations (Grey Cat and Leopard), including 4 options each:*
  
***- Abs:** Now your khajiit will be able to do more than 100 crunches!*
  
***- Normal Nipples:** For your normal taste.*
  
***- 8 Nipples:** I bet these little khajiit babies are hungry!
  
[i]**- Furry:** Purrrrrrrrr!!
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/75078/75078-1632019660-935493795.png)
  
**EXTRA Package:** ﻿It's the additional content for more chest variations including:
  
**Diffuse Maps:**
  
1. Two Milky Nipples
  
2. Eight Milky Nipples
  
3. No Nipples and Belly Button (Leopard Furry is excluded)
  
**Normal Maps:**
  
1. Two Furry Nipples
  
2. Eight Human Nipples (Exclusive for Abs)
  
3. Eight Furry Nipples (For Furry and No Abs)
  
4. No Nipples and Belly Button
  
5. Furry Abs
  
**Specular Maps:**
  
1. Abs - Eight Nipples
  
2. Abs - No Nipples
  
  
These options are mixed with the previous chest variations (Abs, Furry, etc.)
  
You can choose whatever chest shape you want with any nipple shape you like whether it's diffuse maps, normal maps or specular maps thus creating hundreds of possible outcomes.
  
Example: you can mix diffuse map (Furry - Two Milky Nipples) with normal map (Abs - Two Furry Nipples) with specular map (Abs - No Nipples).
  
The EXTRA package is optional so you still need the main file to use it, I have excluded the options that already exist in the main files, like Abs with Human Nipples, etc.
  
Since Vanilla textures don't have nipples in the first place because it's hidden with underwear, the EXTRA options are extremely less than the other body types, so there are no nipple options.*[/i]
