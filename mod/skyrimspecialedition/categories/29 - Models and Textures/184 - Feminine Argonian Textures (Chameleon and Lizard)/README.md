[font=Courier New]*Ever wanted to have a sexy reptile in your game? This mod will make your Argonians look sexier than ever, Feminine Argonian comes with two main variations (Chameleon and Lizard), including 5 options each:
  
  
**- Abs:** An athletic Argonian is better than a normal one especially when climbing wet rocks.
  
**- Bare Chest:** When your humanoid genes are strong nothing can stop the smooth belly you got.
  
**- Heavy Scales****:** More protection to your chest, Now you can take more bullets.. I mean arrows!
  
**- Less Scales:** Sometimes less is more, or maybe not! who cares? just enjoy your scales.
  
**- Standard Scales:** Your standard issue scaly chest, when your mutation drives you away from your humanoid ancestors.
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/75078/75078-1632019660-935493795.png)
  
**EXTRA Package:** ﻿It's the additional content for more chest variations including:
  
**Diffuse Maps:**
  
1. Colored Nipples (Exclusive for Chameleon)
  
2. Dark Nipples (Exclusive for Lizard)
  
3. Light Nipples
  
4. No Nipples and Belly Button
  
**Normal Maps:**
  
1. Alternative Nipples
  
2. Human Nipples
  
3. No Nipples and Belly Button
  
4. Standard Scales with Abs: (alt nips, human nips and no nips)
  
**Specular Maps:**
  
1. With Nipples
  
2. No Nipples
  
  
These options are mixed with the previous chest variations (Abs, Heavy Scales, etc.)
  
You can choose whatever chest shape you want with any nipple shape you like whether it's diffuse maps, normal maps or specular maps thus creating hundreds of possible outcomes.
  
Example: you can mix diffuse map (Less Scales - Dark Nipples) with normal map (Standard Scales - Abs) with specular map (No Nipples).
  
The EXTRA package is optional so you still need the main file to use it, I have excluded the options that already exist in the main files, like Abs with Human Nipples, etc.
  
Since Vanilla textures don't have nipples in the first place because it's hidden with underwear, the EXTRA options are extremely less than the other body types, so there are no nipple options.*[/font]
