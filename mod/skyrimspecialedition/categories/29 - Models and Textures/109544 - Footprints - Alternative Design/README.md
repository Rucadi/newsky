# Footprints - Alternative Design
- Author: Artisto
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/109544


This mod is a direct successor to the [Footprints Sand patch](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/50484), crafted in collaboration with its author [Modal1](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/20337504). It stands as an expansion to the original [Footprints](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/3808) by [Hexabit](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/2492841), reimagined for the next generation of immersive Skyrim experiences. Building on the foundation of its predecessors, it takes an in-depth approach to recreating footprints with photorealistic properties embracing the challenge of adapting them to various terrains with an advanced blending technique. Whether you're traversing sandy beaches, muddy swamps, or snowy landscapes, it ensures that every footstep is tailored to unique characteristics of the environment, taking into account the nuances of soil composition and natural factors.
