**About:**
  
  
A completely brand new, high detailed re-make of **The Crown of Barenziah** and the **Stones of Barenziah.**
  
  
Appearance will most definitely vary based on lighting / ENB / non-ENB settings.
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/IZkX3NC.png)
  
  
**What this mod includes:**
  
  
New "Crown of Barenziah" Mesh/Textures
  
New "Unusual Gem" Mesh/Textures
  
  
**Wearable Crowns of Barenziah (ALL RACES)**
  
  
Upon completion of "**No Stone Unturned**", you will now be able to craft:
  
  
Replica Crown of Barenziah - No Armor Rating
  
Replica Crown of Barenziah (H) Heavy Armor - 22 Armor Rating
  
Replica Crown of Barenziah (L) Light Armor - 16 Armor Rating
  
  
**Ingredients:** x5 Gold Ingot, x25 Flawless Ruby
  
  
**Note: "JS Barenziah SE - Johnskyrim.esp" is ONLY required for the wearable crowns. It is NOT required for the updated models/textures.**
  
  
**Do NOT optimize the textures!!**
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/IZkX3NC.png)
  
  
**Installation:**
  
  
Vortex/NMM or Manual extract to skyrim/data
