Replacer for the five Spell Tomes in game. I created new textures, including the paper used for these models,
  
while trying to stay close to vanilla.
  
  
The meshes have been UV tweaked to remove some stretches and misplacements, that were present in the vanilla files.
  
This also covers the Vanilla and SMIM (smim lantern textures incl.) Loadscreen meshes.
  
  
You can choose between 4K, 2K and 1K resolution.
  
  
It covers
  
  
- Alteration
  
- Conjuration
  
- Destruction
  
- Illusion
  
- Restoration
