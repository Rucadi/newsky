# Picta Series - Improved Sky Meshes
- Author: WeatherPainterAshelia
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/58263
**- [Description]**
  
﻿﻿
  
**Picta Series - Improved Sky Meshes** improves most of the Sky related Meshes, it also fixes a few bugs such as buggy UV Mapping and Horizon Seam.
  
(Click on the Link to see the comparisons)
  
  
  
**- Atmosphere Mesh**
  
  
 - [Fixed Horizon Seam](https://imgsli.com/ODEyMzQ)
  
  
 - [Improved Gradient](https://imgsli.com/ODEyMzI)
  
  
**- Clouds Mesh**
  
  
 - [Fixed Cloud Layer 12 UV Mapping](https://imgsli.com/ODEyMzM)
  
  
 - [Adjusted the positions of some Clouds such as the Horizon Clouds to make them look less odd](https://imgsli.com/ODEyMzU)﻿﻿
  
  
**- Mountain Fog Meshes** - [Added Double Sided Shader Flag to make them not disapear when looked at from the back](https://imgsli.com/ODEyMzY)
  
  
 - [Reduced Emissive Multiple from 1.5 - 1.15 to make them less bright and avoid overbrightness in certain scenarios](https://imgsli.com/ODEyMzc)**- Blood Moon Mesh** -[General Improvement](https://imgsli.com/ODEyNzU)﻿**- Sovngarde Mesh** - [Added Vanilla Galaxy, Stars and Constellations](https://imgsli.com/ODEyNzc)
  
  
 - [Entire Mesh is much larger](https://imgsli.com/ODEyNzg)
  
  
 - [General Improvements﻿](https://imgsli.com/ODEyNzk) - [Improved Sovngarde Sun](https://imgsli.com/ODEzMDM)
