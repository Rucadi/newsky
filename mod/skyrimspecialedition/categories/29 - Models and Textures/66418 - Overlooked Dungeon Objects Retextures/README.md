**Description**:
  
 ﻿This mod retextures objects untouched by most texture mods. Parallax map available.
  
  
Contain retextures for
  
- Potema Door (ruinsms06wolfdoor)
  
- Morthal Quest Coffin (ms14coffin01)
  
- Dark Brotherhood Clutters (nightmothercoffin, dbdoor01, dbtenets01, dbwindow01)
  
- Dawnguard Quest Clutters (dlc01casketstone01, dlc01stonebowl01, dlc01vampirestatue01)
  
- Ancestor Glade Boulders (dlc01mothgladeboulder03, dlc01mothgladetrim01)
  
- Giant Clutters (giantpaint, giantrock01)
  
- Dragon Priests Mask Busts (dragonmaskhead01)
  
- Ustengrav puzzle (ruinsdragonstone01 and ridgedstone01dragonswitch)
  
- Atronach Forge (middenconjure01)
  
- Nightingale and Nocturnal Objects (nightingalecircle01, nightingaleclutter01, tg08standingstone, blackpool)
  
- Portal to Sovngarde (sovngardeseal01)
  
- Barenziah's Crown Stand (barenziahscrownstand)
  
- Windhelm's Butcher Altar (ms11altar01)
  
- Jurgen Windcaller's Tomb (windcallertomb)
  
- Calcelmo's Stone (falmerrosettastone) matching my [Stony AF Markarth and Dwemer Ruins](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/68815)
  
  
Available in 2K and 4K
  
  
**Recommendation**:
  
 ﻿[Praedy's repository](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/44644) for other clutters
  
 ﻿[Rudy HQ - Nordic Ruins SE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/19365) or [Numinous Nord Ruins](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/146766). These ruins match my ruin clutter.
  
 ﻿[Rally's Banners of Skyrim](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/116289) if you don't like my banner. His method made it so my clutter textures could be used alongside his banner without overwriting.
