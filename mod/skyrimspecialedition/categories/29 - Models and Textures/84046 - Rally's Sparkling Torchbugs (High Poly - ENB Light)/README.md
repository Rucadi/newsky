Texture and mesh replacer for Torchbugs, Blissbugs (require CC - Saints&Seducers) and Jars (1K Resolution).
  
Ingredients will use the new textures.  
  
The meshes have been modified to a reasonable degree, so there´s little to no performance impact.
  
  
While i was at it, i fixed the purple glass on Vanilla Jars (see last image) :)
  
  
Available versions in the FOMOD
  
  
- Vanilla Light / ENB Light
  
- 2 wings / 4 wings
  
- Sparkle / Default
  
- Vanilla Jars / TMD Jars (TMD mod required)
  
﻿
  
  
These changes have been made
  
  
- Subdivided just the body.
  
- Slimmer shape of the wings   
  
- Antennae have been moved closer to the foremost part of the head (to better match RL images)
  
- Decided to add a second pair of animated wings (instead of carapaces). 25% smaller than main wings
  
(see images)
  
- includes fixed collision and wing position by [wiseman303](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/users/545863)
  
  
Vanilla Torchbug Tris: 1270
  
My Torchbug Tris: 1714 (2 wings) - 1724 (4 wings)
