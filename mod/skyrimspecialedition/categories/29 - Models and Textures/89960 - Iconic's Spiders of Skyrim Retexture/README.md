# Iconic's Spiders of Skyrim Retexture
- Author: Iconic
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/89960


**﻿Spiders of Skyrim**

  
  
Retextures the Frostbite spiders, and the Albino Solstheim spiders.
  
Includes support for [Dynamic Animal Variants](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/72978) (highly recommended).
  
Available in 4K-2K, and 2K-1K.﻿
  
  
  
  

![](https://media.tenor.com/images/371ef4a1e4db50a0af2e2ad6e8c12cde/tenor.gif)﻿﻿

  
  
**Compatibility**Compatible with everything except other spider retextures.
