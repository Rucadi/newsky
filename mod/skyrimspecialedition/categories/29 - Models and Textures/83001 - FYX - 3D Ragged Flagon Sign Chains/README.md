# FYX - 3D Ragged Flagon Sign Chains
- Author: Yuril
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/83001


From the good suggestion of [Mur4s4me](https://www.nexusmods.com/users/3126484).
  
  
Replace 2D chains with 3D chains for the Ragged Flagon Sign.
  
Useful if you use [Opulent Thieves Guild](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/931) or [GG's Thieves Guild Headquarters](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/64018) or [Rally's Ragged Flagon Sign](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/32548) ESP or [Faction - Pit Fighter](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/22513) and any mod that adds this mesh.
  
  
- Add 3D chains
  
- Add Cubemap
  
- Option **01** or **02** (The color will mainly depend on your texture mods, otherwise it will be the SMIM base color)
  
- Optional new Textures "**Rusty**" and "**Classic Damaged Meta**l" for the chains
  
  
  
No ESP. Safe to intall/uninstall anytime !
  
  
Enjoy !
  
  
> New textures for wood are available in the mod [Rally's Ragged Flagon Sign](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/32548?tab=description)

  
  
> If you the [Rally's Ragged Flagon Sign](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/32548) or [Ragged Flagon Sign 4k 2k](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/30476)﻿ intall this FYX after﻿
