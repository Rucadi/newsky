# HFs - Horker Meat
- Author: Halffaces
- Game: skyrimspecialedition

[font=Georgia]Meshes and textures replacers for vanilla horker raw meat and horker coocked meat.
  
[/font]Slightly ***improved meshes*** and new HD BC7 textures for vanilla *horkermeat.nif* and *horkermeatcooked.nif* (***not**vanilla **retexture***).
