Animated Market Stalls with SMIMed and fixed meshes, new textures and optional 3D trellis.
  
It covers all Market stalls in game. 
  
  
- Markarth
  
- Riften
  
- Solitude
  
- Whiterun
  
- Windhelm
  
- the small crates and wooden displays found at market stalls and vendors
  
  
All meshes have been heavily UV-tweaked to get rid of seams etc.
  
I also **fixed** a ton of bugs on the various meshes (see images for some examples).
  
This includes:
  
  
- Fixed clipping of fabric with wood beams on all according meshes.
  
- Added backlighting where needed
  
- Rescaled/Repositioned some iron parts of the Solitude market stall
  
- Fixed an invisible part of a table on a Windhelm stall, that was transparent when looking from the underside﻿﻿
  
 
  
I created this mod to compliment my recently released mods﻿ [Rally's Crates](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/80417?tab=description&BH=0), [Rally's Barrels](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/80061?tab=description&BH=1) and [Rally's Handcarts](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/80828)﻿.﻿

Since this replacer has a tons of UV edits and new textures, it is **not** compatible with other market stall and trellis replacers or modifiers.
  
It has to be loaded after conflicting mods.﻿ Patches can only be offered, if i have permission to use the conflicting meshes,
  
my textures won´t work without editing the UV maps.
  
  
Added all needed changes to make it fully compatible with lighting mods (ELFX, Lux).
