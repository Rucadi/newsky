# Sigils of Shalidor
- Author: powerofthree
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/14103
**SIGILS OF SHALIDOR****[![](https://i.imgur.com/PMbnf6v.png)](https://www.patreon.com/bePatron?u=8408266)**
  
  
[heading]Description[/heading] 
  
All sigils found in Shalidor's Maze glow blue in vanilla. Pretty plain, and boring. This mod revamps them with new colours based on their School of Magic, and adds support for ENB light.
  
  

  
* Alteration - Green
Conjuration - Purple
  
* Destruction - Red
Restoration - Yellow
  
* Illusion - Blue

  
[heading]Compatibility[/heading]
  

* Incompatible with any mesh replacers that edit *magicstonealteration01*, *magicstoneconjuration01*, *magicstonedestruction01,* *magicstonerestoration01, [i]magicstoneillusion01*[/i]
