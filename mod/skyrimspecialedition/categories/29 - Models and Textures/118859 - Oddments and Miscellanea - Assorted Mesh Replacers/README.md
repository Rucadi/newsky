# Oddments and Miscellanea - Assorted Mesh Replacers
- Author: sotha simp
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/118859


![](https://i.postimg.cc/Bnq1m6FC/thumboam.gif)
  
  
An eclectic assortment of oddments and miscellanea for the discerning Dovahkiin. All mid-poly no ESP mesh replacers, mostly 2k textures with 4k for larger objects.
  
  
So far we got:
  
- Winterhold Bookcase
  
- Mounted Antlers
  
- Noble Lectern
  
- Noble Rack
  
- Noble Head (literally noblehead.nif, idk what it is)
  
- Butterchurn
  
- Noble Corner Shelf
  
- Knife and Fork
  
- Noble Table Thin
  
  
And I'll be adding more as I think of furniture etc meshes to improve. Feel free to make suggestions!
  
  
Pictured in screenshots: [Skyrim 3d Furniture](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/23552), [Extravagant Interiors - Solitude](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/41621), [LUX](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/43158), [PI-CHO ENB](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/35082)
