This mod retextures the Iron armor, shields, weapons, targe of the blooded, Headman's axe, CC Iron Plate, CC iron bolt and cork arrows and bolts. This mod also includes optional consistency retextures for [Amidianborn's Content Addon](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/35390), NordwarUA's Iron Lamellar, iron mercenary, iron northerner and iron weapons , [Gearshout](https://next.nexusmods.com/profile/gearshout/about-me?gameId=1704)'s Rogue Nord, Vigilants of Stendarr Templar armor and Mercenary Light Iron armor, [Unique Uniques](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/3334)' Aegisbane, [Oblivion Artifacts'](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/10644)Axe of hatred, [Falskaar](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/2057)'s Skullcrusher, [Skyrim Immersive Creatures](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/12680)' Hornless iron helmets Also includes patches for [Cathedral Armory](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/20199)﻿ , Creation Club Open Helmets
  
  
  
The Iron armor and weapons textures are based on [Cabal120's aMidianBorn Book of Silence](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/35382)﻿. I used their textures as a base to create mine. aMidianBorn Book of silence is not required for this mod to work.
  
  
Fomod lets you pick and choose what you want. 
  
  
Textures are 2k except Chest armor textures, which are 4k.
  
  
Half-res version available in Optional files (2k chests, shields and weapons, 1k everything else)
  
Low-res version is also available in optional files (1k chest, shields and weapons, 512 everything else)
  
  
  
**﻿FOMOD OPTIONS**
  
  
  
**Choose what to install**
  
  
Choose which textures to install and if you want to install textures-only or my Cubemap addon versions. Cubemaps are used to create metallic-looking reflections. Most metallic armors in Skyrim use them, but iron does not. The cubemap addons will overwrite all your meshes. The fomod will provide meshes for popular bodyslides and mods. Please see "patch tutorial" further down this mod page, to learn how to create your own patches for these cubemap addons. It is quite simple and quick to do.

**CONSISTENCY PATCHES**
  
  
Consistency patches are optional retextures of modded armors, clothes and/or weapons I like. The goal of these is to make these items match my retextures. I do not ever claim that my versions are objectively better, all authors who's work I cover are extremely talented people! The original mods are always a hard requirement.
