# Skeleton Key Rework
- Author: PLSET
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/164983


Reworked The Skeleton Key has been , with new effects and abilities added to its original functionality.
  
  
（This mod is ESL Flag, no need to take precious ESP slot)
  
  
[size=4]Skeleton Key
  
**（This mod does not include the option to retain**[/size]**Skeleton Key[size=4]; please select a mod that retains [/size]Skeleton Key**[size=4]**yourself.）**
  
  
• Main changes
  
Item value increased
  
  
New Passive Effect
  
Blessed by the Skeleton Key's good fortune, nearby dead enemies will drop extra coins. Skeleton keys are indestructible lockpicks that will not break during the unlocking process, enemy critical hit rate is reduced by <30%>, critical hit rate is increased by <10%>, pickpocketing probability is increased by <20%>, and movement speed is increased by <10%>. A <Lockpick> can be converted into a <Key> by offering it as a sacrifice. <XX><Lockpick> has been sacrificed as a <Key>. A maximum of <100> <Keys> can be sacrificed. **Locks can be unlocked automatically using <1> <Key>.**
  
[/size]
  
  
[size=4]• New Skills
  
(New skills are used similarly to race powers, or are passive effects.)
  
When you Owned Skeleton Key, 4 skills are automatically added to you.
  
[/size]
  
  
• Key Sacrifice: Offer up the <lockpicks>, and use it as the <key>. It will be used by the Skeleton Key.
  
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/lFu9uc1.gif)
  
  
  
• Shadow Gate Leap: Consume <2> <Keys> to open the Shadow Gate, which has been established by cause and effect, using the Skeleton Key. Quickly teleport to any location within sight. Enemies will not be able to detect your presence during the teleportation, and your critical hit rate will be increased by <10>% for <10> seconds. Cooldown<10>seconds.
  
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/AeMuKpD.gif)
  
  
  
• [size=4]Luck Shift:  Consume <15> <Keys> to shift your luck using the Skeleton Key. When attacked, random restore <15%> of your health, magicka, or stamina, and increase your critical strike chance by <7%> and critical strike damage by <20%>, stacking up to a maximum of <35%> critical strike chance and <2>time critical strike damage. There is a <2>-second cooldown after being attacked again, lasting <15> seconds. Luck Shift lasts <30> seconds. Cooldown<80>seconds.[/size]
  
  
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/JpAqwpT.gif)
  
  
  
  
• Veil of Unluck: Consumes <20> <Keys> to use the Skeleton Key to disrupt the enemy's luck. Enemies within <30> yards will be affected by misfortune, suffering unprovoked bad luck, becoming more vulnerable to critical hits, experiencing reduced movement speed, reduced attack damage, a chance for attacks to miss, inability to critically hit, reduced spell damage, a chance for spells to miss, significantly increased spell cost, and significantly increased power attack stamina consumption. They will also randomly suffer the following curses: Falling, Stagger, Daze. The "Veil of Unluck" lasts <60> seconds. Cooldown<160>seconds.
  
  
  
(The cooldown effect of all skills is universal. If the cooldown effect of a skill has not ended, all skills cannot be used.)
