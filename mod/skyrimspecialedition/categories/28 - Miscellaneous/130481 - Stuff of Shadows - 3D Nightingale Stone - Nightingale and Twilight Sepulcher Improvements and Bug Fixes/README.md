**Description**

  
* [Change 1](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/130481/130481-1727631006-163532020.png)﻿: 3D asset
    
  [list]
Updates the Standing Stone in front of Nightingale Hall to a 3D version.
  
* Uses your high resolution mountain textures. Appearance may vary with your texture.
Screenshots taken with SRO and [Northfire's Photoreal Mountains 2](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/7612).
  
* If you don't like it. Delete tg08standingstone.nif.

[\*][Change 2﻿](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/130481/130481-1727631027-709127502.png)﻿: Quest change
  

* Changes [Trinity Restored](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trinity_Restored)'s "Nightingale Armor Stone" to Nightingale Armory Chest.
Instead of receiving the armor from a stone via magic, you grab it from the chest.
  
* After the quest, you can use these chests as safe storage.
This uses [Base Object Swapper](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/60805).
  

[\*][Change 3](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/130481/130481-1727631019-895536973.png)﻿: Bug fix
  

* Fixes bug where the shaft that opens up in the [Twilight Sepulcher](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Twilight_Sepulcher) reappears after cell reset.
To apply the fix, re-enter the [Twilight Sepulcher](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Twilight_Sepulcher).

[\*]No worldspace or cell changes.
  
[\*]Small objective text change in TG08A quest change via [powerofthree's Papyrus Extender](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/22854?tab=posts). No conflicts.

**Installation/Uninstallation/Update**
  

  
* Whenever. Doesn't matter.
If you leave items in the chest then uninstall, the chest turn back into stone, and you won't be able to grab the items until you install the mod again. (I know it's hard, but use your brain and don't be stupid)
