# Thrones Expanded
- Author: MorganLongsfellow
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/139544


[size=3]This mod aims to overhaul the thrones of the Jarls of Skyrim, giving them seats truly fitting for their noble position and specially catered to their hold and history.
  
  
**The new thrones are in:**
  

  
* [size=5][size=3]Solitude
[/size]
  
[size=3]Whiterun[/size]
  
- [size=3]Winterhold[/size]
[size=3]Dawnstar[/size]
  
- [size=3]Morthal[/size]
[size=3]Falkreath[/size]
  
  
I chose these cities because of their lack luster thrones. The seats of Windhelm, Markarth and Riften have been left as is because I find them perfectly acceptable and unique. I have no plans to update either.
  
  
*Disclaimer : The collisions are not finished, these thrones use vanilla collision boxes. The mod is 100% functional and this will not cause any game or graphical glitches when you or the Jarls sit on their thrones. A patch is currently underway to give the seat fully custom collisions.*
