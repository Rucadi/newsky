# I'm Talkin' Here
- Author: AlmightySE
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/93694
[font=Tahoma]
  
Want to enjoy a meaningful conversation with an NPC without your followers talking about the weather?
  
Introducing **I'm Talkin' Here**, a mod designed to hush your followers when you're in dialogue.
  
  
It's compatible with all custom and vanilla-voiced followers.
  
  
What are the requirements?
  
SKSE is needed.
  
  
Does it work with SE/AE/VR?
  
Yes.
  
  
Does it work with other mods, such as RDO?
  
Yes.[/font]
