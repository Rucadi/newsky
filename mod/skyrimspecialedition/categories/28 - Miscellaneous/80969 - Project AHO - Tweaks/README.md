# Project AHO - Tweaks
- Author: Decaroidea
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/80969


A collection of modular tweaks and edits for **Project AHO**
  
Meant to enhance the experience, create synergy between other mods
  
or simply fix pet peeves
  
All the files are ESL Flagged. i hope you enjoy them!
  
\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_
  
  
**~PROJECT AHO~**➤ Misc Item Descriptions
  
REQUIRES [Description Framework](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/105799) & [powerofthree's Tweaks](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/51073)
  
Patch to give most the miscellaneous items from Project AHO descriptions.
  
Primarily in the form of flavor text or to explain their practical use.
  
*It is highly encouraged that you use a mod to expand the item card size, since the longitude of some descriptions cannot fit on the vanilla space, and will appear cut-off or incredibly small*
  
  
➤ Dragonborn's Bestiary Addon
  
REQUIRES [The Dragonborn's Bestiary](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/123521)
  
Gives most of the creatures from PROJECT AHO unique hand written bestiary entries
  
  
➤ TrueHud Boss Bars
  
REQUIRES [TrueHUD - HUD Additions](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/62775)
  
Gives Important characters and Dungeon Bosses; Boss health bars, so their battles feel as significant as they are.
  
  
➤ Classic Morag Tong Armor replacer
  
REQUIRES [Classic Morrowind Morag Tong armor replacer](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/56289)
  
Replaces the retextured chitin armor the Morag Tong use for the Classic Morag Tong Armor.
  
  
➤ Armors of the Velothi Pt. II Morag Tong Armor replacer
  
REQUIRES [Armors of the Velothi Pt. II](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/69996)Replaces the retextured chitin armor the Morag Tong use for their Armors of the Velothi Pt. II versions
  
  
➤ General Item tweaks
  
a wide arrange of tweaks and edits to the items of Project AHO, to keep it consistent and give items 
  
more descriptive names.
  
Such as:
  
● The Nameless keys just named "Key" have been given names to their respective locations
  
● Duplicated items have been given unique names to distinguish them from their vanilla counterparts
  
● All the moldy Dwemer Clutter has been renamed to "Moldy Dwemer [thing]" 
  
● All the Chess pieces have been renamed to their respective color on their "Yellow/Red [Piece]"
  
● Telvanni bow and arrows have been renamed to: Tel And [Bow/Arrows] 
  
● Unique Dwemer decorated potions found on Bkhalzarf have been renamed to "Antique [Potion]"
  
● The local alchemist will now sell all unique  potions and ingredients originating from Sadrith and Bkhalzarf on her shop.
  
  
➤ Playable Shanath's Robes
  
Now you can loot and equip Shanath's green Telvanni Robe
