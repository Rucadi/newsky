# Frostspire Tower
- Author: PrincessMely
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/44154


[font=Times New Roman]

Description

[/font]Journey on a quest to explore the mysterious tower that has suddenly appeared in the sea of ghosts in Skyrim’s far north east. Meet new characters, discover new powerful spells, and claim Frostspire Tower as your own, in this new mystical experience.
  
  
Upon completing a quest to restore the Tower, Frostspire will become a full-fledged player home complete with an alchemy garden, training rooms, blacksmiths, library, kitchen, bathhouse, and a master bedroom.
  

[font=Times New Roman]Main Features
  
[/font]

[font=Verdana] 

  
* The Altar of Spellcraft : where over 100 spell tomes can be crafted. With over 50 new spells (see below)

  

  
* Staff Altar and Staff Workbench : where you can craft un-enchanted staves at the workbench, and then enchant them at the staff enchanting altar.

  

  
* The Portal Hub : with portals to every major settlement in Skyrim.

  

  
* Training Facilities : where skills can be increased with unique training dummies with dynamic shield

  

  
* Altar of Scrollcraft : where many of the most useful scrolls in Skyrim can be crafted

  

  
* Frostpire Thralls : who can be sent out on assignments, such as collecting alchemical ingredients, or mining rare ore

  

  
* Bath House facilities : that will give temporary buffs after being used

  
* Dressing Hall : where you can change your appearance, and craft clothes using fabric materials

  

  
* Companion Quarters : a place for your companions to stay

  
* Alchemy Garden : where you can grow your own ingredients and brew potions
