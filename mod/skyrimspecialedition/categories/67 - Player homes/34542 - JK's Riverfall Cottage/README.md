[font=Trebuchet MS]**- Mod Description -**[/font]A cozy medium size player home, featuring one master bedroom for the Dovahkiin, room for up 3 followers and 3 childrens, a bathtub, an Armory with a few mannequins, plaques and weapon racks, Kitchen with a cooking station and a Hearthfire oven, plenty of custom containers for all your needs, full smithy with smelter, enchanting and alchemy stations, Hearthfire planters and a fish hatchery. It can be purchased for 15,000 septims from the sign by the road; the key will be added to your inventory.

[Riverside Lodge](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/9567)﻿ is compatible, both player homes work well together.
  
Any other player houses that occupied the same spot will be incompatible.
