[font=Georgia]Witcher Plants Add-On :[/font]
  
  
[font=Tahoma]A Standalone Addon placing models from [Witcher plants resource pack - hedge and rhododendrons](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/132962) around Riverfall Cottage.
  
Note for Nature of the Wild Lands Users : You can use this as standalone but will need Nature of the Wild Lands Patch from optional files.[/font]
  
[font=Tahoma]OR You can pair this with My [/font][font=Tahoma]Nature of the Wild Lands Addon which will need NOTL And Witcher Plants Patch from optional files.

[font=Georgia][size=4]Lanterns of Skyrim II Add-On & Patch :[/font]
  
  
[font=Tahoma]Replaces all exterior light sources with the one from [Lanterns Of Skyrim II](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/30817).
  
Also replaces candles with lanterns & adds more lanterns with models from LOS II. 
  
Lights will automatically turn on after 19.30, and will turn off in morning as featured in LOS II.
