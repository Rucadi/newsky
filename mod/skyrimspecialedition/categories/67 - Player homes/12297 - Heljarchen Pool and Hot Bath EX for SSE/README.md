[font=Georgia]HELJARCHEN POOL AND HOT BATH EXtra
  
*for Skyrim Special Edition*[/font]

  
Mod includes the following:
  
  

  
* Heated pool and bathtub with autostrip and reequip only for followers and other NPCs
Idle markers for NPCs
  
* Outdoor kitchen and dining area
30 planting plots, whether you're farming for produce or more alchemy supplies
  
* Alchemy and enchanting workstations also at the northern greenhouse
Cooking spit and food storage
  
* Dining area for two
Functional heat and water controls for the tub and pool (heater only)
  
* Archery range and dummies for combat practice
Apiaries to harvest honey
  
* Sauna shack
[size=2]Perimeter wall and lighting can be enabled or disabled according to your needs[/size]
  
* [size=2]If you have Jaxonz Positioner, you can use that to fill out some of the empty spaces for further decoration.[/size]
[size=2]Proper ambient lighting[/size]
  
* [size=2]Frostfall compatible[/size]
[size=2]Checked and cleaned with TES5edit and tested[/size]
  

  
Installation: 
  
  

  
* Enter any interior space then make a savegame
Install this mod using any mod manager or manually extract and copy
  

  
Uninstallation: 
  
  

  
* Retrive all items from storage chests
Enter any interior space then make a savegame
  
* Exit Skyrim and Uninstall this mod

  
**Compatibility:**
  
  
This mod requires Skyrim Special Edition. 
  
  
Some Hearthfire mods that alter Heljarchen may or may not be compatible with this mod; some may cause clipping while others may change the navmesh and thus may cause a CTD.
  
  

* This mod is compatible with Heljarchen Hall Reform (Basic Only).
This mod is not compatible with Fort Heljarchen (navmesh, clipping).
  
* This mod is compatible with Heljarchen Farm.
This mod is compatible with vanilla or Tropical Skyrim.
  
* This mod is compatible with Heljarchen Hall Lights.
This mod is NOT compatible with Hearthfire Extended (navmesh, clipping).
