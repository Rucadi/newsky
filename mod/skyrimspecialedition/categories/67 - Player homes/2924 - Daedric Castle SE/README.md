# Daedric Castle SE
- Author: Lazz
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/2924


Daedric Castle
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/2924/2924-1523338204-1003778826.jpeg)
  
  
Daedric Themed castle, Unique design, located next to Northwatch Keep (see map in the image section) Features lots of storage and displays for unique items.
  
  
[Hearthfire multiple adoptions](http://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/3862/?) required to move your family in (Cast the "Bless Home" spell)
  
  
Must be purchased for 40,000 gold, click the sign by the door.
  
  
Features
  
Main Hall - Pool, Guest Rooms (6beds), Enchanting, Smithy
  
Bedrooms - Master, Kids (6 beds), Private Study
  
Dining Hall - Kitchen, follower rooms (16 Beds)
  
Library
  
Display Hall
  
Auto undressing for NPC's at pools
  
NPC's & Auto undressing can be disabled by buttons in master bedroom
  
Teleport Spell
