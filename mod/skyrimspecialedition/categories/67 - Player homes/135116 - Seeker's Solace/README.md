**Seeker's Solace**
  
  
"Seekers solace is a series of towers to the west of Winterhold that has been owned by many a mage or arcanist in years past in the ultimate pursuit of knowledge. There have been rumors of strange magics radiating from the fortress as well, drawing many more to attempt to discover and claim the secrets hiding within."
  
  
I've always loved the idea of a mage tower, and Seekers solace is my attempt at making one! this is my first player home I've made from scratch uploaded to the Nexus, and I had a lot of fun working on and living in it over the course of my own mage playthrough! I sincerely hope you enjoy as well if you give it a download!
  
  
**The towers include basic player housing amenities and a bit of extras, such as**
  
- An entryway Library that has teleports to move quickly around the towers (and a few "secret" entrances")
  
- A Bedchamber with a few mannequins, storage chests and weapon racks
  
- A Study with book storage and a few storage chests
  
- A Rooftop with alchemy supplies and a place to sit
  
-A combination Alchemy and Enchanting Lab
  
- A Foyer for the main tower
  
- Dining hall and Kitchen combo with raised lofts
  
- A Blacksmith Forge with all basic crafting tables
  
- An ample storage area for miscellaneous supplies
  
- An entire pocket realm house within the house itself
  
-Quarters in the north tower for companions
  
- A Vault hidden in the study
  
  
**Location**Just west of Winterhold past the mountains, the staircase leading up can be found along the shore.
