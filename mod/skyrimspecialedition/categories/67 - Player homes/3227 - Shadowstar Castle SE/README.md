# Shadowstar Castle SE
- Author: Lazz
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/3227


Shadowstar Castle
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/65356-0-1478470982.jpg)
  
  
A more traditional noble castle, located near Dead Men's Respite and not to far from Morthal (see map in the image section) Features lots of storage and displays for unique items.
  
  
[Hearthfire multiple adoptions](http://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/3862/?) required to move your family in (Cast the "Bless Home" spell)
  
  
Must be purchased for 50,000 gold - click the sign to the left of the main door.
  
  
![](http://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/65356-4-1430473685.png)
  
  
  
  
Features
  
Throne Room - Conference Room, Crafting and Smithing Downstairs
  
Dining Hall - Kitchen, Reading area
  
Display Hall - Massive Library, Storage, Themed Display area
  
Bedrooms - Master, Kids (6 beds), Guest Rooms (10 beds)
  
Undercroft - Guard's Barracks (6 beds) Dining Area, Prison
  
About 30 NPC's
  
Auto undressing for NPC's at pools
  
Teleport Spell
  
NPC's and Auto undressing can be disabled by the buttons in the master bedroom
