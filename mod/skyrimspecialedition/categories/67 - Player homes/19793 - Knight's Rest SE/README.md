# Knight's Rest SE
- Author: Lazz
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/19793


Knight's Rest
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/93453/93453-1536032349-423430593.jpeg)
  
  
A smaller castle/home located near Shor's Stone. lots of displays for unique items, follower friendly and compatible with Hearthfire multiple adoptions.
  
  
[Hearthfire multiple adoptions](http://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/3862/?) required to move your family in (Cast the "Bless Home" spell)
  
  
Must be purchased for 25,000 gold - Click the sale sign near the door
  
  
Features
  
Ground Floor- Dining, crafting/smithing rooms, pool, guest beds (4 beds)
  
Second Floor - Master bedroom, kids/Follower room (6 kids beds or 3 follower beds) library, rec room, Green room, guest room (2 beds)
  
Basement - Storage & Displays
  
3 Npc's - Housecarl, Librarian, Guard
  
Custom Armor
  
Auto undressing for NPC's at pools
  
NPC's & Auto undressing can be disabled by buttons in master bedroom
  
Kids room can be converted to follower room by button in master bedroom
  
Teleport Spell (book by the entrance)
