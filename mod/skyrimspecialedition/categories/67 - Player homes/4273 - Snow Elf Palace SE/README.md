# Snow Elf Palace SE
- Author: Lazz
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/4273


Snow Elf Palace
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/4273/4273-1525221915-2141290039.jpeg)
  
  
IMPORTANT: You will need to disable borders to get to the Palace, locate your Skyrim.ini in
  
C:\Users\username\Documents\My Games\Skyrim Special Edition\Skyrim.ini
  
  
And put in the [General] section:
  
bBorderRegionsEnabled=0
  
  
Or use the ini file in the optional files section
  
  
Snow Elf themed Palace Located east of Windhelm and past Refugees' Rest (See map in the image section)
  
Must be purchased for 40,000 gold - Click the sale sign at the main gate
  
  
[Hearthfire multiple adoptions](http://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/3862/?) required to move your family in (Cast the "Bless Home" spell anywhere inside)
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/76551-55-1466670253.png)
  
  
Features
  
Throne Room
  
Bedrooms - Master, Kids (6 beds), Guest Rooms (16 beds)
  
Dining, Kitchen and Library area
  
Alchemy, Enchating and smithing area
  
Outdoor recreation area
  
Auto undressing for NPC's at pools (can be disabled by button in master bedroom)
  
Displays for:
  
Dragon Claws
  
Dragon Priest & DB Masks
  
Bug Jars
  
Black Books
  
Elder Scrolls
  
Daedric Artifacts
  
15 NPC's (can be disabled by button in master bedroom)
  
Teleport Spell
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/4273-0-1496371721.jpg)
  
Craftable Snow Elf Weapons by [Ghosu](http://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/52445/?)
