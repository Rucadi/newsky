# Critical Damage Fixes
- Author: Exalderan
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/138690


![](https://willeshaben.de/skyrimmods/Projectenhancedvanillaexperience.png)﻿

**﻿

Details**Tired of Skyrim's critical damage falling flat? In vanilla, critical hits are severely limited because critical damage doesn’t scale with general damage increases—it’s based on a fixed weapon stat. This makes crit builds useless in the late game, where weapon damage can be boosted by up to 500%, but critical damage barely improves. This mod fixes Skyrim’s critical damage scaling by making it scale with *everything* that boosts general damage. Perks like Armsman, Barbarian, Dual Savagery, and even the Orc Berserker power and the Dragon Aspect Shout now also amplify critical damage. Enchantments that improve One-Handed, Two-Handed, or Archery skills? They all boost critical damage by the SAME amount too. Effectively making critical hits always be 1,5times stronger than a normal hit.
  
  
Additional fixes include:
  

  
* **Lucky Dagger**: Properly adds +25% critical chance instead of setting it to a flat 25%.
**Perks (Bladesman, Deep Wounds, Critical Shot)**: Also now add to your critical chance rather than overwriting it.
  

With these changes, critical builds are finally worth pursuing—scale into the late game and unleash devastating crits!
