**Description**
  

  
* Now you can buy and sell [torches](https://en.m.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Items#Torch)﻿.
Bethesda wanted people to be able to buy [torches](https://en.m.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Items#Torch)﻿ because they put ﻿[torches](https://en.m.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Items#Torch) in merchant inventories.
  
* But because of an engine bug, this is impossible:
  > Torches, despite having a value, cannot be bought from or sold to any merchants, even with the Merchant perk. -- [UESP](https://en.m.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Items#Torch)﻿
This mod follows the intended vanilla distribution of [torches](https://en.m.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Items#Torch) to merchants:[list]* 50% chance of 10 [torches](https://en.m.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Items#Torch) at general goods merchants whenever they restock.
If a mod changes these leveled list distributions, this mod will reflect that change automatically.
  

  
[\*]Works with mod-added general goods merchants if they have [torches](https://en.m.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Items#Torch) in their merchant chest.
  
[\*]Fancy SkyUI inventory icon with Inventory Interface Information Injector ([1.5 "SE"](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/87002)﻿/[1.6 "AE"](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/85702)﻿).
  
[\*]No vanilla record edits.
  
[\*]ESL.
  
[/list]**Installation/Uninstallation/Update**

  
* Whenever. Doesn't matter.
