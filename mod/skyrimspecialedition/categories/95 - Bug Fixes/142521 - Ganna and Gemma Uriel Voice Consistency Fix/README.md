**Details****Consistent Scene Dialogue for Ganna and Gemma Uriel**
  
In Skyrim, Ganna and Gemma Uriel occasionally break immersion during scene dialogue by switching personalities. While Ganna is typically cynical and Gemma more optimistic and cheerful, their roles unexpectedly reverse in scenes, leading to inconsistent character portrayals.
  
This mod resolves the issue by rerecording the scene dialogue using AI technology. The new audio retains the original lines and context but maintains the correct tones for each character—Ganna consistently cynical, and Gemma consistently optimisti
  
  
From the UESP Wiki:
  
> Ganna and Gemma appear to switch personalities when talking to each other. While Gemma is naturally cynical and Ganna is more cheerful, this
>   
>  appears to be the other way around in their scenes.

  

﻿

**Key Features:**

  
* **Personality Consistency:** Fixes the personality switch during scene dialogue, ensuring authentic character interactions.
**Immersive Experience:** Enhances narrative immersion by preserving character integrity.
  
* **Lore-Friendly Fix:** Maintains the original dialogue intent without introducing new lines or altering story elements.

This mod is ideal for players seeking a cohesive narrative experience while preserving the characters' intended personalities and dialogue flow.
