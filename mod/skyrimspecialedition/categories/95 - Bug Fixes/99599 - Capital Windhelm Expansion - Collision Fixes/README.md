***Capital Windhelm Expansion - Collision Fixes***
  
**[Capital Windhelm Expansion](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/42990)﻿** **adds many new custom meshes, and many of those have either no, partial, or incorrect collision.**
  
**This mods adds the correct collision to these custom meshes.**

  
[/font]
  
  
[font=Times New Roman]Mod Features:
  
~ Adds the correct collision to over 50 of the custom CWE meshes.  This means you won't be able to move through solid objects, and you'll hear the correct footstep and impact sounds for each material type...wood, stone, metal, mud, etc.
  
  
~ Integrated NIFS from Better Windhelm Ground Meshes, Lux Orbis, and Windhelm Fake Window Fix to make this as compatible as possible.  
  
  
~ Removed all the unnecessary collision cubes/planes since their no longer needed with correct material collision.
  
  
~ Fixed a handful of misc interior and exterior positioning/clipping issues (gaps in walls, etc)
  
  
~ Included a patch for [Windhelm Fence 3D](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/65346) by wSkeever (Custome texture version only)
  
  
~ Esl-flagged plugin!
