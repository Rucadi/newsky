# Kill Camera Misalignment Fix
- Author: MateDeVita
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/96842


The first thing I want to say in advance is that if you have the close-up camera for kills turned on, then you probably don't have this bug
  
  
                                                                                                                           -*Bug*-
  
  
                                                                  ![](https://s1.imagehub.cc/images/2023/07/28/GIF.gif)
  
  
This bug occurs when you use [TDM](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/51614) and turn the kill close-up camera off, and move the camera at the start of the kill animation
  
When you turn off the close-up camera for kills in Vanilla, it will force you to face the enemy at the start of the kill animation to avoid this misalignment
  
  
                                                                                                                           -*Vanilla*-
  
  
                                                                [![](https://s1.imagehub.cc/images/2023/07/28/GIF87040944232a6d26.gif)](https://s1.imagehub.cc/images/2023/07/28/GIF87040944232a6d26.gif)This feature doesn't seem to work in environments where [TDM](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/51614) is installed, so I restored it in a similar way
  
  
                                                                                                                           -*Mod*-
  
  
                                                                  ![](https://s1.imagehub.cc/images/2023/07/28/ModGIF.gif)
  
  
  
  
*compatibility*
  
          Compatible with all modules, including all combat modules, or modules about kill animations, this is compatible with everything
  
  
Known issue
  
        Killing camera may shake
  
  
  
*install*           at any time
  
  
  
*remove*           Even though it's a simple script, I still don't recommend you do this mid-game
