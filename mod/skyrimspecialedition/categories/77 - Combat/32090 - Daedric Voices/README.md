# Daedric Voices
- Author: hxhunter15
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/32090


With this mod, random dialogues by the Daedric Princes will be played when using their artifacts
  
[REQUIRES SKSE]
  
  
**Triggering the Dialogues:**
  
*Mace of Molag Bal*
  
  
• Killing an enemy with the Mace of Molag Bal (16 voice lines)
  
• Draw your weapon when in combat with the Mace of Molag Bal Equipped(18 Voice Lines)
  
  
*Spellbreaker*
  
• Drawing your shield when in combat(7 Voice Lines)
  
• Sheathing your weapons after combat (10 Voice Lines)
  
• Blocking a spell with Spellbreaker(11 Voice Lines)
  
  
*Namira's Ring*
  
• Drawing your weapons when in combat(1 Voice Line, rare to happen)
  
• Sheathing your weapons after combat (1 Voice Line, rare to happen)
  
• Feeding from a corpse(6 Voice Lines)
  
• Ring of  Namira must be equipped
  
  
*Dawnbreaker*
  
• Drawing your weapons when in combat(23 Voice Lines)
  
• Sheathing your weapons after combat (12 Voice Lines)
  
• Killing an **Undead** with the Dawnbreaker(15 Voice Lines)
  
  
*Mehrunes Razor*
  
• Drawing your weapons when in combat(11 Voice Lines)
  
• Sheathing your weapons after combat (6Voice Lines)
  
• Triggering the Instakill effect(3 Voice Lines)
  
or...
  
• Killing an enemy(Only if Zim's Immersive Artifacts is installed)
  
  
*Ebony Blade*
  
• Drawing your weapons when in combat(13 Voice Lines)
  
• Sheathing your weapons after combat (13 Voice Lines)
  
• Killing an enemy with the Ebony Blade(11 Voice Lines) 
  
\* Blade must be restored to its glory \*
  
  
*Azura*
  
• Drawing your weapons when in combat(9 Voice Lines)
  
• Sheathing your weapons after combat (4 Voice Lines)
  
•  Azura Star(not Black Star) must be in your inventory
  
• Rare to happen
  
  
*Vaermina*
  
• Hitting a target with the staff(2 Voice Lines)
  
• Rare to happen
  
  
*Volendrung*
  
• Drawing your weapons when in combat(6 Voice Lines)
  
• Sheathing your weapons after combat (6 Voice Lines)
  
• Killing an enemy with the Volendrung(7 Voice Lines)
  
  
*Savior's Hide / Ring of Hircine / Werewolf Transformation*
  
• Drawing your weapons when in combat(8 Voice Lines)
  
• Sheathing your weapons after combat (9 Voice Lines)
  
• Equipping the Ring(1 Voice line)
  
• Transforming into a werewolf ( 7 Voice Lines)
  
• Killing an enemy, as a werewolf(11 Voice Lines)
  
• Ring of Hircine or Savior's Hide must be equipped(Except for the werewolf voice lines)
  
  
**Frequency of the Dialogues && Console Commands:**
  
By default, there's a 35% chance for the dialogues to trigger and a 7 second cooldown. You can change it with the console by typing:
  
  
set \_hxDVCooldownTimer to x
  
  
set \_hxDVDialogueChance to <whatever you want between 0 to 100>
  
  
**Other Commands:**
  
  
set \_hxDagonInstaKillChance to x (The probability of the instakill chance from the razor to trigger)
  
  
set \_hxDVVaerminaChance to x (10 by default)
  
  
set \_hxDVAzuraChance to x (30 by default)
  
  
set \_hxDVMaxVoicesAtOnce to x (2 by default)
  
  
  
  
  
  
**Compatibility:**
  
There are compatibility patches for Zim's Immersive Artifacts and Artificer in the Optional Files
  
  
**Updating:**
  
Uninstall the mod first then make a save file without this mod, then install the new version.
