# Snowmen of Skyrim
- Author: SauteedPanda
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/107342


[![](https://i.imgur.com/oHd8p0g.gif)](https://discord.gg/V6qa82Spxw)
  
  
  
  
[left]**Installation**[/left]

  
* Install with any mod manager
Optional patches included in a FOMOD installer
  

**Features**

  
* Adds a variety of snowmen to the snowy regions of Skyrim
A total of 15 unique snowmen models!
  
* Seasons of Skyrim support
Check images for all locations
  
* ESL Flagged
Want no kahonkas on snowwomen: [Snowmen of Skyrim - No Kahonkas](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/107940)﻿
  
* Want bigger kahonkas on snowwomen: [Snowmen of Skyrim - Bigger Kahonkas](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/107556)﻿
Want a bit of interactivity with the snowmen: [Snowman Secrets](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/137057)﻿
