# Wyrmstooth Integration
- Author: MlemandPurrs
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/66083


Items
  
The taverns 'Candlehearth Hall' in Windhelm and 'The Winking Skeever' in Solitude may offer the 3 unique bewerages imported from Wyrmstooth.
  
At 'The Drunken Huntsman' in Whiterun you may find up to 5 Placeable Bear Traps, with a 25% chance of appearing for sale.
  
Khajiit Caravans may sell up to 3 Placeable Bear Traps, with a 25% chance of appearing for sale.
  
Khajiit Caravans, 'The White Phial' alchemy shop in Windhelm and 'Angeline's Aromatics' shop in Solitude may have the additional 4 alchemical ingredients from Wyrmstooth in stock, available up to 5 with a 25% chance of appearing.
  
  
Spell Tomes
  
Phinis Gestor at the Magic College of Winterhold and Talvas Fathryon at Tel Mithyrn will offer the Spell Tome for 'conjure Skeleton' always.
  
Colette Marence at the Magic College of Winterhold is able to sell Spell Tomes of 'Cure Poison' and 'Cure Disease' if you have appropriate level perks(Adept and Expert) of Restoration.
  
Drevis Neloren at the Magic College of Winterhold is able to sell Spell Tomes of 'Fade Other' and 'Transmogrify' with player having the appropriate Expert level perk of Illusion.
  
Drevis Neloren and all Court Wizards are also able to offer Spell Tomes of 'Nighteye'.
  
Enthir and Faralda at the Magic College of Winterhold, also Talvas Fathryon at Tel Mithyrn may offer the Spell Tome for 'Ice Volley' if you have the appropriate Expert level perk of Destruction.
  
Tolfdir and all Court Wizards are able to offer Spell Tome of 'Ease Burden'.
  
The secrets of conjuring Mudcrabs can be bought from Master Neloth.
  
The Spell Tomes for conjuring Draugr Wight, Draugr Scourge, Draugr Deathlord might be found inside the boss chests of Draugr related Dungeons with a meager 10% chance of appearing.
  
Additionally the Spell tomes for 'conjure Skeleton', 'Ease Burden' and 'Nighteye' has been added to relevant leveled lists so that they have a chance to be found on dead Warlocks, Bandits or Draugrs, or inside boss chests.
