# Azura's Star Rework
- Author: PLSET
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/153639


Reworked Azura's Star and Dark Star to make them more useful and valuable.
  
（This mod is ESL Flag, no need to take precious ESP slot)
  
  
  
Azura's Star
  
  
  
• Main changes
  
Item value increased
  
  
New Passive Effect
  
The glimmer of stars flows through the crystal, increasing the charge of all soul gem by <20%>, increasing the enchantment strength of items by <50%>, increasing magicka by <200> points, increasing magic resistance by <20%>, and slowly regenerating the equipped weapon's charge by <20> points per second when not in combat. <(Does not restore daedric artifacts)>.
  
  
  
• New Skills
  
(New skills are used similarly to race powers, or are passive effects.)
  
When you Owned Azura's Star, 3 skills are automatically added to you.
  
  
  
  
• Twilight Shards:Consumes your own magicka, reducing your magicka by <50> points, reducing your recovery speed by <30%>, and splitting into <5> Soul Gem Shards equivalent to the Azura's Star, which can hold the same soul as the Azura's Star and can be used infinitely. Cooldown <30> seconds.
  
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/gGSiq1C.gif)
  
  
  
• Star Siphon: Release the star of Azura's Star. After entering combat, Azura's Star will emit an aura that siphons souls. The souls of non-human targets who died within <30> yards will be captured and restored <5> times their magicka based on their level. Cooldown time <15> seconds.
  
  
﻿![](https://i.imgur.com/EH6iTdF.gif)
  
  
  
• Crystal Woven Shield : Summons the Crystal of Azura, weaving a powerful crystal shield around you that reduces all magical and physical damage you take by <50%>, negates incoming spells, and reduces spell Magicka cost by <50%> for <30> seconds. Cooldown <130> seconds.

The Dark Star
  
  
  
• Main changes
  
Item value increased
  
  
New Passive Effect
  
Dark stars whisper a forbidden fate, all soul gem increase the charge of daedric artifacts by <30%>, increasing the enchantment strength of items by <20%>, increasing the strength of all magic by <30%>, increasing spell absorption by <20%>, and slowly regenerating the equipped weapon's charge by <10> points per second when not in combat. <(Does not restore daedric artifacts)>.
  
  
  
  
• New Skills
  
(New skills are used similarly to race powers, or are passive effects.)
  
When you Owned Dark Star, 3 skills are automatically added to you.
  
  
  
• Dark Shards: Consumes your own vitality, reducing your health by <50> points, reducing your recovery speed by <30%>, and splitting into <2> Soul Gem Shards equivalent to the Dark Star, which can hold the same soul as the Dark Star and can be used infinitely. Cooldown <30> seconds.
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/Br1qCwQ.gif)
  
  
  
• Dark Siphon: Release the star of Dark Star. After entering combat, Dark Star will emit an aura that siphons souls. The souls of human targets who died within <30> yards will be captured and restored <3> times their Health based on their level. Cooldown time <15> seconds.
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/tDX6C1o.gif)
  
  
  
• Dark Disintegration Ray : Summons a Dark Crystal that fires powerful dark disintegrationray at enemies, dealing <80> health, magicka, and stamina damage per second to targets within <10> yards for <30> seconds. Cooldown <150> seconds.
  
  
  
  
  
(The cooldown effect of all skills is universal. If the cooldown effect of a skill has not ended, all skills cannot be used.)
