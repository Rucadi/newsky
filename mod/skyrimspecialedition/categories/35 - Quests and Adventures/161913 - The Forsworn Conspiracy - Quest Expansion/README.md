# The Forsworn Conspiracy - Quest Expansion
- Author: Anbeegod
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/161913


Please ENDORSE and VOTE for this mod if you like it! It helps more people to know this mod!
  
  
[![](https://i.imgur.com/dUdih61.png)](https://ko-fi.com/anbeegod)[![](https://static.vecteezy.com/system/resources/thumbnails/023/986/480/small/youtube-logo-youtube-logo-transparent-youtube-icon-transparent-free-free-png.png)](https://www.youtube.com/@anbeegod/featured)
  
  
  
*[size=4]"Kill a dozen savages, and they paid you a pouch. Kill a hundred, and they venerated you in songs. Kill an entire city of them? And you became Jarl in all but name. It has been so... until YOU came along!"*
  
  
  
**Greatly expanded the Forsworn Conspiracy questline to give new choices, new roleplaying opportunities, new epic stories. Includes over 200 lines of character development, story, post-quest remarks. Two new quests to extend this questline.**The vanilla paths will still be available until you cross the points of no-return!
  
  
  
**New choices include:**

  
* **Save Eltrys** and his wife from the guards
**Save Betrid** (Thonar's wife) from the Forsworn
  
* Run from or kill the guards who frame you **(no more forced to enter the prison like a wimp)**
Save or kill Madanach **without being framed and put into prison**
  
* Kill Thongvor Silver-Blood and **wipe out the most evil family of Skyrim**
**Marry Betrid** if you save her and kill Thongvor - inherit the Silver-Bloods' wealth after you wipe them out!
  

  
  
**Compatibility:**

  
* The fixes by USSEP are rolled over into this mod
Mods like At Your Own Pace will require a patch
  
* Civil War mods are compatible
Any mod that modifies the quests "MS01", "MS02", "MS01GuardAmbushQuest" will need to be examined
  

  
  
**IMPORTANT:**

  
* **I****nstall in a save where you haven't entered Markarth at all yet**
**You cannot join the Stormcloaks or continue their questline after killing Thongvor.** This is because Thongvor is required for the Stormcloaks. However, **you can still save Eltrys and Betrid then go to jail like vanilla.** This will be compatible with Stormcloak playthroughs, and there will still be new epilogue.
