# Extended Cut Saints and Seducers - Dylora's Reward
- Author: TheShiningAbsol
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/78977


Requires [Skyrim Extended Cut - Saints and Seducers](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/72772)!﻿
  
  
---
  
  
This mod will let you ask Dylora if she's willing to help you anytime after you complete the questline and Staada gives you your reward.
  
  
Dylora will give you her unique (and otherwise unobtainable) helmet, as well as a spell tome to summon her whenever you desire, just like Staada.
