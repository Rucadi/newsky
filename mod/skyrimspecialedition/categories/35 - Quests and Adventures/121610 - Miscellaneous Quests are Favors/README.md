# Miscellaneous Quests are Favors
- Author: Enodoc
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/121610


[Favors](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Favors) are ﻿certain [Miscellaneous Quests](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miscellaneous_Quests)﻿ which can increase the [disposition](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Disposition) of NPCs, thereby progressing any objective to [assist the people of a hold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Making_Friends_and_Influencing_People) as part of the quest to become a [Thane](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thane) of that hold (among other things). Almost all Miscellaneous Quests for NPCs are Favors already; this mod adds missing Disposition increases to all the Freeform and Favor Miscellaneous Quests which didn't have them.
  
  
Features
  
  

  
* Adds Disposition increases to the following quests, which now contribute to Thaneship:[list]
[Return to Grace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Return_to_Grace) (Greta, Solitude)
  
* [Fit for a Jarl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fit_for_a_Jarl) (Taarie, Solitude)
[Payment Delivery](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Payment_Delivery) (Pactur, Stonehills)
  
* [Hunter and Hunted](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Moss_Mother_Cavern_(quest))﻿ (Valdr, Falkreath)
[Falion's Secret](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Falion%27s_Secret)\* (Falion, Morthal)
  
* [Bring It!](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bring_It!) (Harrald, Riften)
[Erik the Slayer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Erik_the_Slayer_(quest)) (Erik, Rorikstead)
  
* [A Bad Trade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unfinished_Quests#A_Bad_Trade)﻿ (Birna, Winterhold)
[Drowned Sorrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Drowned_Sorrows) (Dagur, Winterhold)
  

  
[\*]Adds Disposition increases to all seven [Favor Quests in Raven Rock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock#Miscellaneous_Quests)﻿.
  
[\*]Adds Disposition increase for [Convincing Nikulas to stay in Skaal Village](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Convince_Nikulas_to_stay_in_Skaal_Village).
  
[\*]**New in v1.2: Bounty Quests are Favors** - Adds Disposition increases to all [Bounty Quests](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty_Quests) (applied to the Jarl's Steward).
  
[/list]\* Only if you give Falion your word that you won't tell anyone. If you instead decide to tell the Jarl, Falion's disposition will decrease as a result.
  
  
**Additionally:**
  
  

  
* Adds objectives to [A Bad Trade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unfinished_Quests#A_Bad_Trade) and [Yngol's Barrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yngol%27s_Barrow) and fixes some scripting in the dungeon.
Adds objectives to [Drowned Sorrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Drowned_Sorrows) and fixes Dagur's dialogue about Isabelle Rolaine.
  
* Adds objectives to [Falion's Secret](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Falion%27s_Secret) and fixes the final scene between Falion and the Jarl.

  
Compatibility
  
  
This mod edits numerous scripts and quest fragments, including those for all of the above quests and/or associated dialogue, and will not be compatible with anything else that edits those scripts. Check for *loose file overwrite* conflicts.
  

  
* **Compatible** with [Dungeon Quests are not Miscellaneous](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/123559).
**Compatible** with [A Steward for Dawnstar](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/129588).
  
* **Compatible** with [Bring Meeko To Lod](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/25246), which adds a Disposition increase to Lod if you bring him Meeko.
**Load After** any files from USSEP.
