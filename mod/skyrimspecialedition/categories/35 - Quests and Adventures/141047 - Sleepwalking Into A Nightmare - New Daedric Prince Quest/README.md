# Sleepwalking Into A Nightmare - New Daedric Prince Quest
- Author: TheLootist
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/141047


**[﻿](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/105310?tab=description)﻿If you enjoy the mod, please come back and endorse as it helps other people find it, thank you!**
  
![](https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54309944592_7c46eedac4_k.jpg)
  
  
  
  
In Sleepwalking into a nightmare, you will help Ralforn track down his wife Gretska who has disappeared overnight. The quest has multiple endings with different rewards. 
  
  
More Info (Contains mild spoilers):
  
Spoiler
  
While tracking down Gretska you will venture into her nightmares, overcoming them in order to free her from the tendrils of a Daedric prince.
  
  
  
This mod includes the following, to find all (other than quest rewards) ensure you explore the new areas fully.
  

  
* 1 new bow (Potential quest reward)
1 new ring (Potential quest reward)
  
* 1 new helmet (light or heavy with enchantment variations)
2 new spells (one has three power levels)
