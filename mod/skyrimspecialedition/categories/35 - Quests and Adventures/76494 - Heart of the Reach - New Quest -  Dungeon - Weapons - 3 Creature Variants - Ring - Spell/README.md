# Heart of the Reach - New Quest -  Dungeon - Weapons - 3 Creature Variants - Ring - Spell
- Author: TheLootist
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/76494


![](https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/52402079344_6e0b9b49d4_o.png)
  
  
  
Heart of the Reach is a quest mod that sends you into the Ever-Bog, an underground swamp in the reach in order to help a priest who is searching for help. You can either choose to side with or against the priest through the quest and have different rewards/ending depending on your decision.
  
  
Mod is now out on xbox: [Bethesda.net | Heart of the Reach - New Quest - Dungeon - Weapons - 3 Creature Variants - Ring Mod](https://mods.bethesda.net/en/skyrim/mod-detail/4348724)
  
  
Read about the mod in this [article by GfinityEsports](https://www.gfinityesports.com/gaming-news/skyrim-heart-of-the-reach-quest-mod/) or watch this video by Zero Period Productions
  
  
  
  
The mod comes with the following content:
  

  
* 2 new bows
1 new sword
  
* 1 new spell - Summon new Spriggan
3 new creatures - variants of Skyrim creatures
  
* New ring - Quest reward from certain choice.
Voiced NPCs
  
* New flora and ingredients

To start the mod you will need to **talk to an NPC named Gwilym** in the [Silver Blood Inn located in Markarth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver-Blood_Inn#:~:text=Silver%2DBlood%20Inn%20is%20an,and%20sells%20food%20and%20drink.). He will start you off on your journey into the Ever-Bog. Gwilym can be seen below normally lingering around the fire place in the Inn.
