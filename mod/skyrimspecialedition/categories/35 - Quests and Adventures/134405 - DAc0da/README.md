**The Quests:**[line]
  
**How to start**
  
When Player Lv >= **15** (Global Variable : **zDcdGStartLevel**) and visit Solitude, Numidium appears.
  
Talk with Lokir or go to light house of Solitude.
  
  
**Main Quest**
  
  
Athletic theme park in Numidium.
  
To expel the Numidium from the timeline, you break into the interior and go to the core.
  
(Followers are not recommended)
  
**Side Quests**
  
**![](https://i.imgur.com/0hI1zDK.jpeg)
  
Episode Yngol :**East side of Sea of Causality. 
  
Basically 1-on-1 battle.
  
The whereabouts of Yungol, who was caught in a time distortion and drifted ashore. Decide the fate of Yungol.
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/Rt9GG5T.jpeg)
  
**Episode Mannimarco :**West side of Sea of Causality.
  
Group Battle.
  
Sload Legion is trying to use the numidian effect to summon the god of worm. Together with Vanus Galerion, who became a tortoise, crush the ambitions of the necromancer.
