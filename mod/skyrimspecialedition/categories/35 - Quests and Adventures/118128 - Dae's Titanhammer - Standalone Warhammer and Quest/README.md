# Dae's Titanhammer - Standalone Warhammer and Quest
- Author: TREBoy and Dae
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/118128


[![](https://iili.io/21tAMue.png)](https://discord.gg/K5e9UR5K)﻿﻿   [![](https://iili.io/2bwIQPs.png)](https://patreon.com/TREBoy)
  
![](https://iili.io/Jg3ar5N.png)﻿﻿Do you have what it takes to confront the **Dwemer**'s most powerful creations?
  
 Do you wish you did?
  
 Embark on this journey to the depths of **Dwemer ruins** to find the answers to these questions.
  
You, aided by a **mysterious character**, embark on a perilous journey through ancient ruins and treacherous depths to uncover **extremely powerful weapons**, facing formidable foes and unraveling secrets hidden within.
  
Will you be able to claim the masterpieces of **Dwemer warfare**?
  
  
**Dae's Titanhammer** is a **Quest** mod, and the last of the **Dae's Dwemer** weapons series. The mod features a very difficult adventure, starting at **Level 25**, and going from the depths of **Skyrim**'s most treacherous ruins to a completely new **Dwemer** structure on the far lands of **Solstheim**. You will be facing many **never-seen-before enemies**, the strongest in the **Dwemer**'s arsenal. As with the other two mods of this series, the ultimate goal is to find a very powerful weapon, that will strike fear on your enemies' hearts. **Dae's Titanhammer** is an extremely strong **Warhammer**, lacking in speed to make up for it.
  
﻿
  
**![](https://iili.io/JStFLCl.gif)﻿**
  
  
**Mihail's Automaton Pack compatibility**For the sake of smaller mod sizes, I changed the original mod's textures to be of lower quality in this. If you want the full extent of Mihail's grace, and have some extra disk space, you should also download Mihail's Automaton Pack, and install the compatibility version of Dae's Titanhammer instead of the standalone one.
  
  
**How to start the quest**
  
Upon reaching level 25, the courier will bring you a mysterious letter.
  
﻿﻿
  
**Recommendations**As previously stated, this adventure won't be easy.
  
The first part of it is mostly vanilla level.
  
The second part is already a big leap in difficulty, so it's recommended that you've at least finished the equivalent to the entire main story quest and a DLC's.
  
For the last part, you'll have to travel to Solstheim. So be sure to pack a lot of potions and only your strongest arsenal. It is relentless...![](https://iili.io/JgFfFoP.png)﻿
  
﻿**Features﻿**A somewhat small three-part questline with great rewards
  
  
Custom Dwemer Warhammer made from mostly vanilla assets
  
  
Many new Dwemer enemies and loot, implemented from Mihail's mods
  
  
A massive new Dwemer dungeon, based in Solstheim
  
  
5 Easter Egg items to be found
