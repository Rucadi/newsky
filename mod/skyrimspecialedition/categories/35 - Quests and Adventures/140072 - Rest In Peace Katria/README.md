# Rest In Peace Katria
- Author: 6VioletMoon9
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/140072


**- She Gave You Her Valuable Reward... Her Discovery -**[left]
  
Features:
  
[/left]- Give Katria a proper burial in Falkreath cemetery**.**- Bring flowers for her grave every month "in game time".
  
- Pay your respect with different animations for female and male players.**The quest will start as soon as you complete Katria's "Lost To The Ages".
  
  
You can install it mid-game, [b]even if you already completed her quest**, no need for new save.[/b]
