# Konahrik's Accoutrements
- Author: Edhelsereg
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/22206


![](https://i.imgur.com/O9mNiEs.png)﻿
  
*Take a journey through time in the lost Dragon Priest temple, Revakheim. Unlock unique gear and create armor not seen since the First Era.
  
Gain new powers and become stronger than ever. Fight ancient enemies and fulfill your destiny as the true "*Konahrik*" Warlord.*
  
  
![](https://sites.google.com/site/konahrikaccoutrements/_/rsrc/1386930029772/images/konah_ed_50px.png)

  
  
Key Features
  
  
❖ Explore a Massive New Dungeon
  
Find the lost Dragon Priest temple, Revakheim, and uncover it many wonders.
  
  
❖ Become the True Warlord
  
Collect all of Konahrik's armor by traveling to Revakheim's past.
  
  
❖ Dragon Priest Armory
  
Find and craft all 26 Dragon Priest sets.
  
  
❖ New Powers, Enchantments, Magic and more
  
Cleanse the High Dragon Priests' armor and uncover their powerful magical properties using the forgotten Abjuration magic.
  
  
❖ Dragon Priest Rematch
  
Resurrect slain Dragon Priests by using their masks in a special ceremony.
  
  
Have questions? Need a guide? Check out the [Official Site](https://sites.google.com/site/konahrikaccoutrements/) for full documentation.
