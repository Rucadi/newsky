# Unslaad SE
- Author: Vicn
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/11789


Middle size quest about half dragon.
  
Half dragon can convert dragon souls to health, magicka, stamina, perk point.
  
Add many unique weapons, armors, shields, rings and clothes.
  
  
V3.0.0 Added New quests and worlds.

[font=Georgia]**Installation :**[/font][line]
  

  
1. Check that you have all required mods and DLCs installed and enabled
Install Unslaad.esm and Unslaad.bsa using your Mod Manager of choice
  
2. Wait or sleep several hours ingame. Save, close your game, and then reload.

  
  
  
**Updating :**[line]

  
* Updatable 3.0.x to 3.0.4 safely
Updatable 2.6.x to 3.0.x safely
  

  
If version < 2.6.0
  
outdated version
  
You should start new game or use a save that has never had Unslaad installed before.
  
  
  
[font=Georgia]**The Quests:**[/font][line]Act1 : White cat leads you to half-dragon, Ulliss.
  
  
How to start : Activate Doll.
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/80425-0-1479938333.jpg)
  
  
Act2 (v2.0.0) : You find the way to future of Unslaad. 
  
How to start : Open the secret door of Guest room after Act1.
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/80425/80425-1549579187-1588716750.jpeg)
  
Act3 : You find the child of Ulliss at furthur future.
  
How to start : Give your dragon souls to Dying Ulliss.
  
  
Act4 : You find the place of your death at cradle of dragons.
  
How to start : Talk to Black Owl at Jhunal's mural.
  
  
Lover Letter:
  
After Act 4 is completed and Jokul is released, the Insect Prophet tells you to collect the fragments.
  
(Jokul is prisoned the north-east cave and the Prophet lives in the north tower.)
  
  
The Last Journey(v3.0.0)
  
A story for the end of Last Dragonborn.
  
It begins when you jump off the dragon's peak after obtaining the love letter from the 5th Era.

[font=Georgia]**MCM :**[/font][line]
  
on the MCM, you can change the Health (Health Mult) and the Attack Power (Attack Power Mult) of bosses.
  
  
**Health**
  
Each time the value is increased by 1, Health of Bosses is increased by 10%.
  
  
**Attack Power**
  
Each time the value is increased by 1, attack power of Bosses is increased by 20%.
  
  
**Animation Stability**
  
If NPCs are shaking while attempting to use additional animations, activate this option.
