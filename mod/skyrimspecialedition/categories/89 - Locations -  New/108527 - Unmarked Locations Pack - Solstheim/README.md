**Description
  
Another mod in the Unmarked Locations Series. This time I tried to make each unmarked location even more unique, raising the standard of quality for myself. In addition to brand new locations, you will also find locations from the Bloodmoon DLC for TES 3: Morrowind. Go out and explore!**
