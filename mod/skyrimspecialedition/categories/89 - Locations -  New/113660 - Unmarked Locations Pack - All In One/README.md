**[font=Trebuchet MS]Features[/font]**﻿

  
* **More than 150 new locations to visit.**
**Some unique interactions in the locations (For Example: Sealed Crypt near Markarth).**
  
* **Some unique encounters in the locations (For Example: new boss fight in Hjaalmarch Swamps).**
**Some Easter eggs.**
  
* **Optional addons (including Description Framework support).**
**Brand new locations.**
  
* **Additional fixes and improvements.**
**Compatibility with Lux Via, Northern Roads, Depths of Skyrim and Creation Club content!**
  

﻿
  
**Description
  
After more than a year since the release of the first mod in this series, I am proud to present an all in one version. This version includes all Unmarked Locations Pack mods, as well as many additional bug fixes, improvements and brand new locations. Now you will always find something new in your game!
