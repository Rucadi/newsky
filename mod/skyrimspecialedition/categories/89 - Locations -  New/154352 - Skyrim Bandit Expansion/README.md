# Skyrim Bandit Expansion
- Author: MissingLukey
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/154352


**Key Features:**
  
  
**14 new bandit camps** all compatible with radiant quests, placed throughout Skyrim, ranging from small ambush sites to fully fortified strongholds, caves, and hidden outposts.
  
  
**2 custom mines:**

  
* One reworked into a frozen, glacial lair with icebound pathways.
Another turned into an operational gold and iron mine, occupied by a ruthless Orcish gang.
  

**Unique Regional Themes:**

  
* Mountain peak and ridgeline camps
Forest encampments in Falkreath
  
* Windswept plateaus
Shipwreck-based camps along Winterhold's northern coast
  
* Cliffside outposts in the Rift
Harsh winter camps in the colder holds
  
* Ruined towers repurposed by bandits - once part of Skyrim's lost defenses

**Lore-rich worldbuilding:**

  
* Notes, letters, journals, and ambient clues are scattered around many camps.
reveal the bandits' inner conflicts, lost hopes, and hidden motive.
  
* Broken families, unfulfilled promises, and quiet sacrifices add emotional depth.
Environmental storytelling with hidden stashes.
  

**Compatibility:**

  
* Fully navmeshed.
Uses Vanilla bandit variants for compatibility and enhance gameplay when used alongside mods such as ([OBIS](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/4145) , [Lawless](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/88080) , [Rogues 'n Raiders](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/47504))
  
* Includes a FOMOD installer with two options: install as an All-In-One package, or select individual camps for better compatibility with large mod lists and cell edits.
ESL Compatible in FOMOD installer
  
* Uses only vanilla assets - no scripts or heavy edits
Should be compatible with most mods
  
* If you encounter conflicts with mods that edit the same cells, feel free to report them - patches can be provided as needed
