# Ayleid Piercings - CBBE 3BA - HIMBO - SoS
- Author: Imp
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/76204


This is a ~~tiny~~ *(not so tiny anymore!)* accessory mod I threw together for fun. 
  
There are various styles of piercings for both male and female. 
  
  
**Crafting Recipes -**
  
***You need at least one of the corresponding gem in your inventory for the recipes to show in the smithy!***
  
  
**Varla Stone Piercings, Amulet, Earrings, Labia Rings -** 2 Diamonds, 1 Gold Ingot
  
  
**Enchanted Varla Stone Piercings (Arcane Blacksmith Perk) -** 2 Flawless Diamonds, 1 Gold Ingot. (+100 Magicka) 
  
  
**Varla Belly and Clit Rings -** 1 Diamond, 1 Gold Ingot
  
  
**Varla Circlet -** 3 Diamonds, 1 Gold Ingot
  
  
**Enchanted Varla Circlet (Arcane Blacksmith Perk) -** 3 Flawless Diamonds, 1 Gold Ingot (+40 Magicka)
  
  
  
**Welkynd Stone Piercings, Amulet, Earrings, Labia Rings -** 2 Sapphires/Emeralds/Rubies, 1 Quicksilver Ingot
  
  
**Enchanted Welkynd Stone Piercings (Arcane Blacksmith Perk) -** 2 Flawless Sapphires/Flawless Emeralds/Flawless Rubies, 1 Quicksilver Ingot (100% Faster Magicka/Stamina/Health Regen)
  
  
**Welkynd Belly and Clit Rings -** 1 Sapphire/Emerald/Ruby, 1 Quicksilver Ingot
  
  
**Welkynd Circlet -** 3 Sapphires/Emeralds/Rubies, 1 Quicksilver Ingot
  
  
**Enchanted Welkynd Circlet (Arcane Blacksmith Perk) -** 3 Flawless Sapphires/Flawless Emeralds/Flawless Rubies, 1 Quicksilver Ingot (100% Faster Magicka/Stamina/Health Regen)
  
  
  
**Alteration -** 1 Flawless Amethyst, 1 Flawless Sapphire, 1 Quicksilver Ingot
  
  
**Conjuration -** 2 Flawless Amethysts, 1 Quicksilver Ingot
  
  
**Destruction -** 1 Flawless Emerald, 1 Flawless Garnet, 1 Quicksilver Ingot
  
  
**Illusion -** 1 Flawless Emerald, 1 Flawless Sapphire, 1 Quicksilver Ingot
  
  
**Restoration -** 1 Flawless Emerald, 1 Flawless Ruby, 1 Quicksilver Ingot
  
  
Unenchanted versions use "flawed" gems.
  
  
  
***While the Plugin for Rare Curios isn't required, the textures are.***
