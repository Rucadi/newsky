# Priestess of Time BHUNP and 3BA
- Author: TectonicLeader
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/141486


![](https://i.imgur.com/upKf7ZR.png)![](https://i.imgur.com/o0U6lg4.jpeg)

  

![](https://i.imgur.com/6q8I6UB.png)

  

This is a Rework of the Time Priestess outfit that includes a BHUNP Conversion, Cleaner 3BA sliders and physics, SMP
  
physics added to the
  
skirt of Priestess of Death Outfit and translated to English. 
  
Adjustment sliders have also been added to the Circlet, Veil, and both
  
sets of earrings.
  
  
The Mod Contains the **White** and **Gold** as well as the **Black** and **Silver** versions of both outfits.  Both dresses have SMP Skirts.
