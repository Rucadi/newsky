# Towels
- Author: tau owl
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/145113


Hat towel, shoulder towel, body towel, waist towel.
  
  
The shoulder and waist towels have SMP cloth physics.
  
  
If the cloth physics doesn't behave well, try increasing the simulation quality in the FSMP configs, if it tanks your fps, buy a 5090. SMP is very finicky with frame rate and sampling rate affecting the simulation outcome. Your mileage may vary, but it works on my setup.
  
  
Requirements:
  
One of the aforementioned body mods
  
[Bodyslide](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/201)
  
[Latest FSMP version﻿](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/57339)
  
  
esl flagged esp. Use [additemmenu](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/17563), [modex](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/137877), or [console](https://elderscrolls.fandom.com/wiki/Console_Commands_(Skyrim)); or you can craft this with 2 tundra cotton and temper with 1. [Build with bodyslide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=45E8qqnqJDQ) first.
