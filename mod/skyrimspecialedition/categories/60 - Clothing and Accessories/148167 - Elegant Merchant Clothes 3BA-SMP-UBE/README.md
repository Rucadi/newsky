[size=4]**[﻿](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/106441?tab=files)﻿Elegant Merchant Clothes 3BA-SMP / UBE**

  
? A set of **standalone** merchant clothes based on the vanilla models, including shoes and gloves.
  
  
Comes in **red** and **blue** variants, and is compatible with vanilla retextures such as [RUSTIC CLOTHING](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/4703)﻿ (which is being used in the screenshots).
  
  
✂️ Both variants are **craftable** at the tanning rack with balanced material requirements. All pieces can be enchanted.
  
  
The skirt/loincloth section is **physics-enabled.** Beast races are also supported and patches are available for **SPID** and **Hands Redone**.
  
  
⚠️ You will need to **build the meshes in BodySlide** (for convenience, select the Elegant Merchant 3BA group or filter by any of those keywords). Please use the custom slider and zaps to your taste.[/size]
  
  
**Requirements**
  
[Faster HDT-SMP](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/57339)﻿
  
[BodySlide](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/201)﻿ to build the meshes in your chosen preset
  
[3BA](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/30174)﻿ or [UBE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/92989)
  
[BEES](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/106441?tab=files) if you have an older version of Skyrim
