# Vampire Clothing Expansion
- Author: Hornclonic
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/97185


[font=Tahoma]**DESCRIPTION**[/font][font=Tahoma]
  
[/font]
  
This mod adds 8 new clothing meshes all carefully crafted from existing vanilla assets,
  
equally divided between male and female characters, adding much needed diversity to your vampire characters.
  
  
All items are not craftable , just like the vanilla vampire clothing.
  
Currently there are three ways to obtain these items: via Console Commands or AddItemMenu , type ''help "Vampire Clothing"" "" to find FormIDs in game.
  
Through the SPID optional file, which distributes the clothing to unnamed vampire npcs between level 1-10. (25% chance).
  
Or through the optional levelled list esp under misc files.
  
  
Vampire Armor Expansion is planned next.
  
  
[font=Tahoma]**COMPATIBILITY**[/font]
  
  
Bodyslide files are provided, feel free to build to fit custom bodies.
  
Prebuilt meshes are provided for HIMBO and CBBE Curvy.
  
My esp is compatible with everything and the load order doesn't matter.
