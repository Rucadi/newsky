# Pierced Ears - Earrings SE
- Author: Mossa
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/13571


Pierced Ears - Earrings 
  
  
-adds 5 Earrings for the left ear and 5 Earrings for the right ear
  
-male and female 
  
-Beast Races not supported (Khajiit,Argonian)
  
-seperate meshes for Humans,DarkElfs,HighElfs,WoodElfs and Orcs (105 meshes)
  
-positioned for Vanilla Head Meshes and Ear Morphs
  
-followers will equip them
  
-both earrings can be enchanted
  
-3 different Materials available (Steel/Ebony/Gold)
  
  
Have fun!
  
...and feel free to upload your Screenshots (as long as they are SFW)... 
  
  
Review Video by [FeuerTim](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCuOyXRkpN_S1Hyyt3kyRG7g)(Video is of LE version/Still same look and quality)
  
  
  
  
Requirements:
  
-none (Skyrim)
  
  
How to get:
  
-craft them at the forge under Jewelry
  
  
Body Slots used:
  
Right Earrings use Slot 43
  
Left Earrings use Slot 55
  
  
  
Install/Uninstall:
  
use NMM or MO
  
  
Problems/Workarounds:
  
If you use ECE: not tested, not supported - but seems to work
  
If you use alternate Head Meshes: not supported
