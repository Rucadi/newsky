Hi
  
Here it is: Nocturnal Dress.
  
The mod includes two versions of the dress, one without breast movement and one with.
  
The dress comes in two colors.
  
The panties and shoes also come in two colors.
  
It also includes two types of jewelry.
  
Everything is SMP.
  
Enjoy!
