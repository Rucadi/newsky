# Torches Candlelight and Lanterns
- Author: i329
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/162694


[left]﻿**Features**
  
- 26 unique lanterns with SMP physics for the player and NPCs including Dunmer lanterns, hold lanterns, faction lanterns, and more. The Dunmer lanterns also replaces the common Skyrim lanterns found in Solstheim.
  
- Dynamic gameplay where lanterns start in hand and placed on hip during combat both for NPCs and the player when toggled with the hotkey (or a toggle item for gamepads). No fiddling with inventories, acquire one of the lanterns press the hotkey and play.
  
- The hotkey will also equip a torch or cast Candlelight if they're available instead
  
- An optional fuel system that can be managed in the MCM menu.
  
- Lanterns and fuel can be found on NPCs, in stores, and randomly in dungeons
  
- Candlelight and torches are distributed for more diversity
  
SMP for all

**Instructions**
  
Set a hotkey or use the Toggle Lantern for gamepads.
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/DZAzPkQ.jpeg)
  
Acquire a lantern and fuel if required.
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/28t0ErT.jpeg)
  
-> Press the hotkey and play. Lanterns will start in hand and placed on hip when you equip weapons or spells to your left hand or hold block. -> Press the hotkey to return the lantern to hand. -> Press the hotkey again to switch to an unlit lantern. \* If you do not have a lantern or fuel the hotkey will use torches or the vanilla candlelight spell if those are available instead.
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/Bvyq23g.jpeg)﻿
  
**Other**
  
Mages in the wilds can be found using Candlelight and Torches can still be found in use.
