# Sorcerer Outfits
- Author: Collygon
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/137725


**[left][/left]****A set of Sorcerer outfits for male and female characters in a variety of colors.
  
  
﻿﻿![](https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54238041316_38eaa785cd_c.jpg)﻿

  
Requirements:**
  
 ﻿1. Skyrim
  
 ﻿2. UNP Based Body for Female Characters
  
 ﻿3. HDT Physics
  
  
**Installation:**
  
 ﻿1. Unzip and drop into the Skyrim data folder or install the .zip into your preferred mod manager
  
 ﻿2. Enjoy in game!
  
  
**Includes:
  
 ﻿**- 6 different colors: Blue, Black, Green, Red, White, and Purple
  
 **﻿**- 2 Types of Hoods. One that hides the hair and one that does not (not guaranteed to fit with every hairstyle!)
  
 ﻿- Outfit, Sleeves, and Hoods come in the 6 color types
  
 ﻿- 2 Boot options: Black and Brown
  
  
**FAQ:
  
    Q: How do I acquire the outfit in game?
  
    A**: You can craft all pieces at a tanning rack.
  
  
    **Q: Why isn't this armor for UUNP, CBBE, or HIMBO?**
  
    **A:** I don't like using bodyslide. You are free to make/release your own conversions if you wish.
  
 ﻿[BHUNP 3BA HIMBO Conversion by Myst](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/138514)
  
  
**Q: My game doesn't detect the ESP?**    **A:** If on an older version of Skyrim (prior to 1.6.1130), you need [BEES](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/106441)
