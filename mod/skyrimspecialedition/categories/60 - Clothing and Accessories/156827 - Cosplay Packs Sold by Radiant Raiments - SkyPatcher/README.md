# Cosplay Packs Sold by Radiant Raiments - SkyPatcher
- Author: GUNDAMmk2
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/156827


中文简介：Spoiler
  
使用 Skypatcher 让 Radiant Raiments 能够出售来自 Cosplay Pack 系列 MOD 中的服饰
  
没有ESP，没有MOD冲突。
  
  
**简介：**
  
这个MOD受到了 [Buy (rare) armour from Grelka](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/151454) 的启发。它让 Radiant Raiments 出售 [Caenarvon](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/88049288) ﻿大佬出色的Cosplay服装，让你获得更合适的着装品味和礼仪。
  
你无需安装所有的Cosplay Pack系列MOD，本MOD只会对你所安装的服装MOD起效。
  
没有ESP，没有冲突。
  
  
  
**可选额外内容：**
  
**Cosplay Packs 50% off Patch**
  
让所有 Cosplay Pack 系列MOD的服饰都以半价出售
  
  
**Cosplay Pack Patcher**
  
去除 Cosplay Pack - hdt SMP (CBBE 3BA) 的ESP文件对原版LL记录的编辑，改用SkyPatcher分发，以提高对各种大修MOD的兼容性
  
  
**Cosplay Packs Addon Patch**
  
让Radiant Raiments出售 [Cosplay Packs Addon](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/160858?tab=description)﻿ 中的服装，需要 Cosplay Packs Addon
  
  
  
  
**Description:**
  
- This mod was inspired by [Buy (rare) armour from Grelka](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/151454). It allows Radiant Raiments to sell the excellent cosplay outfits created by [Caenarvon](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/88049288), bringing more taste and sophistication to your wardrobe.
  
- You do not need to install all the Cosplay Pack series mods, tthis mod will only work with the corresponding outfit mods you have installed.
  
- No ESP, no conflicts.
  
  
  
  
**Optional Add-ons:**
  
**- Cosplay Packs Half Price Patcher**
  
  Makes all outfits from the Cosplay Pack series available at half price.
  
  
**- Cosplay Pack Patcher**
  
  Removes the ESP file's edits to the original leveled list (LL) records from [Cosplay Pack - hdt SMP (CBBE 3BA)](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/90892) and instead uses SkyPatcher to distribute them, improving compatibility with various overhaul mods.
  
  
**- Cosplay Packs Addon Patch**
  
  Allows Radiant Raiments to sell costumes from the [Cosplay Packs Addon](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/160858). No Esp. Cosplay Packs Addon Required.
