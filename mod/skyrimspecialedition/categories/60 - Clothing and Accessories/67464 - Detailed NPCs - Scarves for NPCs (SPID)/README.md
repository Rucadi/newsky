**Description**
  

  
* Mod uses [Spell Perk Item Distributor (SPID)](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/36869)﻿ to distribute scarves from [Scarves of Skyrim](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/16660/)﻿ to NPCs﻿﻿

ESL-flagged
  
* No records edited

Overview[/b]
  
Mod utilizes a small script that runs once (at game initialization) to distribute and equip scarves to [Elder](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elder)NPCs. I have edited the distribution so that only about half of the [Elder](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elder)﻿NPCs are covered. See \_DISTR.ini
  
  
*Three versions*:
  

  
1. *All-in-One*: Mod randomly distributes either a muffler or long scarf to NPCs. Sixteen (16) texture variations.
*Muffler Only*: Mod distributes a muffler to NPCs. Sixteen (16) texture variations.
  
2. *Long Scarf*: Mod distributes a long scarf to NPCs. Sixteen (16) texture variations.

I have used the terrific textures from [Scarves of Skyrim - Lind's Version](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/77760). That is, Lind's textures are included in my mod. The very generous permissions from [Lind001](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/users/6129727) made this possible. Be sure to thank [Lind001](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/users/6129727) and endorse [Scarves of Skyrim - Lind's Version](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/77760).
  
  
**Mod can be re-configured to distribute scarves to any NPC and / or any group of NPCs that you wish!** Edit the \_DISTRI.ini that comes with this mod, and add (or delete) any Form ID that you wish.
  
  
**Compatibility**
  
Highly compatible with most setups, and with most other mods. 
  

  
* [Spell Perk Item Distributor (SPID)](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/36869)﻿ does not edit levelled lists, and does not modify or generate new records.
Body slots, however, are not compatible. Choose wisely, or edit the body slot. I will not alter body slots.
  
* Clipping will likely occur depending on setup. I will not resolve clipping issues.

  
**Installation and Uninstallation**
  
Use the mod manager of your choice to install. Or manually. To uninstall, remove with mod manager. My understanding is that SPID will not impact save games in any way. Safe to uninstall at anytime. 
  
  
**Configuration**
  
Huge number of configuration options available in the \_DISTR.ini. Some knowledge of [Spell Perk Item Distributor (SPID)](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/36869)﻿ is useful to achieve desired results.
