# Mage Clothing Expansion - 3BA Uniboob
- Author: Hornophobic aka Hornclonic
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/128838


A quick but thorough 3BA Uniboob conversion of the awesome [Mage Clothing Expansion](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/128173)﻿. I added body physics and hand tweaked a lot of bone weights and sliders. You can find my Bodyslide edits under the names "**0MCE\_3BA\_....**" or the "**Mage Clothing Expansion 3BA**" Group Filter.
