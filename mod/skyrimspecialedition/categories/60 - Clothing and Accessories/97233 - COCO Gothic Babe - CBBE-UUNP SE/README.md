# COCO Gothic Babe - CBBE-UUNP SE
- Author: COCO
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/97233


[font=Comic Sans MS]**﻿![](https://i.imgur.com/H4SMPaZ.png)﻿﻿**[/font]**A made from scratch outfit by COCO, featuring a unique Gothic style Dress. The outfit comes with CBBE 3BA & COCOBody CBBE/UUNP bodyslides support. The Hair, Top, Arms, and Necklace come with HDT-SMP cloth physics.**
