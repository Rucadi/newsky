# Mage Clothing Expansion Expanded (and Skypatched)
- Author: Ennui
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/128950


**FEATURES**
  
- Renamed all clothing to Mage Robes for uniformity.
  
  
- **Five tiers of mage clothing**Novice, Apprentice, Adept, Expert, and Master Mage Robes with corresponding enchantments.
  
  
- **Enchantments**:
  
Magicka Regeneration Rate 50%, 75%, 100%, 125%, and 150% to Novice, Apprentice, Adept, Expert, and Master tiers, respectively.
  
  
- **Skypatched**:
  
Distributed to Warlock, Necromancers, and Vampires using mage robes leveled lists - will be seen at around levels 1 (Novice), 8 (Apprentice), 16 (Adept), 24 (Expert), and 32 (Master).
  
  
- Crafting is left untouched.
