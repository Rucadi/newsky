This mod adds 6 wizard brim hats to the game, each with 4 different hair options. 
  
  
There weren't enough good wizard hats for men, so I did these :)
  
  
They are designed to match the [Traveling Mage Robes by Fuse00 and Xtudo](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/73593), plus an additional brown hat. 
  
  
And now, also a grey hat to match [Windcaller Robes](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/128033).
  
  
Thanks to [LevinLozenges](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/3414648) for assisting in adding these variants and getting the mod working as it's my first mod.
  
  
These hats are good for male, female and beast characters and [utilise Fuse's hair variants from the Inquisitor set](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/73980). There is also a no hair option.
  
  
There are crafting recipes for the traveling mage colour versions at a tanning rack.
  
  
Or, add via console commands. hit ` tilde and type:
  
help "brim hat"
  
Then type:
  
player.additem <reference id> 1
  
  
plain hat ID is no hair - operates as a circlet
  
hat A is short hair
  
hat B is long hair
  
hat C is ponytail hair
