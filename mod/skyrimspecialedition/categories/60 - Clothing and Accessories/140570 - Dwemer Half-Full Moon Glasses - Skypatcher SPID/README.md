# Dwemer Half-Full Moon Glasses - Skypatcher SPID

- Game: skyrimspecialedition

Description
  
![](https://pixelsafari.neocities.org/dividers/3pinkflowers.png)**

  
Adds Dwemer Half/Full Moon Glasses to levelled lists and NPCs, with the power of Skypatcher and SPID, flagged esl
  
  
? Requires [Dwemer Half Moon Glasses](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/140543) for the Half version only
  
? Requires ﻿[Dwemer Half Moon Glasses](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/140543) and [Dwemer Full Moon Glasses](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/140667) for the Half and Full moon version﻿
  
? NPC glasses can be edited through SPID ini, if you want to change/add/remove, there is the place. Distribution is made by Race, NPC and Factions.
  
? Distribution to levelled lists made through Skypatcher
  
? esp flagged as esl
