This mod is part of my series to add new interactions with the non-follower NPCs and insert roleplaying opportunities for the player.
  
  
  
  
**What does this mod do:**
  
  
This mod gives Ulfric and Galmar more immersive dialogues focused on their **motives, past, plans and personal lives.**
  
There are even new dialogue options with **significant lore implications.**
  
  
---
  
  
**Features:**
  
1. Expands Ulfric Stormcloak and Galmar Stone-Fist with 150 lines, revolving around their motives, past, plans and personal lives
  
2. New option in "Missing in Action" to parallel the route where you ask Tullius for help
  
3. New banters between Ulfric and Galmar about how they are managing Skyrim's Jarls
  
  
This mod is **100% compatible with any mod**, including dialogue expansion mods from other authors.
  
  
**Safe to add mid-save.**
  
  
There will be **future expansion** with Ulfric.
