# Each Stone of Barenziah Gives You a Bonus
- Author: VanKorno
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/37122


[font=Arial]Each Stone of Barenziah in your inventory gives you a small carry-weight bonus (10 points). Upon finishing the quest you'll get a permanent bonus of 250 cw points in addition to the vanilla reward (Prowler's Profit).
  
  
There are add-ons available [HERE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/149046), if you want to have other values than the default 10 points.
  
[/font]
  
The mod is absolutely compatible with everything. It makes no edits to the stones themselves or something like that. It just gives you a new perk, that adds the CW bonus.
