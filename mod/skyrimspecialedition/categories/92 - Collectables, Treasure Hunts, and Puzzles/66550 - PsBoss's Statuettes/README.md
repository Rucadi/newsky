[font=Times New Roman]How Crafting Statuettes?[/font]
  
[font=Trebuchet MS]1. Buy Sketches for statuettes, from Merchant: URAG GRO-SHUB, College of Winterhold.
  
2. Buy (merchant ULFBERTH WAR-BEAR) or Craft - Statuette Base: Silver or Gold.
  
3. Buy or Find in Skyrim Ingots (Silver, Gold,...etc).
  
4. Crafting in the Smithing Forge.[/font]
