# Wintersun - Tweaks and Enhancements
- Author: Ixion XVII
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/107675
**Enhances and rebalances key elements of Wintersun for a more engaging gameplay experience.
  
  
Power levels of the strongest and weakest elements have been adjusted based on extensive feedback from my [Discord community](https://discord.gg/UF6aV3S), ensuring a
  
fairer and more enjoyable game for all players.****Full Changelog:**
  
*- Follower of Talos/Shor abilities now applies to falmers, since they're also elves
  
- Talos/Shor/St. Alessia favor now increases when killing falmers
  
- Increased the favor gain of specific Daedra tenets (e.g. Meridia Undead kills, Azura Soul trapping, Sanguine drinking, etc.)
  
  
- Baan'Dar no longer decreases buy prices with other races, instead increases sell prices with fences
  
- Mara now grants 2 health regeneration out of combat
  
- Morwha fruits now restore hunger if Survival Mode is used, increased the magnitude/duration of fruit buffs, reworked Pineapple effect
  
- Jephre Devotee ability now grants half the stamina regeneration while in combat, improved description
  
- Nocturnal devotee ability increases pickpocket chance and sneak during the night
  
- The HoonDing devotee ability now grants immunity to stagger rather than resistance based on favor
  
- The HoonDing follower ability now also works out of combat, duration increased from 5 to 30
  
  
- Riddle'Thar devotee boons duration from 30 minutes to 60 minutes, rebalanced the boon magnitude (up to 100% increases)
  
- Talos no longer increases critical hit damage, now increases damage against non-elves by half the bonus, devotee ability range from 40 to 50
  
- Vaermina follower ability only works out of combat but also reduces the manacost of mind-altering spells by 50%, devotee ability favor drain from 10% to 5%
  
- Tall Papa devotee ability favor drain from 10% to 5%
  
[i][size=2]- Removed the Hermaeus Mora activator during the Miraak boss fight*
  
  
*- Magnus spell mana reduction from 75% to 60%
  
- Meridia devotee ability damage multiplier based on favor from x5 to x3
  
- Stendarr devotee ability no longer increases attack damage
  
- Hermaeus Mora devotee ability favor drain from 5% to 10%
  
- Z'en potion/food duration bonus from 100% to 50%
  
- The Hist follower ability no longer increases health
  
- Auriel skill increases now only work during the day, favor gain for completing quests from 25 to 20
  
- Molag Bal favor gain from being a vampire from 5 to 3, killing a person from 0.75 to 1.5, trapping a soul from 0.75 to 1*[/size]
