# Requiem - Sacrilege
- Author: Ixion XVII
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/72578
*Fixed all compatiblity issues between the mods
  
Rewrote some texts to be in line with Requiem
  
  
Removed all level scaling mechanics
  
Removed destruction skill increasing armor (it still increases damage)
  
  
Vampire Rank magnitude scaling effect regarding Vampire spells reduced by 75%
  
The Vampire Rank scaling nerf is required because of the increased base damage of Requiem
  
Increased the damage of vampire spells by 7-times (Requiem drain life = 8-times stronger)
  
Increased the damage of vampire lord spells by 5-times
  
Removed manacost reduction from vampire related to normal manacost reduction perks
  
Vampire related spells scale with destruction
  
Rebalanced manacost/magnitude of all vampire spells
  
  
You only receive potion of bloods when killing enemies while being a Wood Elf or Nord (= removed Potion of Thaumaturgy)
  
Removed Potion of Thaumaturge from loot tables
  
Searing Sun debuff from 100/200/300/400 reduced stats to 75/125/175/225
  
Fire Weakness debuff from 75/85/100/150% to 50/75/100/125%
  
Resist Frost buff from 95/85/75/50 to 90/80/70/60
  
Reduced base health/stamina by 50
  
NPC vampires also received tweaks according to the changes
  
Drain Life reduced magnitude by 10 (because of vampire Rank scaling)
  
  
Removed the ability to drain health on attacks (the lifesteal perk is rewritten and still intact)
  
Rebalanced some perks to be in line with Requiem
  
A ton of additional changes, read the changelog for more information*
  
  
*[size=5]Load Sacrilege after Requiem[/size] (and the patch after both)
  
Load this patch after Undeath if used
  
  
**Known Issue:***- Gaining skills in Vampire form doesn't work as intended (you can read up on that in the bugs section of Growl)
