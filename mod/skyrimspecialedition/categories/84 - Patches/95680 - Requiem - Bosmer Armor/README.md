# Requiem - Bosmer Armor
- Author: Ixion XVII
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/95680
**Balance and Compatibility patch for [Requiem](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/60888) and [Bosmer Armor Pack](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/37289)ï»¿. (also requires [Bosmer Armor Pack Fixed and Balanced](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/31304))**
  
- Includes Engraved Bosmer Armor and Wild Hunt Armor (Heavy) + 2 weapons + 1 arrows *(everything else is removed)*
  
- Rebalanced the armor/weapons/arrows
  
- Rebalanced the crafting/temper recipes
  
- Bosmer Armor is available to buy in the drunken huntsman, craftable with elven smithing, some NPCs wear it
  
- Wild Hunt Armor (Heavy) is available from a new boss in Shadowgreen Cavern
  
- The boss also has a new enchanted variant of the Bosmer Bow, rebalanced the boss (level 50)
