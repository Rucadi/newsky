# Requiem - Exhaustion and Stamina Tweaks
- Author: Ixion XVII
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/67232
[size=4]Traversing the lands of Skyrim in Requiem can be really tedious. This attempts to fix that while still somewhat keeping in line with base Requiem.**﻿Stamina:** **﻿Before****After**
  
  
*Power Attack    20        18
  
Bashing             25        20
  
Power Bash      40        35
  
  
Jumping           4         2
  
Running            0.25    0.05
  
Swimming        2         1
  
  
Reduced Heavy Armor sprinting penalty from 5 to 3
  
Reduced low stamina/health movement penalty from 25% to 10%
  
  
Spell cost penalty at low stamina from 100% to 50%
  
Power attack damage penalty at low stamina from 50% to 25%
  
Blocking penalty at low stamina from 75% to 50%
