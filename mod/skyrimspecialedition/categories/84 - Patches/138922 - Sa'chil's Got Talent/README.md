# Sa'chil's Got Talent
- Author: Spark22551
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/138922


Sa'chil will now acknowledge your presence as you practice your instruments around her!
  
  
You can also ask her to play music with you, so you can both now bring wonderful music to the people of skyrim!
