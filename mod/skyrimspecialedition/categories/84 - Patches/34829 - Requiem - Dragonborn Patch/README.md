**Major changes:**
  

  
* **[b]Almost all actors will have static level**[/b]
**[b]Almost all actors will have appropriate perks and stats**[/b]
  
* **[b]Crafting, tempering, cooking recipes were modified to fit Requiem**[/b]
**[b]Potions, alcohol drinks, ingridients were modified to fit Requiem**[/b]
  
* **[b]Leveled lists were modified to fit Requiem**[/b]
**[b]Some of the armour/weapons/ammo stats were modified to fit Requiem**[/b]
  
* **[b]Some of the spells were modified to fit Requiem**[/b]
**[b]The damage from the traps is now huge and static.**[/b]
  
* **[b]Renamed potion similar to the Requiem. The effect is protracted, not instant.**[/b]
**[b]Reworked the loot in the Apocrypha.**[/b]
  
* **[b]Bandits and Guards now have classes: Marksmen, Archers, Swordsmen, and so on. Bandits have random race with a predominance of the Nords and Dunmer. Bandits have occasional random quality equipment, including Chitin and Bonemold.**[/b]
**[b]Seekers and Lurkers is now very serious opponents.**[/b]
  
* **[b]The main storyline Dragonborn should start after completing the quest "Blade in the Dark."**[/b]
**[b]Ingredients are now harvest in an amount of several pieces.**[/b]
  
* **[b]Increased fines for crimes in Raven Rock, in analogy with the Requiem.**[/b]
**[b]Added recipes for destruction Nordic equipment to quicksilver ingots.**[/b]
  
* **[b]Stalhrim armor and weapons now have a worse stats, but ALL enchantings 25% better.**[/b]
**[b]Added explosive Stalhrim/Nordic arrows/bolts.**[/b]
  
* **[b]Added new weapons.**[/b]***Stalhrim:*****Battlestaff,****Crossbow,****Longsword,****3 kinds of magical staves,****Katana.*****Nordic:*****Crossbow, Katana.**

  
* **And a lot of changes and innovations.**
