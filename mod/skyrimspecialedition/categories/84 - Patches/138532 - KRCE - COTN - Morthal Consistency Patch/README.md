# KRCE - COTN - Morthal Consistency Patch
- Author: CinamonPizza
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/138532


Another small and simple patch, due to the cell edits COTN: Morthal makes to Falions House, some quest items for Kaidan are left hanging in the void. This moves the two items inside of the house and onto the Enchanting Table, so you don't have to clip out of bounds or cheat in order to obtain them!
