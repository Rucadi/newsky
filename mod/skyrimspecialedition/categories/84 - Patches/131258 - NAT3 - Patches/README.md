# NAT3 - Patches
- Author: Hawksbill
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/131258


NAT III - Absolutely beautiful ENB preset and weather mod. Anyone that hasnt tried this ENB is missing out. It offers the best contrast lighting of any ENB preset.
  
  
Summerset Isles - Perhaps the best Landmass and quest mod on the Nexus. This author put some serious work into this and it really deserves more endorsements.
  
  
Skyrim Extended Cut - Fantastic enhancement of the Saints and Seducers Creation.
  
  
Wyrmstooth - One of the oldest and best landmass / quest mods on the Nexus.
  
  
Wyrmstooth patch is now here: <https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/87821?tab=description>
  
  
Put them together and you will get a bunch of weather related errors that show up when the NAT3 ENB Preset is enabled.. These patches aim to alleviate this by removing incompatibilities. I am in the process of tweaking weathers for better transition and reducing overexposure.Please give me some feedback with thoughts or if any errors pop up.
  
  
I have also uploaded a separate mod that is just for fun. It changes all weather in Summerset to Sovngardeclear. You can see it in the last three screen shots.
