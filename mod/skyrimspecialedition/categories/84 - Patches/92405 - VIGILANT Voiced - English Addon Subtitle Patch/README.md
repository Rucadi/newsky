# VIGILANT Voiced - English Addon Subtitle Patch
- Author: Samerz004
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/92405
**DESCRIPTION**
  
This **~revolutionary~** mod changes the subtitles to match the voices in Vigilant Voiced - English Addon~~It'll even tie your shoes and read you a bedtime story!﻿~~
  
  
**INSTALLATION**Use the mod manager of your choice and drag in like every other mod (I personally recommend [MO2](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/6194) if you don't use it already, you will thank your existing brain cells). You could also be a psycho and manually add it into your Skyrim data folder.
  
  
**MOD LOAD ORDER:**
  
[VIGILANT SE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/11849)
  
[VIGILANT Voiced - English Addon](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/11894)
  
VIGILANT Voiced - English Addon Subtitle Patch (you are here)
  
  
Make sure that "Vigilant Subtitle Patch.esp" overwrites "Vigilant.esm" and "Vigilant Voiced.esp"
