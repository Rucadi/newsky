**H E E L S   -   S O U N D   R E C O R D   D I S T R I B U T O R   ( S R D )**
  
  
[line]
  
  
**DESCRIPTION**
  
  
This mod patches various armor mods to have footstep heels sound by **Heels Sound**. It makes use of **Sound Record Distributor** (SRD) to be as compatible as possible and only contains a single YAML file and no ESP/ESL. Armor mods not available will just be skipped by SRD. You can install, update and deinstall this at any time. Plug-and-play.
  
  
**INFORMATION**
  
  
The patch distributes through **EditorIDs**. Technically it should be compatible even with merged versions of the armor mods as long as the EditorIDs match (may not work for all ESL [-.esl] files). The purpose is to get rid of time-consuming manual patching of individual mods, which do not always come with a corresponding heels sound patch, while at the same time (indirectly) remove the hard dependency for the Heels Sound plugin.
  
  
[A New Realistic High Heels Sound Bank (HQ)](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/92903) and [Better Heels Sound FX 1.0.6](https://www.loverslab.com/files/file/21344-better-heels-sound-fx/) improve the original sounds and are highly recommended. Use [Heels Sound Volume](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/122633) to customize volume. [Heels Noise](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/167070) is also recommended.
