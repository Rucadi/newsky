# Ghosts of Akavir Balance Tweaks
- Author: Soulmancer
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/84032


-Re-tuned the bosses balance to be more in line with [SRCEO Releveled](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/27201) and intended to be "mid tier" bosses
  
  
-The weapons and armor have also been adjusted to be more appropriately balanced in line with vanilla and the bosses difficulty. Value also adjusted 
  
  
-Fixed the broken armor tempering recipes
  
  
-Made a few armors unlootable/unplayable due to them being very visually broken on the player character
  
  
-Disabled the master ritual spells tomes that allow you to conjure the bosses
  
  
-Moves Agyo from Bloated Man's Grotto to Kathspire Entrance. 
  
  
-The Akavrir faction and Forsworn faction are now allied
  
  
-Disables the cheat loot chest
