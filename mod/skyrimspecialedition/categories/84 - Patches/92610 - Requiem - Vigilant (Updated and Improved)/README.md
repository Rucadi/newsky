# Requiem - Vigilant (Updated and Improved)
- Author: Ixion XVII
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/92610
**A continuation of the old [Requiem - Vigilant patch](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/76235) with many improvements and additional balancing.** 
  
  
**Requires** 
  
[Requiem Vigilant](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/76235)
  
[Vigilant - Delayed Start](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/57961) (choose the level you prefer, quest versions untested)
  
  
**Highly Recommended**
  
[Vigilant Immersion Tweaks](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/131649?tab=description)
  
  
*- Devotion/Radiance cost to obtain permanent buffs from 3 to 12
  
- Updated many entries of the Requiem patch (e.g. missing factions, wrong scripts etc.)
  
- New generic "Vigilant of Stendarr" now have Requiem stats/perks/equipment/items etc.
  
- Reduced the loot in the Temple of Stendarr, along with some other small improvements
  
- Added better compatibility with [no summons mod](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/69400) (this mod is **HIGHLY** recommended, Nerfed Rewards is not recommended)
  
  
- Reduced the damage and health of many enemies (simple enemies sometimes had over 600 unarmed damage)
  
- Rebalanced the resistances of many enemies, reduced magic resistance of many enemies
  
[size=3]- Unleveled NPC spells and improved some of the distribution
  
- Fixed an issue that variants of the same creature had different stats/perks*
  
- Skeletal enemies now have skeleton resistance
  
- Vampire enemies now have vampire resistance
  
  
- Rebalanced food items, added survival mode support
  
- Rebalanced alchemical ingredients
  
ï»¿[/size]*- Updated the names of some NPCs
  
- Rebalanced the loot of many enemies (e.g. removed treasures from creature loot)**- Removed duplicates of Dawnbreaker
  
- Many weapons with unique effects are no longer enchantable
  
- Rebalanced many unique items/weapons/armor
  
- Removed the sweep (AoE) attack property from all weapons
  
- Rebalanced and fixed arrows/projectile gravity
  
- Rebalanced all bows and changed them to heavy/light bows according to their appearance*
  
  
*- A ton of other small changes here and there*
  
  
And much more... Check out the **changelog** for more information!
