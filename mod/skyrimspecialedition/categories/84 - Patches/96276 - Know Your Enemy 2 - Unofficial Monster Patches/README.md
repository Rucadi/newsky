# Know Your Enemy 2 - Unofficial Monster Patches
- Author: ironeyes
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/96276


**These patches require** [Know Your Enemy 2](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/93258)**! [[size=4][b]Additional patches can be found at Orca255's modpage](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/124247)**.[/b][/size]
  
  
Base spell resistances were changed to fit KYE2's various monster profiles, but much of the flavor of each individual monster was kept. These patches have .esps and SPID files, except where otherwise noted.
  
  
 **Recommended Settings:**
  

  
* **Physical Effect Intensity -** 3 or 4
**Magical Effect Intensity -**3 or 4
  
* **Boost Silver Damage Against Undead** - ON

***Usage notes:***
  

  
* Yes, .esps are esl flagged.
I don't do small animals that are intended to die in a single hit.
  
* Replacers are almost always compatible, assuming they maintain the raceid.
KYE2's inspect features will work for patches with an .esp, but won't show all monster-specific weaknesses/resistances. SPID-only patches will work perfectly.
