# Requiem - Mihail Black Ooze and Voriplasm
- Author: Ixion XVII
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/89443
**Compatibility and Balance patch for [Requiem](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/60888) and [Black Ooze](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/79287) + [Voriplasm](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/79064).** 
  
  
**Black Ooze**
  
- Rebalanced spells/spell effects/health/stamina/damage/level/ingredient
  
- Renamed ingredient and remains
  
[size=3]- They receive reduced fall damage and have sneak attack immunity
  
- They have very high resistances, fight them with fire/blunt weapons[size=3] (They have 90% piercing and ranged resistance)[/size]
  
  
**Voriplasm**
  
- Rebalanced spells/spell effects/health/stamina/damage/level[size=3]/ingredient
  
- Redone how the creature works (how often it splits, when it drops loot)
  
- They receive reduced fall damage and have sneak attack immunity
  
- They have very high resistances, fight them with frost/blunt weapons (They have 90% piercing and ranged resistance)[/size]
