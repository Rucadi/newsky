# Sons of Skyrim - More Patches
- Author: Blackread
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/104261


A few patches for [Sons of Skyrim](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/68656) which I deemed necessary for my load order. These are for version 2.0 and the Main Archive file only.
  
  
[Capital Windhelm Expansion](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/42990):
  
Swaps the armor and weapons used by NPCs affiliated with Stormcloaks or guards.
  
  
[Diverse Guards](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/2324?tab=files&file_id=110361&nmm=1):
  
Conflict resolution between edited NPC records.
  
  
[Enhanced Solitude](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/27816):
  
Distributes Sons of Skyrim weapons to the new guards.
  
  
[Guard Dialogue Overhaul](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/22075):[size=3]
  
Conflict resolution of edited dialogue conditions. For Guard Dialogue Overhaul 3.0+.
  
  
[/size][Lux Orbis](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/56095):
  
Removes torches from the new guard gear sets added by Sons of Skyrim to mimic what Lux Orbis does with the vanilla guard gear.
  
  
[Morrowloot Ultimate](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/3058):
  
Conflict resolution on Shield of Solitude. Items placed by Sons of Skyrim have not been changed.
