# Skyrim Sewers 4 -  Mihail Monsters and Animals's Rats Patch
- Author: Lolilofol
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/137700


**This patch will add around 200 points of apparition for the [rats created by Mihail](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/137591)﻿ in the sewers and underground areas of** [**Skyrim Sewers 4**](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/9320)﻿
  
**A version added 25 giant rats from [Giant Bats and Rats- Mihail Monsters and Animals](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/140134)﻿**
  
  
  
  
**Details**
  
Most of the added rats are placed where potential food can be found. This means close to crates and barrels, deads body and around living areas.
  
Adding a few swarms, with smaller rats.
  
Dogs aren't cats, but rats certainly don't like the presence of dogs.
  
Nibbling on a draugr's feet can easily be a hearty meal for a rat!
  
The absence of rats may indicate the presence of another breed of animal.
