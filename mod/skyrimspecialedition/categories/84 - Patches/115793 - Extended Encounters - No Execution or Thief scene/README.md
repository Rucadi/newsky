# Extended Encounters - No Execution or Thief scene
- Author: Jonx0r
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/115793


I love [Extended Encounters](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/44810)﻿ by Jonx0r but the execution scene always bugged me. It felt a bit out of place in the middle of Whiterun, so I just changed the chance of it happening to 0. Prisoners rejoice!
  
  
Now prevents the thief stealing torso armour in the middle of a city as well.
  
  
If you want to reenable either of these events, go into xEdit, look under "Global" and set the chance for EE\_WI098Chance (Execution) or EE\_WI102Chance (Thief) back to 100, or just delete the entry.
