# Armor Variants Expansion - Patch Collection
- Author: TheHessiJames
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/35176


﻿Overview:
  
This mod merges the leveled list edits of Armor Variants Expansion to be compatible with:
  
  
**Morrowloot Ultimate** *- Armor Variants Expansion now fits the balance of MLU, in-depth patch which does more than just merging leveled lists*
  
**Summermyst** *- merged a big amount of changes together*
  
**Organized Bandits Of Skyrim** *- keeps the OBIS stuff e.g. the custom bandit shields but adds the chance of the new armor variations*
  
**Lore Weapons Expansion** *- fixed a minor overwrite***Common Clothes and Armors** *-* *fixed a major overwrite***Cutting Room Floor** *- fixed a minor overwrite***Weapons Armor Clothing & Clutter Fixes (WACCF)** - *added keywords and fixed some major overwrites,
  
                                                                                           you should disable/hide the "WACCF\_BashedPatchLvlListFix.esp" that comes with WACCF***WACCF & Improved Closefaced Helmets** - *solves a major conflict between them (you still need the AVE - ICFH Addon located [here](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/34100?tab=files))*
  
**Armor & Clothing Extension** *- fixed some major overwrites*
  
**Immersive Armors & Cloaks of Skyrim** *- fixed some minor overwrites***amidianBorn Content Addon** - *fixed some major overwrites*
  
**Bosmer Armor Pack** - *fixed some major overwrites, the patch should be used with* **Bosmer Armor Pack Fixed and Balanced
  
  
Enchanting Adjustments** - *fixed some major overwrites***aMidianBorn Armor Variants Lite** *- [i]fixed some major overwrites*[/i]**Skyrim Alchemy Fixed***[i] - fixed some major overwrites*[/i]**USSEP***- fixed some minor mini-overwrites***Distinct Elven Armor Variants** - *[i]fixed some major overwrites*[/i]**Ars Metallica** - *adds AVE armors to the smelting lists*
  
**JS Armored Circlets** - *[i]fixed some major overwrites*[/i]
  
**Lawless - A Bandit Overhaul** - *fixes some major overwrites***Skyrim Immersive Creatures** - *fixes some major overwrites*Additionally, new **Mesh and Consistency Fixes** for Armor Variants Expansion are included!
  
  
  
*[i]Without these patches, based on your load order, those mods will overwrite each other which leads to a loss of content.*
  
[/i]
  
*\*New\* The fomod-Installer lets you choose and combine every patch available for Armor Variants Expansion.
  
             Now you can simply install all the patches you need for your personal mod setup!*
  
  
Installation and Load Order:
  
1. [Original Mod](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/34100?tab=description) is needed, feel free to give Darkgondul an endorsement if you like his awesome work!
  
2. Simply install this patch with your favorite mod manager and pick your options in the Installer.
  
3. If you choose the respective patch, the mod it's for will be needed as master.
  
4. Place this patch AFTER its masters. Optimally, you should sort your plugins via **LOOT**!
  
  
*The plugin is super lightweight and flagged as ESPFE. Enjoy!*
  
Patches for **CCOR**, **Improved Closefaced Helmets** and **Cathedral - Armory** can be found on the [original mod page](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/34100?tab=files).
  
A patch for Immersive Sound Compendium can be found [here](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/55157).
  
A patch for Description Framework can be found [here](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/163966).
