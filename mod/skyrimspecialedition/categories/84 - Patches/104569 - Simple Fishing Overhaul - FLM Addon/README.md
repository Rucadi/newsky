# Simple Fishing Overhaul - FLM Addon
- Author: Mura
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/104569


**FEATURES**Full list of added ingredients can be found below, which were chosen following the original mod's own ingredient logic.Spoiler
  

  
* Vanilla: Skeever Tail, Chaurus Eggs, Falmer Ear, Nordic Barnacles and Slaughterfish Scales.
[Saints & Seducers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Saints_%26_Seducers): All new bug ingredients.
  
* [Lepidoptera](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/68669): All new butterfly wing ingredients.
[Hunterborn](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/7900): Eye of Fox, Eye of Hare and Eye of Wolf. All other eyes, including Polished versions, are intentionally omitted due to value and rarity.
  
* [SpiRally's Beautiful Butterflies Enhanced](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/85583): All new butterfly wing ingredients.
[Complete Alchemy and Cooking Overhaul](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/19924): All new pondfish, butterflies, dragonflies and Goat Eye.
  
* [Wyrmstooth](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/45565): Deep Moth Wing
[ESO Imports](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/11729)﻿: Eye of Nightfall Sabre Cat
  
* [CC Goblins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goblins)﻿: Goblin Ear

**INSTALLATION**

  
* Download and install [Simple Fishing Overhaul](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/103440), [FLM](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/74037)﻿ and all their requirements.﻿﻿
Download and install ﻿this with your preferred mod manager.
  

**COMPATIBILITY**
  

  
* If there's anything you want to remove from my list, you can simply open my ini with Notepad and delete the proper items. They're all referred to by their EditorID
If you want me to add other ingredients in the list that I personally think it would make sense, you can request it in the comments and I may consider it.
