**Ascension 2**

  
  
 Ascension is a Destruction Perk Addon using Custom Skills Framework. Ascension was made to support non vanilla damage type spells from my spell packs.
  
  
**Requirements**
  
  
Ascension requires the most recent version of [Custom Skills Framework](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/41780).
  
  
**Perks**
  
  
**Destruction**
  
  
Spoiler
  

  
* **Path to Ascension:** Your Magicka Regeneration is inceased by 25%

  

  
* **Raw Power (30/70):** Unaspected spells deal 25/50% more damage.
**Aspection (60):** Unaspected spells trigger additional effects depending on their damage type.
  
* **Execution (80/100):** Unaspected spells deal 50/100% extra damage to targets who fall below half Health.

  

  
* **Forbidden Power (40/90):** Destruction spells deal 25/50% more damage but your Health is decreased by 50/100 points.

  

  
* **Magic Mantle (40/90):** You have 10/20% Magic Resistance while under the effect of a cloak spell.
**Destructive Barrier (60):** Cloak spells last twice as long when dual cast.
  
  
**Notes**
  
** Unaspected spells are spells with a special keyword. Not every spell will benefit from those perks.
Use the Acension power to acess the perk tree.
  
* Ascension uses normal perk points.
Ascension uses destruction skill level.*

**Mod List**
  
  
Spoiler**Abyss**
  
  

  
* **Shadow Anguish** - Shadow spells reduce movement speed by 15% and 10% additional magic resistance for 5 seconds.

  
**Natura**
  
  

  
* **Stone Shred** - Stone spells reduce armor rating by 150 for 5 seconds against targets below 50% Health.
**Deep Waters** - Water spells reduce Magicka and Stamina by 300 for 10 seconds against targets below 50% Health.
  
* **Wind Force** - Wind spells have a 15% chance to blow targets below 50% Health away. Chance is lower with concentration spells.

  
**Bloodmoon**
  
  

  
* **Haemorrhage** - Blood spells deal additional damage overtime.

  
**Desecration**
  
  

  
* **Necropolis** - When your Necrotic Spells hit an enemy, it has a chance to spawn a Flaming Necrotic Familiar. Increases the duration of the weapon damage reduction from Necrotic Spells to 10 seconds.

  
  
**Arcane**
  
  

  
* **Arcane Corruption** - Arcane spells reduce magic resistance by 15% and magicka regeneration by 100% for 5 seconds.

  
**Notes**
  
** Also shown in the active magic effects.*
  
  
**Compatibility**
  
  
Ascension requires the most recent version of [Custom Skills Framework](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/41780).
  
  
Do not use Ascension together with my other Ascension mod, they do the same thing.
  
  
For other spell mods that dont use vanilla keywords:
  
Ascension comes with an injected keyword for unaspected spell damage. This needs to be added to spells if they should get affected by the perks above.
