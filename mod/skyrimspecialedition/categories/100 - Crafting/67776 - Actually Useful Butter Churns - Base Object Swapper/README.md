**Description**
  

  
* Ultra low effort mod to turn useless Hearthfire [Butter Churns](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kitchen)﻿ into something useful.
Before:
  
[list]
  
* Butter Churn is some sort of plant that magically grows [Butter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Food#Butter).
Butter Churn grows a pitiful 1 stick of [Butter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Food#Butter) every 10 days. 2 if you have the [Green Thumb](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy#Skill_Perks) perk.
  
  
* Butter Churn doesn't grow butter if you're in its presence. You need to leave your house for the [Butter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Food#Butter) to respawn.

[\*]After:
  

  
* Use the new Butter Churns to turn [Jugs of Milk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Food#Jug_of_Milk)﻿ into sticks of [Butter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Food#Butter)﻿.

[\*]Installer lets you choose between 2 options:
  

  
* **No Plugin** (ESL, ESM, ESP) if [Farming Creation](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Farming)﻿ is installed.
ESL if [Farming Creation](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Farming)﻿ is not installed.
  

[\*]Uses [Base Object Swapper](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/60805).
  

* Works on every Butter Churn, even from mods.
No conflicts.
