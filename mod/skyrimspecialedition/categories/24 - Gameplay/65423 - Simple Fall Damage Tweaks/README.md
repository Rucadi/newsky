# Simple Fall Damage Tweaks
- Author: Ixion XVII
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/65423
**- Minimum Distance where fall damage is applied is lowered by about 35%
  
- Since damage is calculated for how much distance the player falls minus the minimum distance, this will increase fall damage
  
  
- NPC's now take the same fall damage as the player (= overall slightly reduced)**
