# The Dragon Eggs
- Author: Ryokoh
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/158176


> **This mod adds and places a drop rate on the dragon list,
>   
> when a dragon dies, there will be a chance to get an egg and a crystallized dragon soul.****Each dragon has its own egg, there are six types of eggs, fire, frost, blood, ancient, revered and legendary.
>   
> In this same way, each dragon will also have the possibility of giving you its own soul in its crystallized form.
>   
>
