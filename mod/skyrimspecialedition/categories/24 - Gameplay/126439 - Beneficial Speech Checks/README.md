# Beneficial Speech Checks
- Author: dabiggieboss - shazdeh2
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/126439


Made at the suggestion of [Dabiggieboss](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/5843790)ï»¿ ([Youtube](https://www.youtube.com/@biggie_boss)), this mod grants you a small buff whenever you pass persuasion (5% reduced shout cooldown) or intimidation checks (5% stronger shouts). Also if you have Experience mod installed, it grants a small amount of XP. Buff is applied after you leave the dialog and lasts 10 minutes.
  
  
**Compatibility**
  
This mod does not touch any vanilla records, should be compatible with everything. ESP-FE.
