# Elven Supremacy - Currency Swapper for Thalmor
- Author: Tate Taylor
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/129717

[/size]

> *﻿"**Behold the future! Behold the Thalmor!"*
>   
> *— Thalmor Justiciar*

  
The Aldmeri Dominion is a new political power led by the Thalmor, who believe that elven supremacy is the only acceptable future for Tamriel. They have rejected the use of the septim and have begun trading in sancar, a new gold coin of their own design.
  
  
Since the signing of the White-Gold Concordat, many Thalmor agents have been dispatched to Skyrim. As a result, sancar can occasionally be seen in areas where Thalmor presence is strong. While the average merchant will likely refuse elven coins, the Khajiit caravans will happily transact in this currency, as it is what they use back home in Elsweyr.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/PierreDespereaux/PierreDespereaux/master/assets/images/banners/Features.png)

  

  
* Distributes a new fully functional Aldmeri Dominion currency to Thalmor characters and locations: the **sancar**!
Khajiit Caravan merchants will now be able to transact with sancar.
  
* Replaces all instances of septims in Thalmor locations with sancar.
Adjusts prices to simulate the sancar's value relative to the septim.
