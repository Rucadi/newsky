[left]**Overview**
  
Vitrium offers an immersive, animated and well balanced pack of spells, tools and consumables.[/left][left]Compliments Skyrim's gameplay loop and adds preparation gameplay. Animations work **only in 3rd person and are player exclusive**

Requirements / Installation /**Recommendations
  
  
Requirements[/size]

  
* [Nemesis Unlimited Behavior Engine](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/60033)

**Recommendations**
  

  
* [True Directional Movement](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/51614?tab=description) - allows you rotate camera and player freely (I recommend using "fixed spine" setting)
[Simple Dual Sheath](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/50049)**+** [Immersive Equipment Displays](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/62001) see your equipment while they are unequipped
  
* [TrueHUD](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/62775) - see damage you deal and take + a lot of other great features
[IED - Weapons Repositioner](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/65148?tab=description) - Moves equipment to the correct position when backpacks or cloaks are equipped. 
  
* [Scrambled Bugs](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/43532) Set "applyMultipleSpells" to True in "ScrambledBugs.json" to fix various conflicts. Ordinator users should look into [Ordinator- Scrambled Bugs Compatibility](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/51965) as well.﻿

**Installing**
  

  
* Install with a mod manager of your choice and sort load order if needed
Tick Vitrium in Nemesis, update the engine and run Nemesis
  

  
**Uninstalling**
  

  
* Uninstalling mid-playthrough is not recommended, but should not cause any issues if you do so.
Uninstall with your mod manager and run Nemesis afterwards
  

**Compatibility**
  

  
* ESL-flagged esp
Mod uses light cloak scripts to operate. Nothing is distributed to NPCs for good script performance.
  
* Nemesis is not compatible with FNIS

**Versioning**
  
Major.Minor.Patch
  

  
* Major = major changes to scripting, animations or overall mod.
Minor = compatibility updates, mesh changes or texture change etc.
  
* Patch= small errors, hot fixes etc.

  
**Issues**

  
* Some effects return player to first person (if they were to begin with) after the effect has worn off and not when animation finishes.
Vibrant weapons effects don't work with consumables.

 
**Crafting**

![](https://i.imgur.com/dcuYOlV.gif)

  
While you have crafters backpack equipped a lesser power comes available in the magic menu which allows you to craft on the go.
  
You can craft all of the consumables + a few extra items like lockpicks, roll of paper and arrows (perk requirements are on for arrows)
  
  
**Soul Gem Crafting**

![](https://imgur.com/XJcipaz.gif)

  
After requiring and learning the soul gem transfusion spell, you get a lesser power similar to crafting available in your magic menu.
  
With it you can craft soul gems to one tier higher at the price of two.
  
This also makes use of soul gem fragments that are otherwise unused by the game.
  
You can split soul gems to fragments or combine them to make a petty soul gem.
  
  
**Scroll Crafting**
  

![](https://imgur.com/Pw9LBUB.gif)

  
After acquiring the Scroll Crafting book player can use the lesser power to create various scrolls. 
  
Every scroll requires 1...5 soul gem fragments and a roll of paper. 
  
The of fragment cost is based on the value of the scroll. Fragments can be aquired by splitting soul gems up with Soul Gem Transfusion spell. [![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/62276/62276-1647165141-1986236971.png)](https://elderscrolls.fandom.com/wiki/Roll_of_Paper)﻿Roll of paper can be made with crate or backpack crafting, requires 1 tundra cotton.
  
  
**Spectral Pack**

![](https://i.imgur.com/vbYzSL5.gif)

  
After acquiring the Spectral Pack horn, player can use the horn to summon the Spectral Pack at pointed location for one hour.﻿
