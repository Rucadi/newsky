**Description**
  

  
* Tell summons to wait, follow, dismiss, or trade
Dress up reanimated zombies and summons like followers
  
* Summons respond back to you if they have vanilla NPC voice types
Can technically take weapons from summons for free or lose gear forever when summons die, so be careful when trading with spectral or daedric summons
  
* Minions will sneak together with you
Integrated with [Swiftly Order Summons](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/63259)﻿ 1.6.0 (pick the [option](https://www.nexusmods.com/Core/Libs/Common/Widgets/DownloadPopUp?id=280452&nmm=1&game_id=1704)﻿ in Swiftly Order Summons [files section](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/63259?tab=files))
