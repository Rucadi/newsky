# Survival Mode Tweaks
- Author: Ixion XVII
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/65425
**- Freezing Water damage from 5/sec to 2/sec
  
  
- Exhaustion Rate reduced by 5%
  
  
- Very Small Food Hunger bonus from 2 to 10
  
- Small Food Hunger bonus from 18 to 50
  
- Medium/Large bonus is still at 220/380
  
- Removed the attack speed penalty from Hunger (caused a lot of compatibility issues and is overall an annoying mechanic)
