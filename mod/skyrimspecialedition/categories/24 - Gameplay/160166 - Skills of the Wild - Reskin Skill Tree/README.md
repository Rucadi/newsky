# Skills of the Wild - Reskin Skill Tree
- Author: Fernvud
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/160166


My take on the skill trees of the wonderful mod, Skills of the Wild - New Campfire Survival Skill Trees. The original style is a bit awkward, but in my mind, the Dovahkiin is recording or sketching something useful for survival, so I based this reskin on that idea.
  
  
Attention! I am not the author of the mod, I just changed its appearance. All credit goes to the original mod's author - [jayserpa](https://next.nexusmods.com/profile/jayserpa?gameId=1704) original mod - [Skills of the Wild - New Campfire Survival Skill Trees](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/37693)
