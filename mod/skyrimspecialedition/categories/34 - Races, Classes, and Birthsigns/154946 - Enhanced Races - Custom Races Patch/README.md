# Enhanced Races - Custom Races Patch
- Author: PuddingFace
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/154946


Patches for some custom races mods to buff them similar to how I buff the Vanilla races in my Enhanced Races Mod. More patches might come in the future. Many Thanks to [Daedaddy](https://next.nexusmods.com/profile/daedaddy) for the ideas.
  
  
[**Lost Races**](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/69467)

Shock Resistance       50% Shock Resistance
  
  
Snow Elf

Snowborn                 75 extra Health and 25 extra Magicka
  
Snow Elf Blood         25% Magicka and Stamina Regen

Override Command    Command Automatons
  
  
Tsaeci
  
Poison Immunity         Tsaeci have 100% poison resistance.
  
Fast Strike                   10% Faster Striking.
  
Poisoner                      Tsaeci make powerful poisons and poisons applied to weapons last longer.
  
Snakeblood                 50 Stamina plus 50% Stamina Regen
  
Poison Spit                  (Lesser Power)Spit a Poison from your mouth to damage your opponent.
  
Dragon Slayer             25% increased Melee Damage against Dragons. 25% Melee Damage Resistance against Dragons.
