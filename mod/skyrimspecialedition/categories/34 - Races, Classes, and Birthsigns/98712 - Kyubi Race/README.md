*[font=Arial]![](https://imgvb.com/images/2023/11/29/78342b2b331d9e01c536f52c1198d3d2.png)﻿﻿﻿
  
  
[/font]**Kyubi Race is a custom race with half-fox, half-human details. It was inspired around the great mod made by hidemaro: [Inari Race](https://www.nexusmods.com/oblivion/mods/43047)﻿*
  
  
***As of 1.7, the ears become those made by Dint999***
  
  
> [font=Lucida Sans Unicode]*Thank you very much [Dint999](https://www.patreon.com/dint999)﻿ for giving me permission to use your assets, please leave your endorsements!*[/font]

  
  
*During this mod, I was not the only one with similar ideas when it came to wanting to make a mod on this theme, but when I tried each one that I came across, I felt that none of them convinced me, so on account I decided to make this mod from scratch myself, and take that idea into my head, which at first I thought would be stupid, since it was my first time making a mod.**[font=Arial]
  
  
[/font]**But among so many setbacks, details and above all, support from the community, is how this mod came to light.*[font=Arial]﻿

* *Support for both men and women*
*Vampire and Werewolf compatibility*
  
* *It uses your default body, so it is both CBBE, UNP, 3BA, BHUNP and TBD compatible*
*Fully compatible with mods that add additions to races (in some exceptions it will only need a patch)*
  
* *Its body is based on that of a Breton, so it is compatible with any preset*
*Mods that add shaders to the character (Soaking wet, Dirt and Blood, R.A.S.S, etc) fully compatible.*
  
* *Ears and tails adaptable to hair color*
*Option for glow ears*
  
* *Option for color and glow tips*
*High Poly Head and Alternate High Poly Head Support*
  
* *Fully customizable from 1 to 3, 5, 9 and Big tails*

1. *[RaceMenu](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/19080): Required to select the head parts*
*[FSMP - Faster HDT-SMP](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/57339): Required for the physics of the ears and tail*
  
2. *[Race Compatibility SKSE](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/122592)﻿: Required for Skyrim to be able to read custom races*
*[Backported Extended ESL Support](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/106441)﻿: Only if you have a version below 1.6.1130*
  
3. *[Hair Colour Sync NG](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/156414)﻿: Only if you have the discoloration bug on the ears and tails*
*And of course, all the requirements of each previously mentioned mod*

***Why are my ears and tails on the ground?***
  
  

  
* [color=#f4f4f4]*Check the requirements carefully, surely you need to install something related to SMP.*
  
[/color]
  
***Why does it remove the ears and tail when using a preset?***
  
  

  
* *The preset was made with a face or body that was not made with Kyubi Race, just go to the Face part section, and put the KyubiRace/KyubiRaceHighPoly/KyubiRaceAlternateHighPoly parts.*

  
***Add any spell, racial ability or unique perk?***
  
  

  
* *I chose not to do it this way, as many mods that add racial abilities and spells do a better job than I would, so I opted if you want new spells better download the ones of your choice. Thus, by the way, it favors compatibility with these.*

***Compatible with SE/AE/VR?***
  
  

  
* *Yes.*
