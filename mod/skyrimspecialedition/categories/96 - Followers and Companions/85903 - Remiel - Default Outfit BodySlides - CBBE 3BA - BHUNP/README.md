# Remiel - Default Outfit BodySlides - CBBE 3BA - BHUNP
- Author: megaman2k - SunJeong
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/85903


[Remiel](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/51874), everyone's favorite *dwemechanic*, comes with the gorgeous [Practical Pirate](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/45244) outfit by [QuarantineCouture](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/94213673).
  
  
The standalone version of the outfit comes in the vanilla body shape by default, and no CBBE- or UNP-family BodySlides are provided. This means Remiel may look odd switching from her default outfit to one with a custom BodySlide.
  
  
This mod provides those missing BodySlides, updated to point to the correct mesh/texture locations used by Remiel's default outfit.
  
  
**INSTALLATION**
  
  

  
1. Download, install, and enable **either** the CBBE 3BA version **or** the BHUNP version.
Open up BodySlide and look for the outfits (top-most dropdown) starting with [font=Courier New][Remiel][/font].
  
2. Choose your desired preset (dropdown just below step 2), or select [font=Courier New]- Zeroed Sliders -[/font] if using OBody or AutoBody.
Build.
  
3. Repeat for the gauntlets, boots, and outfit.
