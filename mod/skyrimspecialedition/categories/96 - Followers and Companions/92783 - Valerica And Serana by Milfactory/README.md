Have you ever wondered, why would you drag yourself back to Soul Cairn after finishing the Dawnguard questline just to tell Valerica to go back to Skyrim?
  
Did you really have any motivation to do so? Well now you do. 
  
  
This mod is a complete appearance replacer for Serana and Valerica. Milfactory standards... for immersion reason, of course. This mod does not change any character behavior, combat ai, or dialogue for maximum compatibility with other mods that improve them from gameplay perspective.
  
  
**Some additional info:**
  

  
* They have glowing eye mesh, so I highly suggest you do not cure Serana
Serana has meshes support for Hood with hair 
  
* Valerica has her weight bumped up to 100 for.... maximum milfiness
Serana has her weight increased to 50 to maintain the average look, not too curvy nor to skinny.
  
* All screenshots are taken using BHUNP version.

  
  
  

**Please update the Asset hub to version 1.4 or newer. 
  
Otherwise you will have missing body texture.**
