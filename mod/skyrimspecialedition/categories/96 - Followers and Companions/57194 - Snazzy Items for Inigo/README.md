[font=Times New Roman]Based on several requests I've broken out my custom models/textures for Inigo's unique items from my mod [Legacy of the Dragonborn - Followers Patch (Inigo-Lucien-Auri-Kaidan-M'rissi)﻿](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/40816) for those of you that are not playing with Legacy of the Dragonborn.
