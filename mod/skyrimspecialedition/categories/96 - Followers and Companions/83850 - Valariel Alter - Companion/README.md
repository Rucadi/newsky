In an alternate Nirn, Valariel made an mistake and almost got consumed and possessed by a Daedra. She managed to avoid the twisted fate,
  
but it still ended up changing her forever. Valariel Alter is the dark alternative version of [Valariel - The Auntmer](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/82866). It started out as a fun little project
  
in our discord server, but turns out people really like her. So I decided to release her publicly.
  
  
As a Milfactory custom follower, she requires the[Milfactory Asset Hub 1.2 or newer](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/80819) to work.
  
  
  
Some additional info:
  
  

  
* She is a High Elf
She is essential.
  
* She is marriageable
She has 100 weight.
  
* She is specialized in magic, and can use conjuration, destruction, restoration and alteration up to adept level.
She is at Frostfruit Inn - Rorikstead
  
* All screenshots were taken on BHUNP version.
This mod is compatible with both BHUNP and 3BA version of the [Asset Hub](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/80819)﻿.
  
* Be nice to her.

Q: I got texture mismatch on their body. How to fix that?
  
A: Since the asset hub uses whatever body you have installed in your game, you need to pick the correct version of the Asset Hub
  
  
Q: Can I use her alongside with the original Valariel?
  
A: Yes.
  
  
Q: It overwrite something from the original mod, is it ok?
  
A: No it doesn't matter. They share the same asset minus the eye color, head nif and facetint. Those are seperated.
