[/font]**As beautiful as she is destructive, Valariel is a powerful mage. She and her sister have recently become residents at Solitude city.
  
At some point during her life, she clashed and befriended with a seclusive mage named Delariel.
  
Ever since, they've been holding on a friendly-rivalry.
  
She is currently assisting the Jarl of Solitude at the Blue Palace.
  
  
Valariel is both a friend and rival of [Delariel](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/82715) and the younger sister of [Valaria](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/80460) - Who will be available soon as companion as wel.
  
As a Milfactory custom follower, she requires the[Milfactory Asset Hub 1.2 or newer](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/80819) to work.
  
  
  
Some additional info:
  
  

  
* She is a High Elf
She is essential.
  
* She is marriageable
She has 100 weight.
  
* She is specialized in magic, and can use conjuration, destruction, restoration and alteration up to adept level.
She is at Blue Palace - Solitude
  
* All screenshots were taken on BHUNP version.
Her outfit are from vanilla assets, if you have mod to alternate them, she will have whatever you installed.
  
* This mod is compatible with both BHUNP and 3BA version of the [Asset Hub](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/80819)﻿.
Be nice to her.
