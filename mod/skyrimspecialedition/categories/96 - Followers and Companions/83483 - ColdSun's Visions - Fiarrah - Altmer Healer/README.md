![](https://i.imgur.com/FT1GyM2.png)
  

  
* [font=Verdana][Reverie Skin](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/64314)﻿ 1.11+ by [Novelyst](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/100685828)﻿[/font].
New Visions series uses your installed body. Compatible with 3BA, BHUNP, CBBE, TBD, and UNP bodies.
  
* Visions mod architecture for smaller footprint and better performance. Works with obody and wetfunction.
Summon Scroll to summon her to your side at will
  
* Custom perks

  
![](https://i.imgur.com/F3jxBkY.png)Fiarrah is visiting skyrim from the Summerset Isles. She is here searching for The Chantry of Auri-El to better commune with her deity. She is an expert in the lore of Snow Elves and has even learned some of their unique magic.
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/FjXErXn.png)**You can find her at the Clearpine Pond west of Solitude.**
  
**Features:** Essential, won't break stealth, can be married
  
**Skills**
  
Restoration, Light Armor, Alteration, Destruction
  
  
[color=#f97316][size=3]**Special Spells**
  
**Snow Elf Healing Magic:** Area of effect healing magic capable of healing an entire party.
