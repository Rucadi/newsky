**[size=6]-Souri-**
  
[/size]A standalone unarmed/conjuration companion suitable for your mid/late-game adventures. **Souri** is a khajiit and wood elf hybrid who came from a parallel dimension in search of **Daedric Artifacts**. She doesn't like sharing her past much, therefore it's still a big mystery. **Souri** is very intelligent and hardy, but lacks in damage, making her a very strong tank/summoner. Her main weapon is a **Daedric Relic** she claimed from her dimension, which she uses as claws. Her real story goes far beyond the patterns stablished in Tamrielic magic, as she actually comes from a parallel dimension in where the race Lermer [font=Arial](Created to the initial purpose of winning wars; “Murdering People”, from the Aldmeri language, created from the breeding of khajiit and bosmer)[/font] dominates the battlefields because of its racial bonuses. **I do plan on making a quest mod involving her story in the future.
  
  
![](https://iili.io/JX6MKVR.png)﻿﻿**
  
  
**Location:**Statue of Meridia, Mount Kilkreath.
  
![](https://iili.io/JX6MBKN.png)﻿﻿﻿
  
This time, I'll only tell you that the satchel with her bikini is very near the place she's in, on some rocks. Once you find it, you'll have to have her equip it by removing all items with the console or with a follower management mod that allows outfit editing.
  
 Good hunting!
  
  
**-Features﻿****-**[size=6][/size]
  
Own custom race, with increases in speed and unarmed damage
  
Custom skin textures based on existing ones
  
Custom "weapon" and spell
  
Unique body shape, with edited clothing to fit her
  
A mix of Stella Mithril's and Merta Black Rose's armor, both by DeserterX
  
FemaleCondescending voice (custom voice in planning)
  
Though she levels with the player, might seem overpowered in early and mig-gameBikini included, find it if you can
