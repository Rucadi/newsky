# Julia - Standalone High Poly Imperial Follower ESL
- Author: TREBoy
- Game: skyrimspecialedition

[![](https://iili.io/21tAMue.png)](https://discord.gg/K5e9UR5K)﻿﻿   [![](https://iili.io/2bwIQPs.png)](https://patreon.com/TREBoy)
  
![](https://iili.io/JQfD7z7.png)﻿
  
A standalone **Spellblade** companion suitable for your mid/late-game adventures. **Julia** is a very cautious and extremely skilled **Imperial**, threading ever so lightly wherever her path might lead her and always seeking to learn ways to improve her arsenal. Her life has never been easy, being sent to **Honorhall Orphanage** after losing both her parents, and struggling to make any friends. In her teens, **Julia** ran away from the orphanage and started living in the shadows of **Riften**, being able to live marginally through stealing from others. One day, she tried to pull one of her tricks on a certain **Dark Elf**, **Karliah**, who saw right through her. **Karliah** ended up presenting **Julia** to the **Thieves Guild**, and both ended up becoming very close friends. Some time after, during her adulthood, **Julia** was invited by **Karliah** and her lover, **Gallus Desidenius**, to be the third member of the **Nightingales**, which she promptly rejected due to the jealousy she felt towards **Karliah**'s relationship. Her place was later filled in by **Mercer Frey**. After **Karliah**'s exile, **Julia** was one of the very few members of the **Thieves Guild** who believed **Karliah**, if not the only one, and was extremely skeptical of **Mercer**. She's been able to talk to **Karliah** once briefly, where she told **Julia** what had happened and to not try to find her, immediately using one of the **Nightingales**' blessings to completely disappear. Since then, **Julia**'s been waiting for another chance to talk to her, and help her get her revenge, while holding deep hatred towards **Mercer Frey**.
  
**If Julia eventually returns to the Thieves Guild while Mercer Frey is still there, she might no be able to control her rage.
  
  
Julia** is very skilled in combat, making her a great **DPS**. Her main weapons are the **Eclipse**, a very powerful weapon forged and enchanted by herself with some help from **Balimund** and **Wylandriah**, and a strong destruction spell called the **Rosé Thunder Cloak**, which she's also developed on her own.

Custom weapon
  
Custom armor
  
Custom spell
  
Custom skin textures
  
ESPFE plugin, so it doesn't take a space in your load order
  
Custom body shape
  
Bodyslide sets for her body and armor
  
FemaleYoungEager voice
  
Though she levels with the player, might seem overpowered in early and mid game
