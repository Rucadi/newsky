* Cute, romance-able elf follower with lore-friendly ESO-style bosmer features - dark schlera, large slanted eyes and brows, antlers.
Carefully sculpted heads using the Charmers of the Reach meshes to look good under various angles and lighting.
  
* Uses your teeth mesh and textures instead of the COtR Teeth.
No unexpected nudity. Auri will use your default body mesh/preset.
