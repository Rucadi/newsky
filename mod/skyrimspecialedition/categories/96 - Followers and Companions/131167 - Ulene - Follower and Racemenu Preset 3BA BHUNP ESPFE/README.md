**[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]*Ulene*[/font]**
  
*Never following the rules and listening to her own instincts, an elf with unique characteristics nothing seen before in the Skyrim lands, for some she is like a exotic beast with a big prize on her head, after being countless times captive and brake her chains... she now looks to start over and get more powerful while looking for revenge on those who caused her pained past.*

  
Race: Imperial
  
She has more perks in Heavy armor but can run light too, daggers dual wielding and some frost spells, perks with custom combat and class
  
Dont affect stealth, marriageable and essential
  
She starts at level 1 and grows with you
  
You can find her in Markarth Hall of the Dead
