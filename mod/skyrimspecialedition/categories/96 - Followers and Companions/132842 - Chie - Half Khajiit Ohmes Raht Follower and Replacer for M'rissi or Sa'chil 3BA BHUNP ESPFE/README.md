Chie Follower 
  
Perks in 1H, Swords and Bow, Dual wield, Custom Class and Combat Style
  
Essential, Doesn't trigger traps, Marriageable
  
She can be found in Ivarstead's farm/mill area.
  
  
M'rissi and Sa'chil Replacers
  
Only thing that changes is the appearance, they will maintain the same perks, stats, clothes and etc.
