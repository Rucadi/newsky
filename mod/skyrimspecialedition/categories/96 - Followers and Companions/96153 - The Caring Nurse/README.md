# The Caring Nurse
- Author: shadowman2777
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/96153


One of my last mods with vanilla voices. Many months in the making. Enjoy.
  
  
-338 lines of dialogue
  
-122 idles
  
-149 lines of problems to request help from the Nurse.
  
-You can ask her for a shoulder or leg massage, and for her to wash you.
  
-She can heal you from diseases and restore your health.
  
  
-Run FNIS
  
-To hire the Nurse, wear any ring. It works on any NPC with femaleyoungeager voice. You can hire multiple Nurses if you wish.
  
-To fire a Nurse, wear the dark brotherhood outfit.
