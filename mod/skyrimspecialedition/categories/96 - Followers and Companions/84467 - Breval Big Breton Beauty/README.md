Breval was once a happy housewife. But her happier days soon ended when her late husband suddently passed away in middle of night due to fatal exhaustion 
  
of unknown cause. After spending years mourning her husband, she decided to spend her life devoted to Mara. With the hope of one day the Goddess shall bless her with love and a family again.
  
  
Breval is not a particulary strong fighting companion. She has hold her own with alteration magic and ward to negate any harm come to her.
  
But as a popular and widely admired lady, whenever caught in trouble, she can summon her local s̶i̶m̶p̶s̶ supporters to help her in combat.
  
  
Spoiler
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/OqDzOdk.png)
  
  
  
  
Some additional info:
  
  

  
* She is a Breton.
She is essential and is marriageable
  
* She has 100 weight.
She has some basic magic, mostly restoration and alteration.
  
* She has an unique spell to call for her supporters. They are generated randomly and scale up to lv25.
She is at Temple of Mara - Riften.
  
* All screenshots were taken on BHUNP version.
This mod is compatible with both BHUNP and 3BA version of the [Asset Hub](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/80819)﻿.
  
* Be very very nice to her.

  

**Please update the Asset hub to version 1.3 or newer. 
  
Otherwise you will have missing body texture.**
