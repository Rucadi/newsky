# Ghenara - A Follower Mod
- Author: ENR
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/132989


![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/60464/60464-1730584754-493392048.png)﻿﻿﻿
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/60464/60464-1729896009-281968371.png)
  
*A mercenary for hire, she's looking to make use of her talent with firearms in the service of a worthy cause.
  
  
From a young age, her abilities were honed as one of the few women selected to become a royal bodyguard.
  
She grew close to her young lord, a youth from a little known clan.
  
 Taking on a guiding role, she intended to mould the youthinto a cautious and just leader...
  
and so it was with her aid,that the young lord grew to be wise and strong.
  
  
Yet, none too many appreciate an upstart...
  
The other regional lords, threatened by this rising power, conspired against this youthful leader
  
who would soon meet an end at the tip of a dagger thrust in jealousy and fear.
  
  
Having failed to protect her lord, Ghenara chose exile.
  
  
Drifting from port to port, lending her protection to wayfaring ships for lodging and board...
  
all in search of a worthy lord.***Location:***You'll find her hanging out at the* ***Winking Skeever*** *at night or
  
patrolling the **Solitude docks** during the morning.*
  
**Stats:***She is a ranged fighter wielding a* ***slow-loading musket****.
  
In close quarters she's more likely to back away and use her* ***pistol and machete*** *to keep her distance.
  
Her weapons and Ammo can be crafted.
  
  
File is espfe.
  
Uses the FemaleCommander Voice type.*
