This mod adds Sieglinde "Sswaye's mom" into the game as a companion. She is very.... extremely.... ultimately the most requested preset of Milfactory to be turned into a follower. And now here she is. 
  
  
Sieglinde is a basic two-handed and heavy armor warrior, armed with a steel battleaxe and ready to join your adventure at Moorside Inn - Morthal. 
  
As the first Milfactory companion, this mod requires[Milfactory Asset Hub](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/80819) to work.
  
  
  
**Some additional info:**
  

  
* She is essential.
She is marriageable
  
* She has 100 weight.
She is specialized in two-handed weapons and heavy armor.
  
* She is at Moorside Inn - Morthal.
All screenshots were taken on BHUNP version.
  
* This mod is compatible with both BHUNP and 3BA version of the [Asset Hub](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/80819)﻿.
Be nice to her.
