The three sisters - Gariel, Kariel, Nariel - are rumored to come into Skyrim through an other-worldy rift. Nobody knows exactly where are they from, or what kind of elf are they, and yet they usually passed as Altmer. The people of Skyrim love them for their charming nature and kindness. With the land in turmoil with dragons, vampire and other threats,  the elven sisters are always willing to aid the dragonborn on their journey, should they seek out their help and companionship. 
  
  
**Some additional info:****Nariel** is the youngest, she can heal the player and launch AoE attack on the enemies. 
  
**She is staying in Whiterun - Temple of Kynareth**
  
She has option to toggle between regular and SMP hair.
  
Her custom voice type is Lilia from Simply Open Source Voice Pack
  
Her optional outfit addon is [Queen Marika Dress](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/83261) | [BHUNPv3 Conversion](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/83597) | [BHUNPv4 Conversion](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/138126)
  
  
**Gariel** is the middle one. She charm the enemies into Frenzy state with her magical bow.
  
**She is staying in Kynesgrove - Braidwood Inn**
  
She has unique tanline on her body.
  
Her custom voice type is Allison from Simply Open Source Voice Pack
  
Her optional outfit addon is [Elle - Devil Parade](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/86103)**Kariel** is the eldest. She can summon her skeleton army to aid her during combat as well as raise undead to fight.
  
**She is staying in Whiterun - Hall of the Dead**
  
She is a certified cougar :^)
  
Her custom voice type is Aria from Simply Open Source Voice Pack
  
Her optional outfit addon is [Priestess of Time BHUNP and 3BA](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/141486)
  
﻿
