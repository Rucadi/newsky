**[size=6]-Kari-**
  
[/size]A standalone unarmed/Shout companion suitable for your mid/late-game adventures. **Kari** is a khajiit and wood elf hybrid who came from a parallel dimension to seek for aid in her world's affairs. She is her dimension's current **Dragonborn**, and her feats are endless. While very fierce in combat, she has a very serene tone to her voice, which her enemies fear most about her. Her feelings are very hard to comprehend, since she doesn't usually show any emotion, and people end up think she's just a very cold person. She loves Deathbells. **Kari** is very agile and resilient, with a huge arsenal of **Shouts** at her disposal, making her a very strong tank/fighter. Her main weapon are her **Dragonbone Swords** attached to her gauntlets. Her real story goes far beyond the patterns stablished in Tamrielic magic, as she actually comes from a parallel dimension in where the race Lermer [font=Arial](Created to the initial purpose of winning wars; “Murdering People”, from the Aldmeri language, created from the breeding of khajiit and bosmer)[/font] dominates the battlefields because of its racial bonuses. **I do plan on making a quest mod involving her story in the future.
