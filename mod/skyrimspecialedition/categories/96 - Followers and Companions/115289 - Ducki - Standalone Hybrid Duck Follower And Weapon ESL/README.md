﻿[size=6]**- Ducki -**
  
[/size]A standalone 2-handed companion suitable for your mid/late-game adventures. **Ducki** is a **Hybrid Race** companion filled with an adventurous spirit, who is fascinated with the **Dwemer**. She has always liked exploring **Dwemer Ruins**, ever since she was a little duckling. A little air-headed, **Ducki** claims to be able to see and talk to yellow chubby creatures that resemble ducks, one of which wears an iron horned helmet. **Ducki**is unnaturally strong, making her a great **Bruiser**. She comes equipped with a **Dwarven Battleaxe**, but she seems very eager to find a special **Dwemer Weapon**...
  
  
﻿![](https://iili.io/JjL7rhu.png)﻿﻿
  
﻿**- Dae's Titanblade -**A sword of legend, only rumored to be lost to time. Seemingly, no one knows of it's exact whereabouts. But **Ducki** seems to have found some hints, and maybe she has it *in her inventory somewhere*.
  
  
  
**Location:** Silver-Blood Inn, Markarth.
  
![](https://iili.io/JjLlxCg.png)﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
  
  
**-Features﻿****-**
  
Duck Hybrid follower with proficiency in Heavy Armor and Two-Handed
  
Custom weapon
  
Custom skin textures based on existing ones
  
ESPFE plugin, so it doesn't take a space in your load order
  
Unique body shape, with edited clothing to fit
  
Nisetanaka's wolf and steel armors
  
FemaleYoungEager voice
  
Though she levels with the player, might seem overpowered in early-game
  
  
![](https://iili.io/Jwv2UlV.png)﻿﻿﻿
