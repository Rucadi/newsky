Caryalind Thallery is the son of the Great overseer and the heir to the Aldmeri throne. However, after having enough of seeing the horrid conditions his people inflict on others, he has taken it upon himself to help others instead of staying in his castle. Be prepared for a lot of charisma and flirting with this gorgeous Altmer. Just find your way to a city or town and wait for a courier to give you a strange fancy letter.﻿﻿

  
  
[![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/fbc91436ea332c7b3e392549db07ace5/079b98f6d0263817-79/s2048x3072/a6316beda4c310d6be2eda096f39425dc9792140.png)](https://rabbittwinrithings.tumblr.com/)

An Altmer follower.

A backstory so you can learn more about him. 
  
* Detailed personality and backstory.
Romance Questline.
  
* Matchmaking system to get him with another custom voice follower.
Commentary on the Main Questline!
  

  
Match Making:
  
If you want Caryalind around, but don't want to romance him. You can still get him a sweet BF! Caryalind now can be romantically involved with the custom voice follower, Kaidan. Just click the option that says to get the boys together in dialogue with Caryalind. If you just want them to remain friends, click the opposite option in the dialogue. (You'll know it when you see it.)

FAQ:
  
 Where can I find Caryalind?
  
Caryalind will send you a letter the next time you're in a town or city. This will give you further instructions on where to find him. 
  
  
 I can’t talk to Caryalind the first time I meet him.
  
This is a bug in the game. Go ahead and quick save and reload the game. 
  
  
 Does he react to any quests?
  
At the moment no, but there are future plans to make Cary react to every major questline. 
  
  
 Is he romanceable?
  
Yes he is! Just wear an amulet of mara and ask him what he thinks of it.
  
  
 I found a bug.
  
Oh no! Please let me know about it right away!
