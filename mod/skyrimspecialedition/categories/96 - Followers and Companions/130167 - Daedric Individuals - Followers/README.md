# Daedric Individuals - Followers
- Author: diegorom7
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/130167


New 1.4 main version with updated faces, and new armors for them to wear which are available in **optional files**.
  
  
For BHUNP version, you may want to try the cool E2A series by Naz [diegorom7's Daedric Individuals - Enemy 2 Ally Patch at Skyrim Special Edition Nexus - Mods and Community (nexusmods.com)](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/130434?tab=files), or alternatively use my Default main file
  
  
These followers start at lvl 1 and level up with the player, having their own custom class and combat style, perks, spells, dont affect stealth, marriageable and essential, i hope they are decent enough and not op.
  
  
 
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/raFzQpd.jpeg)

***Rannis***
  
  
*A lost and lonely soul without purpose, this little one found happiness in reading books and got experienced in the magic arts, now she awaits to give herself a chance of overcoming her solitude nature and experience Skyrim adventures like the ones from her books.*
  
Shes a full mage with a decent repertory of spells which includes Destruction (Ice and Shock), Restoration and Conjuration magic schools mostly, can take support role and likes to create her own distance in combat.
  
Race: Breton
  
Location: Windhelm Hall of the Dead
  
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/d1icPtn.jpeg)﻿
  
  
***Alvira****Her path has never been more clear the moment she feels the magicka waving around her fingers to try and manipulate any obstacle that interposes between her next treasure.*Shes an Illusion-One Handed Spellsword with Destruction spells at her disposal, tries to make a more controlled approach mixing up melee and magic.
  
Race: Imperial
  
Location: Solitude Hall of the Dead
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/d1JdXIB.jpeg)
  
  
***Glyseria***
  
  
*By strength or allure its how she maintains her life of fist fights and skooma, but deep down she knows her strong skill can be put to a better use.*
  
  
She specializes in Two Handed and Heavy Armor, in combat she will be very aggresive and take all the risks.
  
Race: Nord
  
Location: Riften Mausoleum
