[font=Georgia]**‧͙⁺˚\*･༓☾   KOEMIA ☽[b][color=#5b0f00]༓**[/color]**[color=#660000]･****[b][color=#cc4125][b][color=#85200c]\***[/color][/b][/color][/b][/color]**˚****⁺****‧͙**[/b][/font]

  
She uses unique combat of vampire bites, greatsword, & dual daggers, switching everytime she finds a new opponent.
  
She rides a massive wolf as her mount, although the wolves have not been so kind to her in the past.. leaving her with bloody scars and a half-opened eye.
  
  
![](https://i.ibb.co/VY3sLnq/koemiakookie6.png)
  
*![](https://i.ibb.co/qNqG9fG/638163722257638431.png)Dynamic* bloodthirst ![](https://i.ibb.co/qNqG9fG/638163722257638431.png)
  
[font=Times New Roman]*Once she has bonded with you, she needs to feed on you to contain her bloodlust, forever.*
  
→ If you do not let her feed on you for 24 hours, she becomes bloodstarved, her eye color changing from green to pink.
  
→ If you don’t let her feed from you in 48 hours, corrupted veins appear on her face, her chance to drink from enemies in battle tripled
  
[/font]
  
![](https://i.ibb.co/5RSHmZF/koemiakookie138.png)
  
  
**[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]Spun from fairytales and magic, she's aware of real day holidays and reacts to nearly everything you do with her own little twist.[/font]
  
[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]An enchanted raven perches on her shoulder as her familiar, animated to keep an eye out for her! Koemia adores werewolves and the undead, telling tales of her travels across all of Tamriel and through time. She'll grow fond of you as her only companion, and will crave to drink your blood. Nothing is more intimate than fangs to skin...[/font]**
  
[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]She will teleport herself back to you if she gets lost, you can summon her anytime and view her on your map, and she will comment on almost everything you do! She is extremely clever and aware, reacting to quests, gifting you lockpicks if you run out, sharing food, hugs, and blood with you, & will feed off of you and her opponents. 
  
[/font]Are you a vampire too? She'll still love to drink your blood!! Vamps welcome~
