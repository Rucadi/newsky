# RDO - FDE Compatibility Patch
- Author: Anbeegod
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/115506


**The problem:**
  
  
RDO **adds many new greetings** to the followers. However, as they have no cooldown, they often **override the new unique greetings** that FDE adds, making **FDE's greetings appear infrequently**.
  
By giving them a cooldown, both RDO and FDE's greetings can **coexist peacefully**.
