Sa'chil is a Dagi-Raht Khajiit spellsword from Hammerfell. [She is found on the cliff next to Reachwind Eyrie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reachwind_Eyrie)﻿. She is nimble, clever, stubborn, optimistic, and a joker.
  
  
  
She prefers to fight like an aggressive spellsword with her fire-spells, but you can always change out her weaponry!
  
  
  
After a quick chat, she joins you on your adventures.

* [font=Lucida Sans Unicode]**Follower-Framework mods**[/font]
    
  [font=Lucida Sans Unicode]
    
  [/font]

[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]Please don't use any heavy follower-frameworks on Sa'chil!
  
She has her own framework, so it will result in her whole brain breaking!
  
  
  
[/font]

  
* [font=Lucida Sans Unicode]**Body mods**[/font]
    
  [font=Lucida Sans Unicode]
    
  [/font]

[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]Sa'chil has her own SFW CBBE model for her Redguard clothing & body.
  
This means that her body will not change with your own mods.
  
If you want to add her to the body overhauls, a 3rd-party-patch is needed as I will not make those
  
patches myself as I want to keep her SFW.
  
Sa'chil's body preset is an optional file. These will work on other vanilla & CBBE-compatible armor/clothing
  
  
  
  
[/font]

  
* [font=Lucida Sans Unicode]**Reachwind Eyrie mods**[/font]
    
  [font=Lucida Sans Unicode]
    
  [/font]

[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]Sa'chil DOES NOT directly edit Reachwind Eyrie, but will disable the vanilla teleport-doors if chosen.
  
Sa'chil's interior of Reachwind Eyrie is copied and does not overwrite anything.
  
  
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/UjhBjT6.png)
  

Install Sa'chil and add-ons with your mod-manager.

* **[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]Where can I find Sa'chil?[/font]**

[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]**Sa'chil is found outside Reachwind Eyrie,** on the cliffside next to the tree.
  
  
  
[/font]

  
* **[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]How do I start her questline?[/font]**

[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]**When you're above relationship level 5**. She will want to talk to you with enthusiasm.
  
  
  
  
[/font]

  
* [font=Lucida Sans Unicode]**What is a Dagi-Raht?**[/font]

[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]**You have different types of Khajiit all over Tamriel!** A Dagi-Raht is a smaller version of the Khajiit common in Skyrim.
  
  
  
  
[/font]

  
* [font=Lucida Sans Unicode]**How do you marry her?**[/font]

[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]**After the "Mending of the Shattered Hearth" Quest**, ask her in "About Us"
  
[/font]

  
* **[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]Is Sa'chil safe to install mid-game?[/font]**

[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]**Yes, but I do not recommend it.**
  
Some minor slipups can happen like commenting on your quests mid-playthrough, even if she shouldn't know any context.
  
I recommend a new savefile, to make her more grounded in your loadorder.
  
  
  
  
[/font]

  
* [font=Lucida Sans Unicode]**Is Sa'chil safe to update mid-game?**[/font]

[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]**Yes, absolutely.** She has no all-time-running scripts, so it is okay!
  
Make sure that Sa'chil is not saying or doing anything while saving your game for the update, just in case a script is running.
  
  
  
  
[/font]

  
* [font=Lucida Sans Unicode]**When does she start commenting on vanilla quests?**[/font]

[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]**Basically during the rough start of the quests.** The vanilla main-quest when you're in Riverwood, the Dragonborn main-quest after the Cultists have died, and so on.
  
[/font]

  
* **[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]Can I learn her more spells?[/font]**

[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]Not yet! **That is planned for one of the big updates.** Feel free to used console-commands if you want her to have more spells right now.
  
  
  
  
  
[/font]

  
* [font=Lucida Sans Unicode]**I can't find her near the cliff!**[/font]

[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]If you have version 1.0.2 or later, **you have a spell in your inventory that makes her move there!** No spell? Sa'chil was not correctly installed.
  
  
  
  
[/font]

  
* [font=Lucida Sans Unicode]**Where is her custom home?**[/font]

[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]It's a custom decorated version of Reachwind Eyrie, **this will be unlocked after she returns from the Wrath of Hammerfell quest.**
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/PnviSB8.png)
  

Sa'chil sometimes bugs out, Skyrim is Skyrim! here's a step-by-step on how to fix her.

**1. Disable & Enable her through console**
  
Still not fixed?
  
  
**2. Use the command "ResetAI"**
  
Still not fixed?
  
  
**3. Use the debug-button in Debug room, you can get there with the command "coc SachilDebug"**
  
Still not fixed?
  
  
  
**4. Try cleaning your loadorder and downloading any patches you might need.**
