# A Proper Thane for Winterhold Evelyna Visual Overhaul
- Author: GolinskiPRH
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/146446


[font=Comic Sans MS]A basic,Hi-poly head visual overhaul of Evelyna, the housecarl added by [A proper thane for winterhold](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/40290).
  
Its not intended to be overly glamorous or anything like that, just an overhaul to make her face look consistent with a heavilly modded game, and to bring her more on par with [Pura](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/123387) (as I like the idea of having two housecarls from a decaying town, sorta like the jarl is overcompensating you to live there lol, and yes, they are compatible with each other, funny thing huh).
  
  
she will use whatever body texture you have installed (or vanilla, if you don't have one, wich you should. I'm using BnP in screenshots )
  
she comes with a non-SMP hair from [vanilla hair remake﻿](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/63979) and glasses from [Glasses Pack Vol.1](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/115653), for, y'know, the hot scholar vibes (her glasses are enchanted with a small extra spell absorption and frost resistance). You cannot disenchant or sell her glasses, its part of her!﻿
