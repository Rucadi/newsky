# Varrick Veil - The Silver Fox
- Author: Axarien
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/81480


Overhauls Varrick's appearance with a high poly version. ESP flagged as light, will not take up a spot in your load order. Standalone with no additional appearance mods required.
  
  
Varrick will look slightly more or less rugged depending on your skin texture install. In my screenshots I'm using Tempered Skins for Males with Elder & Harsh options.
