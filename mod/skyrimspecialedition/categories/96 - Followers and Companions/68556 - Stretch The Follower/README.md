# Stretch The Follower
- Author: Michael Moraes and Canberk
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/68556
This time it is a sad story.
  
  
Stretch, being his nickname, was a late father of one of our community members.
  
With this mod we want to offer another adventure with that great man. That's it really. Nothing else. Inappropriate comments about this mod will be deleted by me.
  
  
Only uses vanilla assets.
  
  
  
Location: Four Shields Tavern / Dragons Bridge Voice: Nord Male Commander
  
Skills/Perks: Barbarian, Skullcrusher, Bone Breaker, Conditioning and Fists of Steel
  
  
He is a 2 handed warrior and a trusting sight to have along.
