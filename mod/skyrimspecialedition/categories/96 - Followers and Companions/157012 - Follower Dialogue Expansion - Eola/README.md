*[size=4]"The strong will always devour the weak, just as the wolf must consume the lamb. It's in our nature."*
  
  
  
**How is Eola envisioned and touched on in this mod:**
  
  
In this mod, Eola is a **dark, zealous follower of Namira** the Goddess of Decay. She believes in **survival of the fittest** to the extreme, that the strong will always feast on or take advantage of the weak. She **sees the worst in people** and believes everything is corruptible with the right temptation.
  
  
She joins you after you **complete the quest "The Taste of Death" in Namira's favor.** As a follower, she is all-rounded but the most effective in one-handed and magic.
  
  
Eola has a **dark, ominous demeanor.**  Beyond her cannibalism, she also has a deeply rooted contempt for the world.
  
  
Will you fix her, or will she make you worse?

**Features:**
  
1. Expands Eola with **233 immersive, lore-friendly lines**, all voiced by high quality AI (see the video for reference)
  
2. **Full quest awareness** for the Companions, Thieves Guild, Dark Brotherhood, vanilla main quest, DLC main quest
  
[(Refer to this list for details)](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1Plt8XaDzvZADjeLU-UYVDTrrUsjGfVPMw8BLaLKBXqo/edit?usp=sharing)
  
3. **Awareness to her surroundings**, such as location and weather
  
4. New **story-driven interactions**
  
5. New **romantic lines** if she is married
  
6. **Completely lore-friendly**, expanding rather than completely rewriting the character
  
7. Immerse into your game world as a **character who lives in it**, rather than taking over the show
  
  
This mod is **100% compatible with any mod**.
