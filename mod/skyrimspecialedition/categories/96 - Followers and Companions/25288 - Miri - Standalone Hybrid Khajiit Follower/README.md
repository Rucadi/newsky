**[size=6]-Miri-**
  
[/size]A standalone unarmed companion suitable for your mid/late-game adventures. Miri is a khajiit and wood elf hybrid who doesn't remember much about her past, the only thing being that she lived in the wild for most of her life. She named herself Miri after the sound she thought was what a cricket made ([font=Georgia]M-ree[/font]). There wasn’t much interaction between her and other human-like creatures, so she doesn’t understand the basics to live in a society. Miri is very agile and strong, but lacks in consistence, making her a very strong assassin. She uses her ebony daggers attached to her gauntlets as her main weapons, which she found while investigating a nordic ruin. Her real story goes far beyond the patterns stablished in Tamrielic magic, as she actually comes from a parallel dimension in where the race Lermer ([font=Georgia]Created to the initial purpose of winning wars; [/font]“Murdering People”, from the Aldmeri language, created from the breeding of khajiit and bosmer) dominates the battlefields because of its racial bonuses. **I do plan on making a quest mod involving her story in the future.**

![](https://iili.io/JX6AYKJ.png)﻿﻿
  
  
If you can't find her, open the console with the Tilde (~) key and type:
  
**player.moveto [color=#b6b6b6]XX01EE07
  
  
[/color]**[color=#b6b6b6]**XX** is the hex code for the mods position in your load order. To find that out[/color], just type
  
**help "miri's cuirass"**and check the first 2 digits of Miri's Cuirass' ID. Replace the 2 X's in the first command with these 2 digits.
  
If you followed the instructions correctly, you should be teleported to Miri's actual position.
