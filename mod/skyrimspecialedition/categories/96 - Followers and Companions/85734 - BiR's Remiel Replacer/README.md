[font=Arial]**ABOUT**
  
  
Let me start by saying that this replacer wouldn't have been possible without the help of **[megaman2k](http://www.nexusmods.com/users/136969333?tab=user+files)**, who I am very grateful for doing all the legwork for getting permissions, making my clumsy replacer a complete standalone one and even creating a FOMOD for everyone's convenience. Be sure to check out their other mods too!

* [font=Arial]Upscaled Eye Textures[/font]
[font=Arial]Glasses[/font]
