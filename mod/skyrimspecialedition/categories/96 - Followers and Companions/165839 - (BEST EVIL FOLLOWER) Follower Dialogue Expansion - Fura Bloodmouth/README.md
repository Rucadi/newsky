**How is Fura envisioned and touched on in this mod:**
  
  
In this mod, Fura is made into a **fully fledged follower** and expanded as a **brutal, indifferent and self-preserving** vampire warrior. Once betrayed by her superior, she was left for dead on the plains of Whiterun, but a **dark pact** shall save her life and change her soul for all eternity. Having seen treachery, she believes **power and survival are all that matter.**
  
  
She joins you after you **complete the quest "The Bloodstone Chalice".** Throughout the questline, she will reveal more about her past, and **eventually grow to trust you more.**
  
  
Fura has a **direct, cynical demeanor.** She believes everyone is out for their own survival, and **she will not hesitate to kill** if it meant serving her goal and Harkon's.
  
  
**Will you fix her, or will she make you worse?**

**Features:**
  
1. Makes Fura a fully fledged follower, and expands Fura with 614 lines, all voiced by high quality AI (see the video for reference) 
  
2. Peer into the cunning vampire's past and earn her favor
  
3. Significant amount of new story-driven interactions, full of Roleplaying opportunities
  
4. Two personal quests
  
5. Progressive relationship with Serana
  
6. Banter and reaction to Aranea, Sapphire, Serana
  
7. Quest awareness for all guilds, vanilla main quest, DLC main quest, multiple side quests
  
8. Romance Fura and marry her
  
9. Awareness to her surroundings, such as weather and location
  
10. Completely lore-friendly, expanding rather than completely rewriting the character
  
  
This mod is **100% compatible with any mod**.
  
  
**Safe to add and update mid-save.**
  
  
There is a potential for **future expansion** with Fura including **optional** synergy with the overarching Shezarrine Saga

**Requirements to recruit Fura:**
  
1. Join Clan Volkihar
  
2. Complete the quest "The Bloodstone Chalice"
  
3. Talk to Fura after hearing Harkon's speech about the Moth Priest, recruiting her
