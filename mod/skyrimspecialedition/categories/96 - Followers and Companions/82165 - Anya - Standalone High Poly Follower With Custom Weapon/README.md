﻿[size=6]**- Anya -**
  
[/size]A standalone ranger companion suitable for your mid/late-game adventures. **Anya** is a very resourceful **Nord**, being one of the few surviving members of the **Blades**, being forced into hiding. Unlike Delphine, **Anya** decided to live on her own, while maintaining a low profile living in what was left of **Sky Haven Temple**. She claims to have **Akaviri** roots, as unlikely as it seems, but not much else is known about her past. Her hatred for the **Thalmor** and what she heard about dragons is what keeps her going, facing many adversities during her time in hiding. **Anya** is very agile and proficient with the bow, making her a great DPS. Her main weapon is **Daedalon's Wing**, a bow she made herself from remnants of dragon bones she found.
  
  
![](https://iili.io/JX6Xbzg.png)﻿
  
﻿**- Daedalon's Wing -**Custom **Bow**made by mixing a couple of **Dragonbone** weapons. Very high stats all around.
  
  
  
**Location:** Sky Haven Temple.
  
![](https://iili.io/JX6h9qv.png)﻿﻿﻿﻿
  
  
**-Features﻿****-**
  
Nord follower with proficiency in Heavy Armor and Archery
  
Custom weapon
  
Custom skin textures based on existing ones
  
ESPFE plugin, so it doesn't take a space in your load order
  
Unique body shape, with edited clothing to fit
  
Nisetanaka's blades armors
  
FemaleCondescending voice
  
Though she levels with the player, might seem overpowered in early-game
