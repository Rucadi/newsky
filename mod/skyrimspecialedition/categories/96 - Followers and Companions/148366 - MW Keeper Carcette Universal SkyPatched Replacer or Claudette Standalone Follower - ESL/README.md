# MW Keeper Carcette Universal SkyPatched Replacer or Claudette Standalone Follower - ESL
- Author: MW
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/148366


**- WHAT YOU GET -**
  
  
A visual overhaul for the courageous but unfortunate Keeper **Carcette**, the Vigilants of Stendarr's leader in the province of Skyrim.
  
  
Otherwise, the neato Breton follower **Claudette**, that can be either a newly enlisted Vigilant or a nasty evul vampire.
  
  
  
  
**- WHY YOU GET -**
  
  
There are a couple mods that add game content or a visual replacer for Carcette, but they either require multiple patches or are simply left without a visual replacer/patch.
  
This replacer mod is made to cover all possible combo of extra mods, using only SkyPatcher.
  
  
It will work with any one, or some, or all, or NONE of these mods: 
  
- [A Cure for the Keeper](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/126834) (here you can find the patches for the scripted mods to work together, also to remove the Carcette duplicates, all is ESLified)
  
- [Keeper Carcette Survives](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/9476) 
  
- [Stendarr Rising - Carcette Returns](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/77968) 
  
- [Unplayable Faction Armors](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/30051) 
  
- (suggest more mods in the comments if you know them)
  
  
In the FOMOD you will be able to choose if her left eye will be damaged after the attack or not, for human, vampire and cured versions (she will nevertheless get the scars).
  
  
---
  
  
If you have no need for a new Carcette replacer, but you still want my NPC as a follower, I also have alternative downloads to be used as standalone (use one at a time, MAW still required)
  
  
**Claudette** (Human)
  
Race: Breton
  
Class: Vigilant of Stendarr
  
Level: min.5 max.500, she levels at 1x ratio to the Player, +50 offset to Health, +50 offset to Magicka
  
Perks: Heavy Armor, One Hand(Swords), Destruction(Flames,Sparks), Light Foot
  
Equipment: Vigilant Armor, a Silver Sword
  
Location: Moorside Inn, Morthal
  
She is checking Morthal because too many shady things going on in those marches.
  
  
**Claudette** (Vampire)
  
Race: Breton
  
Class: Vampire Infiltrator
  
Level: min.5 max.500, she levels at 1x ratio to the Player, +50 offset to Health, +50 offset to Magicka
  
Perks: Heavy Armor, One Hand(Swords), Destruction(Drain,Sparks), Light Foot
  
Equipment: Tavern clothes, a Steel Dagger
  
Location: Moorside Inn, Morthal
  
She is checking Morthal because too many cool things going on in those marches (Laelette distant cousin berhaps?)
  
  
Both are flagged [X] ESSENTIAL and [X] MARRIAGEABLE. 
  
  
No bodies or skins included, for maximum compatibility.

**Can you stop making Breton dames, at least for a while?**Yes.
  
  
**Do I need this?**
  
Yes!
  
  
**Are the eyes included?**The vampiric eyes yes, the normal eyes will use your default texture Amber and Amber/Blind.
  
  
**I would like my Carcette Survivor to have head bandages?**
  
Open the Console, click on her and make sure you have selected her, type *equipitem 4F000 1*, enter. Those are vanilla normally unobtainable bandages.
  
Otherwise, there are mods that adds really usable bandages you can give her as a follower.
  
  
**My Stendarr Rising Carcette Returns is not injuried?**
  
Yes SRCR re-uses the same vanilla NPC, I cannot give her the injuries.
  
  
**I see a burn injury on the face of the vampire Claudette follower in the images?**Ye sorry, ignore that. The 2 Claudette of course will have no injuries, scars or burns.
