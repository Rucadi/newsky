# TrustyPilot's Follower pack - High Poly Edition
- Author: deleted53019336
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/62996
[font=Arial]What this mod contains?
  
18 of my previous followers converted to high-poly head.
  
high-poly head is not required as it is included into mod.
  
All merged into one ESP-FE Plugin
  
followers use your installed body files.
  
Ver 1.0 followers use their own texture files.
  
All are marriable.
  
levels with player with no cap.
  
More details? check my old pages for more details
  
Like them? please endorse ^^
  
[/font]

Locations?
  
**Mary Jane** - hall of attainment, top floor.
  
**Cassadora** - High Hrothgar, usually sits on the left or praying in centre.
  
**Elrynn** - Hall of the dead in Solitude, sitting at table.
  
**Rhaiella** - Yarls Longhouse in falkreath, sitting near main door.
  
**Alina** - Jorrvaskr, sitting near the stairs.
  
**Myrrin** - CandleHearth Hall, Windhelm, upstairs near fireplace.
  
**Malerith** - FrostFruit Inn at Rorikstead.
  
**Lysandra** - Old Hroldan Inn.
  
**Milliandris** - Understone Keep, On the right, just before the Jarl's throne room.
  
**Soneya** and VelRosi - Both in the NightGate Inn.
  
**Errika** - temple of Mara in Riften, right side.
  
**Nilaan** - Vilemyr inn at Ivarstead.
  
**Serenity** - temple of the divines, Solitude, sitting at the front.
  
**Auresh** - Fort DawnGuard, turn left and she's sitting near the map.
  
**Clarynthe** - Hall of the dead, whiterun.
  
**Yrsa** - Birna's Oddments in Winterhold.
  
**Klarissa** - Great white hall in DawnStar.

Any requirements?
  
you will require to have a compatible body mod installed,
  
as the followers will use this for the source body. 
  
  
  
Compatibility?
  
  
Ver 1.0
  
CBBE ver = Normal CBBE, CBBE SMP, CBBE 3BA, TBD, e.t.c.
  
UNP ver = BHUNP, UNP, e.t.c.[/font]
