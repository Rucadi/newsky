# MW Jenassa SkyPatched or Regular Replacer - Follower Fixes - Racemenu Preset - ESL
- Author: MW
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/164819


**- WHAT YOU GET -**
  
  
A visual overhaul for the artistically bladey-shadowy dark elf hireling **Jenassa**. Based on the lethal assassin **Kenassa**I made for the mod [Mephala Patch](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/153208).
  
  
Also her Racemenu preset, Dunmer race. Should work with other elf/human races, and I am providing the Sculpt Files.
  
Be advised, all of my files will be inside subfolders called MW. Keep it or change it as you like it, or download manually and unpack only the files you want. 
  
  
Make sure to check and download the Makeup mods in the Credits, or you will get the fabled Purple Textures of Missing.
  
Otherwise, simply change/switch off the layers and you can make it work even without those mods. Of course, she will look different. 
  
 
  
3 hair choices (screenshots in the gallery).
  
  
3 warpaint/makeup choices (screenshots in the gallery).
  
  
SkyPatched, no bodies or skins included, for maximum compatibility.
  
  
---
  
  
**1.1 UPDATE:**Being a vanilla follower, Jenassa suffers from the usual lack of perks, a low-capped max level etc. 
  
For my game, I wanted to do some fixes like I did for [MW Adelaisa](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/89090). So the hireling now feature an "upgrade" with the following characteristic: 
  
  
**Jenassa**
  
Level: min.5 max.500, she levels at 1x ratio to the Player
  
Perks: Light Armor, One Hand, Archery, Light Foot
  
Special: no more "Opposite Gender Anims"
  
  
You can now choose in the FOMOD if my Replacer/Upgrade is SkyPatched, or alters the real vanilla HirelingJenassa NPC: If you have other mods touching her, you may get a darkface and need patches, but in this version the SkyPatcher is no longer necessary.
  
Otherwise, the Full SkyPatched version is selectable in the FOMOD, compatible with everything. I think it """JUSTWORKS""", but let me know, I am not an expert SkyPatcherer...
