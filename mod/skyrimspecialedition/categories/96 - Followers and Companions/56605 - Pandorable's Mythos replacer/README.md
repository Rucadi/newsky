# Pandorable's Mythos replacer
- Author: Pandorable
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/56605


ABOUT
  
A replacer for Karna and Bacchus from the mod [Mythos](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/51576) by Craftian, who also made [Citizens of Tamriel](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/29069). Mythos is a very well made mod and I highly recommend you check it out if you haven't already, along with Craftian's other work.
  
Karna and Bacchus will use whatever body and skin mods you have installed so they might look very different in your game compared to my pictures. In my screenshots I am using bijin skin for female NPCs and vitruvia for males.
  
  
[LEGENDARY EDITION](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/109359)
  
  
INSTALLATION & REQUIREMENTS
  
Install using a mod manager of your choice, manual installation isn't recommended unless you know what you're doing. Requires [Mythos](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/51576), load this replacer after that mod.
