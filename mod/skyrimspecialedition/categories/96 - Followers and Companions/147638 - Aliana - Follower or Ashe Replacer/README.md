[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]**Ashe Replacer**
  
  
She has BnP skin with Ashe's usual features baked into the textures. Included an option to remove barcode in the fomod too, that might interest some folks.
  
  
Not much else to say, make sure to give me any feedback about her looks :D. This is only a visual replacer and doesn't modify anything else.
  
  
  
Aliana Follower
  
  
She's a spellsword and uses Ice Magic + Sword.
  
**Located** at Temple of Kynareth in Whiterun.
  
Essential, Doesn't trigger traps, Marriageable.
  
You can choose between 2 looks. White hair or Redhead.
  
Option to wear Master Thief Armor by FafnyB.[/font]

**FAQ:**
[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]
  
  
***>Can't find her!***
  
  
If you can't find either Aliana or Ashe then you probably need [BEES](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/106441), lmk if this doesn't fix the problem.﻿
  
  
  
***>Why does the racemenu preset look so different in my game?***
  
  
ENB and Skin make a big difference in NPC looks, I use Cabbage ENB and BnP - Bijin adaptation for the skin.
