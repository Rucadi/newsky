﻿[size=6]**-Kathryn-**
  
[/size]A standalone 2-handed companion suitable for your mid/late-game adventures. **Kathryn** is an unhesitating Nord, who was called up to train with the **Greybeards** at a very young age. She was born in **Shor's Stone**, but ever since she lost her parents and home on the massacre led by the Imperials, she lives with **Ulfric Stormcloak** as his protegee. Not long after, **Ulfric** recognized her aptitude with the **Thu'um** and sent her on a journey to High Hrothgar, to be further tutored. Kathryn is very strong and skilled, making her a great tank. Her main weapon is the **Snowdon's Chopper**, an ancient weapon she uncovered in a nordic tomb while venturing Solstheim.
  
  
![](https://iili.io/JX6EH0P.png)
  
﻿**-Snowdon's Chopper-**Custom **Battleaxe** made by mixing a couple of weapons from **Solstheim**. Very high stats, lacking in speed to make up for it.
  
  
  
**Location:** Palace of the Kings, Windhelm.
  
![](https://iili.io/JX6E2dF.png)﻿﻿﻿
  
  
**-Features﻿****-**
  
Nord follower with proficiency in Heavy Armor and Two-Handed
  
Custom weapon
  
Custom skin textures based on existing ones
  
ESPFE plugin, so it doesn't take a space in your load order
  
Unique body shape, with edited clothing to fit
  
Nisetanaka's steel and iron bikini armors
  
FemaleEvenToned voice
  
Though she levels with the player, might seem overpowered in early-game
