**The Corrupted Guardians**

  
This is a set of three standard followers and one transforming follower that require the [Natura](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/77826) magic mod. Install that along with this mod. 
  
  
**What is this enemy to ally thing?**
  
  
All E2A followers come equipped with a very special feature. They no longer sit around waiting for you while clogging up valuable inn space. Instead, each follower is placed out in the world for you to discover and defeat. Only then will you be considered worthy of their time and have the ability to recruit them as a companion. 
  
  
**But where are they?**
  
  
That's for me to know and you to find out by traversing Skyrim and playing as you normally would. You're bound to run across them as they are not hidden. 
  
  
Note: If you really must cheat you can look at the included text document in the zip file for their possible locations. Yes. You read that right. Each follower has three possible locations where they might spawn. 
  
  
**Important: Please Read**
  
  

  
* While Caralia is in her alternate form (Ynareza), she will not have any follower dialogue but will continue to follow you around as if she was a follower. If you wish to dismiss her or make her wait you will have to speak to Ynareza and use the dialogue option "I can see your true form" so that she transforms back into her Caralia form.
Please do not import Ynareza into any follower frameworks as I will not provide support if you do this.
  
* If you wish to change the outfit for Ynareza I'd recommend using a mod like [Simple Outfit System](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/48974).
Please note that the follower teleportation spell will only work while she is in her Caralia form. 
  
* Ynareza has a custom enchanted greatsword that will never run out of charge. I'd recommend letting her keep her beloved weapon.
