# (Reworked) Mage Armor- Flesh Replacer
- Author: veslye
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/131353


**Rework of [Mage Armor- Flesh Replacer (No Runes Version)](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/77628).
  
  
Main Changes:**
  
-Reworked all 5 mage armor spell textures
  
﻿-Smoother and better blending textures
  
﻿-Reduced darkening of skin and armor due to previous compression methods
  
﻿-Reduced artifacts
  
  
**Notice:** These files were left abandoned on my drive since I had uninstalled and stopped playing/modding. I found them again recently so I wanted to share them. The demo image is taken on Vanilla Skyrim with no other mods. User images welcome.
  
  
**Versions:**
  
Two Editions (Download ONE only):
  
-Standard: Applies effects on the skin only
  
-Overlay: Applies effects on both skin and armor
  
  
**Spells Affected:**-Dragon Hide
  
-Ebony Flesh
  
-Iron Flesh
  
-Stone Flesh
  
-Oak Flesh
  
\*Cosmetic changes only, no stat modifications are made.
  
  
**Features:**
  
-Esl-flagged esp (Will not Count Towards Mod Limit)
  
-Animated visuals
  
-Applies to NPCs who use mage flesh spells
  
\*Effect brightness may vary based on your ENB/Lighting setup
  
  
**Compatibility:**-Load file at end of load and plugin order for priority
  
-Not compatible with mods that remove shaders effects such as "Glow Be Gone"
