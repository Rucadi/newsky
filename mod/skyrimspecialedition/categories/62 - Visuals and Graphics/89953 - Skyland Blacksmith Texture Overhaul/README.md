**Skyland Blacksmith Texture Overhaul**
  
  
Featuring a total texture overhaul of all blacksmith workstations, tools, and materials found throughout Skyrim. New blacksmith textures cover the smelter, forge, tanning rack, workbench, anvil, and sharpening wheel. Other textures include the small billows, leather, hammer, tongs, irons, charcoal, and leather straps.

  
  
**Install**:
  
Use your mod manager or manually unzip files into your data folder.
  
  
**Compatibility:**
  
Vanilla, SMIM, SMIM type mods like FYX
  
  
Embers XD and Embers HD Patches available!
  
Thanks to [StoneSpiralGaming](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/95141903)﻿
