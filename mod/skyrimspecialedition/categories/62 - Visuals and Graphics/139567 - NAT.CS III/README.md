Natural and Atmospheric Tamriel for Community Shaders[/size][/color]**
  
[/font]

[font=Arial]
  
This is a patch which ports NAT.ENB III to Community Shaders, taking full advantage of its latest features. As Community Shaders is updated, this patch will also be updated to make use of any additions. This is the essential weather mod for Community Shaders.
  
 ﻿
  
  
**REQUIREMENTS**[/font]

  
* [font=Arial]**[NAT.ENB - ESP WEATHER PLUGIN v3.1.1C](https://www.nexusmods.com/Core/Libs/Common/Widgets/DownloadPopUp?id=394915&game_id=1704)﻿, ONLY.**[/font]
**[font=Arial][Community Shaders](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/86492)﻿﻿ [/font]**
  
* [font=Arial][**Skylighting**](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/139352)[/font]
[font=Arial][**Cloud Shadows**](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/139185/)﻿[/font]
  
* [font=Arial]This mod overwriting the NAT III weather plugin.[/font]

[font=Arial]
  
  
 **CHANGES**
  
[/font]

  
* [font=Arial]Almost completely redone image spaces and a new "HDR" sun texture thanks to [Doodlum](https://next.nexusmods.com/profile/doodlum?gameId=1704)[/font]
[font=Arial]Applied Filmic tone mapping ﻿as seen in Azurite Weathers III and Faithful Weathers[/font]
  
* [font=Arial]A very huge amount of weather color changes, while trying to keep the majority of it the same.[/font]
[font=Arial]Replaced the rain and snow textures for CS compatibility[/font]
  
* [font=Arial]Majority of changes are purely color and lighting adjustments to better match the quality of the old NAT ENBs.[/font]
