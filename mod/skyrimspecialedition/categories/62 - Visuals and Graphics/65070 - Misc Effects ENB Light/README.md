# Misc Effects ENB Light
- Author: Mura
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/65070


**FEATURES**

  
* ENB Light added to random effects I find that could have some.
Feel free to make a request, and I might consider it!
  

  
![](https://media.giphy.com/media/jeGBW5FNfBKCO46w1a/giphy.gif)
  
  
**INSTALLATION** 

  
* Install this mod with your preferred mod manager﻿﻿﻿.
Make sure Complex Particle Lights are enabled in your ENB settings. Most presets come with it enabled.
  

**COMPATBILITY**
  

  
* Not compatible with other mesh replacers for these items.﻿**﻿**
