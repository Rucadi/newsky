**Skyking Alchemy Ingredients**
  
  
An all in one texture and mesh pack for Skyrim alchemy ingredients. Featuring 2k textures and new meshes for many ingredients found in Skyrim, this mod's goal is to give you a single solution to retexture your alchemy ingredients.

**What this mod DOESN'T do**
  
  

  
* This mod doesn't include ingredients covered by flora mods. That could potentially cause issues with flora mods and inconsistencies with visuals. For instance, if your flora mod covers Creep Cluster or Tundra Cotton, they should also include the ingredient mesh (if it's a mod worth its value).
This mod does NOT alter, change, update ingredient values, how alchemy works, or change recopies. This is not an alchemy overhaul in terms of game play. Just visuals.
  

  
  
**Install:**
  
Install with your mod manager of choice and place below other mods that offer meshes or textures for alchemy ingredients.
  
  
**Compatibility:**
  
Compatible with anything as long as this overwrites mods supplying alchemy ingredient meshes and textures.
