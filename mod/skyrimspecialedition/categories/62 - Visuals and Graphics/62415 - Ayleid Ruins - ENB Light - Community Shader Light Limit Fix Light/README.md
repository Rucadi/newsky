**Description**
  

  
* Adds ENB lights to Ayleid ruins in [The Cause](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cause)﻿
Adds ENB lights to Ayleid ruins in [Beyond Skyrim Bruma](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/10917)
  
* Adds ENB lights to Welkyn Stones and Varla Stones from [The Cause](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cause) and [Rare Curios](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rare_Curios)

* Fixes unnecessary alpha blending on [Beyond Skyrim Bruma](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/10917)'s Welkyn Stones and Varla Stones
Features fancy new way to center inventory items ([a bug in older ENB light mods](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/55582/55582-1648512528-897042385.png)﻿)
