# Saints and Seducers Flora ENB Light
- Author: Mura
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/64659
**FEATURES**

  
* ENB Light added to Root Weeds, Flame Stalks plants, and Flame Stalk ingredients (works for both Rare Curious and S&S).
When the Flame Stalk plants are harvested, their light will disappear properly.
  

  
**INSTALLATION** 

  
* Install the requirements, which can be the CC paid DLC, the free 1.6x update or AE. It also works with a [downgraded game](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/57618)﻿ that used the "best of both" patcher.
Install this mod with your preferred mod manager﻿﻿﻿.
  
* Make sure Complex Particle Lights are enabled in your ENB settings. Most presets come with it enabled.

**COMPATBILITY**
  

  
* Not compatible with other mesh replacers for these items.﻿**﻿**
