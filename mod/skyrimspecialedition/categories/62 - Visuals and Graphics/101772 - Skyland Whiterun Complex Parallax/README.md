[size=6]**SKYLAND WHITERUN COMPLEX PARALLAX**

  
  
Introduce the complex parallax effect to Skyland Whiterun or Skyland AIO with Skyland Whiterun Complex Parallax. This mod will add complex parallax to the stones, walls, streets and other architectural features in Whiterun. 
  

**How to Use**
  
Download to your mod manager, activate and place below Skyland Whiterun or Skyland AIO. Adjust your ENBseries.ini file (instructions below) and you're done! You don't have to go to many mod pages to get  the whole package.

[/size][/size][/size][size=5]**Install**[/size]
  
• Ensure you have parallax meshes or use [**Auto Parallax**](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/79473)
  
• Ensure you are using [Skyland Whiterun](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/13015) or [Skyland AIO](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/34179)
  
• Use the latest ENB version
  
• Adjust ENBseries INI file (instructions below)
  
• Download this mod and place below Skyland Whiterun or Skyland AIO
  
**[size=5]
  
Setting Up The ENB**[/size]
  
*Use these settings in ENBSeries.ini*
  
  
EnableTerrainParallax=false
  
EnableComplexParallax=true
  
EnableComplexParallaxShadows=true
  
EnableTerrainBlending=true
  
EnableComplexTerrainParallax=true
  
EnableComplexTerrainParallaxShadows=true
  
EnableComplexMaterial=true
