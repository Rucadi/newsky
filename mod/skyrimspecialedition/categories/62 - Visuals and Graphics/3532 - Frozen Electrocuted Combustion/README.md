**DESCRIPTION**
  
  
This mod applies VFX to NPCs/player when killed by magic status effects\*. 
  
*air, dragons, drain, fire, frost, fear, lightning, poison, soultrap, steam and sun damage*
  
  
**Fire**[size=3]*Corpses burn and either char, skeletonize, or vaporize into ash piles.*
  
**Frost***Corpses freeze solid and shatter or become encased in ice.
  
**Shock**[size=3]Bodies spasm and char, explode into guts and goo, or turn translucent with luminescent skeleton.
  
[size=3]**Drain***[i]Victims become desiccated corpses or age rapidly.
  
**Poison**Victims turn sickly and show signs of cyanosis.[/size][/i][/size]
  
**Air**[/size]
  
*Air is bent out of the target's lungs, causing suffocation and death.***Fear***Skin turns pale and hair becomes white with shock.*
  
**Soul Trap**[size=3] 
  
*Enhances vanilla soul trap effect with soul wisps and glow effects.*[/size]
  
**Soul Absorb***Dragons will absorb the player's soul upon death (Player only).*
  
**Sun Damage**[size=3]Targets burst into flames and disintegrate.[/size]
  
**Vampire Lord***Vampire lords will disintegrate into bone and red ash (Player only).*
  
  
**FAQ**
  
  
> ﻿Q : The game takes a long time to reload after dying??
>   
> A : The death camera duration has been increased to 8 seconds. Vanilla is 5 seconds. Disable this option under Settings in FEC MCM
>   
>   
> Q: How do I trigger Air effects?
>   
> A: Requires magic spell mod with "MagicDamageWind/MagicDamageAir" keywords (ie. Elemental Destruction Magic Redux or similar mods).
