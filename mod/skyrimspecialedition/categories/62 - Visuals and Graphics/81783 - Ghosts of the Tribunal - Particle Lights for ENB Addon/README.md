# Ghosts of the Tribunal - Particle Lights for ENB Addon
- Author: Kris Takahashi and AgentW
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/81783
**[font=Georgia]Ghosts of the Tribunal[/font]**- Particle Lights for ENB Addon -
  
**Support me on [Patreon](http://patreon.com/AgentW)﻿or [Buy Me a Coffee](https://www.buymeacoffee.com/agentW)**

**Description:** ﻿This mod adds ENB particle light to meshes added by Ghosts of the Tribunal Creation Club, namely new candles and lanterns. Provided in 2 options:
  
- Vanilla: no edits to meshes except adding particle lights
  
- Rudy: replace candles with modified Rudy candles. Requires normal and glow maps from [Rudy HQ - Miscellaneous](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/19867) or [Rudy HQ - Nordic Ruins](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/19365).
  
  
**Requirements:
  
-** Ghost of the Tribunal CC
  
- an ENB (Screenshots taken with [Nordwind ENB](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/81886))
  
- [Rudy HQ - Miscellaneous](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/19867) or [Rudy HQ - Nordic Ruins](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/19365) for Rudy version
  
  
**Compatibility:**This mod edits a few meshes and adds an ESL-flagged esp needed to change model paths. There shouldn't be any conflict but let this overwrite any exists.
