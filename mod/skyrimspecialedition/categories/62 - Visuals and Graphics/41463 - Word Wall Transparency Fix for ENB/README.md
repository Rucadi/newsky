Removes a subtle dark rectangle around word of power on word walls and greybeard shouts for ENB users
  
  
**Details**
  

  
* Removes "Decal" and "DynamicDecal" flags on the word mesh. I assume this is very similar to the [Korodic's Footprints Transparency Fix](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/41268)﻿ mod, which inspired me to make this.
No plugin, just a bunch of nif changes.
  
* v0.3: updated [to works well](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/41463/41463-1692154497-303059401.png)﻿ with non-singlepass snow material like [Simplicity of Snow](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/56235)﻿.
