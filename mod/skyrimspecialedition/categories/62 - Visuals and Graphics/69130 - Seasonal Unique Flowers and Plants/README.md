# Seasonal Unique Flowers and Plants
- Author: DragonBlame
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/69130


Hides all flowers and plants from [Official Unique Flowers and Plants](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/29154) during winter and mainly flowers during autumn by using [Seasons of Skyrim](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/62861).
  
  
Mod's plugin is ESLified ESP (won't count toward your plugins' limit).
  
Recommended order - Official Unique Flowers and Plants, Seasons of Skyrim, Seasonal Unique Flowers and Plants.
  
You don't need to rerun any of LOD generation tools for this patch to work.
