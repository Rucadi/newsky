[size=5]Introduce the complex parallax effect to Skyland Landscapes or Skyland AIO with *Skyland Landscapes Complex Parallax in matching texture resolution.* 
  
This mod will add complex parallax to all landscape, landscape meshes, bridges, mountains, dirt cliffs, mines, caves, and ore veins.
  
  
**How to Use**
  
[size=4]With ***one simple and easy to use mod***, just download to your mod manager, activate and place below Skyland Landscapes or Skyland AIO. Adjust your ENBseries.ini file (instructions below) and you're done! You don't have to go to many mod pages to get the whole package. No clogging up your mod manager, and no confusion. We're not DP fishing here.

[/size][size=5]**Install**[/size]
  
• Ensure you have parallax meshes so I use [PG Parallax.](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/120946)
  
• Ensure you are using [Skyland Landscapes](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/3820) or [Skyland AIO](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/34179)
  
• Use the latest ENB version
  
• Download this mod by pressing "download with manager" or "Download manually" and install into your mod manager of choice (MO2 or Vortex) and place below Skyland Landscapes or Skyland AIO
  
**[size=5]
  
Setting Up The ENB**[/size]
  
*Use these settings in ENBSeries.ini*
  
  
EnableTerrainParallax=false
  
EnableComplexParallax=true
  
EnableComplexParallaxShadows=true
  
EnableTerrainBlending=true
  
EnableComplexTerrainParallax=true
  
EnableComplexTerrainParallaxShadows=true
  
EnableComplexMaterial=true
