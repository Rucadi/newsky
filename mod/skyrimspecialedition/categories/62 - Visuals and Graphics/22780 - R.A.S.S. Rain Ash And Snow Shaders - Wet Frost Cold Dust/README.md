[font=Arial]"[/font][font=Georgia]R.A.S.S.[/font][font=Arial]" adds visual effects to the Player and NPCs during certain conditions. [/font]

![](https://i.ibb.co/SNp0mbY/Features.png)

[\*]**[font=Verdana]Ash[/font]** -[font=Verdana]During ash storms player and NPC will cover with ash and dust particles will fly apart.[/font]
  
[left][\*][font=Verdana]**Wetness** - During rainy weather player and NPC will cover with watery shader and droplet particles will fall from the body.[/font]
  
[\*][font=Verdana]**Frost** - During snow weather player and NPC will cover with snow and snowflake particles will fly apart. [/font]
  
[\*][font=Verdana]**Bubbles** - Adds bubbles when swimming. Slaughterfishy is now happy fishy.[/font] 
  
[\*][font=Verdana]**Cold** - Adds Cold Breaths particles to player and NPC in cold/snow regions.[/font]
  
[\*][font=Verdana]**Camera** - Adds camera lens effects during rainy/snow/ash weather and when swimming.[/font]
  
[\*][font=Verdana]**Immersion** - Adds VR compatible texture package for better visual quality and immersiveness.[/font]
  
[\*]**[font=Verdana]Performance[/font]** - [font=Verdana]Will not degrade your perfromance in any way.[/font]
  
[\*][font=Verdana]**MCM**[/font]- [font=Verdana]You can toggle on and off all effects as you wish.[/font]
  
[/left]
