# Skyking Ash Piles and Dust Piles
- Author: Skyking2020
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/127412


**Skyking Ash Piles & Dust Piles**
  
  
This mod offers new 2k/2k textures for ash piles and dust piles. Featuring larger charred chunks and ash and higher quality details.

  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/127412/127412-1724348179-1574713789.jpeg)
  
  
  
**Install:** Simply download and place in the textures section of your load order. Overwrite mods that retexture ash and dust piles. Does not affect ash and dust pile meshes or their placement.
  
  
**Compatibility:** Compatible with any mod that doesn't change the ash and dust pile UV mapping.
  
  
**Features:** 2k/2k textures for ash piles.
