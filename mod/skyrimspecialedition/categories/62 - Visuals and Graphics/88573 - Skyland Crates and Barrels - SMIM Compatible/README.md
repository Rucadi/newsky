# Skyland Crates and Barrels - SMIM Compatible
- Author: Skyking2020
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/88573


New 2k textures for all crates, SMIM static barrels, and SMIM storage barrels. Also includes new East Empire Company sign.
  
[size=3]*Doesn't include those small little kegs you see on countertops.
  
  
Not compatible with mods that change the barrel and crate meshes or texture paths other than SMIM.*

[/size]
