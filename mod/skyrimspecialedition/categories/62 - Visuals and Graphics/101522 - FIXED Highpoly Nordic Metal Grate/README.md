[font=Georgia]This mod replaces the nordicmetalgratex01.nif with an highpoly version adding realistic collisions, improved UV, HD textures and metallic cubemap. Now, for example, you can shoot an arrow through the grate while previously they were blocked even by aiming into the empty spaces between the metal bars.
