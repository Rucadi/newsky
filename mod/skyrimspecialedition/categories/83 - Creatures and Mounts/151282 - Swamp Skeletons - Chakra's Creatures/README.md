This mod adds a new enemy type known as *''Swamp Skeletons''* to Skyrim.
  
  
[/font]
> [font=Times New Roman]﻿Swamp Skeletons are reanimated corpses of those who perished in wetlands. Unlike typical undead, their bones are stained green with algae. Tangles of moss and swamp muck cling to their frames, often hiding them among the mire until it’s too late...[/font]

[font=Times New Roman]
  
Swamp Skeletons will begin spawning and wandering around Morthal's Swamps at level 11.
  
  
Plugin is Flagged ESL.
  
  
There is 1 main file for this mod.[/font]
