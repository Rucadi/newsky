[font=Times New Roman]This mod adds a new enemy type known as *''Scribers''* to Skyrim.
  
  
[/font]
> [font=Times New Roman]Those who dare hoard knowledge from Hermaeus Mora within his realm are subject to a cruel transformation by the Daedric Prince, leaving them trapped in a frail body and forced to serve the Seekers.[/font]

[font=Times New Roman]
  
Scribers will begin spawning and wandering around Apocrypha at level 10. **Arch Scribers** will begin spawning at level 20.
  
  
Skypatcher is required without console commands.
  
  
Plugin is Flagged ESL.
  
  
There is 1 main file for this mod.[/font]
