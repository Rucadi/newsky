![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/21685/21685-1544915659-374388125.jpeg)﻿****Thank you for placing "Marvelous Mudcrabs" among the Hot Mods!****[ [ [ MOD DESCRIPTION ] ] ]****If you're like me and have been disappointed with the vanilla Mud-crabs, then this mod is for you.  This mod replaces the vanilla Mud-crab skeleton with a new custom skeleton meant to give the Mud-crabs of Skyrim a more proportional appearance. Working within the confines of the vanilla mesh, I enlarged the limbs and made several subtle changes to the Mud-crabs, and the result is a Marvelous Mud-crab that will immerse you even more into the world of Skyrim. This affects all Mud-crabs in Skyrim.****SEEING IS BELIEVING!!!

**[ [ [ MOD COMPATIBILITY ] ] ]****Marvelous Mud-crabs uses a custom skeleton for the vanilla Mud-crabs and will overwrite any mod that makes changes to the vanilla Mud-crab skeleton.  Mods that make sweeping changes to vanilla skeletons e.g."Realistic Ragdoll and Force"****should be compatible as long as "Marvelous Mudcrabs" is installed after those mods and allowed to overwrite their files.**
  
  
**Should be fully compatible with any texture mods, since this mod contains no textures of it's own only a skeleton.  Which also makes it compatible with most other creature mods that do not edit vanilla skeletons.****[ [ [ INSTALLATION ] ] ]****Recommend using Nexus Mod Manager or your favorite mod manager.****Install "Marvelous Mudcrabs" after any mod that makes changes to the vanilla Mud-crabs and click "Yes to all" when prompted to overwrite any files.
  
  
 There is no"esp plugin" with this mod.**
