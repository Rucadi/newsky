# Ancient Coastal Mudcrabs -SE- ESL

- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/129333


**[font=Courier New]Hello, all. I was hankering for some good ol' fashion giant crabs in my
  
Skyrim, now they're here. Adds NEW Giant Mudcrabs now ready for you to
  
encounter all along the coast of Skyrim and Solsthiem. These are 
  
calm Mudcrabs...until you get too close! They are little devils they are.
  
 Mudcrabs will lurk in the sands and only pop-out when they want to feed.
  
Called the scourge of the seas, these Giant beasts have been known to
  
take sailors and wreck entire ships! The dangerous of the open seas are
  
now present on the coast. [/font]**
