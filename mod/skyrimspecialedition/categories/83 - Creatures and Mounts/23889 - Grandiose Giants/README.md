![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/23889/23889-1553343486-1683675072.jpeg)﻿****[ [ [ MOD DESCRIPTION ] ] ]****If you're like me and have been disappointed with the vanilla Giants, then this mod is for you.  This mod replaces the vanilla Giant skeleton with a new custom skeleton meant to give the Giants of Skyrim a more intimidating and hardier****appearance. Working within the confines of the vanilla mesh, I** **re-proportioned****the limbs, the head and made several subtle changes to the Giants, and the result is a Giant that will test your courage, intimidate and immerse you even more into the world of Skyrim. This affects all Giants in Skyrim.****! ! ! Seeing is believing ! ! !

Giant Club clipping into shoulder fixed with new version!****A great video provided by Mod Showcase!****[ [ [ MOD COMPATIBILITY ] ] ]****Grandiose Giants uses a custom skeleton****(Version 1.1 also includes a custom giant club nif)****for the vanilla Giants and will overwrite any mod that makes changes to the vanilla Giant skeleton.  Mods that make sweeping changes to vanilla skeletons e.g."Realistic Ragdoll and Force"****should be compatible as long as "Grandiose Giants" is installed after those mods and allowed to overwrite their files.**
  
  
**Should be fully compatible with any texture mods, since this mod contains no textures of it's own only a skeleton.  Which also makes it compatible with most other creature mods that do not edit vanilla skeletons.****[ [ [ INSTALLATION ] ] ]****Recommend using Nexus Mod Manager or your favorite mod manager.****Install "Grandiose Giants" after any mod that makes changes to the vanilla Giants and click "Yes to all" when prompted to overwrite any files.
