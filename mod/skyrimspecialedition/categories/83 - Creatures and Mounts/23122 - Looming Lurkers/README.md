Looming Lurkers among the Hot Mods!****![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/23122/23122-1549846535-1008206186.jpeg)﻿****[ [ [ MOD DESCRIPTION ] ] ]****If your like me and have been disappointed with the vanilla Lurkers, then this mod is for you.  This mod replaces the vanilla Lurker skeleton with a new custom skeleton meant to give the Lurkers of Apocrypha a more menacing, monstrous and immersive appearance. Working within the confines of the vanilla mesh, I adjusted the head, neck and limbs of the Lurkers. These subtle changes to Apocrypha's Lurkers have transformed them to look more monstrous and terrifiy . Immersing you even more into the world of Skyrim with every encounter on the island of Solstheim and the realm of Apocrypah!****! ! ! SEEING IS BELIEVING ! ! !

![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/96029/96029-1549124491-551857285.jpeg)****[ [ [ MOD COMPATIBILITY ] ] ]****Looming Lurkers uses a custom skeleton for the Vanilla Lurkers and will overwrite any mod that makes changes to the vanilla Lurker skeleton.  Mods that make sweeping changes to vanilla skeletons e.g."Realistic Ragdoll and Force"****should be compatible as long as "Looming Lurkers" is installed after those mods and allowed to overwrite their files.****Should be fully compatible with any texture mods, since this mod contains no textures of it's own only a skeleton.  Which also makes it compatible with most other creature mods that do not edit vanilla skeletons****[ [ [ INSTALLATION ] ] ]****Recommend using Nexus Mod Manager or your favorite mod manager.****Install "Looming Lurkers" after any mod that makes changes to the vanilla Lurkers and click "Yes to all" when prompted to overwrite any files.
