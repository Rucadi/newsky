**[ [ [ MOD DESCRIPTION ] ] ]**
  
  
**If you're like me and have been disappointed with the vanilla goats, then this mod is for you. Skyrim's goats just don't have proportions of most long haired goats, wild or** **domesticated****. This mod replaces the vanilla goat skeleton with a new custom skeleton meant to give the goats of Skyrim a more realistic and immersive appearance to more closely resemble actual long haired mountain goats.  Working within the confines of the vanilla mesh, I adjusted the head, neck, and tail of Skyrim's goats to give them a hardier** **appearance****!  These new subtle changes to Skyrim's goats have transformed them to more closely resemble the typical long haired goat breeds of Eastern Europe and other parts of the world (There exists too many to mention here).  Now you can enjoy encountering realistic and hardy goats in the mountains of Skyrim. Immersing you even more into the word of Skyrim with every encounter!****! ! ! Gritty Goats ! ! !****[ [ [ MOD COMPATIBILITY ] ] ]****Gritty Goats uses a custom skeleton for the Vanilla goats and will overwrite any mod that makes changes to the vanilla Goat skeleton.  Mods that make sweeping changes to vanilla skeletons e.g."Realistic Ragdoll and Force"****should be compatible as long as "Gritty Goats" is installed after those mods and allowed to overwrite their files.**
  
  
**Should be fully compatible with any texture mods, since this mod contains no textures of it's own only a skeleton.  Which also makes it compatible with most other creature mods that do not edit vanilla skeletons.**
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
**[ [ [ INSTALLATION ] ] ]****Recommend using Nexus Mod Manager or your favorite mod manager.****Install "Gritty Goats" after any mod that makes changes to the vanilla Goats and click "Yes to all" when prompted to overwrite any files.
