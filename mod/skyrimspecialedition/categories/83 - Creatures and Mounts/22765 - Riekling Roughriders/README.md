**A Huge Thank You for placing Riekling Roughriders among the Hot Mods!****![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/1704/images/22765/22765-1576540990-766414268.jpeg)﻿****[ [ [ MOD DESCRIPTION ] ] ]****If your like me and have been disappointed with the vanilla Riekling riders, then this mod is for you.  This mod replaces the vanilla Riekling rider skeleton with a new custom skeleton meant to give the Riders of Solstheim a more realistic, warrior-like and immersive appearance. Working within the confines of the vanilla mesh, I adjusted the head, neck and limbs of the riders. These subtle changes to Solstheim's Riekling riders have transformed them to look more akin to Goblin warriors . Immersing you even more into the word of Skyrim with every encounter on the island of Solstheim!****! ! ! SEEING IS BELIEVING ! ! !****![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/95786/95786-1547903245-1252723827.jpeg)﻿****Animations, Combat and Kill-Moves still work perfectly!****![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/95786/95786-1547903261-763786916.jpeg)﻿
  
  
  
Overhaul of Wild Boar same as Bristleback Boars!****[ [ [ MOD COMPATIBILITY ] ] ]****Riekling Roughriders uses a custom skeleton for the Vanilla Riekling riders and will overwrite any mod that makes changes to the vanilla Riekling rider skeleton.  Mods that make sweeping changes to vanilla skeletons e.g."Realistic Ragdoll and Force"****should be compatible as long as "Riekling Roughriders" is installed after those mods and allowed to overwrite their files.****Should be fully compatible with any texture mods, since this mod contains no textures of it's own only a skeleton.  Which also makes it compatible with most other creature mods that do not edit vanilla skeletons.****[ [ [ INSTALLATION ] ] ]****Recommend using Nexus Mod Manager or your favorite mod manager.****Install "Riekling Roughriders" after any mod that makes changes to the vanilla Riekling Riders and click "Yes to all" when prompted to overwrite any files.
