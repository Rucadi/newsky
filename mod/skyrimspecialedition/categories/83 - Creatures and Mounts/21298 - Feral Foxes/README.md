**[FERAL FOXES LEGENDARY EDITION!!!](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/94696?tab=description)﻿****[ [ [ MOD DESCRIPTION] ] ]**
  
**If your like me and have been disappointed with the vanilla foxes, then this mod is for you.  This mod replaces the vanilla fox skeleton and mesh with a new custom skeleton and mesh meant to give the foxes of Skyrim a more stealthy, realistic and feral appearance.  After looking through tons of pictures, sculptures and paintings and working within the confines of the vanilla mesh, I shortened the limbs, enlarged the neck and made several other subtle changes to the foxes and the result is a more** **stealthy,****realistic and feral fox that will immerse you even more into the world of Skyrim. This affects all foxes in Skyrim**

**[ [ [ MOD COMPATIBILITY ] ] ]****Feral Foxes uses a custom skeleton for the vanilla Foxes, so mods that make sweeping changes to vanilla skeletons e.g."Realistic Ragdoll and Force"****should be compatible as long as "Feral Foxes" is installed after those mods and allowed to overwrite their files.****Should be fully compatible with any texture mods, since this mod uses only vanilla textures.  Which also makes it compatible with most other creature mods.**
  
**For****[FUSA FUSA PROJECT](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/68920)****Compatibility install Feral Foxes then install****[FUSA FUSA PROJECT](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/68920).**
  
  
  
**[ [ [ INSTALLATION ] ] ]****Recommend using Nexus Mod Manager or your favorite mod manager.****Install "Feral Foxes" after any mod that makes changes to the vanilla foxes and click "Yes to all" when prompted to overwrite any files.
  
  
This mod includes an "esp plugin", although load order shouldn't matter, try to keep it below any major creature mods to be on the safe side.****[ [ [****UNINSTALLING & UPDATING****] ] ]**
  
**Simply uninstall with your favorite mod manager.
  
  
Then make a clean save before reinstalling a new version of this mod.**
  
**[ [ [ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ] ] ] 
  
  
Question: Is the "esp plugin" really necessary?
  
  
Answer: Yes, the mod will not work without using the plugin.
  
  
  
Question: Will this work with your other mod Savage Wolves?
  
  
Answer: I****f you already have "Savage Wolves" installed, I've included a version that has the two mods combined. You'll need to uninstall any old version of Savage Wolves. You can also download the new version of "Savage Wolves and Feral Foxes" on the Savage Wolves Mod page as well.**

**Question: Will you create a version without an "esp plugin"?
  
  
Answer: Vanilla Foxes share a skeleton with Vanilla Wolves, so the "esp plugin" segregates the two by giving the foxes there own skeleton that does not affect the Vanilla Wolves.
