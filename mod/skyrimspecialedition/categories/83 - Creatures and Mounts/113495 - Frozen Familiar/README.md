This is just another small mod I had the idea for while making something else.
  
  
The Frozen Thrall acts just like its warmer cousin: once summoned it runs up to enemies and explodes, dealing damage to friend and foe alike.
  
  
It can be found just west of The Chill, on the frozen corpse of its summoner.
