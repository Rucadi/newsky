# Chickens and Rabbits Can Attack
- Author: AndrealletiusVIII
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/56613


**[b][color=#a4c2f4][size=4]Description**[/color][/b][/size]
  
  
While working on other mods, I noticed that chickens and rabbits do not have any attack animations in their behaviors. For that reason, they cannot attack. However, you can cast spells like Raise Dead and Frenzy on them, but they'll just run up to a target and not actually attack.
  
This little mod takes care of that.
  
Now you can actually encounter killer chickens or even the famous [Rabbit of Caerbannog](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rabbit_of_Caerbannog)ï»¿! /s
  
  
This mod can also be used as a resource to give other creatures a animationless melee attack.
  
  
When a rabbit/chicken gets within range of a target, it will cast a dummy spell, to simulate a melee attack. A sound will play and the chicken/rabbit will do a little jump.
  
  
**[b][color=#a4c2f4][size=4]Optional File: Killer Bunny**[/color][/b][/size]
  
  
This optional file uses mihail's meshes from [this mod](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/88905), to recreate the Rabbit of Caerbannog. I've took a wolf lair in the Rift and made it into the actual Lair of Caerbannog. It also has everything enabled for radiant quests, so certain radiant quests might point you to go there.
  
  
Be warned though! The Killer Bunny is not to underestimated. Compared to regular rabbits, he deals significantly more damage (can one-shit lower level characters) and his Health is much higher than any normal rabbits.
  
  
  
  
**[b][color=#a4c2f4][size=4]Compatibility**[/color][/b][/size]
  
  
Any mods that edits the rabbit or chicken race records might revert my changes.
  
  
A zombie edition of this mod can be found [here](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/56968). Don't use my mod and that one together, since both do the same thing.
  
  
[The Roost](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/126444) actually expands the Chickenmancer encounter, using this mod as a framework.
