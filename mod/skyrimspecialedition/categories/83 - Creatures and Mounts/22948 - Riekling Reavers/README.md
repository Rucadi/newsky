[RIEKLING REAVERS LEGENDARY EDITION](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/95926?tab=description) ﻿! ! !****[ [ [ MOD DESCRIPTION ] ] ]****If your like me and have been disappointed with the vanilla Rieklings, then this mod is for you.  This mod replaces the vanilla Riekling skeleton with a new custom skeleton meant to give the Rieklings of Solstheim a more realistic, warrior-like and immersive appearance. Working within the confines of the vanilla mesh, I adjusted the head, neck and limbs of the Rieklings. These subtle changes to Solstheim's Rieklings have transformed them to look more akin to Goblin warriors . Immersing you even more into the word of Skyrim with every encounter on the island of Solstheim!****! ! ! SEEING IS BELIEVING ! ! !****![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/95926/95926-1548500273-1343903543.jpeg)﻿**
  
  
  
**Animations, Combat and Kill-Moves still work perfectly!
  
  
  
![](https://staticdelivery.nexusmods.com/mods/110/images/95926/95926-1548500294-8262506.jpeg)﻿****[ [ [ MOD COMPATIBILITY ] ] ]****Riekling Reavers uses a custom skeleton for the Vanilla Rieklings and will overwrite any mod that makes changes to the vanilla Riekling skeleton.  Mods that make sweeping changes to vanilla skeletons e.g."Realistic Ragdoll and Force"****should be compatible as long as "Riekling Reavers" is installed after those mods and allowed to overwrite their files.****Should be fully compatible with any texture mods, since this mod contains no textures of it's own only a skeleton.  Which also makes it compatible with most other creature mods that do not edit vanilla skeletons
