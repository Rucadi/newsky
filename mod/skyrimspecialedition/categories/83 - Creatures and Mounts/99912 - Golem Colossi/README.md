# Golem Colossi
- Author: vkukko123
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/99912


[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]![](https://i.imgur.com/hLVEjlh.png)
  
Prepare to fight in the shade as these titans are sure to block out the sun.
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/LvLMdkE.png)
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/NVVLGGr.png)﻿﻿
  
Two hostile stone golems of large stature with high quality textures by [IconicDeath](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/34739755).
  
Many people were asking for more colossal monsters in the comments section of [Spider Colossi](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/99565)﻿, so this is continuation to the series.
  
  
Sample of golem voices here:
  
[soundcloud]https://soundcloud.com/verikukko/golem-colossi-sample?si=5ceaaf630b184be2b6cba3c51ef893d9&utm\_source=clipboard&utm\_medium=text&utm\_campaign=social\_sharing[/soundcloud]
  
  
Their levels scale from 40 to 60. They have a lot of health and decently powerful attacks. I recommend level 20+ before trying to fight them, unless you are a beast at avoiding their attacks. Melee characters can try to get hits to their shin/ankle area from side or behind before they turn.![](https://i.imgur.com/nUyqNR0.png)![](https://i.imgur.com/E23qCCW.png)﻿**Mountain Golem** - Solitude hold, near fallen Pinefrost Tower.
  
**Ashstorm Golem** - Ashforest in Solstheim, near a spot marked as DLC2POISpider01.
  
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/Huijfw9.png)﻿
  
Might not fit with mods that add stuff to their living areas. Safe to install/uninstall in the middle of a save. **ESL** flagged. No reqs.[/font]
