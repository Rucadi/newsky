\*Description
  
  -"Chaos Dragons (CD)" is an evolution Mod of "The New Dragons (TND)" Mod.
  
     Added the 45 new dragons, and strengthened all vanilla dragons.
  
  
  -All unique dragon have a unique look.
  
  
  -A new Dragon has been added to the leveled list, it will appear in the field.
  
  
  -You can create a new Items, from a material that can be taken from the dragon.
  
  
  -This Mod adjusts dragons, being aware that they are not too strong.
  
  
  -This Mod is to compete to the Dragon strengthening Mod.
  
    (AI change Mod is not limited to this.)
  
  
  -There are no plans to issue a compatible patch.
  
  
  -I think that it is not a Lore Friendly.
  
    But I'm not going to make a Lore Friendly patch
  
  
  -Power to attract the dragon to your location will be added.
  
    This power will be added by killing the Mirmulnir.
  
    If you are already killing Mirmulnir is power added after the data load.
  
  
  -Fight the all dragons in the Dragon Arena.
