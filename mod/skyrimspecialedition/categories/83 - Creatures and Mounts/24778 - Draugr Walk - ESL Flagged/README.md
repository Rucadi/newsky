[font=Tahoma]![](https://i.imgur.com/PH68NSj.png)
  
[/font]
  
  
**Introduction**
  
  
Draugr cannot run anymore or do forward lunging attacks. They also cannot do disarm and unrelenting force shouts anymore, as well as no more frost atronach summons. I tried to make the draugr more zombie-like with these changes.
  
  
  
**[font=Tahoma]Features[/font]**[font=Tahoma]
  
[/font]
  

  
* Draugr cannot run anymore
Draugr cannot do forward lunging attacks anymore
  
* Draugr cannot do disarm and unrelenting force shouts anymore
Draugr cannot summon frost atronachs anymore
  

  
  
**[font=Tahoma]Compatibility[/font]**[font=Tahoma]
  
[/font]
  
Incompatible with mods that edit the draugr templates and/or draugr move data. Without a patch, the latest mod in the load order will overwrite the previous one.
  
  
Requests can be made for unavailable patches.
