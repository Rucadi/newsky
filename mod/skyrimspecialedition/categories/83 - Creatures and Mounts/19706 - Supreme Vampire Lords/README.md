**[ [ [ MOD DESCRIPTION] ] ]****If your like me and have been disappointed with the vanilla Vampire Lords, then this mod is for you. This mod replaces the vanilla skeleton with a custom skeleton that increases the wing size of vanilla Vampire Lords, as an added bonus this mod also fixes the ridiculous leg clipping of the winged animation. This mod has kept compatibility in mind, combat and kill move animations should still work perfectly.****SEEING IS BELIEVING!!!

**[ [ [ MOD COMPATIBILITY] ] ]**
  

**Supreme Vampire Lords replaces the vanilla skeleton of the Vampire Lord and will overwrite any mod that makes changes to the vanilla Vampire Lord skeleton.  Mods that make sweeping changes to vanilla skeletons e.g."Realistic Ragdoll and Force"****should be compatible as long as "Supreme Vampire Lords" is installed after those mods and allowed to overwrite their files.**
  
  
**Should be fully compatible with any texture or new mesh mods, since this mod contains no textures of it's own only a skeleton.  Which also makes it compatible with most other Vampire Lord mods that do not edit the vanilla Vampire Lord skeleton.****Should also be compatible with creature mods that use the vanilla Vampire Lord Skeleton such as [Immersive Creatures](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/12680)﻿.**
