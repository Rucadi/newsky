# Dragonborn Reskin - Wheeler
- Author: borokoshow
- Game: skyrimspecialedition

[font=Trebuchet MS]**![](https://i.imgur.com/EJH8DQM.png)****A hand-drawn style reskin for the Wheeler Quick Action Wheel mod!****[color=#fff2cc]**![](https://i.imgur.com/ndTY3wi.gif)﻿﻿﻿﻿[/color][/font][font=Trebuchet MS]
  
**[b][b][b][color=#ffe599]![](https://i.imgur.com/1ioRGcy.png)**[/b][/color][/b][/b][/font][font=Trebuchet MS]
  
**450+ new hand-made icons!**
  
Rustic style that aims to fit Skyrim's medieval theme
  
Clear background designed to offer better visibility of your surroundings
  
Very customizable, 9 wheel styles and 4 fonts to choose!
  
  
![](https://i.imgur.com/rSYEjH9.png)
  
  
Icons for base game weapons and some mod added weapons too (whip, claws, rapier, etc)
  
"Magic hand" icons for offensive spells, for better differentiation in the heat of battle
  
Icons for all types of elemental/utility arrows and bolts
  
Icons for many popular mods (Campfire, Hunterborn, KittyTail spells, Darenii spells, Mysticism, Gourmet, Book of Shadows and many more to come!)
  
Icons for summon/teleport followers (Serana, Auri, Gore, Katana, Xelzaz, Inigo, and more!)
  
[font=Trebuchet MS][size=4]Icons for potions, amulets and tools![/font][font=Trebuchet MS]
  
[/font]Roleplaying as a bard? Do it with more style, there are icons for lute, drum and flute[/size][/font][font=Trebuchet MS]**[b]**[/b]
  
**﻿[font=Trebuchet MS][size=4]![](https://i.imgur.com/rcUyMbp.gif)[/font]**I made this based on goredev's wheeler reskin, I really love the rustic, hand-drawn style icons, it's minimalistic without being too modern. This is my atempt to build upon it by **redesigning** some of the icons, **adding** some more and **tweaking all** of them for **better readability** under different light conditions.
  
  
[/size]Featured in [Lorerim](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/112590) - A Modern RPG Modlist!
  
  
[font=Trebuchet MS][size=4] ![](https://i.imgur.com/uoCZiGd.png)
  
Download and install with your mod manager, **load after** Wheeler Quick Action Wheel of Skyrim.[/font]
  
  
To avoid dreadful [font=Trebuchet MS]style mash-ups I[font=Trebuchet MS][size=4] recommend using only one wheeler icon mod, but it's your game[/font]. Just don't ask me how to use this with other similar mod.[/size][/font]
