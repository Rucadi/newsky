# GV's TrueHUD Boss Bar Integration for various Quest and New Lands Mods
- Author: GuitarthVader
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/64949


**Short and simple this time.** 
  
Well, this basically does what it says on the tin. ;)
  
  
These are just a collection of patch INI's I made for some of the lesser known quest, new lands and boss mods out there; all of which aren't getting much attention in terms of TDM/TrueHUD support *('twas a pain in the ass to look at each NPC formid individually believe me*).
