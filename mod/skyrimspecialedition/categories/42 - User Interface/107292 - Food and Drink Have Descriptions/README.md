# Food and Drink Have Descriptions
- Author: kuroishitam
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/107292


This mod gives description to most food and drink from vanilla skyrim and from a few mods.
  
  
Foods and drinks from DLCs and CCs now have descriptions! More immersion! I have also made descriptions for food and drink from several mods.
