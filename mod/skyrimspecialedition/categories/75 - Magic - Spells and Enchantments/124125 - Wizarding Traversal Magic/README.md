[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]? New Spells![/font]
  
[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]
  
[color=#fceab0] Apparition • This spell instantly transports the caster to a distant location.
  
 Apparition Travel • This toggle spell can only be used by the player. It allows the player to fast travel instantly.
  
 Mist Apparition • Turns invulnerable for a short moment and gains bonus speed and jump height.
  
[/color][/font]
  
  
  
  
[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]? Additional Information:[/font]
  
[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]
  
These abilities are powers so they take Voice slot to cast.
  
The spells benefit from Alteration cost reduction perks but they are not classified as Alteration spells.
  
The visual effects of Apparition Travel requires SKSE.
  
  
The spell tomes can be obtained from Alteration magic vendors when you reach skill level 75.
  
With SPID, NPCs above level 18 will use these abilities in combat as well! They have a cooldown of 10 seconds per casting.
  
  
[/font]
