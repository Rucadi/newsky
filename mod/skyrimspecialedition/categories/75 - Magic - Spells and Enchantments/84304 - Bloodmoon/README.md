# Bloodmoon
- Author: Darenii
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/84304


**Bloodmoon**

  
  
Bloodmoon is a blood spell pack designed to be balanced around vanilla spells.
  
  
Perk integration: [Ascension](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/89223) or
  
Perk integration: [Ascension 2](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/92000)
  
  
**Destruction**
  
  
**Spell List**
  
  
Spoiler**Novice**
  
  

  
* **Blood Spray** - Deals 8 magic damage per second. Targets take extra damage over time.

  
**Apprentice**
  
  

  
* **Blood Bolt** - Deals 20 magic damage. Targets take extra damage over time.
**Blood Rune** - Creates a rune that explodes when enemies approach, dealing 40 magic damage. Targets take extra damage over time.
  
* **Blood Touch** - Deals 30 damage in melee range. Targets take extra damage over time.

  
**Adept**
  
  

  
* **Blood Preasure** - Deals 20 magic damage per second. Targets take extra damage over time.
**Blood Aura** - For 60 seconds, nearby enemies take 8 magic damage per second.Targets take extra damage over time.
  
* **Blood Tear** - Deals 40 magic damage in a large area. Targets take extra damage over time.
**Blood Splash** - Deals 60 damage in melee range. Targets take extra damage over time.
  

  
**Expert**
  
  

  
* **Blood Javelin** - Deals 60 magic damage. Targets take extra damage over time.
**Blood Pool Rune** - Creates a rune that explodes when enemies approach, dealing 80 magic damage. Targets take extra damage over time.
  
* **Blood Grasp** - Deals 90 damage in melee range. Targets take extra damage over time.
**Wall of Blood** - Creates a wall that deals 20 magic damage per second. Targets take extra damage over time.
  

  
**Master**
  
  

  
* **Blood Deprivation** - Deals 40 magic damage per second. Targets take extra damage over time.
**Blood Discharge** - Deals 80 magic damage in a large area. Targets take extra damage over time.
  
* **Blood Vortex** - Deals 30 magic damage per second, in a large area around the caster. Targets take extra damage over time.
**Blood Tether** - Deals up to 100 magic damage in a massive area around the caster. Nearby targets take extra damage. Targets take extra damage over time.
  
* **Blood Palm** - Deals 120 damage in melee range. Targets take extra damage over time.
  
**Notes**
  
** Aspection: Blood spells also absorb 25% of its damage as Health overtime.
Absorb does not work on Undead and Automatons.*
  
  
**Conjuration**
  
  
**Spell List**
  
  
Spoiler**Adept**
  
  

  
* **Conjure Blood Anomaly** - Summons a Blood Anomaly for 60 seconds.

  
**Master**
  
  

  
* **Conjure Blood Pool Anomaly** - Summons a Blood Pool Anomaly for 60 seconds.
  
**Notes**
  
** Its red.*
  
  
**Power**
  
  
**Power List**
  
  
Spoiler
  

  
* **Blood Mist** - For 4 seconds, you take 75% less damage. Cost 100 Magicka.
**Blood Sacrifice** - Sacrifice 2% Health per second to increase spell damage by 25%.
  
* **Blood Rush** - Your Health Regeneration is increased by 300% for 10 seconds. Cost 50 Magicka to cast and can only be used once every 60 seconds.
**Blood Siphon** - Your weapon attacks deal 10 magic damage for 10 Magicka.
  
  
**Notes**
  
** Powers are lesser powers.
Spells that add damage to your attacks have a base damage of 10. Their damage is increased on heavy attacks and is more effective on two-handed weapons than one-handed. The spells do not stack with spells that do the same from my other mods. Only one can be active at the same time. Spells are toggleable.
  
* Blood Sacrifice is toggleable.*
  
  
**How to get them**
  
  
They also get sold by a new Merchant found in the Moorside Inn in Morthal with the [Dareni's Spells - Merchants](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/109762) addon.
  
Optional SPID file for items can be found from Vampires.

[Scrambled Bugs](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/43532)ï»¿ enable: Perk Entry Points: Apply Multiple Spells
  
  
**Compatibility**
  
  
Bloodmoon was created to be used together with Mysticism and Adamant, but they are not mandatory.
  
Bloodmoon is built-in compatible with Scion.
  
SPID file included to enable certain spells for npcs. It does not distribute spells and is not required when the spells are not added to npcs.
