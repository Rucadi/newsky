# Golden Hour Candlelight and Magelight
- Author: Mina
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/109410


Hear me out: I wanted them all.
  
  
For years, I've been rocking [Longer Candlelight and Magelight](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/2188) along with [Candlelight and Other Minor Spells as Lesser Powers](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/19603)﻿ in every playthrough. I finally looked at the new mods that have come out and WOW! So many new things. I tried all the hottest remakes. I loved a piece of each of them, but nothing checked all the boxes.
  
  
So I made a version to check all my boxes.
  
  

  
* Toggle-able like [Better Candlelight](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/4553) (and Elegant Candlelight)﻿
ENB and spark effects like [Elegant Candlelight](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/64435)﻿ 
  
* Warm glow like [Spellights](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/6060) (and Velexia's)﻿
Practical effect radius like [Enhanced Candlelight and Magelight](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/2363) (and Velexia's, Spellights)
  
* No fuzz like [Velexia's Candlelight and Magelight](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/28987) (and Elegant Candlelight)﻿
Longer duration like [Longer Candlelight and Magelight](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/2188) (and Better Candlelight, Enhanced Candlelight, Spellights)﻿
  
* Optional shout integration like [Candlelight and Other Minor Spells as Lesser Powers](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/19603)﻿

  
Magelight now lasts **5 minutes**.
  
Candlelight now lasts **15 minutes**. Toggle it off early by activating it again. The second activation needs to be the same type as the first (cast or shout). That's right, Candlelight is available to learn as a shout, too.
  
  
This will replace the vanilla Candlelight and Magelight spells, so NPCs will use these new versions. Also works with Candelight scrolls.
  
  
  
**Compatible**
  

  
* Safe to use with casting animation mods.
[Advanced Magelight](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/52985)﻿ references its own unique spells, projectiles, effects, etc. Checked in xEdit. 
  
* [Candlelight and Other Minor Spells as Lesser Powers](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/19603) references its own unique spells and effects. Checked in xEdit.

  
**Minor Conflicts**
  

  
* [1st Person Candlelight Fix](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/9736)﻿ replaces Candlelight's mesh, restoring the light spikes and vanilla blue hue. Untested.
[Battle Ready Candlelight Fixes](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/16296) replaces Candlelight's mesh, restoring the light spikes and vanilla blue hue. Checked in NifSkope.
  
* [Candlelight Magelight Multiclarity](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/82882)﻿ changes meshes to its own new versions. Doesn't touch settings. Untested.
[Candlelight Redone](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/4686?tab=files)﻿ changes the Candlelight mesh textures, replacing the little star with runes. Untested.
  
* [ER Moveset Collection (MCO)](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/72220) causes Candlelight's float animation to wig out when dual-wielding weapons. Affects vanilla, too. Tested.
