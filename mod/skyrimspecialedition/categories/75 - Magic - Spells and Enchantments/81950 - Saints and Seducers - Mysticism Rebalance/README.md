# Saints and Seducers - Mysticism Rebalance
- Author: Slampire
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/81950


**Saints and Seducers: Mysticism Rebalance**
  
  
A simple patch for Mysticism and the Saints and Seducers Creation, changing the spells added by it to adhere to the design of Mysticism.
  
  
**Adept:**
  

  
* **Conjure Golden Saint Hunter:**Summons a Golden Saint Hunter for 60 seconds.
**Conjure Dark Seducer Soldier:** Summons a Dark Seducer Soldier for 60 seconds.
  

**Expert:**

  
* **Conjure Golden Saint Champion:** Summons a Golden Saint Champion for 60 seconds.

  
* **Conjure Dark Seducer Assassin:** Summons a Dark Seducer Assassin for 60 seconds.

**Master:**

  
* **Conjure Staada:** Summons Staada for 60 seconds.

  
**Extended Cut Saints and Seducers: Mysticism Rebalance**
  
An alternative patch for Mysticism and the excellent Extended Cut Saints and Seducers, changing the spells added by it to adhere to the design of Mysticism.
  
  
**Adept:**
  

  
* **Conjure Golden Saint:**Summons a Golden Saint for 60 seconds.
**Conjure Dark Seducer:** Summons a Dark Seducer for 60 seconds.
  

**Master:**

  
* **Conjure Staada:** Summons Staada for 60 seconds.

Spell tome values and names for both versions are changed accordingly and appear in the same locations as the original Creation.
