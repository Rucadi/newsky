# Abyss
- Author: Darenii

**Abyss**

  
  
Abyss is a shadow spell pack designed to be balanced around vanilla spells.
  
  
Perk integration: [Ascension](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/89223) or
  
Perk integration: [Ascension 2](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/92000)
  
  
**Destruction**
  
  
**Spell List**
  
  
Spoiler**Novice**
  
  

  
* **Shadow Drain** - Deals 8 magic damage per second and reduces 10% magic resistance for 5 seconds.

  
**Apprentice**
  
  

  
* **Shadow Touch** - Deals 6 magic damage per second for 5 seconds and reduces 10% magic resistance, in melee range.
**Shadow Bolt** - Deals 4 magic damage per second for 5 seconds and reduces 10% magic resistance.
  
* **Shadow Rune** - Creates a rune that explodes when enemies approach, dealing 8 magic damage per second for 5 seconds and reduces 10% magic resistance.

  
**Adept**
  
  

  
* **Abyssal Shadow Drain** - Deals 20 magic damage per second and reduces 10% magic resistance for 5 seconds.
**Shadow Burst** - Deals 8 magic damage per second for 5 seconds and reduces 10% magic resistance, in a large area.
  
* **Shadow Aura** - For 60 seconds, nearby enemies explode on death dealing massive magic damage and reduces magic resistance.
**Abyssal Shadow Touch** - Deals 12 magic damage per second for 5 seconds and reduces 10% magic resistance, in melee range.
  
* **Shadow Ambush** - Teleport to the target and explode for 12 magic damage per second for 5 seconds and reduces 10% magic resistance, in a small area.

  
**Expert**
  
  

  
* **Shadow Orb** - Deals 12 magic damage per second for 5 seconds and reduces 10% magic resistance.
**Shadow Grasp** - Deals 18 magic damage per second for 5 seconds and reduces 10% magic resistance, in melee range.
  
* **Abyssal Shadow Rune** - Creates a rune that explodes when enemies approach, dealing 16 magic damage per second for 5 seconds and reduces 10% magic resistance
**Shadow Detonation** - After 7 seconds, explode for 40 magic damage and reduces 10% magic resistance for 5 seconds , in a small area. Damage is increased by 50% for each enemy.
  
* **Shadow Wall** - Creates a wall that deals <mag> magic damage per second and reduces 10% magic resistance for 5 seconds.

  
**Master**
  
  

  
* **Shadow Torrent** - Deals 40 magic damage per second and reduces 10% magic resistance for 5 seconds.
**Shadow Eye** - Deals 30 magic damage per second and reduces 10% magic resistance for 5 seconds, in a massive area.
  
* **Shadow Eruption** - Deals up to 20 magic damage per second for 5 seconds and reduces 10% magic resistance, in a massive area around the caster. Nearby targets take extra damage.
**Abyssal Shadow Grasp** - Deals 24 magic damage per second for 5 seconds and reduces 10% magic resistance, in melee range.
  
* **Shadow Void** - Deals 16 magic damage per second for 5 seconds and reduces 10% magic resistance, in a large area.
  
**Notes**
  
** Shadow Spells benefit from Ascension Perks.*
  
  
**Illusion**
  
  
**Spell List**
  
  
Spoiler**Novice**
  
  

  
* **Shadow Chains** - For 30 seconds, reduces 25% Movement Speed, in a large area.

  
**Apprentice**
  
  

  
* **Shadow Enfeeblement** - For 30 seconds, reduces 75 Armor Rating, in a large area.

  
**Adept**
  
  

  
* **Shadow Bane** - For 30 seconds, reduces 50 Health, Magicka and Stamina, in a large area.
**Shadow Step** - Step through the shadows.
  

  
**Expert**
  
  

  
* **Shadow Grave** - For 30 seconds, enemies below 15% Health are slain, in a large area.
**Shadow Regeneration** - For 120 seconds, Health, Stamina and Magicka Regeneration is increased by 100% during Invisibility.
  

  
**Master**
  
  

  
* **Shadow Soul** - For 30 seconds, reduces 25% Magic Resistance, in a large area.
**Shadow Mantle** - For 60 seconds, Critical hit chance is increased by 100% during Invisibility.
  
* **Abyssal Shadow Step** - Step through the shadows.
**Shadow Mark** - For 30 seconds, you cannot be seen. Return to your previous position when broken.
  
  
**Notes**
  
** Shadow Cloak and Shadow Mantle don't stack.*
  
  
**Conjuration**
  
  
**Spell List**
  
  
Spoiler**Novice**
  
  

  
* **Conjure Shadow Wolf** - Summons a Shadow Wolf for 60 seconds.

  
**Apprentice**
  
  

  
* **Conjure Shadow Atronach** - Summons a Shadow Atronach for 60 seconds.

  
**Master**
  
  

  
* **Conjure Shadow Monarch** - Summons a Shadow Monarch for 60 seconds.
  
**Notes**
  
** Shadow Atronach attacks deal magic damage and can reduce magic resistance.*
  
  
**Power**
  
  
**Power List**
  
  
Spoiler
  

  
* **Shadow Body** - Your attacks deal additional 10 magic damage and reduces magic resistance over 5 seconds for 10 Magicka.
**Dark Shroud** - You cannot be seen for 3 seconds. Cost 50 Magicka.
  
* **Shadow Form** - You deal and take 10% more spell damage for 120 seconds. Cost 50 Magicka.
  
**Notes**
  
** Powers are lesser powers.
Spells that add damage to your attacks have a base damage of 10. Their damage is increased on heavy attacks and is more effective on two-handed weapons than one-handed. The spells do not stack with spells that do the same from my other mods. Only one can be active at the same time. Spells are toggleable*
  
  
**How to get them**
  
  
They also get sold by a new Merchant found in the Windpeak Inn in Dawnstar with the [Dareni's Spells - Merchants](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/109762) addon.
  
New Vedors: Josiette at Anise's Cabin and Thernven at the Adventurer's Campsite East of Chillwind Depths.
  
Optional SPID file for distributing them to NPCs.
