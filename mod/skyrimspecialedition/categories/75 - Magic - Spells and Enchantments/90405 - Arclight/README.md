# Arclight
- Author: Darenii
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/90405


**Arclight**

  
  
Arclight is a shock spell pack designed to be balanced around vanilla spells.
  
  
**Destruction**
  
  
**Spell List**
  
  
Spoiler**Novice**
  
  

  
* **Arclight Sparks** - Deals 8 Shock damage per second. Shocked targets take extra damage over time.

  
**Apprentice**
  
  

  
* **Arclight Bolt** - Deals 20 Shock damage. Shocked targets take extra damage over time.
**Arclight Touch** - Deals 30 Shock damage in melee range. Shocked targets take extra damage over time.
  
* **Arclight Rune** - Creates a rune that explodes when enemies approach, dealing 40 Shock damage. Shocked targets take extra damage over time.

  
**Adept**
  
  

  
* **Arclight Chain** - Deals 40 Shock damage in a large area. Shocked targets take extra damage over time.
**Arclight Aura** - For 60 seconds, nearby enemies take 8 Shock damage per second. Shocked targets take extra damage over time.
  
* **Arclight Contact** - Deals 60 Shock damage in melee range. Shocked targets take extra damage over time.

  
**Expert**
  
  

  
* **Arclight Wall** - Creates a wall that deals 20 Shock damage per second. Shocked targets take extra damage over time.
**Arclight Flash** - Deals 60 Shock damage. Shocked targets take extra damage over time.
  
* **Arclight Wave** - Deals 50 Shock damage in a large area. Shocked targets take extra damage over time.
**Arclight Grasp** - Deals 90 Shock damage in melee range. Shocked targets take extra damage over time.
  
* **Arclight Charged Rune** - Creates a rune that explodes when enemies approach, dealing 80 Shock damage. Shocked targets take extra damage over time.

  
**Master**
  
  

  
* **Arclight Discharge** - Deals 80 Shock damage in a large area. Shocked targets take extra damage over time.
**Arclight Detonation** - Deals 80 Shock damage in a massive area around the caster. Shocked targets take extra damage over time.
  
* **Arclight Palm** - Deals 120 Shock damage in melee range. Shocked targets take extra damage over time.
**Arclight Storm** - Deals 40 Shock damage per second. Shocked targets take extra damage over time.

**Power**
  
  
**Power List**
  
  
Spoiler
  

  
* **Arclight Body** - Your weapon attacks deal Shock damage for 10 Magicka. Shocked targets take extra damage over time.
  
**Notes**
  
** Powers are lesser powers.
Spells that add damage to your attacks have a base damage of 10. Their damage is increased on heavy attacks and is more effective on two-handed weapons than one-handed. The spells do not stack with spells that do the same from my other mods. Only one can be active at the same time. Spells are toggleable.*
  
  
**How to get them**
  
  
They get sold by a new Merchant found in the Winterhold The Frozen Hearth in Winterhold with the [Dareni's Spells - Merchants](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/109762) addon.
  
Optional SPID file for items can be found from Altmer.
