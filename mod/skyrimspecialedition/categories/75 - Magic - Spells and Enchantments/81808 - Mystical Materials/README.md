**[left]﻿[b][size=5]Intro**[/left][/size][/b]
  
A while ago I've stumbled across the post  "[Any Material Enchantability Mods?](https://www.reddit.com/r/skyrimmods/comments/zskd8i/any_material_enchantability_mods/)﻿﻿" from [u/R-Wiley](https://www.reddit.com/user/R-Wiley/) on Reddit. The idea clicked and I decided to implement such a mod. So I've hit the lore to find more about materials origin and their properties. As a result **Mystical Materials** mod was created.
  
  
  
**Overview**
  
> ﻿﻿*"Calling Stahlrim an ore is like calling my forge a campfire. Some say it's "enchanted ice," but I think there's more to it than that."*― [Glover Mallory](https://elderscrolls.fandom.com/wiki/Stalhrim)

  
The concept of "enchanted material" is already present In Vanilla Skyrim ― [Stalhrim has this unique property](https://elderscrolls.fandom.com/wiki/Stalhrim#Enchanting)﻿ that makes Frost-related enchantments 25% stronger. So **Mystical Materials** expands this idea and adds some hidden magical properties to most of crafting materials, so that they synergize with different Enchantments to produce a more potent variant.
  
  
Here is a short summary of the mod:
  
  
The following 9 materials are now considered to be *Mystical Materials*:
  
  

  
* Dwarven Metal (+15%)
Ebony (+20%)
  
* Dragon Bones (+25%)
Moonstone (+15%)
  
* Malachite (+25%)
Orichalcum (+15%)
  
* Stalhrim (+25%)
Daedric (+25%)
  
* Silver (+25%)

  
They are grouped based on their "primary" element:
  
  

  
* Fire - (Fire, Health, Frenzy)
Shock - (Shock, Magicka, *Silence*)
  
* Frost - (Frost, Stamina, Paralyze)
Dark - (Absorb, Poison, Fear, Soul Trap, Conjuration, Destruction)
  
* Light - (Sun, Turn Undead, Banish, Restoration, Speechcraft)

  
Each group has one material that is of **Mythical**tier: **Dragon Bones**, **Malachite**, **Stalhrim**, **Daedric**, **Silver**. These materials have more potency and as such better synergize with Enchantments.
  
  
In order to benefit from **Mystical Materials** player must obtain a *Mystical Materials* perk. Mod provides two ways to get it:
  
  

  
1. Instantly grant the perk when the mod is loaded
Add the perk to Enchanting Skill tree, for player to unlock.
