# Arcane
- Author: Darenii
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/91602


**Arcane**

  
  
Arcane is a magnus spell pack designed to be balanced around vanilla spells.
  
  
Perk integration: [Ascension](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/89223) or
  
Perk integration: [Ascension 2](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/92000)
  
  
**Destruction**
  
  
**Spell List**
  
  
Spoiler**Novice**
  
  

  
* **Arcane Mist** - Deals 4 magic damage to health and twice as much to magicka per second. Damage to health is tripled against enemies without magicka.

  
**Apprentice**
  
  

  
* **Arcane Touch** - Deals 15 magic damage to health and twice as much to magicka, in melee range. Damage to health is tripled against enemies without magicka.
**Arcane Bolt** - Deals 10 magic damage to health and twice as much to magicka. Damage to health is tripled against enemies without magicka.
  
* **Arcane Rune** - Creates a rune that explodes when enemies approach, dealing 20 magic damage to health and twice as much to magicka. Damage to health is tripled against enemies without magicka.

  
**Adept**
  
  

  
* **Arcane Blast** - Deals 20 magic damage to health and twice as much to magicka, in a large area. Damage to health is tripled against enemies without magicka.
**Arcane Aura** - For 60 seconds, nearby enemies take 4 magic damage to health and twice as much to magicka. Damage to health is tripled against enemies without magicka.
  
* **Charged Arcane Touch** - Deals 30 magic damage to health and twice as much to magicka, in melee range. Damage to health is tripled against enemies without magicka.
**Charged Arcane Mist** - Deals 10 magic damage to health and twice as much to magicka per second. Damage to health is tripled against enemies without magicka.
  

  
**Expert**
  
  

  
* **Arcane Orb** - Deals 30 magic damage to health and twice as much to magicka. Damage to health is tripled against enemies without magicka.
**Arcane Grasp** - Deals 45 magic damage to health and twice as much to magicka, in melee range. Damage to health is tripled against enemies without magicka.
  
* **Charged Arcane Rune** - Creates a rune that explodes when enemies approach, dealing 40 magic damage to health and twice as much to magicka. Damage to health is tripled against enemies without magicka.
**Arcane Wall** - Creates a wall that deals 10 magic damage to health and twice as much to magicka per second. Damage to health is tripled against enemies without magicka.
  

  
**Master**
  
  

  
* **Arcane Eye** - Deals 15 magic damage to health and twice as much to magicka per second, in a massive area. Damage to health is tripled against enemies without magicka.
**Arcane Explosion** - Deals 40 magic damage to health and twice as much to magicka, in a massive area around the caster. Damage to health is tripled against enemies without magicka.
  
* **Charged Arcane Grasp** - Deals 60 magic damage to health and twice as much to magicka, in melee range. Damage to health is tripled against enemies without magicka.
**Arcane Devastation** - Deals 40 magic damage to health and twice as much to magicka, in a large area. Damage to health is tripled against enemies without magicka.
  
* **Arcane Radiant Mist** - Deals 20 magic damage to health and twice as much to magicka per second. Damage to health is tripled against enemies without magicka.
  
**Notes**
  
** Arcane Spells benefit from Ascension Perks.
**Arcane Corruption** - Arcane spells reduce magic resistance by 15% and magicka regeneration by 100% for 5 seconds.*
  
  
**Alteration**
  
  
**Spell List**
  
  
Spoiler**Novice**
  
  

  
* **Arcane Draw** - Pull enemies to the caster and return them after 5 seconds. Pulled enemies can not be pulled again for 10 seconds.

  
**Apprentice**
  
  

  
* **Arcane Pull** - Pull enemies to the caster. Pulled enemies can not be pulled again for 10 seconds.

  
**Adept**
  
  

  
* **Arcane Warp** - Pull enemies in a large area to the caster and return them after 5 seconds. Pulled enemies can not be pulled again for 10 seconds.

  
**Expert**
  
  

  
* **Arcane Vortex** - Pull enemies in a large area around the caster to the caster. Pulled enemies can not be pulled again for 10 seconds.

  
**Master**
  
  

  
* **Arcane Distortion** - Pull enemies in a large area around the caster to the caster and return them after 5 seconds. Pulled enemies can not be pulled again for 10 seconds.
  
**Notes**
  
** Dual casting pull spells reduces the cooldown by 50%.*

**Power**
  
  
**Power List**
  
  
Spoiler
  

  
* **Arcane Body** - Your attacks deal additional magic damage to health and twice as much to magicka for 10 Magicka. Damage to health is tripled against enemies without magicka.
  
**Notes**
  
** Powers are lesser powers.
Spells that add damage to your attacks have a base damage of 10. Their damage is increased on heavy attacks and is more effective on two-handed weapons than one-handed. The spells do not stack with spells that do the same from my other mods. Only one can be active at the same time. Spells are toggleable.*
  
  
**How to get them**
  
  
New Vedors: Mirailde in the Windperk Inn in Dawnstar and Dryore in the Bee and Barb in Riften.
  
They also get sold by a new Merchant found in the Winterhold The Frozen Hearth in Winterhold with the [Dareni's Spells - Merchants](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/109762) addon.
  
Optional SPID file for items can be found from Warlocks.
  
  
**Requirements:**
  
  
[Scrambled Bugs](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/43532)ï»¿ enable: Perk Entry Points: Apply Multiple Spells
  
  
**Compatibility**
  
  
Load before any mod that changes interiors like Lux or other lighting mods.
  
Arcane was created to be used together with Mysticism and Adamant, but they are not mandatory.
