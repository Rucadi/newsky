[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]? Additions:[/font]
  
[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]
  
• Improves color gradient, shading, and UV map scaling.
  
• Adds new meshes and particles. (For the changes in style as well)
  
• Gives higher particle count overall.
  
• Decouples lightning spells from the caster's hand.
  
• HDT-SMP Force Fields support.
  
• Optimization.[/font]
  
  
[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]This is just models replacer, no esl. Should be compatible with anything that doesn't alter the same meshes.[/font]
  
  
  
  
[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]? This mod replaces these effects:[/font]
  
[font=Lucida Sans Unicode]
  
• Lightning Bolt, Chain Lightning, Thunderbolt projectiles.
  
• Dwarven Spider shock attack, Sparks, and Lightning Storm concentration projectiles.
  
• Casting hands effects.
  
• Generic explosion effect used by Lightning Rune and mass explosion of Lightning Storm.
  
• Lightning Cloak effect.
  
• Impact effects for each of the spells.
  
• Wall of Storms hazard art.
  
• Inventory art.
