# Darenii Spells - Fire Frost and Shock Affinities
- Author: Darenii - Berill
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/122932


The idea of ​​the mods is to adapt Darenii spellmods for playing with [Know your enemy 2](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/93258?tab=posts).
  
What is done:
  
1) Mods got corresponding affinities:
  
Abyss - Shock
  
Arcane - Shock
  
Bloodmoon - Fire
  
Desecration - Fire
  
Necrotic - Fire
  
Nature - Fire for Stone Spells, Frost for Water Spells, Shock for Wnd Spells
  
2) There are two versions of files with and without "Raw Power". "Raw Power" spells retained original keyword and so should be compatible with [Ascension 2](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/92000).
  
3) Spells shoud scale with fire, frost and shock perks.
