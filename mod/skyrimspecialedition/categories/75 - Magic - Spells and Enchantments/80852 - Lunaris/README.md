# Lunaris
- Author: Darenii
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/80852


**Lunaris**

  
  
Lunaris is a moon spell pack designed to be balanced around vanilla spells.
  
  
**Restoration**
  
  
**Spell List**
  
  
Spoiler**Novice**
  
  

  
* **Moonlight** - Deals 8 damage per second to the undead and slows them by 25%.

  
**Apprentice**
  
  

  
* **Moon Fire** - Deals 20 damage to the undead and slows them by 25%.
**Moonlight Rune** - Creates a rune that explodes when enemies approach, dealing 40 damage to the undead and slows them by 25%.
  
* **Moonlight Touch** - Deals 30 damage to undead in melee range and slows them by 25%.

  
**Adept**
  
  

  
* **Lunar Fire** - Deals 40 damage to the undead in a large area and slows them by 25%.
**Lunar Touch** - Deals 60 damage to undead in melee range and slows them by 25%.
  
* **Lunar Aura** - For 60 seconds, undead in melee range take 8 damage per second and are slowed by 25%.
**Luminous Moonbeam** - Deals 20 damage per second to the undead and slows them by 25%.
  

  
**Expert**
  
  

  
* **Lunar Bolt** - Deals 60 damage to the undead and slows them by 25%.
**Lunar Grasp** - Deals 90 damage to undead in melee range and slows them by 25%.
  
* **Luminous Moonlight Rune** - Creates a rune that explodes when enemies approach, dealing 80 damage to the undead and slows them by 25%.
**Wall of Moonlight** - Creates a wall that deals 20 damage per second to the undead and slows them by 25%.
  

  
**Master**
  
  

  
* **Lunar Singularity** - Deals up to 100 damage to undead and slows them by 25% in a massive area. Nearby targets take extra damage.
**Lunar Eclipse** - Deals 120 damage to undead in melee range and slows them by 25%.
  
* **Lunar Detonation** - Deals 80 damage to the undead in a large area and slows them by 25%.
**Lunar Beacon** - Deals 40 damage per second to the undead and slows them by 25%.
  
  
**Notes**
  
** Moon spells cause sun damage, they benefit from sun perks.
Moon spells are the "frost" counterpart of sun (fire) spells. They do not damage stamina.*
  
  
**Power**
  
  
**Power List**
  
  
Spoiler
  

  
* **Lunar Body** - Your attacks deal additional damage to undead for 10 Magicka.
  
**Notes**
  
** Powers are lesser powers.
Spells that add damage to your attacks have a base damage of 10. Their damage is increased on heavy attacks and is more effective on two-handed weapons than one-handed. The spells do not stack with spells that do the same from my other mods. Only one can be active at the same time. Spells are toggleable.*
  
  
**How to get them**
  
  
They also get sold by a new Merchant found in the Temple of Mara with the [Dareni's Spells - Merchants](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/109762) addon.
  
Optional SPID file for items can be found from Khajiits.
