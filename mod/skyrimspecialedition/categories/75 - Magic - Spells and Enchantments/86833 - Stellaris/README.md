**Stellaris**

  
  
Stellaris is a cosmic spell pack balanced around vanilla spells.
  
  
**Restoration**
  
  
**Spell List**
  
  
Spoiler**Novice**
  
  

  
* **Astral Dust** - Deals 8 Magic damage per second to Health and Magicka.
**Astral Reconditioning** - Restores 4 Health and reduces damage taken by 15% for 5 seconds.
  
* **Astral Hand** - Restores 6 Health and reduces damage taken by 15% for 5 seconds to allies in melee range.

  
**Apprentice**
  
  

  
* **Astral Bolt** - Deals 20 Magic damage to Health and Magicka.
**Astral Touch** - Deals 30 Magic damage to Health and Magicka in melee range.
  
* **Astral Rune** - Creates a rune that explodes when enemies approach, dealing 40 Magic damage to Health and Magicka.
**Astral Recreation** - Restores 8 Health and reduces damage taken by 15% for 5 seconds.
  

  
**Adept**
  
  

  
* **Astral Aura** - For 60 seconds, nearby enemies take 8 Magic damage per second to Health and Magicka.
**Astral Star** - Deals 40 Magic damage to Health and Magicka in a large area.
  
* **Astral Contact** - Deals 60 Magic damage to Health and Magicka in melee range.
**Radiant Astral Dust** - Deals 20 Magic damage per second to Health and Magicka.
  
* **Astral Wave** - Deals 40 Magic damage to Health and Magicka in a large area.
**Astral Constitution** - Restores 12 Health and reduces damage taken by 15% for 5 seconds.
  

  
**Expert**
  
  

  
* **Astral Shard** - Deals 60 Magic damage to Health and Magicka.
**Wall of Astral Dust** - Creates a wall that deals 20 Magic damage per second to Health and Magicka.
  
* **Astral Grasp** - Deals 90 Magic damage to Health and Magicka in melee range.
**Astral Radiant Rune** - Creates a rune that explodes when enemies approach, dealing 80 Magic damage to Health and Magicka.
  
* **Astral Fusion** - Restores 16 Health and reduces damage taken by 15% for 5 seconds.

  
**Master**
  
  

  
* **Astral Epicentre** - Deals 30 Magic damage to Health and Magicka per second in a large area around the caster.
**Astral Core** - Deals 80 Magic damage to Health and Magicka in a large area.
  
* **Astral Cancellation** - Deals 120 Magic damage to Health and Magicka in melee range.
**Astral Star Shower** - Deals 40 Magic damage per second to Health and Magicka.
  
* **Astral Star Detonation** - Deals up to 100 Magic damage to Health and Magicka in a massive area. Nearby targets take extra damage.
**Astral Recovery** - Restores 20 Health and reduces damage taken by 15% for 5 seconds.
  
  
**Notes**
  
** Astral spells damage is increased by Sun damage perks.
Damage reduction from healing spells does not stack multiple times.
  
* Damage reduction from healing spells requires SPID to work on allies.*

**Power**
  
  
**Power List**
  
  
Spoiler
  

  
* **Astral Body** - Your attacks deal 10 additional Magic damage to Health and Magicka for 10 Magicka.
**Astral Motion** - You jump three times higher and take half damage from falling for 30 seconds. Cost 50 Magicka.
  
* **Astral Void** - Your Magic Resistance is increased by 50% for 10 seconds. Cost 100 Magicka.
  
**Notes**
  
** Powers are lesser powers.
Spells that add damage to your attacks have a base damage of 10. Their damage is increased on heavy attacks and is more effective on two-handed weapons than one-handed. The spells do not stack with spells that do the same from my other mods. Only one can be active at the same time. Spells are toggleable.*
  
  
**How to get them**
  
  
They get sold by a new Merchant found in the Temple of Mara with the [Dareni's Spells - Merchants](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/109762) addon.
  
Optional SPID file for items can be found from Khajiits.
  
  
**Requirements:**
  
  
[Scrambled Bugs](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/43532)ï»¿ enable: Perk Entry Points: Apply Multiple Spells
  
  
**Compatibility**
  
  
Stellaris was created to be used together with Mysticism and Adamant, but they are not mandatory.
  
SPID file included to enable certain spells for npcs. It does not distribute spells and is not required when the spells are not added to npcs.
