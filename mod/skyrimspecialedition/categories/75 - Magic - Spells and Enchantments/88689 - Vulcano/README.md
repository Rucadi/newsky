# Vulcano
- Author: Darenii
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/88689


**Vulcano**

  
  
Vulcano is a fire spell pack designed to be balanced around vanilla spells.
  
  
**Destruction**
  
  
**Spell List**
  
  
**SpoilerNovice**
  
  

  
* **Molten Ash** - Deals 8 Fire damage per second and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds.

  
**Apprentice**
  
  

  
* **Molten Bolt** - Deals 20 fire damage and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds.
**Molten Touch** - Deals 30 fire damage and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds in melee range.
  
* **Molten Rune** - Creates a rune that explodes when enemies approach, dealing 40 fire damage and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds.

  
**Adept**
  
  

  
* **Molten Lava** - Deals 40 fire damage and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds in a large area.
**Molten Aura** - For 60 seconds, nearby enemies take 8 fire damage per second and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds.
  
* **Molten Contact** - Deals 60 fire damage and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds in melee range.
**Molten Skoria** - For 120 seconds, nearby enemies affected by fire damage have a chance to errupt and deal fire damage and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds to nearby enemies.
  

  
**Expert**
  
  

  
* **Molten Wall** - Creates a wall that deals 20 fire damage per second and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds.
**Molten Fragment** - Deals 60 fire damage and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds.
  
* **Molten Blade** - Deals 50 fire damage and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds in a large area.
**Molten Grasp** - Deals 90 fire damage and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds in melee range.
  
* **Molten Lava Rune** - Creates a rune that explodes when enemies approach, dealing 80 fire damage and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds.

  
**Master**
  
  

  
* **Molten Earth** - Deals 30 fire damage and reduce attack speed by 25% per second in a large area around the caster.
**Molten Core** - Deals 80 fire damage and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds.
  
* **Molten Lava Detonation** - Deals 80 fire damage and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds in a massive area around the caster.
**Molten Palm** - Deals 120 fire damage and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds in melee range.
  
* **Molten Ray** - Deals 40 fire damage and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds.
**Molten Eruption** - For 120 seconds, after being hit heat up and explode for 40 fire damage and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds. This effect can occour every 10 seconds.

**Power**
  
  
**Power List**
  
  
Spoiler
  

  
* **Molten Body** - Your weapon attacks deal Fire damage and reduce attack speed by 25% for 3 seconds for 10 Magicka.
**Molten Heart** - Restore 50% of missing Health. Cost 50 Magicka.
  
  
**Notes**
  
** Powers are lesser powers.
Molten Heart has a 10 second cooldown.
  
* Spells that add damage to your attacks have a base damage of 10. Their damage is increased on heavy attacks and is more effective on two-handed weapons than one-handed. The spells do not stack with spells that do the same from my other mods. Only one can be active at the same time. Spells are toggleable.*
  
  
**How to get them**
  
  
They also get sold by a new Merchant found in the The Retching Netch in Ravenrock with the [Dareni's Spells - Merchants](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/109762) addon.
  
Optional SPID file for items can be found from Dunmers.
