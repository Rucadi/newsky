- Author: Darenii
- Game: skyrimspecialedition

**Necrotic**

  
  
Necrotic is a spell pack designed to be balanced around vanilla spells.
  
  
**Conjuration**
  
  
**Spell List**
  
  
Spoiler**Novice**
  
  

  
* **Corpse Explosion** - Explode a Corpse for 50 Magic damage.
**Inject Vile Shard** - Increase Health by 50 of summoned Undead for 60 seconds. Consumes 1 Necrotic Shard.
  
* **Necrotic Dagger** - Summons a Necrotic Dagger for 120 seconds.
**Petty Corpse Sacrifice** - Sacrifice a Corpse to spawn a level 5 Necrotic Spirit for 120 seconds.
  
* **Conjure Necrotic Skeleton** - Summons a Necrotic Skeleton for 60 seconds.
**Conjure Necrotic Spirit Hound** - Summons a Necrotic Spirit Hound for 60 seconds.

**Notes**
  
** Dualcasting a shard consuming spell increases shard consumtion by 1.
Sacrifice spells count was summoning spells.
  
* Corpse explosion damage is unaspected damage and will get affected by Ascension perks which increase damage.
Killing enemies with Necrotic Weapons will collect a Necrotic Shard.*
  
  
**Power**
  
  
**Power List**
  
  
Spoiler
  

  
* **Convocation** - Pull friendly summons in a large area around the caster to the caster. Can be used once every 30 seconds.
**Necrofication** - Increase your Armor Rating by 100, reduce weapon damage of nearby enemies by 10% and increase damage of nearby summons by 10% for 60 seconds. Consumes 1 Necrotic Shard.
  
* **Necropolis** - Sacrifice all nearby corpses to spawn Necrotic Spirits for 120 seconds. Each Spirit consumes 1 Necrotic Shard. Can only be used every 300 seconds.
  
**Notes**
  
** Powers are lesser powers.
The eye effect of Necrofication is fixed on the normal position of the eyes. Changing the eye position will not update the effect position.*
  
  
**How to get them**
  
  
They are sold by a new Merchant found in the Moorside Inn in Morthal with the [Dareni's Spells - Merchants](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/109762) addon.
  
Optional SPID file for items can be found from Necromancers.
  
  
**Compatibility**
  
  
Necrotic was created to be used together with Desecration, Mysticism and Adamant, but they are not mandatory.
