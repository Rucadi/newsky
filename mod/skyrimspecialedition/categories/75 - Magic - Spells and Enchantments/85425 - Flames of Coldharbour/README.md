- Author: Darenii

**Flames of Coldharbour**

  
  
Flames of Coldharbour is a spell pack designed to be balanced around vanilla spells.
  
The new spells deal frost damage and scale of frost Perks.
  
  
**Destruction**
  
  
**Spell List**
  
  
Spoiler**Novice**
  
  

  
* **Cold Flames** - Deals 8 Frost damage per second. Targets on cold fire take extra damage over time.

  
**Apprentice**
  
  

  
* **Cold Fire Bolt** - Deals 20 Frost damage. Targets on cold fire take extra damage over time.
**Cold Burn Touch** - Deals 30 Frost damage to enemies in melee range. Targets on cold fire take extra damage over time.
  
* **Cold Fire Rune** - Creates a rune that explodes when enemies approach, dealing 40 Frost damage. Targets on cold fire take extra damage over time.

  
**Adept**
  
  

  
* **Cold Fire Ball** - Deals 40 Frost damage in a large area. Targets on cold fire take extra damage over time.
**Cold Flame Cloak** - For 60 seconds, nearby enemies take 8 Frost damage per second. Targets on cold fire take extra damage over time.
  
* **Greater Cold Flames** - Deals 20 Frost damage per second. Targets on cold fire take extra damage over time.
**Cold Flame Touch** - Deals 60 Frost damage to enemies in melee range. Targets on cold fire take extra damage over time.
  

  
**Expert**
  
  

  
* **Cold Combustion** - Deals 60 Frost damage. Targets on cold fire take extra damage over time.
**Wall of Cold Flames** - Creates a wall that deals 20 Frost damage per second. Targets on cold fire take extra damage over time.
  
* **Cold Fire Grasp** - Deals 90 Frost damage to enemies in melee range. Targets on cold fire take extra damage over time.
**Greater Cold Fire Rune** - Creates a rune that explodes when enemies approach, dealing 80 Frost damage. Targets on cold fire take extra damage over time.
  

  
**Master**
  
  

  
* **Cold Devastation** - Deals 80 Frost damage in a large area. Targets on cold fire take extra damage over time.
**Cold Flame Tempest** - Deals 40 Frost damage per second. Targets on cold fire take extra damage over time.
  
* **Cold Fire Storm** - Deals up to 100 Frost damage in a massive area. Nearby targets take extra damage. Targets on cold fire take extra damage over time.
**Cold Scorch** - Deals 120 Frost damage to enemies in melee range. Targets on cold fire take extra damage over time.
  
  
**Notes**
  
** Cold Fire Spells benefit from Frost Perks.
Cold Fire Spells deal Frost damage.
  
* Cold Fire Spells do not damage stamina.
Cold Fire Spells slow enemies by 25%*

**Power**
  
  
**Power List**
  
  
Spoiler
  

  
* **Cold Fire Body** - Your weapon attacks deal 10 Frost damage for 10 Magicka. Targets on cold fire take extra damage over time.
  
**Notes**
  
** Powers are lesser powers.
Spells that add damage to your attacks have a base damage of 10. Their damage is increased on heavy attacks and is more effective on two-handed weapons than one-handed. The spells do not stack with spells that do the same from my other mods. Only one can be active at the same time. Spells are toggleable.*

**How to get them**
  
  
Spell Tomes and Staves are obtainable in the Atronach Forge.
  
They also get sold by a new Merchant found in the Moorside Inn in Morthal with the [Dareni's Spells - Merchants](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/109762) addon.
  
Optional SPID file for items can be found from Vampires.
  
  
**Compatibility**
  
  
Flames of Coldharbour was created to be used together with Mysticism and Adamant, but they are not mandatory.
  
  
Screenshots were taken with [Inferno - Fire Effects Redux](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/29316).
