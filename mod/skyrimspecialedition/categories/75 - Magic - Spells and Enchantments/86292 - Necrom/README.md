# Necrom
- Author: Darenii

**Necrom**

  
  
Necrom is an Apocrypha themed poison spell pack balanced around vanilla spells.
  
  
**Restoration**
  
  
**Spell List**
  
  
Spoiler**Novice**
  
  

  
* **Eldritch Tentacles** - Deals 8 poison damage per second and slows by 25%.

  
**Apprentice**
  
  

  
* **Eldritch Bolt** - Deals 20 poison damage and slows by 25%.
**Eldritch Touch** - Deals 30 poison damage and slows by 25% in melee range.
  
* **Eldritch Rune** - Creates a rune that explodes when enemies approach, dealing 40 poison damage and slows by 25%.

  
**Adept**
  
  

  
* **Eldritch Shred** - Deals 40 poison damage and slows by 25% in a large area.
**Eldritch Splash** - Deals 60 poison damage and slows by 25% in melee range.
  
* **Eldritch Aura** - For 60 seconds, nearby enemies take 8 poison damage per second and are slowed by 25%.

  
**Expert**
  
  

  
* **Eldritch Orb** - Deals 60 poison damage and slows by 25%.
**Eldritch Grasp** - Deals 90 poison damage and slows by 25% in melee range.
  
* **Eldritch Tentacle Rune** - Creates a rune that explodes when enemies approach, dealing 80 poison and slows by 25%.

  
**Master**
  
  

  
* **Eldritch Flash** - Deals 80 poison damage and slows by 25% in a large area.
**Eldritch Disruption** - Deals up to 100 poison damage and slows by 25% in a massive area around the caster. Nearby targets take extra damage.
  
* **Eldritch Injection** - Deals 120 poison damage and slows by 25% in melee range.
**Eldritch Eye** - Deals 30 poison damage per second and slows by 25%, in a large area around the caster.

**Power**
  
  
**Power List**
  
  
Spoiler
  

  
* **Eldritch Infection** - Your weapon attacks deal poison damage and slows by 25% for 10 Magicka.
**Black Tome: Eldritch Shield** - Your Armor Rating is increased by 300 but your magic resistance is reduced by 50%.
  
* **Black Tome: Eldritch Surge** - Your spell damage is increased by 25% but your stamina regenerates 100% less.
**Black Tome: Eldritch Frenzy** - Your weapons deal 25% more damage but your Armor Rating is reduced by 200.
  
* **Black Tome: Eldritch Corrosion** - Your and nearby enemies poison resistance is reduced by 50%.
  
**Notes**
  
** Powers are lesser powers.
Spells that add damage to your melee attacks have a base damage of 10. Their damage is increased on heavy attacks and is more effective on two-handed weapons than one-handed. The spells do not stack with spells that do the same from my other mods. Only one can be active at the same time. Spells are toggleable
  
* Only one Black Tome can be active at the same time. Black tomes are toggleable.*
  
  
**How to get them**
  
  
They also get sold by a new Merchant found in the The Retching Netch in Ravenrock with the [Dareni's Spells - Merchants](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/109762) addon.
  
Optional SPID file for items can be found from cultists, seekers and lurkers.
