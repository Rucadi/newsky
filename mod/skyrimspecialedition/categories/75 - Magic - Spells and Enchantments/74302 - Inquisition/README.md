# Inquisition
- Author: Darenii
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/74302


**Inquisition**

  
  
Inquisition adds 9 new summonable holy weapons to the game.
  
  
**Restoration**
  
  
**Spell List**
  
  
Spoiler**Novice**
  
  

  
* **Holy Dagger** - Create a Holy Dagger made of sun energy for 600 seconds.

  
**Apprentice**
  
  

  
* **Holy Sword** - Create a Holy Sword made of sun energy for 600 seconds.
**Holy Shield** - Create a Holy Shield made of sun energy for 600 seconds.
  
* **Holy Mace** - Create a Holy Mace made of sun energy for 600 seconds.
**Holy War Axe** - Create a Holy War Axe made of sun energy for 600 seconds.
  

  
**Adept**
  
  

  
* **Holy Bow** - Create a Holy Bow made of sun energy for 600 seconds.
**Holy Greatsword** - Create a Holy Greatsword made of sun energy for 600 seconds.
  
* **Holy Battleaxe** - Create a Holy Battleaxe made of sun energy for 600 seconds.
**Holy Warhammer** - Create a Holy WarHammer made of sun energy for 600 seconds.
  
  
**Notes**
  
** Holy weapons do not benefit from bound weapon perks.
Physical damage is iron tier against non undead enemies.*
  
  
**Weapon Sun Damage**
  
  
Spoiler

  
* **Holy Dagger** - 13.4
**Holy Sword** - 20
  
* **Holy Shield** - 17.8
**Holy Mace** - 24.5
  
* **Holy War Axe** - 22.5
**Holy Bow** - 26.8
  
* **Holy Greatsword** - 40
**Holy Battleaxe** - 42.4
  
* **Holy Warhammer** - 46.8
  
**Notes**
  
** Damage is increased up to 100% depending on Restoration level.*
  
  
**Power**
  
  
**Power List**
  
  
Spoiler
  

  
* **Retaliation** - You reflect 100% damage back for 10 seconds. Cost 50 Magicka.
**Lay on Hands** - Restore 500 Health. Can only be used once every 300 seconds.
  
  
**Notes**
  
** Powers are lesser powers.*
  
  
**How to get them**
  
  
They also get sold by a new Merchant found in the Temple of Mara with the [Dareni's Spells - Merchants](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/109762) addon.
  
Optional SPID file for items can be found from Vigilants of Stendarr.

[Scrambled Bugs](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/43532)ï»¿ enable: Perk Entry Points: Apply Multiple Spells
  
  
**Compatibility**
  
  
Inquisition was created to be used together with Mysticism and Adamant, but they are not mandatory.
