# Subtler Illusions for Mysticism
- Author: Cowerton
- Game: skyrimspecialedition
- Mod Page: https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/127152


[font=Arial]**Descri****ption**[/font]

[font=Arial]I like to feel stealthy when playing an illusionist so throwing glowing balls at people I want to gaslight just doesn't feel right to me. That's why I gave the spells in the Calm, Command, Fear, Frenzy, Paralyze and Silence archetypes near-instant travel time (so the projectile is functionally invisible) as well as removing their impact force (so not even the master spells send loose objects flying everywhere).
  
  
Optionally, you can make necromancy spells work the same way with Subtler Conjurations.
  
  
[/font]

[font=Arial]ï»¿**Installation**[/font]

[font=Arial]Install as you would any other mod - with a mod manager (recommended) or directly to the data folder. Load after Mysticism. If you use Subtler Conjurations, load after Subtler Illusions.
  
  
[/font]

**[font=Arial]Compatibility[/font]**

[font=Arial]Should be compatible with anything that doesn't directly edit the affected spells, scrolls, staves or magic effects; before you ask, Sorcerer does not do that.
