**?** **A Complete Experience**

  
* *Constellations is crafted to offer a comprehensive and cohesive role-playing experience, aiming to engage players for hundreds of hours*
*Every mod is carefully considered, with most receiving custom changes to ensure a seamless experience*
  
* *Features more than 50 custom-made unreleased mods and a multitude of mods specifically released for this collection*
*Regularly updated - refer to changelogs for the latest developments*
  

**⚔️** **Combat Redesign**

  
* *Heavily modified combat flow through **[True Directional Movement](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/51614)** and **[Precision](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/72347)** while ensuring it still feels smooth and grounded*
*Movement and attack animations are improved and modernized*
  
* *Enhancements include Accurate weapon hitboxes, Timed Block, Animation Cancelling, Lock-On, Dodging, and Magic Sneak Attacks*

**⚖️** **Gameplay Rebalance**

  
* *The mod list is based on*[*Requiem*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/60888)***.** This is a thorough overhaul of every aspect of gameplay mechanics, adds new content, and unlevels the world, which means that nothing scales with player level*
*Constellations touches on pretty much every aspect of Requiem balancing and includes a complete custom overhaul of many features and is constantly evolving to provide the best experience possible*
  

**?** **Main Quest**

  
* *Break free from the conventional Dragonborn story. In Constellations, you define your own path*
*Choose to be anyone you wish: a bard, thief, beggar, mage, druid, fighter, or something entirely unique*
  
* *Constellations was designed with replayability at its core. Each playthrough brings a unique experience with an endless array of adventures, ensuring no two journeys are the same*

**?️** **Survival**

  
* *Survival mechanics that immerse the player more deeply in the world without disrupting gameplay*
*Players need to sleep when they're tired, eat when they're hungry, and equip themselves against the cold*
  
* *All activities advance time, e.g. harvesting animals, crafting armor, brewing potions*

**? Magic**

  
* *Adds over 200 new spells and over 120 enchantments that seamlessly integrate into the base game*
*New spell themes include: Annihilation, Earth, Soul, Poison, Corrosion, Wind, Druid, Shadow*
  
* *Learning new spells is no longer as simple as consuming books and comes with a whole slew of new mechanics without being needlessly complicated*

**?️****Weapons and Armor**

  
* *Adds over 150 new weapons, including new weapon types with new animations (e.g. spears, halberds, katanas) and a lot of new unique weapons*
*Adds over 200 new armor and clothing pieces, including a lot of variations of existing armor and completely new armor sets*
  

**?** **Expanded Worlds and Adventures**

  
* *Adds major expansions like*[*Bruma*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/10917)*,* [*Wyrmstooth*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/45565)*,* [*Vigilant*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/11849)*,* [*Extended Cut: Saints and Seducers*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/72772)*,* [*Clockwork*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/4155)*,* [*Moon and Star*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/4301)*,* [*Undeath*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/40802)*,* [*Sirenroot*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/70917)*,* [*Heart of the Reach*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/76494)*,* [*Siege at Icemoth*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/109541)*, and much more*
*Features numerous new dungeons, points of interest, and complete redesigns of all towns, cities, and landscapes*
  
* *Improved many of the lackluster aspects of Creation Club content and integrated it seamlessly into the game*

**? Interactive Stories**

  
* *New Followers such as* [*Inigo*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/1461)*,* [*Auri*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/11278)*,* [*Lucien*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/20035)*,* [*Xelzaz*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/62893)*,* [*Remiel*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/51874)*,* [*Hoth*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/16137)*,* [*Astrid*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/132350)*and many more*
*Dialogue options have been drastically expanded, providing richer NPC interactions and enhancing player choices*
  
* *Significantly expanded Vanilla quests, providing more choices and extended narratives*
*Missives and new world encounters introduce random elements throughout your adventure*
  

**? Character Creation**

  
* *Players can choose their Race, Class, Religion, Birthsign, Faction, and Starting Location/Spells/Equipment at character creation to fine tune each playthrough*
*Added tons of new character customization options including new hairstyles, beards, warpaints, bosmer antlers, and much more*
  

**?️ Interface**

  
* *Modernized the UI with features such as boss health bars, floating health bars, a new HUD layout, Underwater breath bar, and much more*
*A completely redesigned UI and HUD skin with a consistent visual style based on* [*Nordic UI*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/49881)
  
* *Added beautiful paper maps for all world spaces, including modded ones (Examples:* [*Skyrim*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/62705)*,* [*Soul Cairn*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/55387)*,* [*Bruma*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/64037)*,* [*Vigilant*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/123407)*)*

**?️ Graphics**

  
* *Massive graphical improvements that stay true to Skyrim’s art direction while improving visual fidelity to modern standards*
*Realistic and atmospheric lighting overhaul using* [*Lux*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/43158)*and*[*Lux Orbis*](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/56095)*for interiors and exteriors*
